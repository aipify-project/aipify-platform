Phase 52 — Predictive Intelligence Engine (PIE)

Reusable multi-tenant prediction layer: future bottlenecks, churn risk, workload
risk, growth opportunities, follow-up risk, and SLA risk.

V1 uses historical trends, threshold rules, workflow analysis, and anomaly
detection — no advanced ML.

Safe-by-default: tenant isolation, no employee surveillance, workflow/process
focus, AI actions require approval, full audit trail.

Implementation:
- Tables: aipify_predictive_alerts, aipify_prediction_models, aipify_prediction_settings
- Worker jobs: detect_future_bottlenecks, detect_customer_inactivity,
  detect_workload_risk, detect_sla_risk, detect_growth_opportunities
- UI: /app/predictions, /app/settings/predictions
