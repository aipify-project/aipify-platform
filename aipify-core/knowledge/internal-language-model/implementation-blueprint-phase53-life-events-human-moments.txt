Implementation Blueprint Phase 53 — Life Events & Human Moments Engine
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)
Extends Gratitude & Recognition Engine A.89

Mission
Help people honor meaningful life and professional moments with warm, optional, consent-based recognition.

Philosophy
Human moments matter — celebrate with respect, never pressure. Consent before visibility; metadata before raw dates.

ABOS principle
Aipify strengthens human connection through thoughtful celebration — people decide what to share; Aipify prepares gentle recognition.

Objectives
1. Birthdays — optional, respectful, consent required (🌹)
2. Work anniversaries — 1, 5, 10 year Aipify tenure (🔔)
3. Certification celebrations — Sales Expert, Elite (🦉)
4. Sales milestones — partner portal Phase 41 cross-link
5. Community contributions — mentorship thanks (🦉🌹)
6. Personal achievements — reflection, gratitude, progress (❤️)

Birthday experiences
- Consent required before visibility or notification
- Never store raw date of birth without explicit opt-in
- Future scaffold: birthday_month_day_hash — documented only
- Gentle acknowledgment — dismissible anytime
- Pass-forward Digital Recognition Rose optional

Professional anniversaries
- 1 year: steady presence honored
- 5 years: lasting impact in everyday moments
- 10 years: decade of dedication
- Tenure metadata only — no external employment history

Certification celebrations
- Cross-link Certification A.37 / Enterprise Deployment Phase 37
- Sales Expert and Elite tiers
- Expertise earned through human review

Community contributions
- Mentorship thanks — Phase 47 Sales Expert community
- Employee Knowledge cross-link
- Advisory council and forum participation metadata

Self Love connection
- Reflection, gratitude, honest progress
- Never perfectionism or guilt-based motivation
- Personal achievements optional to share

Companion principles
- Warm, respectful, optional, non-intrusive
- Companion Identity A.84 tone
- Cultural sensitivity — en/no/sv/da locales

Privacy principles
- human_moments_settings — consent flags per user/org
- display_preference: private, team, organization
- No PII in RPC payloads — aggregate counts only

Distinctions — NOT
- Presence & Comfort A.90 comfort roses
- Sales Expert Phase 41 partner portal milestones (cross-linked only)
- PAME important people storage model
- LifeOS personal life areas
- Raw birthday dates without consent architecture

Prefer phrasing
- If you have chosen to share your birthday, Aipify may offer a warm acknowledgment — you can dismiss it anytime.
- Tenure milestones honor sustained contribution — not comparison leaderboards.
- Would you like to send a Digital Recognition Rose to someone whose support mattered?
- Metadata protects privacy — the person owns their story.

Avoid phrasing
- Announcing birthdays without consent
- Age disclosure or pressure to participate
- Artificial or intrusive celebration tone
- Storing raw dates of birth by default
- Leaderboard-style comparison for human moments
