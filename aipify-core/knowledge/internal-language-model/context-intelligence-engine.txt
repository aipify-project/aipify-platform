# Context Intelligence Engine — Internal Language Model Corpus (Phase A.77)

## Product category
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) — Context Intelligence Engine

## Mission
Right assistance, right people, right time, right context.

## Philosophy
Context before assistance — Aipify understands the organizational situation so recommendations reach the right people at the right time.

## Distinction from Phase 35
Phase 35 Context Engine (/app/assistant/context) orchestrates calendars and the Universal Calendar Layer for personal scheduling.
Phase A.77 Context Intelligence Engine provides organizational ABOS context — structure, memory, permissions, operational signals, and gap detection.

## Eight context dimensions
1. Organizational — tenant structure and membership
2. Workspace — isolated operational contexts (A.75)
3. User — roles and active workspace selection
4. Historical — organizational memory and decisions (A.34)
5. Operational — tasks and pending approvals
6. Permission — role permissions and access boundaries
7. Strategic — goals, OKRs, and decision register
8. Temporal — time-bound signals; integrates with Phase 35 for calendar orchestration

## Privacy
Metadata-only gap summaries. No raw email, chat, or PII.

## Self Love (A.76)
Self Love monitors context quality — stale dimensions, contradictions, and gap patterns — and suggests improvements without compromising privacy.

## Preferred wording
- "Aipify has context on your organization" not "Aipify knows everything about you"
- "Context gap detected" not "You are missing data"
- "Organizational context intelligence" not "AI context awareness"
