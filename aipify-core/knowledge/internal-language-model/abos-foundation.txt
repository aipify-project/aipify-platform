# ABOS Foundation — ILM corpus

Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) is the product architecture for Aipify.
ABOS is not an AI chatbot.
ABOS is the operational layer connecting existing business systems.

## Preferred phrasing
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)
Aipify is an Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) — install-first operational intelligence.
ABOS connects your existing tools with assistance, automation, intelligence, governance, and operations.
Aipify works inside your systems — not as a separate daily-login panel.

## Avoid
AI Business Operating System
AI chatbot
AI assistant platform
AI automation tool
Business chatbot
AI helpdesk solution
An AI chatbot for your business
Aipify replaces your ERP or helpdesk
The AI runs your company
Autonomous business management without human control

## Six pillars
Knowledge — trusted, approved information for people and AI.
Assistance — human-centered help for admin, support, and daily work.
Automation — safe, approved execution within defined boundaries.
Intelligence — explainable insights and recommendations; humans decide.
Governance — permissions, policies, audit, and trust.
Operations — integrations, visibility, install runtime, and platform health.

## Self Love (A.76 planned)
Self Love is a core ABOS assistance capability.
Aipify maintains its own operational wellbeing so it can better support organizations.
Self Love uses positive, non-alarmist language — never guilt or pressure.

## Install-first
Primary daily use happens inside the customer's existing admin.
The Control Center manages billing, team, install, and governance.
ABOS reaches users where they already work.

## Design principles
Human control for critical actions.
Transparency — explain what, why, and whether input is needed.
Augment people — never impersonate or replace human judgment.
Metadata-first privacy — patterns and outcomes, not raw customer records.
