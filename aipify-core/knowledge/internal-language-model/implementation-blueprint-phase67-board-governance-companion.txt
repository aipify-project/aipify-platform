# Implementation Blueprint Phase 67 — Board & Governance Companion Engine

## Mission
Help boards improve decision preparedness, strategic oversight, and accountability — preserve human judgment and independence.

## Philosophy
Governance creates responsible structures; strong boards navigate uncertainty with wisdom and discipline — not controlling every decision.

## ABOS principle
Governance safeguards long-term wellbeing — balances accountability with wisdom.

## Preferred vocabulary
- Board preparation — not board automation
- Strategic oversight — not management control
- Risk awareness — preparedness, not fear
- Decision continuity — historical context for informed dialogue
- Directors decide — Aipify informs

## Companion patterns
📈 Key developments and strategic topics for board review
🦉 Strategic topics and governance dependencies — thoughtful dialogue, not urgency
🔔 Changes since last meeting — policy reviews, violations, milestones
🌹 Achievements and stewardship milestones — recognition without pressure

## Self Love
Good governance often involves patience and thoughtful dialogue.
Reflection, perspective, deliberate pacing, recognition of contribution.

## Trust
What informs observations: policy metadata, violation counts, review status, approval posture.
Assumptions stated. Insights optional. Directors retain independence.
