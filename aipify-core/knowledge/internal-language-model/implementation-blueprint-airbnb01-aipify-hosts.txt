AIPIFY HOSTS — Phase Airbnb 01
Hospitality Business Pack for short-term rental operators.

POSITIONING
Aipify Hosts is the Business Operating System for modern hospitality businesses.
Does NOT replace Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo, Expedia, or direct booking platforms.
Operational layer around them.

TERMINOLOGY
Guest Experience Companion — not AI assistant
Aipify Recommendations — not AI recommendations
Aipify Insights — revenue and occupancy intelligence
Never market as AI-powered

MODULES
guest_operations — unified inbox, message suggestions, escalation
property_operations — maintenance, cleaning, inspections
cleaner_operations — turnover scheduling, quality approvals
guest_experience_companion — check-in, Wi-Fi, local guidance
revenue_intelligence — pricing, occupancy, demand
property_health_score — operational visibility
incident_claims — damages, disputes, audit logged
team_operations — roles, assignments, dashboards
hospitality_knowledge — best practices library
executive_operations — occupancy, revenue, arrivals, recommendations

PACKAGES
hosts_starter — 1–2 properties
hosts_professional — 3–25 properties
hosts_enterprise — 25+ properties, governance, multi-location

GOVERNANCE
Human oversight for sensitive actions
Cleaning documented before guest arrival
Incident actions audit logged
Approval required for claims and escalations

ROUTE /app/aipify-hosts
