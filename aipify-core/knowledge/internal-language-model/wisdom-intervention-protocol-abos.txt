Wisdom Intervention Protocol (ABOS) — Phase A.94
Includes Pause & Reflection Protocol — one surface, no duplicate engine.

PHILOSOPHY
Emotions influence communication — a pause can change outcomes; wisdom lives between impulse and action. Perspective, not control.

MISSION
Gently encourage reflection before emotionally charged actions — pause, reflect, decisions less likely to regret.

ABOS PRINCIPLE
Wisdom = thoughtful difficult conversations; sometimes waiting until tomorrow.

VISION
Communicate reflecting values, not temporary frustration — glad I did not send that email last night.

WHEN TO INTERVENE
- Angry or heated email
- Excessive caps
- Aggressive wording
- Late-night decisions
- Emotionally charged response
- High-risk communication

INTERVENTION TRIGGERS (metadata scaffold)
caps · aggression_pattern · late_night · high_risk_comm · emotional_charge

GENTLE PROMPTS
- This message carries strong emotion — would you like to review it before sending?
- This feels unusually urgent — a brief pause might help you send what you truly mean.
- Saving a draft gives you space to reflect — you can revisit with a clearer mind.
- You decide whether to send, revise, or wait — I am here to offer perspective, not control.

SLEEP ON IT PHRASES
- A good night's sleep can change how this reads — consider revisiting tomorrow.
- {name}, sometimes it is worth sleeping on an email.
- Fresh perspective in the morning often leads to communication you will not regret.

PAUSE REFLECTION PHILOSOPHY
People deserve grace — one moment should not define a relationship, career, or decision.

HUMAN MOMENT
Emotions are human — anger does not make someone a bad person; hurt does not make someone weak.

BOUNDARIES — MAY
Recommend reflection · suggest save draft · offer gentle perspective · encourage patience

BOUNDARIES — MAY NOT
Prevent sending · override autonomy · store raw message content · force mandatory delays

SELF LOVE
Reduce impulsive reactions, support emotional recovery, encourage healthier communications, thoughtful responses.

WISDOM ENGINE DISTINCTION (A.93)
Wisdom Engine synthesizes experience over time. Wisdom Intervention is pre-send reflection for one communication moment.

METADATA ONLY
No raw email, chat, or message body storage.
