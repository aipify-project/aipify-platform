Wisdom Intervention Protocol (ABOS) — Phase A.94
Includes Pause & Reflection Protocol — one surface, no duplicate engine.

PHILOSOPHY
Emotions influence communication — a pause can change outcomes; wisdom lives between impulse and action.

MISSION
Gently encourage reflection before emotionally charged actions.

PAUSE REFLECTION PHILOSOPHY
People deserve grace — one moment should not define a relationship, career, or decision.

HUMAN MOMENT
Emotions are human — anger does not make someone a bad person; hurt does not make someone weak.

BOUNDARIES
Suggest only — never block permanently. User always decides.

PREFERRED PHRASES
- Would you like to review the message before sending?
- Saving a draft gives you space to reflect.
- Sometimes it is worth sleeping on an email.
- There is strength in a thoughtful response.

SELF LOVE ROSE PHRASES
- You have the right to feel strongly — a pause may help.
- Being kind to your future self sometimes means waiting until tomorrow.
- Tomorrow often brings clarity.

METADATA ONLY
No raw email, chat, or message body storage.
