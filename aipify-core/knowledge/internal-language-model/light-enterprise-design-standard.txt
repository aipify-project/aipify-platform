Light Enterprise Design Standard V1 — Aipify Internal Language Model Corpus

PURPOSE
Lock official visual direction. Aipify is a Business Operating System — not gaming, cyberpunk, or dark-mode-first.

CORE PRINCIPLE
Users spend entire workdays inside Aipify. Reduce visual fatigue. Welcoming, calm, modern, professional.

DEFAULT THEME
Light Enterprise Theme — primary across APP, Platform, Growth Partner, Business Packs, Knowledge Center, Companion.

COLORS
Canvas: warm off-white (#F7F6F3) — NOT pure #FFFFFF page backgrounds.
Surface: white cards on warm canvas.
Text: dark slate primary, muted gray secondary — readability first.
Brand: Companion Purple (#7C3AED).
Accent: soft blue-purple (#6366F1) for links, charts, selected states, interactive elements.

NAVIGATION
Professional, organized, structured. Solid Companion Purple for active items — avoid heavy gradients.
No gaming, cyberpunk, neon, or hacker aesthetics.

HIERARCHY
Spacing, typography, layout, iconography, structure — not excessive color.

STATUS SYSTEM
Always icon + text — never color alone:
Completed, Not Allowed, Needs Attention, Information, Restricted, Verified, Waiting.

COMPANION
Match Light Enterprise Theme — Companion Purple, soft blue-purple, calm surfaces.
Helpful, professional, trustworthy, present — not intrusive.

EXECUTIVE EXPERIENCE
Comfortable on boardroom screens, large monitors, investor meetings, customer demonstrations.

AVOID
Black-first dashboards, gaming interfaces, neon, cyberpunk, hacker aesthetics, visual clutter,
excessive gradients, animation overload, Discord-style UI, developer-toy design.

WANT
Companion Purple, soft blue-purple, warm off-white, professional gray surfaces, enterprise appearance,
premium SaaS feel, modern typography, calm UX, executive readiness, long session comfort.

DECISION FILTER
Clarity over effects. Professionalism over trends. Enterprise quality over visual noise.

Unonight owns the night. Aipify owns the workday.
