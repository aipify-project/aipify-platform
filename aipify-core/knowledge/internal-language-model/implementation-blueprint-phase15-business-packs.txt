Implementation Blueprint Phase 15 — Business Packs & Productization Foundation
Engine: Business Packs Foundation Engine (Phase A.43)
Route: /app/business-packs-foundation-engine

MISSION
Package ABOS capabilities into understandable offerings. Customers buy outcomes, not complexity.

PHILOSOPHY
Offerings must be easy to explain and adopt. Productization is a presentation layer on the existing business_packs catalog — never rename pack_key seeds.

ABOS PRINCIPLE
Customers buy outcomes. ABOS delivers them through curated packs humans approve.

PACKAGING PRINCIPLES
- Recognizable problems — support overload, scattered knowledge, commerce friction
- Easy to explain — outcomes first
- Measurable value — faster responses, organized knowledge, clearer operations
- Scale — start with one pack, grow gradually
- Flexible — catalog packs plus modular add-ons from Module Marketplace (A.23)

PRODUCTIZATION PACKS (blueprint display names → catalog pack_key)

SELF LOVE
Reduce overwhelm. Start with what is needed. Grow gradually. Self Love is a value, not a trademark.

TRUST
Transparent review before activation. Explainable activation log. Honest about reserved packs. Distinct from subscription plan tiers.

WEBSITE PRESENTATION
Align with Positioning Foundation — outcomes not engine jargon. Digital coworker for first contact; ABOS for formal category.

DOGFOODING

SUCCESS CRITERIA (live)
