Aipify Home Companion — Future Companion Module
Status: Strategically approved · Planted · Do not implement until Companion Core, Family Companion, Memory, Presence, Action Framework, and Governance are ready

Core principle: A home should be a place of comfort — reduce invisible household workload.
Positioning: Companion Module for practical home life organization and management.

Aipify should: household coordination, maintenance reminders, reduce mental load, family organization, preparedness, simplify everyday life.
Aipify should never: replace professional judgment, financial commitments without approval, override decision-makers, unnecessary dependence, unauthorized actions.
