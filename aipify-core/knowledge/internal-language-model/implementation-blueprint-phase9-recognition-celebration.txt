# Implementation Blueprint Phase 9 — Recognition & Celebration Foundation
# Maps to Gratitude & Recognition Engine Phase A.89 — extend, do not duplicate.

MISSION
Notice effort, celebrate milestones, express appreciation more frequently.

PHILOSOPHY
People thrive when they are seen. Genuine, timely, human, encouraging, inclusive.
Never artificial or transactional.

RECOGNITION CATEGORIES
Individual — support, goals, consistency, growth
Team — milestones, collaboration, customer praise, knowledge
Organizational — major accomplishments, anniversaries, growth, legacy

BELL MOMENTS
Small celebrations — infrequent enough to retain significance.
Gentle presence, not alert spam.

RECOGNITION ROSES
Appreciation and gratitude — NOT romantic intent.
Distinct from Presence & Comfort A.90 comfort roses.

SELF RECOGNITION
Encourage acknowledging own efforts without perfectionism pressure.

SELF LOVE
Principle — not a feature toggle. No ™ in product copy.
Appreciation, sustainable growth, celebrate effort.

TRUST
Authentic recognition — specific observations over generic praise.
Metadata only — no raw messages or PII.

ABOS PRINCIPLE
Recognition strengthens people — small gestures create lasting memories.

VISION
Cultures where appreciation, gratitude, and recognition are natural in everyday work.
