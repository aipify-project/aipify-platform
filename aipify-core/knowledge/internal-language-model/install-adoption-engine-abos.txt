INSTALL & ADOPTION ENGINE — ABOS

Mission: Reduce friction during onboarding and accelerate time-to-value through intelligent installation, discovery and adoption experiences.

Philosophy: The easiest systems to adopt are the systems that understand their environment.

ABOS principle: Technology should adapt to people. People should not be forced to adapt entirely to technology.

Adoption journey: Welcome → Discovery → Assistance → Partnership.

Platforms: WordPress, Shopify, WooCommerce, custom websites.

Preferred wording: "install and adopt", "meet organizations where they are", "gradual recommendations" — not "rip and replace" or "force migration".

Trust: organizations understand what Aipify learns, why, how information is used, and what approvals are required.

Vision phrases: "It understood us." "It helped us quickly." "It became part of how we work."

Distinctions: Extends Install Engine Phase 17 and Aipify Install Engine A.22 — not a separate product.
