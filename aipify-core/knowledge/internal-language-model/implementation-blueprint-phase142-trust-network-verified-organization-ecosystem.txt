Build a voluntary Trust Network where organizations earn credibility through verification, professional participation, and transparent ecosystem contribution — not popularity contests.

PHILOSOPHY
Trust earned through verification and transparency — NOT popularity contests or manipulation.
People First. Metadata and professional signals only. Growth Partner not Affiliate.

DISTINCTION
NOT consumer ratings or social scores.
NOT Phase 116 internal relationship trust patterns (cross-link, extend).
Verification not exclusivity. Trust earned not assumed.
NO five-star ratings, popularity rankings, social scoring, gamified trust.

VERIFIED ORGANIZATION ENGINE

TRUST SIGNALS

Five-star ratings · Popularity rankings · Social scoring · Gamified trust · Universal reputation scores · Hidden scoring algorithms

REPUTATION SAFEGUARDS — DO
Voluntary verification · Professional participation · Certification through standards · Governance participation · Transparent profiles · Procurement documentation
