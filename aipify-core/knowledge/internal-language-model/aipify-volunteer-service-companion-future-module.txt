Aipify Volunteer & Service Companion — Future Companion Module
Status: Strategically approved · Planted · Do not implement until Companion Core, Community Companion, Purpose Companion, Philanthropy Companion, Memory, Presence, and Action Framework are ready

Core principle: Remove friction from doing good — coordination and consistency, not pressure.
Positioning: Companion Module for volunteers, community leaders, and organizations creating positive impact.

Aipify should: coordinate activities, strengthen participation, reduce admin burden, celebrate contributions, improve communication, sustainable involvement.
Aipify should never: pressure to volunteer, judge contribution levels, replace human leadership, manipulate emotions, dependency.

Categories: Community service · Skills-based volunteering · Organizational volunteering · Family volunteering
Volunteer recognition: Authentic celebration — avoid gamification undermining intrinsic motivation.
Insights: Encourage awareness — never guilt.
Actions: Reminders · Schedules · Checklists · Records · Follow-ups · Summaries — user approval required.
Organizations remain responsible for volunteer leadership.

Privacy: Participation privacy · No public disclosure without consent · Minimal data · Complete deletion · Autonomy

Design: Warm · Community-centered · Human-first · Companion-driven · Non-judgmental · Respectful of choice
Vision: Coordinate acts of service with ease, sustainability, and impact.

Distinct from Community/Purpose/Philanthropy Companions (prerequisites), Family Companion (cross-link).
People First. Growth Partner — never Affiliate.
