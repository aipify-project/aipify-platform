CORE PRINCIPLE
Aipify is the product name. Artificial Intelligence is the underlying technology.
Users build familiarity with Aipify — not generic AI product labels.

Artificial Intelligence has → Aipify has

MARKETING
Lead with outcomes and trust — not "AI-powered platform" headlines.
Prefer: Aipify works in the background so businesses can move forward.

COMPANION
Meeting and real-time assistance: Aipify Companion or Aipify Meeting Companion.
Visible to participants — never hidden recording or secret listening.

TECHNICAL EXCEPTION
AI may remain in: compliance docs, investor materials, engineering internals, code identifiers, routes, skill IDs.
Customer UI must still use Aipify-first labels.

ABOS
Product category: Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS).
Never: AI chatbot, AI assistant platform, AI automation tool as category.

VISION
This feels like Aipify — one name across every module.
Aipify informs and prepares. Humans decide.
