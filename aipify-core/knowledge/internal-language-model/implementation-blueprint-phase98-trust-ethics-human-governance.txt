Implementation Blueprint Phase 98 — Trust, Ethics & Human Governance Engine
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)
Extends AI Ethics & Responsible Use Engine A.46 — layered on Phase 54 and Phase 65

Mission
Deploy and use Aipify responsibly — ethical awareness, human oversight, and governance.

Philosophy
Technology serves people. More capability requires more stewardship. Ethics by design.

Vision
We trust Aipify because we understand it, govern it thoughtfully, and use it in ways that reflect our values.

ABOS principle
The future depends on the wisdom of how people choose to use intelligent systems.

Objectives — responsible Aipify practices
1. Responsible Aipify practices — document and classify use
2. Human oversight — approval, escalation, review thresholds
3. Governance transparency — explainability, audit, scheduled reviews
4. Values-aligned deployment — cross-link Purpose & Values Phase 95
5. Ethical review practices — routine examination of use cases
6. Trust stewardship — privacy, dignity, accountability

Ethical questions 🦉🌹❤️🔔
- Human judgment — support, not replace?
- Trust impact — explainable, optional, honest uncertainty?
- Dignity and autonomy — decline without friction?
- Unintended consequences — hidden automation, surveillance feel, accountability gaps?
