Mission:
Build a supportive Sales Expert community where partners learn from each other, share honest success stories, and grow through voluntary mentorship — never alone in the journey.

Philosophy:
Peer learning strengthens everyone. Mentorship is voluntary and respectful. Recognition educates — it does not boast. Community dialogue is constructive, professional, and trust-building.

ABOS principle:
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) grows when Sales Experts support each other — humans decide, Aipify informs and connects.

Objectives:
1. Peer learning
2. Voluntary mentorship
3. Best practices library
4. Community recognition (encouraging not shaming)
5. Collaborative problem-solving
6. Belonging

Mentorship program:
- Voluntary experienced → newer mentor links
- Guidance: demo preparation, follow-up rhythm, confidence, implementation
- Either party may decline or pause — no penalty

Community hub channels:
- Discussions 💬
- Success stories 🌹
- Q&A 🦉
- Inspiration 🔔
- Best practice library 📚

Success story categories:
first_customer, outreach_ideas, challenges_overcome, implementation_wins, renewals, best_practices, mentorship
Summaries max 500 characters — metadata only, no customer PII.

Community recognition badges:
- Mentor 🌹
- Community Contributor 🔔
- Knowledge Champion 🦉
Collaboration over aggressive competition — no public shaming leaderboards.

Trust:
Respect, professionalism, constructive dialogue. No harassment, shaming, or misleading advice.

Recommends discussions, mentors, and resources — complements Coach tab Phase 45/46.
