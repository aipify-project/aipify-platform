IMPLEMENTATION BLUEPRINT — PHASE 13: EXECUTIVE INSIGHTS ENGINE FOUNDATION

Mission: Clear executive guidance from organizational data — healthier decisions, stronger operations, sustainable growth.

Philosophy: Executives need clarity, not more dashboards — surface what matters with explainable metadata summaries.

ABOS principle: Clarity beats noise — explainable executive insights help leaders decide with confidence.

Executive objectives: what changed, why it matters, what needs attention, what is improving, recommended actions.

Overview capabilities: daily/weekly/monthly summaries, risk notifications, growth opportunities, priority recommendations.

Insight categories:
- Operational: health scores, quality findings, operations alerts, workflow bottlenecks.
- Support: case resolution trends, escalation patterns, support AI metrics.
- Knowledge: KC article updates, knowledge gaps, procedure coverage.
- Companion: proactive guidance, bell moments (🔔), gratitude (🌹), reflective pacing (🦉).
- Strategic: opportunities, alignment signals, predictive insights.

Since Last Time: metadata counts since previous login or 7-day fallback — support resolved, KC updated, high-priority tasks completed, bottlenecks, bell moments. No PII.

Companion examples:
- 🔔 Your team resolved 12 support cases since your last visit — steady progress worth celebrating.
- 🌹 Three teammates completed high-priority tasks — would you like to send recognition?
- 🦉 Before the quarterly review — here is what changed in operations health.

Self Love (A.76): perspective, sustainable decisions, team recognition, healthy prioritization — principle only, no ™. Route /app/self-love-engine.

Trust: explain data sources, assumptions, contributing systems — metadata only, full audit via _eie_log.

Distinct from Platform Admin /platform/executive and customer /app/executive briefings.

Dogfooding: Aipify Group — weekly summaries, risk surfacing. Unonight — commerce executive visibility pilot.

Preferred wording: "executive summary", "explainable insight", "leadership review", "metadata only", not "AI runs the company" or "automated executive decisions".
