ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 62 — Change Management Engine
Extends Change Management Engine Phase A.47 at /app/change-management-engine

Mission
Help organizations introduce new systems, processes, structures, and ways of working while supporting people affected.

Philosophy
People resist uncertainty, confusion, and lack of involvement — not change itself. Successful change requires communication, support, and empathy.

ABOS principle
Change is not just implementation — help people move confidently from one reality to another.

Objectives
- Change planning — structured initiatives with impact assessment and milestones
- Communication guidance — announcements, FAQs, leadership talking points, progress updates
- Stakeholder awareness — who is affected, concerns, where to raise questions
- Adoption support — training, companion onboarding, reinforcement — nurtured not assumed
- Progress visibility — milestones and adoption metrics
- Reinforcement strategies — celebration messages and sustained review

Change types (blueprint framing)
Operational — new workflows, process redesign, policy updates
Technology — software implementations, migrations, AI adoption
Organizational — restructuring, leadership transitions, growth-related changes

Change readiness assessment
Why necessary · Who affected · Concerns · Support structures · Success definition
