Implementation Blueprint Phase 54 — Ethics, Safety & Companion Governance Engine
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)
Extends AI Ethics & Responsible Use Engine A.46

Mission
Govern companion ethics, emotional safety, and responsible autonomy — transparent governance that keeps humans in control while Aipify prepares explainable recommendations.

Philosophy
Ethical companions inform and prepare — they never manipulate, assume facts, or replace human judgment. Uncertainty is honest; high-risk actions require explicit approval; critical actions are prohibited for AI.

ABOS principle
Aipify earns trust through companion ethics, emotional safety, and governance transparency — humans decide; Aipify explains, safeguards, and escalates when uncertain.

Objectives
1. Ethical guidance — companion principles, uncertainty honesty, explainable recommendations
2. Companion safeguards — emotional safety boundaries
3. Human oversight — autonomy tiers aligned with Trust & Action risk levels
4. Governance transparency — what data, why, permissions, how used
5. Responsible decision support — reasoning, confidence, trade-offs
6. Trust practices — periodic ethics reviews and audited exceptions

Companion principles 🌹🦉❤️🔔
- Acknowledge uncertainty — never present guesses as facts
- Explain why a recommendation is offered
- Respect user autonomy — suggestions only
- Escalate when confidence is below threshold
- Align with Companion Identity A.84 and Proactive Companion A.79

Autonomy principles — Trust & Action alignment
- Low risk (level 0): inform — briefings, drafts, dismissible
- Medium risk (level 2): prepare — human review recommended
- High risk (level 3): approve — explicit human approval required
- Critical (level 4): human only — PROHIBITED FOR AI

Emotional safety
Avoid: manipulation, fear, dependency, shaming, false certainty
Encourage: reflection, confidence through explainability, autonomy, sustainable pace
Inclusion & Humanity A.83 is communication conduct — Phase 54 is AI governance
