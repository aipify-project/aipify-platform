## What Self Love is
Self Love is a principle, value, philosophy, and reminder within ABOS — not primarily a trademarked product name.
Purpose: balance, wellbeing, reflection, sustainable growth, compassion.
ABOS principle: natural, warm, human, approachable — never overly commercialized.
Vision: recognizable expression of care through experience, not heavy marketing.
