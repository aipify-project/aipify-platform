ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 97 — Organizational Recognition & Appreciation Engine
Route: /app/gratitude-recognition-engine
Engine: Gratitude & Recognition Engine Phase A.89 (extends Phase 9 + Phase 53)

Mission
Strengthen belonging, motivation, and connection through intentional, authentic recognition.

Philosophy
Recognition genuine, not transactional — people who feel appreciated engage more.
Appreciation is not competition — celebrate contribution without forced visibility.

Vision
We celebrate the people who make our mission possible.

Companion recognition prompts (warm, optional)
🌹 Would you like to send a Digital Recognition Rose to someone whose steady support made a difference?
❤️ Customer praise arrived — shall Aipify prepare a celebration summary for the team that earned it?
🦉 A mentorship moment was noted — would a gentle thank-you to the mentor feel appropriate?
🔔 A meaningful milestone was reached — would a quiet bell moment honor the team effort?

Privacy — must avoid
- Forced public recognition
- Popularity contests and leaderboards
- Output-only metrics ignoring quiet contributors
- Celebrating only visible heroics

Self Love connection
You do not need to earn appreciation through burnout.
Steady contribution deserves recognition — not only visible heroics.

Distinctions
- Presence & Comfort A.90: comfort roses during difficulty — not organizational celebration
- Future Technologies repo Phase 97: /app/future-tech — phase number collision only
- Life Events Phase 53: consent-based human moments — preserved and layered

Aipify informs and prepares. Humans decide who to celebrate.
