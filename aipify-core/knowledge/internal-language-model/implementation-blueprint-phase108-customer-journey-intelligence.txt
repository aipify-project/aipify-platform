ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 108 — Customer Journey Intelligence Engine
Route: /app/customer-lifecycle
Engine: Repo Phase 86 Customer Lifecycle & Success Orchestration (_cso_*)

Mission
Strengthen customer relationships through journey understanding — experiences, needs, and opportunities.

Philosophy
Customers remember experiences, not transactions — clarity, confidence, and emotional connection.
Customer success comes before expansion.

ABOS principle
Customer success not extraction. Aipify Customer Success Companion informs and prepares journey mapping, experience optimization, onboarding intelligence, adoption milestones, and advocacy identification. Humans decide every engagement and expansion conversation.

Vision
We understand our customers more deeply than ever before.

Objectives
1. Journey mapping — awareness through advocacy with explainable progression
2. Experience optimization — satisfaction signals without manipulative design
3. Relationship strengthening — meeting summaries and follow-up scaffolding
4. Lifecycle intelligence — onboarding completion and adoption milestones
5. Opportunity identification — training, companion capabilities, Growth Partner, strategic review
6. Long-term success — advocacy and referrals — never pressure expansion

Customer journey stages
awareness → interest → evaluation → purchase → onboarding → adoption → expansion → advocacy

Journey insights (🦉🌹🔔)
🦉 Context before action — journey stage clarity before outreach
🌹 Experience quality — celebrate progress, not pressure gaps
🔔 Pause before push — customer success before expansion

Adoption intelligence
🦉 Adoption strength — explainable usage and confidence components
🌹 Capability expansion readiness — expansion follows value
🔔 Support before scale — adoption gaps need human support first

Customer Success Companion guidance
Use "Customer Success Companion" or "Aipify" — never generic "AI sales bot".
🦉 Would a journey summary help before your next customer check-in?
🌹 Shall Aipify prepare a celebration note for early success milestones?
🔔 Would deferring expansion conversation until the next success review feel wise?

Privacy principles — customer success not extraction
Never use manipulative journey design or dark patterns.
Never hide profiling customers cannot see or question.
Never optimize for sales alone while ignoring wellbeing.
Never ignore adoption gaps for short-term renewal targets.

Self Love connection
Every customer journey moves at a human pace — empathy, curiosity, patience.
Customers are people — journey intelligence supports relationships, not extraction.
