ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 138 — Organizational Purpose Alignment & Values Engine
=============================================================================================

Mission
-------
Deepen organizational purpose alignment across leadership, companions, and daily practice — reflection not enforcement.

Philosophy
----------
Purpose guides action — not words on a website.
Reflection not ideology enforcement.
Culture health reflects organizational patterns — never individual surveillance.
Humans define purpose and values; companions support reflection only.

ABOS principle
--------------
Stewardship through responsibility. Growth Partner terminology — never Affiliate. People First.
Aipify informs and prepares; humans define purpose and retain cultural authority.

Objectives
----------
- Purpose alignment center — statements, reviews, leadership sessions, culture health, dashboards
- Values framework engine — integrity, compassion, curiosity, responsibility, transparency, excellence, community, growth
- Alignment review engine — actions vs values, workflows vs purpose, companion consistency
- Purpose companion — reflection prompts; does NOT define purpose
- Culture health engine — organizational aggregates only; NOT employee surveillance
- Purpose integration — leadership, companions, Growth Partner, KC, learning, transformation, community
- Values memory engine — mission updates, refinements, cultural milestones
- Executive purpose reviews — leadership accountability through reflection

Companion limitations
---------------------
- Never impose beliefs or ideological framing
- Never claim objective truth about organizational purpose
- Never replace leadership purpose definition
- Never suppress diversity of perspective
- Never override governance or Human Oversight gates
