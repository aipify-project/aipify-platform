Relationship Intelligence Engine (Phase A.78 — Organizational)

Category: Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) — Assistance & Operations pillar
Scope: Tenant-level organizational relationships — internal, customer, partner, community
Distinct from: Phase 33 RSI (personal) at /app/assistant/relationships

Philosophy
Understand relationships within and around the organization using metadata only.
Aipify informs and prepares — humans decide. Never manipulate relationships.

Relationship categories
- Internal: employee, team, manager, executive collaboration and escalation paths
- Customer: account health, communication preferences, trust indicators
- Partner: implementation and advisory relationships aligned with Partner Success (A.73)
- Community: ecosystem and public stakeholder engagement trends

Context signals (metadata only)
- Interaction frequency
- Collaboration patterns
- Escalation paths
- Communication preferences
- Trust indicators
- Sentiment hints (illustrative, not judgments)

Applications
- Support AI: customer relationship context for triage and escalation
- Management: team collaboration and handoff visibility
- Operations: partner and community engagement patterns
- Executive: portfolio-level relationship health summaries

Ethical boundaries
- Never impersonate users or auto-send messages
- No raw email, chat, or customer conversation content
- No manipulation or pressure tactics
- Self Love monitors relational health gently — no guilt

Integration links
- Personal RSI: /app/assistant/relationships (personal, distinct)
- Support AI: /app/support-ai-engine
- Partner Success: /app/partner-success-engine (A.73)

Preferred wording
- "Aipify found a collaboration pattern" not "AI detected your team failing"
- "Recommended action" not "You must contact them"
- "Relationship context" not "relationship score"
