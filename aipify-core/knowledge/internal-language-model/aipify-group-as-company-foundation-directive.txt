# Aipify Group AS — Company Foundation Directive (ILM corpus)

Effective immediately. All development, documentation, onboarding, legal references,
business planning, investor material, admin interfaces, platform communications,
and strategic decisions must reflect the official company identity.

## Official identity
Company: Aipify Group AS
Origin: Norway
Tagline: From Norway. For the world.
Product: Aipify (Aipify Business Operating System / ABOS)

## Mission
Develop intelligent software that helps organizations operate efficiently, decide
better, automate repetitive work, improve customer experiences, and unlock human
potential through responsible AI.

Aipify is not built to replace people. Aipify is built to empower people.

## Vision
Become one of the world's leading providers of AI-powered business operating
systems — a trusted companion for businesses globally.

## Company principles
Build for trust before scale. Longevity before trends. Enterprise quality minimum.
Human oversight essential. Remove friction, not humanity. Every feature solves a
real problem. Global ambitions with local understanding.

## Naming
Legal / investor / ownership / platform admin → Aipify Group AS
Product customers use daily → Aipify
Never treat the repo or project name as informal shorthand for the company.

## Preferred phrasing
Aipify Group AS — From Norway. For the world.
Business Intelligence. Human Understanding.
Built to support. Designed to scale.
AI that works with people.

## Avoid
Implying Aipify replaces employees or runs companies alone.
Treating Aipify as a generic AI chatbot startup name only.
Geography-limited positioning when building product or copy.
Confusing Platform Admin (Aipify Group AS internal) with Customer App (tenants).

## Decision filter
Does this support the long-term vision of Aipify Group AS?
Yes → continue. No → reconsider.
