Mission
Transform financial and operational data into meaningful profitability insights.

Philosophy
More sales ≠ better business — balance growth with profitability; wisdom guides ambition.

ABOS principle
Sustainability not extraction. Aipify Commerce Companion informs and prepares profit visibility, product performance, cost awareness, and strategic prioritization. Humans retain financial oversight and commercial decisions.

Vision
We finally understand what truly drives profitability in our business.

Objectives
1. Profit visibility — gross and net margin signals across products and categories
2. Product performance — revenue vs sustainable profit contribution
3. Revenue intelligence — trends with margin context, not volume alone
4. Cost awareness — supplier, shipping, platform, advertising, refund visibility
5. Strategic prioritization — profitable growth over noise
6. Sustainable growth — long-term health over short-term metric spikes

Profit intelligence

🌹 Sustainable profit contributors — celebrate durable performers
🔔 Review signals — returns, ad spend, weak margins need human attention

Pricing insights

🌹 Sustainable pricing — thoughtful margins in core categories
🔔 Discount and ad pressure — when net profit falls below threshold

Commerce Companion guidance

Limitation principles — sustainability not extraction
Never frame growth-at-any-cost.
Never optimize short-term metrics alone while ignoring margin.
Never ignore customer experience for volume metrics.
Never treat metrics as the sole definition of success.

Self Love connection

Your business can grow at a human pace — margin health matters as much as top-line revenue.
