# Implementation Blueprint Phase 78 — Simulation & Decision Lab Engine (ABOS)

Mission: Evaluate significant decisions through collaborative exploration, structured simulations, and thoughtful reflection.

Philosophy: Strongest decisions emerge through exploration, dialogue, and multiple perspectives — objective is wiser decision-making, NOT certainty.

ABOS principle: Simulation improves understanding before action — wisdom emerges through exploration.

Core rule (Phase 78): Simulation predicts. Simulation never acts. Production isolated.

Phase alignment: repo Phase 78 = ABOS Blueprint Phase 78. Layered with Blueprint Phase 22 (_sdlbp_*) and Blueprint Phase 76 (_ssbp_*). Blueprint Phase 78 uses _sdl78bp_* helpers only.

Objectives:
- Strategic experimentation — major strategic choices in safe environments
- Decision preparation — visible assumptions and consequence awareness
- Cross-functional exploration — dependencies across departments
- Scenario comparison — maintain, accelerate, delay paths
- Consequence awareness — unintended consequences deserve discussion
- Leadership learning — reflection strengthens capability

Decision Lab environment (safe spaces — encourage curiosity):
- International expansion — market entry, governance, localization
- New product line — adoption, support load, readiness
- Department restructure — approval paths, continuity, workload
- Strategic investment — ROI assumptions, dependencies, governance

Simulation inputs (quality of reflection depends on quality of assumptions):
- Strategic priorities, org structures, resource assumptions
- Market conditions, operational constraints, organizational knowledge (metadata only)

Scenario comparison:
- Scenario A — Maintain current direction
- Scenario B — Accelerate investment
- Scenario C — Delay implementation
Compare thoughtfully — no simple scores replace human judgment.

Companion guidance (🦉 🌹 🔔):
- Assumptions deserve examination
- Scenario exploration strengthens resilience
- Unintended consequences require discussion

Collaborative decision-making:
- Leadership teams, board representatives, SMEs, cross-functional stakeholders
- Diverse perspectives — simulation supports dialogue; humans decide

Learning through simulation:
- What worked? Valuable assumptions? Concerns emerged? Opportunities visible?
- Reflection strengthens capability — metadata only, no PII

Self Love: patience, perspective, intellectual humility — uncertainty is natural.
Journey phrase: Thoughtful leaders explore before committing.
Route /app/self-love-engine — principle only.

Leadership insights (📈 🦉 🌹):
- Decision readiness summaries
- Emerging strategic observations
- Collaborative thinking examples

Trust: assumptions influencing simulations, limitations transparent, simulations support not replace judgment.

Limitation principles — forbidden:
- Guarantees and certainties
- Overstating confidence
- Reducing complex decisions to simple scores
- Auto-execution from simulation results
