# Aipify Brand Rule — Single Identity

There is only one Aipify.

Do not create multiple AIs.
Do not introduce separate assistant identities.
All capabilities belong to Aipify.
Users interact with Aipify.
Skills, Centers, Specialists, and Companions are extensions of Aipify.
Aipify remains the single trusted companion throughout the platform.

## Correct
Aipify Support capability — not a separate Support AI.
Aipify Executive Briefing — not another AI assistant.
Install the Analytics Center Skill — Aipify extends what your organization can do.
Aipify prepared this summary — not "The AI prepared this summary."

## Avoid
Multiple AIs · separate AI · another AI · our AI assistant · collection of AI tools
Each AI · This AI can · fleet of assistants · AI workforce as separate entities

## Skills Marketplace
Skill names (Center, Specialist, Engine, Guardian) describe business capabilities.
They are part of Aipify — never independent AI products users must manage separately.
