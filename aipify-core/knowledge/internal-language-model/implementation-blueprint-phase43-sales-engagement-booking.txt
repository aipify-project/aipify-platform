Implementation Blueprint Phase 43 — Sales Engagement & Booking Engine

Mission
Help Sales Experts schedule meetings professionally, follow up consistently, and prepare thoughtfully — trust through consistency.

Philosophy
Follow-up demonstrates professionalism. Booking should feel personal and respectful — never pressure or mass outreach.

ABOS principle
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) partners build trust through prepared, consistent engagement — humans decide; Aipify informs and prepares.

Booking center
Personal pages at aipify.ai/book/{slug} — discovery, demos, onboarding, follow-up consultations.
Extends organization_sales_expert_settings.booking_link and email template booking_link placeholder.

Calendar integrations (scaffold)
Google, Outlook, Apple — conflict avoidance metadata; honest pending OAuth status.
Cross-link Context Engine / Universal Calendar — orchestrates calendars, never replaces them.

Follow-up engagement
Companion nudges: rose thoughtful follow-up, bell scheduled reminder, owl prep suggestion.
Leverage organization_sales_expert_follow_ups — consistency demonstrates professionalism.

Meeting preparation
Customer background summaries (metadata), industry context, discovery questions, demo pathways — scaffold only.

Engagement history
Meeting history, upcoming bookings, follow-ups, open opportunities — metadata from customers, opportunities, follow_ups, bookings tables.

Self Love A.76
Sustainable engagement pacing — back-to-back meetings without recovery is not success.

Trust
Honest calendar integration status, metadata-only prep, empty booking notes scaffold, regional date/time via locale and timezone metadata.

Preferred wording
- "Schedule a discovery session" not "Book me now before slots run out"
- "Your follow-ups this week" not "You forgot to follow up"
- "Calendar connection pending" not "Calendar synced" when OAuth is not complete
- "Aipify recommends preparing discovery questions" not "AI will run the meeting for you"
