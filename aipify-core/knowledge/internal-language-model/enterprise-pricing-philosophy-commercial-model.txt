# Enterprise Pricing Philosophy & Commercial Model — ILM corpus

Aipify is priced as a Business Operating System (ABOS) — not a chatbot or token meter.

## Core principle
Value follows operational scope, outcomes, and trust governance — not token consumption or chat volume.

## Avoid pricing comparisons
Per-token or per-message chatbot pricing
Generic AI API meter billing as the primary product
Consumer chatbot subscription comparisons
Usage-only pricing without operational context

## Price on
Operational scope — installs, domains, users, licensed modules
Business outcomes — support resolution, knowledge retention, workflow automation
Governance depth — approvals, audit, enterprise security, SLA
Enterprise implementation and change management
Partner-led consulting via Sales Experts

## Positioning language (avoid → prefer)
AI chatbot with per-message pricing → Aipify Business Operating System with modular suites
Helpdesk AI add-on → Aipify Support installed inside customer systems
Token meter like a generic LLM API → Licensed modules and operational outcomes
Consumer AI subscription → Professional operations platform with human approval and audit

## Plan guidance (USD monthly — sales guidance, not enforcement)
Starter $79–$149 — Aipify Core, Install Engine, Aipify Companion, Aipify Support FAQ, human approval
Growth $199–$399 — Business DNA, autonomous Aipify Support, Workflow Orchestration, Proactive Companion
Business $499–$999 — Employee Knowledge, Onboarding Companion, Meeting Companion, Action Center
Enterprise custom ($1,500+ typical) — all suites, Executive Insights, SLA, dedicated success

## Enterprise implementation (NOK)
NOK 100,000–500,000+ for discovery, integration, governance design, rollout, and training.

## Sales Expert model
Independent business — Customer ↔ Sales Expert for consulting and implementation.
Aipify subscription: Customer ↔ Aipify.
Discovery NOK 2,500–5,000 · SMB implementation NOK 50,000–150,000 · Enterprise rollout NOK 150,000–500,000+

## ABOS principle
Companions (Support, Meeting, Onboarding) are Aipify product capabilities — not separate AI tools.
Never describe Aipify as replacing employees or as a cheap chatbot alternative.

## Vision
Organizations pay for operational partnership — not for counting messages.
Enterprise trust requires governance pricing — not cheapest token rates.
