Implementation Blueprint Phase 45 — Sales Coach & Enablement Engine

Extends Sales Expert Operating System A.95 at /app/sales-expert-engine.
Tab: Coach & Enablement — not a separate engine route.

Mission: Equip Sales Experts with supportive coaching, enablement guidance, and sustainable pacing — never punitive judgment.

Philosophy: Coaching strengthens confidence. Guidance should inspire thoughtful action. Success should never come at the expense of wellbeing or integrity.

ABOS principle: People thrive when they feel equipped and respected. Coaching should reinforce professional growth, not pressure.

Sales Companion roles:
- Mentor 🌹 — patient guidance and professional development
- Strategist 🦉 — pipeline priorities and thoughtful planning
- Motivator 🔔 — encouragement and momentum, never shaming
- Companion ❤️ — supportive presence through setbacks
- Performance advisor 📈 — transparent metrics, strengths first

Coach dashboard: monthly commissions, new customers, renewals, conversion rates, follow-ups, scheduled demos, personal goals, suggested next actions.

Daily briefing: supportive morning guidance — opportunities, renewals, activity comparison, consistency reminders.

Activity recommendations: contact local businesses, follow-ups, schedule demo, revisit inactive opportunity, ask for introductions — supportive never punitive.

Field sales coaching: personal visit, sector demos, in-person meeting nudges.

Demonstration guidance: checklists, industry talking points, discovery questions, recommended next steps.

Objection library: honest professional responses for time, existing system, budget, and think-about-it objections.

Communication coaching: email, meeting prep, discovery, follow-up, proposal guidance.

Performance insights: strengths and opportunities — encourage development, never shame.

Self Love A.76: healthy boundaries, sustainable pacing, recovery after setbacks.

Bell moments: first demo, first customer, first renewal, first enterprise, first year — cross-link Phase 41 _sprbp_* where appropriate.

Training integration: Learning & Training A.36, Certification A.37, Sales Expert Training Center tab.

Roleplay scaffold: practice conversations, demo prep, objection scenarios — cross-link Simulation Lab Phase 22.

Trust: transparent metrics, optional suggestions, metadata-only insight generation.

Dogfooding: Aipify Group Sales Reps and Experts validate coaching tone internally first.

Distinction: Phase 45 coaches daily; Phase 41 celebrates milestones — complementary, not duplicate.
