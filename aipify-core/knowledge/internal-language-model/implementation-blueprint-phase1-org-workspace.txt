IMPLEMENTATION BLUEPRINT — PHASE 1: ORGANIZATION & WORKSPACE FOUNDATION

Mission: Build organizational architecture that supports businesses of all sizes through secure, scalable, isolated environments.

Build philosophy: Build once. Build properly. Scale forever.

Hierarchy: Organization → Workspaces → Users → Roles → Permissions → Companion Experiences.

ABOS principle: Intelligence without structure creates chaos. Structure without humanity creates rigidity. Aipify combines both.

Companion foundation (metadata): communication tone, humor level, bell moments, Self Love reminders, recognition features, presence and comfort settings. Aipify identity stays consistent; customization stays appropriate and safe.

Knowledge Center foundation: each organization receives FAQs, documentation, procedures, values, learning resources, and companion guidance — the base of Organizational Memory.

Dogfooding: Aipify Group AS uses ABOS internally. Unonight is the first external pilot for real-world validation.

Preferred wording: "organization and workspace foundation", "isolated operational context", "tenant boundary", "workspace switching", not "separate admin panel" or "replace your system".
