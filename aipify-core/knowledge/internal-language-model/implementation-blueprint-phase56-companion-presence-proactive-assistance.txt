MISSION
Unify companion presence awareness with responsible proactive assistance — timely guidance that observes context, respects preferences, and prepares humans before urgency arrives.

PHILOSOPHY
Presence means being thoughtfully available — not always visible. Aipify observes operational context, recognizes help opportunities, and offers respectful nudges without interruption, fear, or dependency.

ABOS PRINCIPLE
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) companions observe, inform, and recommend — humans decide. Companion presence and proactive assistance work together: awareness without surveillance, guidance without pressure.

PHASE 56 OBJECTIVES
- Proactive recommendations — metadata patterns; explainable, optional, never auto-executed
- Contextual reminders — calendar, tasks, operational context; supportive tone
- Timely assistance — help before difficulties escalate
- Prioritization — what matters most today; calm focus without guilt
- Human nudges — dismiss, snooze, or act; never silent execution
- Respectful interventions — quiet hours, daily caps, Self Love pacing

COMPANION PRESENCE PRINCIPLES
- Observe — metadata summaries across operational, support, knowledge, executive signals
- Recognize help opportunities — preparation windows before deadlines
- Respect preferences — frequency, channels, quiet hours, category toggles

SHOULD AVOID

- Fear-driven messaging — no anxiety-inducing urgency
- Dependency framing — Aipify augments people, never replaces judgment
- Surveillance — metadata only
- Auto-execution — Trust & Action owns approvals
