Implementation Blueprint Phase 65 — Companion Evolution Council Engine
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)
Extends AI Ethics & Responsible Use Engine A.46 — layered on Phase 54

Mission
Ensure Aipify evolves responsibly by examining whether new capabilities strengthen or weaken founding principles.

Philosophy
Not every capability that can be built should be built. Progress is evaluated through a values lens; companionship requires reflection.

ABOS principle
"What should we build?" matters more than "What can we build?" — wisdom through restraint.

Objectives
1. Ethical reflection — routine examination against founding principles
2. Companion philosophy reviews — warm, wise, human-centered, supportive, trustworthy
3. Capability evaluation — structured review before build commitment
4. Value alignment assessments — cross-link Purpose & Values A.82
5. Community input — voluntary metadata themes, inform not prescribe
6. Long-term stewardship — trust preserved as capability grows

Council responsibilities
- Review proposed capabilities
- Assess Aipify principles alignment
- Identify unintended consequences
- Evaluate companion experiences
- Encourage responsible innovation

Guiding questions 🦉🌹❤️🔔
- Human flourishing — support or replace judgment?
- Trust impact — increase or decrease explainable trust?
- Dignity and autonomy — dismissible without guilt?
- Unintended consequences — privacy, surveillance feel, alert fatigue?

Representation principles
Leadership, product development, Sales Experts, customer representatives, community participants, ethics advisors
