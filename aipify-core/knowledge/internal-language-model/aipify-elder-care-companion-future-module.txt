Aipify Elder Care Companion — Future Companion Module
Status: Strategically approved · Planted · Do not implement until Companion Core, Life Events, Memory, Presence, Family Companion, and Action Framework are ready

Core principle: Help people remain independent — strengthen support systems, never replace family or caregivers.
Positioning: Companion Module for aging individuals and those who care about them.

Aipify should: routines, independence, practical coordination, family communication, reminder support, reduce caregiver stress.
Aipify should never: replace healthcare professionals, medical decisions, override family authority, fear or dependency, diagnose conditions.

Categories: Daily life · Family coordination · Practical assistance · Social wellbeing
Family access: who assists, shared reminders, notifications, emergency contacts, permission levels
Reminders: Respectful and supportive — never anxiety-inducing.
Emergency: Preparedness coordination only — human-led emergency response.

Insights: Promote confidence — never anxiety.
Actions: Reminders · Transportation · Print summaries · Family updates · Calendars · Checklists — approval required.

Privacy: Dignity · Autonomy · Consent-based sharing · Transparency · Complete data control
User control: frequency, shared information, involvement, notifications, permissions

Design: Respectful · Dignity-first · Human-centered · Family-supportive · Companion-driven · Simple
Vision: Maintain dignity, strengthen family connections, navigate later life with confidence.

NOT medical care. NOT emergency response. Medication reminders are scheduling only.
Distinct from Family Companion (prerequisite), Wellness, Life Events, RSI (cross-links only).
People First. Growth Partner — never Affiliate.
