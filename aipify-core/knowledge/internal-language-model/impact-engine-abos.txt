Impact Engine — Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)
Phase A.85

Purpose
Measure and communicate positive outcomes — impact, not activity. Meaningful improvements for people, teams, and organizations.

Mission
Help organizations understand how Aipify contributes to healthier operations, relationships, and outcomes.

Philosophy
Activity ≠ progress. Measure value for people, teams, and organizations — not busyness for its own sake.

ABOS principle
Improve lives, not busyness.

Vision
We are doing better than we were before.

Impact dimensions
1. Operational — smoother workflows, faster resolution, reduced friction
2. Customer — better experience, faster support, clearer communication
3. Human — wellbeing, sustainable workload, reduced burnout signals
4. Knowledge — fewer repeat questions, KC articles preventing requests
5. Strategic — clearer priorities, aligned decisions, evidence of progress

Impact reporting examples
- Support response improvement %
- Knowledge Center prevented requests
- Priority clarity improvements
- Self Love workload distribution

Celebrating progress
Bell milestone examples celebrate meaningful progress — not activity volume. Quiet bells for support improvements, KC deflection, workload balance, priority clarity, and strategic progress.

Self Love connection
Sustainability, wellbeing, recovery, and long-term success — not output-only metrics. Growth never at the expense of dignity or rest.

Trust
Transparent calculations, limitations, and assumptions. Metadata only — no raw customer conversations, emails, or PII.

NOT Value Realization A.48 (/app/value-realization-engine) — baselines and milestones
