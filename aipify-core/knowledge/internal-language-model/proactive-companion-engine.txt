# Proactive Companion Engine — Internal Language Model corpus (Phase A.79)

## Mission
Deliver timely, relevant, responsible proactive assistance before things become urgent.

## ABOS pillar
Assistance — augments people; humans decide, Aipify informs and prepares.

## Distinctions
- A.67 Companion Presence = floating orb, heartbeat, online states — system presence UI only.
- ILM proactive-guidance-language.txt = assistant reply language patterns (observe → inform → recommend).
- A.79 Proactive Companion = organizational engine for assistance categories, org/user preferences, nudge lifecycle.

## Assistance categories
1. Operational — workflow readiness, task follow-ups, operational checkpoints.
2. Support — queue awareness, escalation readiness, response preparation.
3. Knowledge — approved knowledge gaps, documentation review opportunities.
4. Executive — briefing preparation, decision context, strategic alignment.
5. Team awareness — shared coordination cues — never colleague surveillance.

## Companion communication style
Supportive by default — gentle, professional, never anxiety-inducing.

Style examples:
- Supportive: "When you have a moment, three support items may benefit from review."
- Supportive: "Your weekly briefing is ready — would you like a summary before your meeting?"
- Balanced: "Two operational nudges are pending. Review when convenient."
- Minimal: "1 approval pending."

## Boundaries
- No anxiety-inducing urgency language
- No notification flooding — daily caps and quiet hours respected
- No surveillance — metadata summaries only
- Human control — dismiss, snooze, or act on every nudge
- Self Love (A.76 planned) monitors fatigue and may reduce frequency

## User preferences
- Frequency: low, normal, high
- Channels: in_app, command_center, email_digest (metadata routing only)
- Communication style: supportive, balanced, minimal
- Enabled categories per user
- Quiet hours with timezone

## Integrations
- Command Center (A.26) — delivery surface for actionable nudges
- Companion Presence (A.67) — orb reflects attention state; does not generate nudges
- Notification Engine (A.12) — channel distribution with user preferences
- Quality Guardian (A.13) — quality signals may inform knowledge/operational nudges
- ILM proactive guidance — language adaptation for nudge copy via corpus, not duplicated detection
