ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 89 — Community & Collective Intelligence Engine
======================================================================================

Mission
-------
Strengthen learning, innovation, and adaptability through collective experience insights.

Philosophy
----------
Wisdom rarely lives in one individual alone — collective intelligence strengthens people, it does not replace them.

ABOS principle
--------------
Shared learning, not surveillance — Aipify surfaces governed collective patterns; humans decide what to adopt.

Privacy
-------
Shared learning, not surveillance. Avoid hidden profiling, public exposure of private experiences, using contributions without permission, and reducing individuals to data points.

Vision
------
Our greatest ideas did not emerge from one individual alone. They emerged because we learned together.

Objectives
----------
- Collective experience insights from governed, anonymized observations
- Innovation surfacing without prescribing outcomes
- Idea discovery — feature requests, process improvements, customer needs, training, product innovation
- Wisdom amplification — companion guidance strengthens people
- Contribution recognition — learning, support, leadership (cross-link Gratitude A.89)
- Learning organization evolution — KC, FAQ, training, product evolution

Collective intelligence sources
-------------------------------
Customer feedback · Sales Expert experiences · Support observations · Internal communities · KC contributions · Approved suggestions · Community discussions

Community observations (🦉🌹🔔)
-------------------------------
🦉 Pattern across the community — worth reviewing without exposing who shared it
🌹 Shared learning — you are not alone
🔔 Community insight worth exploring — informative, not mandatory

Companion guidance
------------------
Amplify wisdom, not dominate. Collective intelligence strengthens people — optional insights, not prescriptions.
