Aipify Pet Companion — Future Companion Module
Status: Strategically approved · Planted · Do not implement until Companion Core, Memory, Life Events, Presence, Family Companion, and Action Framework are ready

Core principle: Pets are family members — help people care consistently with less stress.
Positioning: Companion Module for everyday pet ownership responsibilities and milestones.

Aipify should: pet routines, practical coordination, preventative care planning, enrichment, preparedness, reduce mental load.
Aipify should never: replace veterinarians, diagnose, recommend treatments, override professional advice, fear or guilt.

Pet categories: Dogs · Cats · Birds · Rabbits · Horses · Fish · Reptiles · Small animals
Pet profile: name, species, breed, dates, veterinarian, milestones, household
Care: feeding, walking, grooming, medication scheduling, training, enrichment
Preventative: vaccinations, checkups, insurance, parasite prevention, dental reminders

Insights: Encourage responsible care — never guilt.
Actions: Reminders · Print summaries · Travel checklists · Appointments · Documentation · Packing lists — user approval required.
Travel: preparation, boarding checklists, emergency contacts, medication packing
Emergency: preparedness only — human-led decisions

Privacy: Minimal data · Transparency · Deletion · Household boundaries · User autonomy
User control: frequency, household access, involvement, notifications, visibility

Design: Warm · Human-centered · Companion-driven · Non-judgmental · Practical · Low friction
Vision: Love, attention, and organization pets deserve — everyday ownership made easier.

NOT veterinary care. Medication reminders are scheduling only.
Distinct from Family Companion, Life Events, Elder Care, Wellness (cross-links only).
