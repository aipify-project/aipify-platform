IMPLEMENTATION BLUEPRINT — PHASE 11: SUPPORT ENGINE FOUNDATION

Mission: Faster support and higher quality through intelligent assistance, Knowledge Center utilization, and responsible escalation.

Philosophy: People want help solved — quick, accurate, respectful resolution. AI assists; humans decide on sensitive topics.

ABOS principle: Support should feel human — technology accelerates routine work while keeping people in control of sensitive decisions.

Support objectives: FAQs, KC utilization, assist agents, drafts, escalate, consistency.

Support tiers:
- Tier 1 Self-Service: FAQs, articles, guided troubleshooting, widget self-help (automatic mode).
- Tier 2 Assisted: recommendations, drafts, enrichment, context (draft mode).
- Tier 3 Human Escalation: sensitive, high-risk, complex, exceptional (manual/draft with escalation).

Case management: priority, status, ownership, AI summary, escalation, resolution, satisfaction — maps to organization_support_cases.

KC connection: repeated questions → FAQ gaps; resolutions → article suggestions; outdated docs → review recs; retrieve_knowledge_for_ai in suggest_support_ai_response.

Self Love (A.76): reduce repetitive work, workload balance, bottlenecks, celebrate resolutions — principle only, no ™. Route /app/self-love-engine.

Trust: customers know when AI assists; orgs understand recommendations, escalations, generation — transparency via _sai_log metadata.

Distinct from ASO at /app/settings/support-operations — ASO governs autonomy/triage; Support AI A.7 handles customer-facing cases.

Dogfooding: Aipify Group — response times, KC gaps, escalation. Unonight — commerce support pilot.

Preferred wording: "AI-assisted draft", "human review", "knowledge-driven answer", "escalate when uncertain", not "fully automated support" or "AI replaces agents".

Integration links: KC A.5, Approvals, Human Oversight A.40, Quality Guardian A.13, Self Love A.76, Unonight Pilot.
