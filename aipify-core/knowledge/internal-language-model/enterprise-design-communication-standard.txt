Enterprise Design & Communication Standard — Aipify Internal Language Model Corpus

PURPOSE
Enterprise-ready design and language for organizations with 50–10,000 employees.
Fortune 500 operational software influence. Trust infrastructure — not cosmetic polish.

DEFAULT ASSUMPTION
Audience: 50–10,000 employee organization. Design bar: Microsoft professionalism, Apple simplicity,
GitHub trust, Stripe clarity, Aipify humanity. Security baseline: 2FA, audit, RBAC expected.

PHILOSOPHY
People First. Technology Second. Self Love. Wisdom before speed.
Companionship before replacement. Stewardship through responsibility. Trust earned, never manipulated.

LANGUAGE — DO NOT
- Create your account / Create account / Sign up (registration)
- AI Assistant / AI Support / Resolved by AI / bot / copilot (customer-facing)
- AI chatbot / AI assistant platform / AI automation tool (product category)
- Affiliate / Affiliate program / Referral partner (use Growth Partner)
- Slang, memes, emoji spam, fake urgency, guilt-based motivation
- Get Started Free!!! / Join the revolution / Supercharged AI

LANGUAGE — DO
- Establish your Aipify Workspace / Establish workspace / Create Aipify Workspace
- Aipify Companion / Aipify Support / Resolved by Aipify
- Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)
- Growth Partner / Growth Partner Operations Center
- Aipify recommends / Your Companion can prepare / Awaiting your approval
- Set up your Aipify Business Operating System in a few guided steps

ONBOARDING
Registration is workspace establishment — Day One metadata for ABOS, not throwaway signup.
Premium but not overwhelming. People First. Security step respectful — skip allowed with clear enable path.

SECURITY COPY
- Two-factor authentication required for organization administrators (where policy applies)
- Protect your workspace — not "extra security hassle"
- Audit trail links beside sensitive actions
- Customer owns data; Aipify owns intelligence layer

GROWTH PARTNER
Official term: Growth Partner — NEVER Affiliate in product UI, marketing, KC, or Cursor specs.

THE AIPIFY STANDARD (five questions)
1. Enterprise-ready for 500–10,000 employee orgs?
2. People First — dignity and human accountability?
3. Clear and calm — next action obvious without hype?
4. Trustworthy — security, limits, audit visible and honest?
5. Distinctly Aipify — ABOS / Companion / workspace language?
