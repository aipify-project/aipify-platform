ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 52 — Collective Learning & Wisdom Engine
===============================================================================

Mission
-------
Compound anonymized collective wisdom across the ecosystem — pattern recognition, best practice evolution, and responsible insights that inform without prescribing.

Philosophy
----------
Wisdom grows when experiences are shared responsibly. Collective observations inform teams — they never prescribe outcomes. Humans decide; Aipify surfaces patterns from governed, anonymized contributions.

ABOS principle
--------------
Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) strengthens when collective learning compounds safely — metadata patterns across the ecosystem, never confidential records or org identity.

Inform not prescribe
--------------------
Collective observations inform — they do not prescribe. Humans decide what to adopt from collective wisdom.

Objectives
----------
- Pattern recognition from anonymized community contributions
- Best practice evolution across support, sales, implementation, operational, training
- Ecosystem observations — never competitive intelligence or confidential content
- Knowledge evolution — KC, training, certification scaffolds
- Continuous improvement through governed participation
- Responsible insights with explainability and optional adoption

Collective observations (🦉🌹🔔)
--------------------------------
🦉 Pattern worth reviewing — without exposing who contributed
🌹 Shared challenge — you are not alone
🔔 Evolved best practice signal — explore when ready
🦉 Ecosystem theme surfaced — informative, not mandatory

Best practice evolution domains
-------------------------------
Support: triage workflows, escalation patterns, template effectiveness
Sales: discovery approaches, renewal patterns, ethical outreach
Implementation: onboarding scope, install adoption, handoff practices
Operational: workflow optimization, approval cadence, automation metadata
Training: role-based paths, certification readiness, academy effectiveness

Anonymization principles
------------------------
Aggregates only — aligned with lib/impact/privacy.ts forbidden-keys mindset.
No identities, PII, confidential records, competitive intelligence, or org identity.
Explicit governance before publication. Observations inform — never auto-adopt.
