ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 21 — Mobile Companion Engine
================================================================

Engine: Notification & Communication Engine (Phase A.17)
Route: /app/notification-communication-engine
Doc: IMPLEMENTATION_BLUEPRINT_PHASE21_MOBILE_COMPANION.md

Mission
-------
Extend Aipify beyond the desk — thoughtful mobile companion experiences; available wherever people work.

Philosophy
----------
Mobile creates clarity, not distraction — the right information at the right moment.
One thoughtful interaction at a time.

ABOS principle
--------------
Companionship is not limited to a desk — support travels with people who need it.

Mapping note
------------
Native mobile app is not built yet. Phase 21 extends A.17 as the mobile-ready companion
communication layer plus mobile dashboard metadata blocks. Do not duplicate Desktop Companion
Phase 61 / Blueprint Phase 20, Desktop Command Center Phase 27, or Companion Presence A.67.

Companion objectives
--------------------
- Since Last Time — Operations Center A.32 via _ocf_since_last_time_summary
- Mobile notifications — A.17 preferences, digests, actionable alerts
- Support queue awareness — Support AI A.7
- Task follow-up — Unified Tasks A.62
- Executive insights — Executive Insights A.35
- Knowledge access — Knowledge Center A.5
- Companion conversations — Proactive Companion A.79
- Recognition — Gratitude Recognition A.89

Companion experiences
---------------------
🌹 Morning attention — calm clarity for the day ahead
🔔 Milestone — celebrate steady progress without pressure
🦉 Strategic development — thoughtful surfacing before reviews
❤️ Self Love intense week — sustainable productivity, never guilt

Mobile dashboard blocks
-----------------------
Today's priorities · Open tasks · Support activity · Executive highlights · Bell Moments · Recognition opportunities
Live counts from existing tables with to_regclass checks — empty-safe, counts only.

Notification principles
-----------------------
Relevant · Configurable · Respectful · Timely
Avoid excessive urgency, notification fatigue, and unnecessary interruptions.

Self Love connection
--------------------
Quiet hours, reflection prompts, celebrate progress, sustainable productivity.
Route: /app/self-love-engine — principle only, not wellbeing content storage.

Trust connection
----------------
Explain why notifications appear, what metadata is accessed, how to adjust preferences.
Future native app — mobile permissions transparency when push ships.
