Mission
-------
Profitable, sustainable dropshipping via intelligent coordination.

Philosophy
----------
Right products + reliable suppliers + positive customer experience — wisdom guides operations, not listing thousands of products.

Vision
------
We understand our operations better than ever before.

ABOS principle
--------------
Humans accountable — Aipify Operations Companion informs and prepares supplier monitoring, order visibility, delivery intelligence, and profitability insights; manual supplier actions and approval gates remain default; no silent auto-removal.

Objectives (6)
--------------
1. Supplier monitoring — reliability, delivery consistency, quality signals, escalation readiness
2. Order visibility — end-to-end tracking from receipt through customer follow-up
3. Profit tracking — margin, shipping cost, supplier fee, advertising impact awareness
4. Delivery intelligence — delay detection, regional disruption, proactive messaging
5. Product lifecycle — launch → monitor → optimize → evaluate profitability → retire
6. Operational decision support — recommendations with rationale; humans decide

Companion guidance (Operations Companion — not AI dropship bot)
----------------------------------------------------------------
🦉 Global Dropship Co delivery times have increased — would a supplier comparison summary help before you decide?
🌹 Nordic Fitness Supply scores remain strong — shall I prepare a limited test-volume checklist for the seasonal push?
🔔 Budget Import Ltd quality signals are mixed — would pausing new orders while you review alternatives feel wise?

- Approval-required automations — no silent supplier or catalog changes
