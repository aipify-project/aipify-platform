ORCHESTRATION OBJECTIVES
- Friction reduction — coordinate repetitive operational steps
- Approval-aware execution — predefined rules and trust-aligned gates
- Cross-module orchestration — Support, Sales Expert, Commerce, Integration Engine, KC
- Explainable automation — reason, trigger, systems, outcomes
- Operational consistency — templates and audit trails
- Human judgment preservation — strategic, financial, legal decisions stay with humans

AUTONOMY LEVELS
Level 1 Observe — approval required — detect patterns, surface signals, recommend orchestration
Level 2 Assist — approval required — prepare drafts, queue steps, assemble context
Level 3 Execute — predefined rules — notifications, tasks, status updates within trust boundaries
Level 4 Orchestrate — governance required — multi-step flows across systems

Sensitive communications — customer-facing disputes — level 2 review
Legal and compliance — policy exceptions — level 4 human review
