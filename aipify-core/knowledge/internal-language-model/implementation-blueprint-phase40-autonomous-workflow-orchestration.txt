Implementation Blueprint Phase 40 — Autonomous Workflow Orchestration Engine
ABOS extends Workflow Orchestration Engine Phase A.42 at /app/workflow-orchestration-engine

MISSION
Turn intentions into action — automation amplifies humans, does not eliminate judgment.

PHILOSOPHY
Organizations remain in control — approved multi-step workflows execute with explainability and human checkpoints, never silent critical automation.

ABOS PRINCIPLE
Approved workflow orchestration inside the Aipify Business Operating System — humans design processes; Aipify prepares, explains, and executes within trust boundaries.

CORE RULE
Workflows are never auto-created by AI. Templates require explicit human instantiation.

WORKFLOW OBJECTIVES
- Recommendations — explainable suggestions from operational signals
- Multi-step automations — human-defined sequences with checkpoints
- Cross-platform actions — coordinated steps via Integration Engine A.8
- Approval checkpoints — Human Oversight A.40 and Trust & Action alignment
- Consistency — templates and audit trails
- Process documentation — KC A.5 and Document Output A.59

WORKFLOW EXAMPLE CATEGORIES
Support: case triage, escalation, stakeholder notification, summaries
Knowledge: FAQ updates, outdated content, documentation suggestions
Sales: follow-ups, welcome sequences, onboarding tasks, Sales Expert notify
Financial: subscription events, renewal reminders
Executive: weekly summaries, strategic concerns, opportunities

APPROVAL PRINCIPLES
Low risk (level 0): notifications, reminders, summaries — may auto-execute
Medium risk (level 2): drafts, customer comms, workflow initiation — review recommended
High risk (level 3): financial, permissions, irreversible — explicit approval required
Critical (level 4): prohibited for AI — human-only via Trust & Action Engine
