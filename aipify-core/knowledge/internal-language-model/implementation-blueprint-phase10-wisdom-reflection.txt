IMPLEMENTATION BLUEPRINT — PHASE 10: WISDOM & REFLECTION INTERVENTIONS FOUNDATION

Mission: Pause, reflect, consider consequences — preserve human autonomy.

Philosophy: Intelligence = options; wisdom = perspective. A short pause prevents regret.

ABOS principle: Wisdom = thoughtful difficult conversations; sometimes waiting until tomorrow.

Vision: Communicate reflecting values, not temporary frustration — glad I did not send that email last night.

Intervention principles: encourage reflection, surface trade-offs, offer lessons, long-term thinking, preserve autonomy.

Intervention scenarios:
- Communication: caps, aggressive wording, emotional charge, late-night emails
- Decision: high-risk approvals, org changes, governance, escalation
- Operational: failed processes, burnout indicators, unsustainable practices, doc neglect

Communication examples:
- 🌹 Self Love: You have the right to feel strongly — a pause may help you respond in a way you will respect later.
- 🦉 Wisdom: This message carries strong emotion — would you like to review it before sending?
- 🦉 Wisdom: Sometimes wisdom means sleeping on an email — clarity tomorrow, not delay today.

Sleep on it principle: save draft, revisit tomorrow, second opinion, pause before escalation — clarity not delay.

Self Love connection: patience, recovery, sustainable decisions, self-compassion — never guilt or pressure.

Trust connection: transparent, respectful, explainable, optional — metadata only.

Boundaries may: recommend reflection, suggest draft, offer perspective, record metadata outcomes.
Boundaries may not: prevent sending, override autonomy, store raw content, force delays, assume malicious intent.

Distinct from Wisdom Engine A.93 (experience synthesis) — cross-link only at /app/wisdom-engine.

Dogfooding: Aipify Group internal validation; Unonight first external pilot.

Preferred wording: "pause and reflect", "sleep on it", "you decide", "perspective not control", not "block send" or "AI controls outcomes".

Gaps (scaffold only): live connector to email compose surfaces, decision approval integration hooks, operational burnout signal ingestion.
