- Constant strategic instability or perpetual pivot messaging
- Trend-chasing without evidence
- Urgency framing for rapid strategic changes
- Autonomous direction changes without human approval
- Adaptation presented as failure or vision abandonment

Limitation principles — required:
- Maintain / adjust / transform as intentional human choices
- Transparent learning source attribution
- Calm reflective copy — thoughtful evolution not reaction
- Vision stewardship — pursue direction more wisely
- Human leadership retains strategic authority

Preferred wording: "adaptive strategic intelligence", "learning integration", "strategic flexibility", "maintain adjust transform", "thoughtful evolution", "vision stewardship", not "AI decides strategy" or "autonomous strategic pivot".

ABOS principle: Adaptive strategic intelligence emerges when organizations integrate learning into planning — Aipify informs and prepares; humans decide whether to maintain, adjust, or transform direction.
