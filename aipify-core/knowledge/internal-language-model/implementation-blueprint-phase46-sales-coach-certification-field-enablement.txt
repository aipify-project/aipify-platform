Implementation Blueprint Phase 46 — Sales Coach Certification & Field Enablement Engine

Extends Sales Expert Operating System A.95 at /app/sales-expert-engine.
Tab: Certification & Field Enablement — not a separate engine route.

Mission: Develop competent professionals — training strengthens confidence; certification reflects genuine competence.

Philosophy: Assessment encourages growth, not fear. Mastery not exclusion. Field enablement supports excellence at a sustainable pace.

ABOS principle: Partners grow through genuine capability — certification means readiness to help organizations work smarter.

Sales training pathway (6 modules):
1. Introduction to Aipify — philosophy, companion, business value
2. Ethical Sales Practices
3. Discovery Conversations
4. Aipify Demonstrations
5. Implementation Fundamentals
6. Customer Success

Sales simulation engine (metadata scaffold): cold conversations, discovery, demos, objections, renewals — Aipify acts as customer.

Telephone sales coaching — five steps: intro, curiosity/discovery, opportunities, demo invitation, next steps. Structure not scripts.

Assessment principles: product understanding, communication, ethical judgment, customer focus, demo readiness — growth not fear.

Certification tiers:
- Certified Sales Representative — 75% minimum
- Sales Expert — 85% minimum
- Elite Sales Expert — 95% minimum + practical excellence

Never use Affiliate publicly — official partner terminology only.

Reassessment: retakes allowed; max 3 attempts before material review — mastery not exclusion.

Certification display: profiles, partner directories, dashboards — dates and identifiers scaffold.

Email enablement: templates with expert details, booking links, certification status — NO mass unsolicited outreach.

Implementation pricing: non-binding illustrative examples — Sales Experts set own pricing.

Installation journey: subscribe → welcome → onboarding → install → companion-led training (5 steps).

Field sales enablement: in-person demos, sector opportunities, certified follow-ups — companion nudges.

Performance culture: customer satisfaction, sustainable growth, implementation excellence, professional development — not revenue comparison.
