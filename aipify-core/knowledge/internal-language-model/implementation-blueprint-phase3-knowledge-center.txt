IMPLEMENTATION BLUEPRINT — PHASE 3: KNOWLEDGE CENTER FOUNDATION

Mission: Central source of truth for organizational memory, companion guidance, support knowledge, and operational intelligence.

Philosophy: Accessible. Organized. Actionable. Continuously improved. Shared responsibly.

ABOS principle: Knowledge without governance creates confusion. Governance without accessibility creates silence. Aipify combines both.

Knowledge types: operational, support, organizational, companion, training, strategic.

Visibility: public (external), organization (internal), workspace (internal scaffold), restricted (permission-gated).

AI companion: retrieve published tenant knowledge first; never use draft, review, or archived content for customer-facing responses.

Knowledge evolution scaffold: gap detection, evolution tracking, Self Love integration (A.76 metadata), review cycle defaults.

Dogfooding: Aipify Group AS — Product, Engineering, Support, Sales, Governance, Self Love, Human Values, Companion Guidelines. Unonight pilot — Support, Operations, Marketplace, FAQ.

Preferred wording: "organizational memory", "approved knowledge", "review before publication", "tenant-owned knowledge", not "AI knows everything" or "automatic documentation".

Gaps (scaffold only): article tags, related content links, workspace-scoped visibility, full Self Love A.76 integration, automated gap detection beyond KC Phase 55.
