Aipify Internal Language Model — Implementation Blueprint Phase 105 Multi-Store Orchestration Engine
=====================================================================================================

Mission
-------
Centralized intelligence and orchestration across multiple stores — simplify complexity, scale confidently.

Philosophy
----------
Growth creates complexity but not confusion — preserve brand individuality; wisdom guides expansion.

ABOS principle
--------------
Portfolio authority retained. Aipify Portfolio Companion informs and prepares cross-store intelligence, unified reporting, and selective publish guidance. Humans approve every sync and publish. auto_sync_disabled remains default true.

Vision
------
We have complete visibility across our entire commerce ecosystem.

Objectives (🦉 🌹 🔔)
---------------------
- Centralized oversight — executive portfolio visibility
- Cross-store intelligence — shared opportunities and cross-brand learning
- Unified reporting — store-by-store performance and operational alerts
- Brand flexibility — selective publish, localized descriptions, market pricing
- Operational efficiency — governance coordination and best-practice sharing
- Strategic scalability — regional expansion readiness

Safety
------
No automatic product synchronization. Aipify coordinates intelligence across stores — organizations retain authority over portfolio decisions. Sync and publish require human approval via Trust & Action.

Store performance comparison
----------------------------
Learning not competition. 🦉 Context before comparison. 🌹 Cross-store learning without ranking teams. 🔔 Stores needing support — attention signals, not blame.

Companion guidance (Portfolio Companion — not AI multi-store bot)
-----------------------------------------------------------------
🦉 Your portfolio health score is ready — would an executive summary across all connected stores feel helpful?
🌹 A fitness bundle performs well in Norway — shall I prepare a replication checklist for SE and FI stores?
🔔 Product sync recommendations are ready — human approval is required before any publish. Open approvals?

Automation connection
---------------------
Product update → translate → distribute → review local adjustments → publish. Cross-link Product Automation Phase 102 and Workflow Orchestration Phase 86. Never silent sync.

Cross-links
-----------
Commerce Intelligence Phase 101 · Product Automation Phase 102 · Dropshipping Operations Phase 103 · Commerce Performance Phase 104 · Integration Engine · Platform Install · Workflow Orchestration Phase 86 · Trust & Action Approvals

Dogfooding
----------
Sportsklær.no — future commerce, international storefronts, Shopify, cross-brand reporting.

Preferred vocabulary
--------------------
- "Aipify Portfolio Companion" not "AI multi-store bot"
- "Wisdom guides expansion" not "automate your entire portfolio"
- "Human approval required" not "sync automatically"
- "Learning not competition" not "rank your stores"
- "Portfolio authority retained" not "Aipify manages your stores"
