ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 64 — Purpose & Values Engine
================================================================

Mission
-------
Clarify, preserve, and actively live purpose and values in daily operations and strategic decisions.

Philosophy
----------
Values are meaningful only when they influence behavior.
Purpose guides priorities.
Culture is practiced intentionally — practical, not decorative.

ABOS principle
--------------
Purpose provides direction; values provide boundaries; together they shape choices over time.
Aipify informs and prepares; humans decide.

Objectives
----------
- Purpose clarification
- Values alignment
- Cultural reinforcement
- Leadership reflection
- Organizational storytelling
- Decision alignment

Purpose discovery (🦉 🌹 ❤️)
-----------------------------
- Why exist beyond financial outcomes
- What positive impact sought
- What customers would miss if we disappeared

Values exploration
------------------
Core values, expected behaviors, leadership commitments, cultural aspirations.
Example values: integrity, compassion, excellence, curiosity, responsibility — practical not decorative.

Values in action
----------------
Respect → listening, feedback, supporting colleagues
Innovation → experimentation, sharing ideas, learning from outcomes
Integrity → honest commitments, explain trade-offs openly
Compassion → recognize effort, sustainable pacing, include diverse perspectives

Decision alignment (🦉 🌹)
---------------------------
- Does this decision align with stated values?
- Which principles should influence the discussion?
- What trade-offs matter most?

Organizational storytelling
---------------------------
Resilience moments, customer success, team achievements, compassion, innovation milestones — stories shape culture.
Metadata summaries only — no raw customer content.

Self Love connection
--------------------
Reflection, appreciation, recognition of contribution, connection to something larger.
"The work you do contributes to the purpose your organization has chosen."

Leadership insights
-------------------
Values engagement summaries, purpose reflection prompts, cultural strength observations — dialogue, not surveillance.

Trust connection
----------------
How values experiences are generated, optional elements, how purpose statements influence companion behavior.
Human control — acknowledge or dismiss reflection prompts.

Distinctions
------------
- Extends Purpose & Values Engine Phase A.82 at /app/purpose-values-engine
- Distinct from Brand Identity & Personhood Standard (Aipify product naming)
- Distinct from Business DNA Engine at /app/settings/business-dna

- Distinct from Impact Engine A.85 and Growth & Evolution A.81
- Engine helpers: _pve_* — blueprint helpers: _pvbp_* (do not collide)

Dogfooding
----------
Aipify Group: companion philosophy, Sales Expert culture, leadership practices, community experiences.
Unonight: first external pilot — customer obsession and commerce purpose values.

Success criteria
----------------
- Clearer purpose articulation
- Values influence behavior
- Cultural consistency
- Employee meaning
- Leadership alignment

Vision
------
Preserve humanity as organizations grow — "We remember why we started."
