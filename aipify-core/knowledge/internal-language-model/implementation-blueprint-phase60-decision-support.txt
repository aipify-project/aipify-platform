ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 60 — Decision Support Engine
Extends Decision Support Engine Phase 38 at /app/assistant/decisions

Mission
Help people navigate important decisions with clarity — structure, perspective, and context. Humans decide; Aipify supports.

Philosophy
Decision support means clarity before choosing — not certainty. Structure options, surface trade-offs, highlight risks — reflect intentionally before acting.

ABOS principle
Clarity before choosing — structure, perspective, and context; humans retain final authority.

Objectives
- Decision preparation — frame the problem and gather context
- Option evaluation — compare alternatives; perspective not prescription
- Trade-off awareness — competing priorities and consequences
- Risk identification — timing, dependencies, opportunity costs
- Scenario consideration — success, underperformance, secondary effects
- Reflection before action — good decisions can still produce difficult outcomes

Decision framework companion questions
- What problem are we trying to solve?
- What options are available?
- What assumptions are we making?
- What risks should we consider?
- What would success look like?
- What if we do nothing?

Decision types
Operational — team priorities, resource allocation, workflow
Strategic — market expansion, product investment, org restructuring
Personal — workload, professional development, goal prioritization

Option comparison companion examples
🦉 Perspective not prescription — Option A vs B trade-offs; you decide
🌹 Values alignment — goal path vs urgent operational need
🔔 Timing awareness — delay reduces pressure but may affect renewal window

Resource limitations, timing, dependencies, opportunity costs — strengthen preparation not paralysis
