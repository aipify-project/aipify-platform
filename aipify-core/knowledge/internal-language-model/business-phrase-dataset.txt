PURPOSE
This document expands Aipify's understanding of how real employees, managers, customer service agents and business owners naturally communicate.
Employees rarely use exact system commands.
Instead, they describe situations, problems, priorities and desired outcomes.
Aipify must recognize these phrases and convert them into structured business actions.

LONG-TERM VISION
The ultimate objective is for employees to communicate naturally.

Examples:
"Can you help me catch up?"
"What should I focus on?"
"Did I forget something important?"
"Take a look at this."
"Can you make sense of this mess?"

Aipify should understand these requests, translate them into structured actions, apply the correct safety level and provide practical assistance.

People should not learn how to speak like software.
Aipify should learn how people actually work.
