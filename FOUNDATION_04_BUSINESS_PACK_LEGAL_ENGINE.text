AIPIFY — PACK FOUNDATION 04
BUSINESS PACK LEGAL ENGINE

Feature owner: PLATFORM FOUNDATION
Layer: Aipify Marketplace Infrastructure
Core principle: No Business Pack may be published without approved legal foundations.

MANDATORY DOCUMENTS (per pack)
- terms_and_conditions (requires acceptance)
- license_agreement (requires acceptance)
- acceptable_use_policy
- privacy_addendum
- subscription_terms
- cancellation_policy
- limitation_of_liability
- governing_law (default: Norwegian Law, jurisdiction Norway)

COMPANY IDENTITY
- All legal document bodies use placeholders resolved from company.config.ts
- Hardcoded legal company information is prohibited in document templates

ROUTES
/app/marketplace/packs/[packKey]/legal — per-pack Legal Center
/app/business-pack-legal-engine — governance dashboard

APIs

GET /api/aipify/business-pack-legal-engine/dashboard
GET /api/aipify/business-pack-legal-engine/card
POST /api/aipify/business-pack-legal-engine/action

ACTIONS

- accept_document — single document acceptance
- check_activation_eligibility
