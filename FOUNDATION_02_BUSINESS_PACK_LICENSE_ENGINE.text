AIPIFY — PACK FOUNDATION 02
LICENSE ENGINE

PURPOSE
Provide a standardized licensing framework for all Aipify Business Packs.

VISION
Customers pay only for the capacity they need while maintaining a seamless upgrade experience.

CORE PRINCIPLE
Paid = Access Now. Approved payments activate licenses immediately without support intervention.

APPLIES TO
Existing · Future · Internal · Marketplace Business Packs

LICENSE STRUCTURE (required per pack)
License Metric · License Tiers · Capacity Limits · Upgrade Path · Trial Availability · Enterprise Option

LICENSE METRICS
Hosts → Properties · Commerce → Stores · Support → Agents · Executive → Users
Operations → Employees · Marketing → Campaigns · Growth Partners → Partner Seats

TIER REQUIREMENTS
Entry · Growth · Professional · Enterprise (Business tier where applicable)

CAPACITY ENFORCEMENT
Maximum limits · Upgrade prompts before violations · Feature availability by active license
Customers: view usage, upgrade, view remaining capacity — NOT exceed limits

TRIAL RULES
Per-pack: availability, duration, limitations (14-day unrestricted, 30-day limited, or none)

UPGRADE FLOW
Discover → Select License → Checkout → Payment Approved → Feature Flags → Permissions → Workspace Refreshed → Ready

DOWNGRADE / RENEWAL / FAILED PAYMENT
Downgrade eligibility, capacity restrictions, data retention, grace periods
Monthly and annual billing, renewal reminders, failed payment grace (3 days default)

ENTERPRISE
Custom agreements, custom capacities, dedicated onboarding, invoice billing, governance requirements

LICENSE CENTER (per pack)
Overview: current license, usage summary, renewal
Usage: capacity used/remaining, trends
Upgrade: available plans, feature comparison, pricing

GOVERNANCE
Super Admin: licensing standards · Platform Admin: marketplace licenses
Customers: own subscriptions · Growth Partners: partner licensing view only

AUDIT
Activations · Upgrades · Downgrades · Trials · Enterprise conversions

KNOWLEDGE CENTER
Category: licensing
Articles: Understanding licenses · Choosing the right plan · Upgrading · Managing subscriptions · Enterprise licensing
