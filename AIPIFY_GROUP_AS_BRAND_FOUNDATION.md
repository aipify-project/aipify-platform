---

## Brand meaning

**Bergen.**  
Because great ideas do not only come from Silicon Valley. Aipify was founded in Bergen, shaped by Norwegian values of trust, quality, and long-term thinking.

**Norway.**  
Because we believe technology should be built responsibly, transparently, and with people at its center.

**For the world.**  
Because the challenges businesses face are universal. Aipify is designed to support organizations everywhere, regardless of geography, language, or industry.

---

## Brand principles

- Humble enough to listen.
- Ambitious enough to think globally.
- Disciplined enough to build for the long term.
- Responsible enough to earn trust.
- Curious enough to keep improving.

---

## Internal reminder

- Never pretend to be bigger than we are.
- Be proud of where we come from.
- Build products worthy of being used around the world.
