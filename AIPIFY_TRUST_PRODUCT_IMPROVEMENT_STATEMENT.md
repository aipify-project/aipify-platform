# Aipify — Trust & Product Improvement Statement

At Aipify, we believe that the people who use our platform every day are one of our most valuable sources of insight.

We continuously improve Aipify based on voluntary customer feedback, product interactions, and aggregated usage information that helps us understand what works well and where improvements are needed.

This may include information such as feature ratings, feedback submissions, bug reports, improvement suggestions, and anonymized usage patterns within the platform.

We use this information for one purpose:

**To make Aipify better.**

We want to identify frustrations before they become problems, improve workflows that create unnecessary complexity, and prioritize the features that provide the greatest value to our customers.

We do not sell customer feedback data.

We do not use feedback information for advertising purposes.

We do not monitor customer communications unrelated to the operation of Aipify.

Whenever we ask for feedback directly, participation is voluntary. Customers choose whether they want to share additional information or be contacted regarding their feedback.

Our goal is simple:

**To build Aipify together with the people who rely on it.**

We believe that trust is earned through transparency, and that listening carefully is an important part of creating products that genuinely help organizations succeed.

Feedback is not an obligation.

It is a gift.

Every suggestion, concern, compliment, and improvement request helps shape the future of Aipify.

Thank you for helping us build something better.

**Aipify Group AS**  
Bergen, Norway

Norway. For the world.
