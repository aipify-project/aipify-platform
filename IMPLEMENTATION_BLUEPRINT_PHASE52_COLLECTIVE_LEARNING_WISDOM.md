# Implementation Blueprint — Phase 52: Collective Learning & Wisdom Engine

**Feature owner:** Customer App  
**Implementation:** Extends [Community & Collective Intelligence — Phase 89](./COMMUNITY_COLLECTIVE_INTELLIGENCE_PHASE89.md) + [Blueprint Phase 24](./IMPLEMENTATION_BLUEPRINT_PHASE24_COMMUNITY_COLLECTIVE_INTELLIGENCE.md)

This document defines **Phase 52 — Collective Learning & Wisdom Engine** of the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS). It compounds anonymized collective wisdom across the ecosystem via the Community & Collective Intelligence layer — observations **inform**, they do not **prescribe**.

> **Mapping:** ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 52 extends **Community & Collective Intelligence Phase 89** at `/app/community`. Do not duplicate Global Learning Network Phase 72, Learning Engine Phase 23, Wisdom Engine A.93, or Organizational Memory A.34 — cross-link only.

## Mission

Compound anonymized collective wisdom across the ecosystem — pattern recognition, best practice evolution, and responsible insights that inform without prescribing.

## Philosophy

Wisdom grows when experiences are shared responsibly. Collective observations inform teams — they never prescribe outcomes. Humans decide; Aipify surfaces patterns from governed, anonymized contributions.

## ABOS principle

Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) strengthens when collective learning compounds safely — metadata patterns across the ecosystem, never confidential records or org identity.

## Anonymization principles

Aligned with `lib/impact/privacy.ts` forbidden-keys mindset:

- Aggregates only — counts, categories, outcome trends
- No identities, PII, confidential records, or competitive intelligence
- No org identity in cross-ecosystem payloads
- Explicit governance before publication
- Observations inform — never auto-adopt
