## Role

**Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170)** — Social Cohesion Center for intentional trust development. NOT manipulation or image management. NO star ratings or social scores.

## Principle

People First. Trust through consistency — not manufactured sentiment. Growth Partner terminology — never Affiliate. Trust Companion supports awareness; it does NOT manipulate emotions, determine who deserves trust, override leadership, suppress criticism, or replace human relationships.
