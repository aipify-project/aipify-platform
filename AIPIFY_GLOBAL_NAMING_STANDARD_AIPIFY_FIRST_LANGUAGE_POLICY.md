## Purpose

Establish a single ecosystem-wide naming policy so every customer-facing surface refers to **Aipify** as the trusted business companion — not generic "AI" product labels. Users build familiarity with the Aipify name; Artificial Intelligence remains the underlying technology.

This Global standard **extends and supersedes** any module-specific companion naming guidance. One policy, all surfaces.

## Core principle

**Aipify is the product name. Artificial Intelligence is the underlying technology.**

**Correct:** Aipify recommends · Aipify Support · Resolved by Aipify · Aipify Insight · Aipify Companion · Generated by Aipify

**Avoid:** AI recommends · AI Support · Resolved by AI · AI Assistant · AI-powered (as product label)

Programmatic enforcement: `applyAipifyFirstLanguagePolicy()` in `lib/internal-language-model/brand-identity-vocabulary.ts` · corpus: `aipify-core/knowledge/internal-language-model/aipify-first-language-policy.txt`

## Applies to (all surfaces)

- Customer App UI (`/app/*`) — navigation, dashboards, notifications, onboarding
- Companion Orb and Desktop Companion
- Support dashboards and self-support flows
- Admin Assistant and executive surfaces
- Meeting Companion, calendar companion, and collaboration intelligence
- Knowledge Center articles and FAQ (customer-facing)
- Workflow approvals, document output labels, and marketing copy
- Assistant replies and proactive guidance (via ILM adaptation)
- Platform Admin customer-facing metrics labels where shown to operators (support value, resolution attribution)

**Does not apply to:** route paths, skill IDs, API paths, database identifiers, or internal code names (e.g. `support-ai-engine` stays).

## Marketing principle

Lead with **outcomes and trust**, not technology labels.

- Prefer: "Aipify works in the background so your team can move forward."
- Avoid: "Our AI-powered platform…" as the primary headline.
- When technology context is required: "Aipify uses advanced intelligence" or "Aipify-powered" — never imply a generic third-party AI service.

## Companion principle

When Aipify joins meetings or assists in real time, use **Aipify Companion** or **Aipify Meeting Companion** — visible, honest, never hidden. Participants should know Aipify is present. See Meeting Companion Teams Integration & Privacy Standard.

## Technical exception

The word **AI** may remain in:

- Investor, security, compliance, and regulatory documentation filenames and body text where "artificial intelligence" is legally or technically required
- Internal engineering docs describing model providers, ML pipelines, or risk classification enums
- User-typed search queries and natural language the user may say ("Hi AI") — Aipify responds as Aipify
- Code identifiers, routes, skill registry IDs, and API paths

Customer-facing UI copy must follow this policy even when backend enums still use `ai_*` keys.

## Implementation requirements

1. **i18n first** — all user-visible strings in `locales/{en,no,sv,da}/customerApp.json`, `platform.json`, and legacy `dashboard.json` where keys remain active
2. **ILM adaptation** — `adaptReplyToBrandIdentity()` applies Aipify-first label replacements on generated copy
3. **Core RPC metadata** — `_aflp_*` helpers expose policy to Companion Identity Engine dashboard
4. **Knowledge Center** — FAQ at `content/knowledge/aipify/brand-identity/faq/aipify-first-language-policy-faq.md`
5. **No route renames** — copy-only migration; preserve install-first architecture and skill IDs
6. **Orchestration surface** — Companion Identity Engine displays unified policy for module consistency reviews

## ABOS principle

Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) is the product category. Individual modules (Support, Meeting Companion, Insights) are **Aipify capabilities** — not separate "AI products." Cross-ref: [ABOS_BRAND_TERMINOLOGY_STANDARD.md](./ABOS_BRAND_TERMINOLOGY_STANDARD.md).

## Vision

Users say **"This feels like Aipify"** because every label, notification, and reply uses one consistent name. People build relationships with names, not technologies.

> Aipify informs and prepares. Humans decide.
