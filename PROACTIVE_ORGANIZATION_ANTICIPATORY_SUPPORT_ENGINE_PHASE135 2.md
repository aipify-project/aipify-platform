# Proactive Organization & Anticipatory Support Engine — Phase 135

## Autonomous Organization Era (131–140)

Phase 135 delivers organization-level early awareness, anticipatory support, and organizational pulse — **distinct from** Proactive Companion Engine A.79 at `/app/proactive-companion-engine` (individual nudges/presence).

## Vision & philosophy

Care not surveillance. Organizational patterns only — never individual employee monitoring. People First. Wisdom before speed. Companions support awareness — do not create unnecessary urgency. Humans responsible; approved governance for any automated intervention.
