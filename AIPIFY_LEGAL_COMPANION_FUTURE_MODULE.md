# Aipify Legal Companion — Future Module

**Status:** Strategically approved · **Planted for future development**  
**Do not implement** until Companion Core, Governance, Memory, Presence, Document Management, and Action Framework foundations are established.

---

## Objective

Enable Aipify to responsibly support individuals, entrepreneurs, and organizations with legal organization, document awareness, renewal reminders, and preparation workflows while preserving the role of qualified legal professionals.

## Core principle

**People should have easier access to legal organization.**

Aipify should improve preparedness — not replace legal advice.

## Positioning

Aipify Legal Companion is a **Companion Module** designed to help users stay organized around contracts, deadlines, renewals, and legal responsibilities.

## Aipify's role

Aipify should:

- Organize legal information
- Coordinate legal reminders
- Support document preparation workflows
- Improve awareness of obligations
- Reduce administrative stress
- Encourage timely professional consultation

Aipify should never:

- Provide legal advice
- Interpret laws as definitive guidance
- Replace attorneys
- Represent users in legal matters
- Make legal decisions independently

## Aipify insights

Examples:

- "This supplier agreement is due for review in 30 days."
- "You typically conduct policy reviews once per year."
- "There are several legal preparation tasks scheduled this quarter."

**Constraint:** Insights should encourage preparation — never provide legal conclusions.

## Legal actions

Examples:

- Schedule reminders
- Print document checklists
- Organize legal documentation
- Coordinate professional meetings
- Generate preparation summaries
- Create governance task lists

**Constraint:** Actions require appropriate approvals.

## Professional network support

Examples:

- Lawyer contact management
- Consultation preparation
- Document gathering reminders
- Meeting follow-up summaries

**Constraint:** Professionals remain responsible for legal advice.

## User control principle

Users and organizations must control:

- Reminder frequency
- Dashboard visibility
- Shared access permissions
- Notification settings
- Professional contact preferences

## Privacy principle

Aipify must:

- Protect legal information
- Minimize unnecessary data collection
- Support complete deletion
- Respect confidentiality expectations
- Maintain transparency

## Ethical principles

Aipify must:

- Encourage professional consultation
- Avoid overconfidence
- Clearly communicate limitations
- Preserve user autonomy
- Support informed decision-making

## Success metrics

- Reminder usefulness ratings
- Deadline preparation completion rates
- Companion trust indicators
- User satisfaction
- Organizational preparedness improvements
- Administrative time savings

## Design principles

- Professional and reassuring
- Human-centered
- Governance-first
- Privacy-focused
- Companion-driven
- Enterprise-ready

## Vision

Legal responsibilities are often stressful because they are complex and easy to overlook.

Aipify Legal Companion exists to help people and organizations stay organized, prepared, and confident while working alongside qualified legal professionals.
