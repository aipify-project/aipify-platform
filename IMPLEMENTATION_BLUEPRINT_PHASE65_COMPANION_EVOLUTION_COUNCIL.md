## Mission

Ensure Aipify evolves responsibly by examining whether new capabilities strengthen or weaken founding principles.

## Core philosophy

Not every capability that can be built should be built. Progress is evaluated through a values lens; companionship requires reflection.

## ABOS principle

"What should we build?" matters more than "What can we build?" — wisdom through restraint.

## Objectives

| Key | Focus |
