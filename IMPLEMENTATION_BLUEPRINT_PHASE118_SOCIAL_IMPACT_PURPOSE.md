## Mission

Help organizations use their influence responsibly — aligning activities with values, measuring social contributions, and creating lasting positive impact.

## Philosophy

Organizations have influence; technology amplifies it. Purpose = action not marketing. People First. Stewardship through responsibility. Humans define purpose; Companions support action.
