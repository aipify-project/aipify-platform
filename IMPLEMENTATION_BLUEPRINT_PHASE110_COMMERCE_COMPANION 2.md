## Mission

Unified Companion for commerce — clarity, confidence, and sustainable success daily.

**Commerce is customers, teams, partnerships, and sustainability — simplify complexity; wisdom guides action.**

Partnership not pressure — stewardship not urgency. Human oversight remains central.

## ABOS principle

**Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) — Commerce Companion aggregates visibility and daily briefings; domain engines Phases 101–109 remain authoritative for operations. Aipify informs and prepares; humans decide.**
