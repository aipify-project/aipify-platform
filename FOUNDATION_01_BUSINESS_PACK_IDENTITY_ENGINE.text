AIPIFY — PACK FOUNDATION 01
BUSINESS PACK IDENTITY ENGINE

PURPOSE
Establish a mandatory identity standard for every Aipify Business Pack.

VISION
Every Business Pack must feel like a complete product rather than a collection of pages.

APPLIES TO
Existing Business Packs · Future Business Packs · Internal Business Packs · Marketplace Business Packs

IDENTITY REQUIREMENTS
Pack Logo · Pack Cover Image · Pack Name · Pack Category · Version Number · Status
Short Description · Long Description · Intended Audience · Key Benefits · Business Value Statement

PACK STATUS
Active · Beta · Coming Soon · Deprecated · Retired

PACK CATEGORIES
Hospitality · Commerce · Support · Executive · Operations · Human Resources
Marketing · Intelligence · Productivity · Governance

LANDING EXPERIENCE (required order)
Logo → Hero → Overview → Features → Knowledge Center → Licensing → Install / Upgrade

FORBIDDEN
Opening packs directly into technical configuration without identity landing first.

VERSIONING
Major.Minor.Patch — visible in Marketplace, installed packs, Knowledge Center, release notes.

GOVERNANCE
Platform Admin: publish and edit pack identity
Super Admin: global standards
Customers: cannot alter pack identity
Growth Partners: view approved pack materials
