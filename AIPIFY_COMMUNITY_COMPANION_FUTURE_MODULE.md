# Aipify Community Companion — Future Module

**Status:** Strategically approved · **Planted for future development**  
**Do not implement** until Companion Core, Verify, Community Safety, Memory, Presence, and Governance foundations are established.

---

## Objective

Enable Aipify to responsibly support communities, clubs, membership organizations, and social platforms by strengthening engagement, improving safety, encouraging participation, and helping members build meaningful connections.

## Core principle

**Communities thrive when people feel welcomed, valued, and connected.**

Aipify should help communities become healthier and more engaging.

## Positioning

Aipify Community Companion is a **Companion Module** designed to support administrators, moderators, and members throughout the lifecycle of a community.

## Aipify's role

Aipify should:

- Welcome new members
- Encourage healthy participation
- Strengthen community engagement
- Support moderators
- Promote safety and trust
- Help members discover relevant connections

Aipify should never:

- Replace human moderators entirely
- Make sensitive moderation decisions independently
- Manipulate member behavior
- Encourage unhealthy engagement patterns
- Override community leadership

## Community categories

- Membership communities
- Interest-based communities
- Professional communities
- Support communities

- Social platforms
- Learning communities

Display:

- Member growth trends
- Community activity indicators

- Upcoming events

## Community health engine

Monitor:

- Participation trends
- Member retention
- Moderation workload
- Community sentiment signals
- Engagement distribution
- Safety indicators

**Health levels:** Vulnerable · Stable · Healthy · Thriving

## Aipify insights

Examples:

- "Several new members have not yet engaged with the community."
- "Community participation is increasing around this interest area."
- "Moderation workload has increased this week."

**Constraint:** Insights should support community leaders — not replace them.

## Member connection support

Examples:

- Recommend relevant groups
- Suggest shared interests
- Highlight upcoming events
- Surface valuable discussions
- Encourage healthy participation

**Constraint:** Recommendations should prioritize member value — not platform addiction.

## Moderation support

Examples:

- Surface guideline violations
- Highlight emerging conflicts
- Prioritize review queues
- Prepare moderation summaries
- Support consistency

**Constraint:** Human moderation remains responsible for final decisions.

## Community actions

Examples:

- Send welcome communications
- Schedule event reminders
- Print community materials
- Organize participation reports
- Coordinate approved announcements
- Prepare moderation summaries

**Constraint:** Actions require appropriate approvals.

Community leaders must control:

- Community policies

## Privacy principle

Aipify must:

- Respect member privacy
- Minimize unnecessary data collection
- Support transparency
- Allow community-specific governance
- Protect member trust

- Companion usefulness ratings

- Verify adoption rates

## Design principles

- Community-centered
- Human-first
- Trust-oriented

## Vision

Strong communities do not happen by accident.

Aipify Community Companion exists to help people create spaces where members feel welcomed, supported, connected, and safe.

## Implementation prerequisites

Before building:

1. **Companion Core** — identity, presence, and companion module framework
2. **Aipify Verify** — member verification and trust signals
3. **Community Safety** — moderation governance and safety patterns
4. **Memory foundations** — metadata patterns only
5. **Presence** — notification and continuity patterns (Phase 292)
6. **Governance** — permissions, audit, and community policy controls

## Planned layer placement (when ready)
