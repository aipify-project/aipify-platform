## Mission

Transform experiences into lasting organizational wisdom for future generations.

## Core philosophy

Knowledge should not disappear when circumstances change — memory strengthens continuity. Wisdom, not accumulation — governed retention with transparency; no surveillance.

## ABOS principle

Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) preserves what matters — organizations decide what wisdom endures; Aipify prepares context, gentle reflection, and cross-links to legacy stewardship without hidden retention.

## Vision

We remember the people, experiences and lessons that helped us become who we are.
