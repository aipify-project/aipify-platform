# Implementation Blueprint — Phase 89: Community & Collective Intelligence Engine

**Feature owner:** Customer App  
**Implementation:** Extends [Community & Collective Intelligence — Phase 89](./COMMUNITY_COLLECTIVE_INTELLIGENCE_PHASE89.md) + [Blueprint Phase 24](./IMPLEMENTATION_BLUEPRINT_PHASE24_COMMUNITY_COLLECTIVE_INTELLIGENCE.md) + [Blueprint Phase 52](./IMPLEMENTATION_BLUEPRINT_PHASE52_COLLECTIVE_LEARNING_WISDOM.md)

This document defines **Phase 89 — Community & Collective Intelligence Engine** of the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS). It layers ABOS preparation standards on the existing Community hub — collective experience insights that strengthen learning, innovation, and adaptability while preserving privacy and human agency.

> **Mapping:** ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 89 extends **Community & Collective Intelligence repo Phase 89** at `/app/community`. **Phase number collision:** Gratitude & Recognition Engine **Phase A.89** at `/app/gratitude-recognition-engine` — recognition connection only, not duplicate. Blueprint Phase 24 and Phase 52 fields are **preserved** — append `community_collective_intelligence_blueprint_phase89` only.

## Mission

Strengthen learning, innovation, and adaptability through collective experience insights.

## Core philosophy

Wisdom rarely lives in one individual alone — collective intelligence strengthens people, it does not replace them.

## ABOS principle

Shared learning, not surveillance — Aipify surfaces governed collective patterns; humans decide what to adopt.

## Privacy principle

**Shared learning, not surveillance.** Avoid hidden profiling, public exposure of private experiences, using contributions without permission, and reducing individuals to data points.

## Community objectives

| Objective | Description |

| **Collective experience insights** | Strengthen learning from governed, anonymized community observations |
| **Innovation surfacing** | Surface innovation and adaptability patterns without prescribing outcomes |

| **Wisdom amplification** | Amplify community wisdom — companion guidance strengthens people |
