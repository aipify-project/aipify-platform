## Role

**Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170) capstone** — Human Flourishing Center for civilizational flourishing and human potential. Technology serves humanity — NOT human worth metrics, productivity-based worth scoring, or employee surveillance.

## Principle

People First. Technology serves humanity — not the reverse. Flourishing Companion supports growth; it does NOT define human value. Growth Partner terminology — never Affiliate.
