## Core capabilities

- **Technology Observatory** — AI, HCI, automation, workplace, collaboration, infrastructure
- **Emerging interface strategy** — voice, multimodal, wearable, ambient, AR, VR collaboration
- **Future readiness assessments** — maturity, innovation readiness, workforce preparedness
- **Scenario planning** — near, medium, and long-term future scenarios
- **Emerging initiatives** — controlled exploration with governance compatibility
- **Pilot opportunities** — customer, partner, and advisory board participation
- **Responsible adoption** — business value, ethics, security, governance, feasibility

## Database tables

- `future_tech_settings` — observatory and exploration configuration
- `future_technology_observations`, `future_trend_reports`
- `future_emerging_initiatives`, `future_pilot_opportunities`
- `future_readiness_assessments`, `future_scenario_plans`
- `future_technology_reviews`, `future_recommendations`
- `future_tech_briefings`, `future_tech_audit_log`

## RPCs

- `get_future_technologies_dashboard()` — full future technologies dashboard
- `get_future_technologies_card()` — summary card
- `generate_future_technologies_briefing()` — executive future readiness briefing
- `advance_emerging_initiative(uuid)` — advance initiative through lifecycle
- `dismiss_future_recommendation(uuid)` — dismiss recommendation

## Integrations

Innovation Lab (controlled pilots), Governance (human approval), Enterprise Deployment (future-ready architecture), Academy (readiness education), Strategic Intelligence (trend analysis)

## Principle

The future should not be feared or chased blindly. It should be understood, evaluated and approached with intention.
