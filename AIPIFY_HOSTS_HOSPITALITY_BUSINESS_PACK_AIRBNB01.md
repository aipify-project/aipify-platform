## PURPOSE

Aipify Hosts is the operational layer around Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo, Expedia, and direct bookings — not a replacement for booking platforms.

**Positioning:** *Aipify Hosts is the Business Operating System for modern hospitality businesses.*
