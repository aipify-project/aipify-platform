AIPIFY GROUP AS — COMPANY FOUNDATION DIRECTIVE
Version: 1.0
Status: Mandatory — Effective Immediately
Priority: CRITICAL (precedes product foundation)

Effective immediately, all future development, documentation, onboarding flows,
legal references, business planning, investor material, administrative interfaces,
platform communications, and strategic decisions must recognize and reflect the
official company identity defined in this directive.

GOVERNANCE ORDER
Company Foundation Directive → Core Foundation → Operating Principles →
Enterprise Design Standard → Architecture → Implementation → Skills

────────────────────────────────────────
§1 OFFICIAL COMPANY IDENTITY
────────────────────────────────────────
Company Name:     Aipify Group AS
Country of Origin: Norway
Global Positioning: From Norway. For the world.

Parent company. Owns the vision, technology, and long-term future of Aipify.

Primary Product:  Aipify
(Aipify Business Operating System / ABOS — product architecture name)

────────────────────────────────────────
§2 MISSION
────────────────────────────────────────
Aipify Group AS exists to develop intelligent software products and services
that help organizations:

• Operate more efficiently
• Make better decisions
• Automate repetitive tasks
• Improve customer experiences
• Unlock human potential through responsible use of artificial intelligence

Aipify is not built to replace people.
Aipify is built to empower people.

────────────────────────────────────────
§3 VISION
────────────────────────────────────────
To become one of the world's leading providers of AI-powered business
operating systems — helping organizations of all sizes work smarter, respond
faster, and focus more on what truly matters.

Aipify should evolve into a trusted companion for businesses around the world.

────────────────────────────────────────
§4 COMPANY PRINCIPLES
────────────────────────────────────────
• Build for trust before scale.
• Build for longevity before trends.
• Enterprise quality is the minimum standard.
• Human oversight remains essential.
• Technology should remove friction, not humanity.
• Every feature should solve a real problem.
• Global ambitions must be supported by local understanding.
• The best products are often built from frustrations experienced firsthand.

────────────────────────────────────────
§5 ORIGIN STORY
────────────────────────────────────────
Aipify was born from a simple realization:

Modern businesses spend too much time navigating complexity, responding to
repetitive questions, managing fragmented systems, and performing tasks that
technology should already be assisting with.

The founders experienced these frustrations themselves.
Instead of waiting for someone else to solve the problem, they decided to
build the solution.

What started as an idea evolved into a vision:

An AI-powered Business Operating System capable of supporting organizations
in their daily operations.

────────────────────────────────────────

────────────────────────────────────────
Parent Company: Aipify Group AS

Future subsidiaries, investments, or specialized entities may be established
under Aipify Group AS as the company grows.

Aipify Group AS may invest in:
• Technology companies
• Digital products
• Artificial intelligence initiatives
• Strategic partnerships
• Real estate
• Other opportunities aligned with the company's long-term vision

Platform Admin (/platform/*) serves Aipify Group AS internal operations.

────────────────────────────────────────

────────────────────────────────────────
§8 GLOBAL MINDSET
────────────────────────────────────────
Although founded in Norway, Aipify is designed from day one for international
adoption.

The platform must support:
• Multiple languages
• Multiple currencies
• Regulatory environments
• Payment providers
• Cultural expectations

Aipify should never be limited by geography.

Internal reminder:
Think globally. Build responsibly. Act professionally.

────────────────────────────────────────
§9 DECISION FILTER (FINAL DIRECTIVE)
────────────────────────────────────────
Whenever future decisions are made regarding product design, infrastructure,
marketing, legal documentation, investment planning, expansion, pricing,
partnerships, integrations, or platform evolution — ask:

"Does this support the long-term vision of Aipify Group AS?"

If yes → continue.
If no  → reconsider.

────────────────────────────────────────
§10 CURSOR / AGENT RULES
────────────────────────────────────────
Before any feature, doc, copy, or architecture change:

1. Recognize Aipify Group AS as the parent company — not merely a repo name.
2. Preserve human empowerment over replacement narratives.
3. Maintain enterprise quality as minimum bar.
4. Design for global adoption from Norway.
5. Use correct company vs product naming in customer-facing and legal copy.
