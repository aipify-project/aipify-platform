# Implementation Blueprint — Phase 7: Self Love Foundation

**Feature owner:** Customer App  
**Implementation:** [Self Love Engine — Phase A.76](./SELF_LOVE_ENGINE_PHASE_A76.md)

This document defines **Phase 7** of the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) implementation blueprint. Self Love as functional wellbeing behavior across user, team, organization, and system health.

## Mission

Turn Self Love from philosophy into functional behavior — healthier, sustainable ways of working.

## Core philosophy

Self Love is a **value** (not trademark). Care, reflection, recovery, and balance. Support hard work without glorifying exhaustion.

## ABOS principle

> Systems that care for themselves are better equipped to care for others.

## Application areas

| Area | Examples |
|------|----------|
| **User Wellbeing** | Break suggestions, sustainable pacing, progress celebration |
| **Team Health** | Handoff reminders, support pressure awareness (aggregate) |
| **Organization Health** | KC cleanup, workflow simplification, admin workload |
| **System Health** | Quality Guardian and Observability aggregate counts |

## Communication

Rose emoji phrases in ILM and dashboard metadata:

- 🌹 Remember some Self Love today.
- 🌹 A little Self Love can go a long way.
- 💚 Self Love recommends taking a short break.

## Configuration

**Organization:** reminder frequency, quiet hours, tone, dashboard insights, workspace settings  
**User:** reminder prefs, tone, channels, pause suggestions

## Trust

Explainable recommendations with reason strings. Metadata only — no PII.

## Success criteria (live)

- Natural companion interactions
- User configuration
- Organization workspace settings
- Explainable recommendations
- System health connected
- Clarity without annoyance

## Dogfooding

- **Aipify Group internal** — admin workload, KC maintenance, platform health
- **Unonight pilot** — support pressure, KC cleanup, workflow simplification

## Distinction

Not Attention Guardian, LifeOS, Companion Identity orchestration, Presence & Comfort, or duplicate Quality/Observability engines.
