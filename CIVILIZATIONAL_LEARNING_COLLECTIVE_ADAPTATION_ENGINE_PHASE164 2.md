Institutions that learn together with humility — adapting through shared reflection while preserving human accountability — because collective wisdom grows when no single voice claims to have all answers.

## Role

**Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170)** — Collective Adaptation Center for shared learning and adaptive intelligence. **NOT** centralized authority or universal consensus.

## Principle

People First. Collective learning through humility and reflection. Growth Partner terminology — never Affiliate. Adaptation Companion supports growth — does NOT determine direction.
