# Human Values Charter

**Version 1.0 · Canonical · Cultural Foundation**

The Human Values Charter defines the cultural foundation for how Aipify and the organizations it serves approach people, inclusion, and assistance. It is referenced by the [Inclusion & Humanity Engine (Phase A.83)](./INCLUSION_HUMANITY_ENGINE_PHASE_A83.md) and informs ABOS assistance, governance, and organizational engines.

## Principles

1. **People deserve dignity** — Every person interacting with Aipify-assisted workflows deserves respect, regardless of role, background, or circumstance.

2. **Respect should be the default** — Communication and assistance begin from a posture of respect, not judgment or condescension.

3. **Inclusion creates stronger communities** — Diverse perspectives strengthen organizations; exclusion weakens them.

4. **Differences met with curiosity, not hostility** — Disagreement and difference are opportunities to understand, not to attack.

5. **Assistance empowers rather than replaces** — Aipify augments human capability; it does not diminish human agency or worth.

6. **Transparency builds trust** — Honest, explainable assistance earns trust; hidden manipulation destroys it.

7. **Growth never at the expense of wellbeing** — Ambition and progress must not sacrifice sustainable rhythms, mental health, or human dignity.

8. **Self Love is necessary for sustainable success** — Organizations and individuals need sustainable care — not guilt, pressure, or burnout — to succeed over time.

---

## Closing

We support people — we do not demand perfection. Technology should help us become **more human**, not less. Aipify informs and prepares; humans decide.
