AIPIFY HOSTS – GUEST INTELLIGENCE ENGINE

PURPOSE
Deliver intelligent guest communication, experience support, and relationship stewardship without replacing human hospitality.

POSITIONING
Thoughtful hospitality at scale. Humans decide. Aipify prepares.

FEATURE OWNER
CUSTOMER APP

ROUTE
/app/aipify-hosts/guest-intelligence

OBJECTIVES
Faster guest response times · Higher satisfaction · Consistent tone · Multi-language support · Reduced host stress

MODULES
1 Guest Operations Center — unified inbox across channels, message threading, history overview
2 Message Preparation Engine — draft suggestions, tone adaptation, template library (never auto-send without approval)
3 Multi-Language Communication — automatic translation, locale-aware responses
4 Guest Experience Companion — property-specific companion for Wi-Fi, parking, appliances, house rules, local recommendations
5 Guest Profile Intelligence — stay history metadata, preference patterns (metadata only — no raw chat storage)
6 Escalation & Priority Engine — urgent requests, communication concerns, response time tracking
7 Welcome & Check-In Engine — arrival instructions, access confirmations, welcome message readiness
8 Guest Follow-Up Engine — post-stay reminders, review prompts (recommendations only)
9 Guest Knowledge Center — communication etiquette, international guest guidance, hospitality best practices
10 Guest Satisfaction Insights — satisfaction indicators, response metrics, improvement recommendations

GOVERNANCE
Never impersonate the host in personal messages
Sensitive actions require approval
No automated personal messaging without explicit consent
Metadata only — never store raw guest conversations in platform metrics

TERMINOLOGY
Guest Experience Companion — not AI assistant or chatbot
Aipify prepares · Host approves · Guest receives human hospitality

INTEGRATIONS
Business DNA tone profiles (cross-link)
PAME/RSI principles — relationship support, not replacement

SUCCESS METRICS
Faster response times · Higher guest satisfaction · Reduced communication gaps
