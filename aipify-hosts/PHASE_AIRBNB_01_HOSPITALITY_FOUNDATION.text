AIPIFY – PHASE AIRBNB 01
AIPIFY HOSTS – HOSPITALITY BUSINESS OPERATING SYSTEM

PURPOSE
Develop Aipify Hosts as a dedicated Business Pack within the Aipify ecosystem for short-term rental operators, Airbnb hosts, property managers and hospitality businesses.

POSITIONING
Aipify Hosts is the Business Operating System for modern hospitality businesses.
Operational layer around Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo, Expedia, and direct bookings — NOT a replacement.

SUPPORTED PLATFORMS
Airbnb · Booking.com · Vrbo · Expedia · Direct bookings · Future hospitality integrations
