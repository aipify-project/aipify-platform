AIPIFY HOSTS — FOUNDATION 01
PROPERTY LICENSING & COMMERCIAL PACK FOUNDATION

PURPOSE
Property-based licensing for Aipify Hosts. One platform, one codebase, one feature set.
Licensing determines operational capacity only — never functionality.

CORE PRINCIPLES
* Same product for everyone.
* No feature fragmentation.
* No separate editions.
* Capacity scales with customer growth.
* Revenue scales with customer success.

PROPERTY TIERS
hosts_solo — 1 property — Individual hosts
hosts_5 — up to 5 properties — Small hospitality businesses
hosts_10 — up to 10 properties — Professional operators
hosts_20 — up to 20 properties — Established hospitality businesses
hosts_enterprise — custom limit — Large operators via sales

ADDITIONAL PROPERTY PACKS
+1 Property License — add capacity without full plan upgrade
Example: Hosts 5 customer with 6 properties purchases one additional license

CAPACITY RULES
assert_aipify_hosts_property_capacity before property creation
IF active_property_count >= property_limit THEN block creation + upgrade workflow
Existing properties remain operational on renewal issues
Downgrades require active properties within new limit before activation

UPGRADE EXPERIENCE
Headline: You have reached your property limit.
Actions: View plans · Upgrade now · Contact sales
Routes: /app/settings/billing/packages · mailto:sales@aipify.ai

PAYMENT PRINCIPLE
After successful payment: license + property_limit update immediately, no logout required
add_aipify_hosts_property_license for +1 packs (post-payment webhook in production)

AUDIT LOGGING
license_plan_change · property_created · additional_property_license_purchased · dashboard_view

INTEGRATIONS
License Center — get_customer_license_center
Commercial Packages — is_tenant_module_enabled('hospitality')
Business pack — business_packs.pack_key = hospitality

ROUTES
/app/aipify-hosts — primary workspace + capacity UI
POST /api/aipify/aipify-hosts/properties — create property
POST /api/aipify/aipify-hosts/licensing/add-property-license — +1 license
/app/settings/billing/packages — plan upgrades
/app/license — Trust & License Center

TERMINOLOGY
Aipify Hosts — Business Operating System for hospitality
Never booking platform replacement · Never feature-tier gating

END OF FOUNDATION.
