AIPIFY – PHASE AIRBNB 08
AIPIFY HOSTS – OWNER PORTAL

PURPOSE
Provide property owners, co-hosts, and external stakeholders a secure read-focused portal for portfolio visibility — distinct from the daily operator workspace.

POSITIONING
Transparency for owners. Control for operators. Trust for everyone.

FEATURE OWNER
CUSTOMER APP (operator configuration) · Embedded read portal (owner access)

ROUTE
/app/aipify-hosts/owner-portal — operator configuration
/portal/hosts/{tenant}/owner — external owner access (future embedded surface)

OBJECTIVES
Owner transparency · Reduced back-and-forth reporting · Secure limited access · Professional property management relationships

MODULES
1 Owner Dashboard — occupancy summary, revenue overview, property health (aggregates only)
2 Owner Reporting Engine — monthly statements, performance summaries, exportable reports
3 Owner Communication Hub — operator-to-owner updates, announcement delivery (no guest impersonation)
4 Access & Permissions Engine — owner read-only, co-host scoped, investor summary roles
5 Document Center — owner agreements, insurance summaries, compliance status (metadata references)
6 Owner Approval Queue — capital expenditure, major maintenance, policy changes requiring owner sign-off
7 Multi-Owner Portfolio View — property management companies with multiple beneficial owners
8 Owner Notification Preferences — arrival summaries, incident alerts, monthly reports
9 Owner Knowledge Center — how to read reports, understanding property health, stewardship expectations
10 Enterprise Owner Governance — portfolio-wide owner policies, audit logging, approval workflows

USER ROLES
Property Owner — read-focused portfolio visibility
Co-Host — scoped operational visibility per property assignment
Enterprise Administrator — portal configuration, owner invitations, governance

GOVERNANCE
Owners never see other tenants
Operators control what owners can view
Financial detail level configurable per owner relationship
All owner portal access audit logged
Sensitive owner actions require two-factor authentication

SECURITY
Separate session scope from operator workspace
Invitation-based provisioning — no open registration
Read-only default — write actions require explicit role grant

INTEGRATIONS
License Center — owner messaging on software licensing vs data ownership
Trust Architecture — metadata-first reporting
Phase Airbnb 05 trust & compliance — incident summaries for owners (approved disclosure only)

TERMINOLOGY
Owner Portal — professional transparency surface
Never expose raw guest PII to owners unless operator explicitly enables approved fields

END OF PHASE.
