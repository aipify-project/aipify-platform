AIPIFY – PHASE AIRBNB 02
AIPIFY HOSTS – CHANNEL & BOOKING INTELLIGENCE ENGINE

PURPOSE
Centralized booking intelligence connecting multiple hospitality channels into a unified operational experience.
Eliminate constant platform switching while preserving each channel's strengths.

POSITIONING
One operational center. Every booking channel.

SUPPORTED CHANNELS
Airbnb · Booking.com · Vrbo · Expedia · Direct Website Bookings · Future OTA integrations

FEATURE OWNER
CUSTOMER APP

ROUTE
/app/aipify-hosts/channel-booking-intelligence

OBJECTIVES
Reduce operational complexity · Prevent booking conflicts · Improve occupancy · Increase revenue visibility · Centralize booking intelligence

MODULES
1 Channel Command Center — channel performance, arrivals, departures, reservation summaries, occupancy timelines
2 Booking Timeline Engine — property calendars, cross-property overview, gap-night detection, heatmaps
3 Smart Gap Filler — short vacancy detection, pricing windows, minimum stay recommendations
4 Cancellation Recovery Engine — newly available inventory, promotional strategies, remarketing
5 Direct Booking Assistant — inquiry management, follow-up reminders, payment status (approval required for financial actions)
6 Booking Risk Intelligence — last-minute bookings, unusual patterns, risk scores (recommendations only)
7 Arrival Preparation Center — cleaning verification, maintenance checks, welcome message readiness
8 Departure Operations Center — turnover tracking, damage inspection prompts, inventory reviews
9 Occupancy Forecast Engine — seasonal patterns, local events, revenue projections, staffing forecasts
10 Executive Booking Insights — occupancy trends, revenue by property/channel, ADR, RevPAN

DASHBOARD INDICATORS
Total active bookings · Revenue by channel · Average stay duration · Cancellation trends · Booking pace

USER ROLES
Host — booking oversight, recommendations
Property Manager — multi-property administration, team coordination
Enterprise Administrator — portfolio visibility, governance approvals, strategic reporting

AUDIT LOGGING
Reservation adjustments · Risk acknowledgements · Recommendation approvals · Channel configuration · Executive actions

KNOWLEDGE CENTER
Booking optimization · Occupancy management · Channel diversification · Cancellation recovery · Revenue best practices

ARCHITECTURE
Built on Aipify Hosts hospitality framework · Shared governance · Shared notifications · Shared Knowledge Center · Shared audit · Metadata-only booking snapshots until connectors approved

TERMINOLOGY
Aipify Recommendations — never AI recommendations
Final decisions remain human-controlled
