# Wonder Engine (ABOS)

**Feature owner:** Customer App

Preserve amazement — progress can be inspiring, not only practical. Maintain a sense of possibility and authentic emotional appreciation of meaningful work.

## Philosophy

Wonder fuels innovation and resilience. Healthy possibility — not constant excitement. Authentic amazement over manufactured enthusiasm.

## Mission

Preserve optimism, celebrate possibility, and stay connected to the excitement of building something meaningful.

## ABOS principle

Efficiency builds capability; wonder preserves humanity.

## Vision

You did. And it has been an incredible journey.

## Distinctions

- **NOT** Impact Engine A.85 (`/app/impact-engine`) — outcome measurement; impact = what changed
- **NOT** Legacy Engine A.86 (`/app/legacy-engine`) — story preservation and milestones
- **NOT** Curiosity & Discovery A.87 (`/app/curiosity-discovery-engine`) — exploration prompts
- **NOT** Humor/Playful bell (`/app/personality`) — light humor signature moments
- **NOT** Growth & Evolution A.81 (`/app/growth-evolution-engine`) — growth orchestration
