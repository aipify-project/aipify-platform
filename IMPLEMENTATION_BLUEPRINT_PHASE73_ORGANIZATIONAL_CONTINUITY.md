# Implementation Blueprint — Phase 73: Organizational Continuity Engine

**Feature owner:** Customer App  
**Engine phase:** Phase 80 extension (Continuity, Resilience & Crisis Management)  
**Route:** `/app/continuity`

> **Mapping:** Blueprint Phase 73 extends **Continuity Phase 80** — backup ownership, critical processes, readiness, and incident mode already exist. Documented in `_ocontbp_distinction_note()`.

## Mission

Preserve operational effectiveness — critical knowledge, responsibilities, and processes remain accessible and sustainable.

## Core philosophy

**People are invaluable** — organizations should not depend on individual memory. Continuity protects organizations and the people within them.

## ABOS principle

Preserve knowledge, distribute responsibility, prepare for change — continuity protects people and progress. Aipify informs and prepares; humans decide.

## Objectives

| Key | Focus |
|-----|-------|
| **Knowledge continuity** | Institutional preservation, documentation support, transfer recommendations, cross-training |
| **Responsibility continuity** | Backup ownership, role coverage, distributed accountability |
| **Leadership continuity** | Leadership transition preparation and succession awareness |
| **Operational resilience** | Single points of failure, dependencies, knowledge concentration, process vulnerabilities |
| **Transition support** | Upcoming transitions, handoff planning — foresight not pressure |
| **Succession awareness** | Responsibility mapping and knowledge transfer guidance |
