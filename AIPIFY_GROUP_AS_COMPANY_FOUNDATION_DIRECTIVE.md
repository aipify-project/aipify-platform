## Mission

Aipify Group AS exists to develop intelligent software products and services that help organizations:

- Operate more efficiently
- Make better decisions
- Automate repetitive tasks
- Improve customer experiences
- Unlock human potential through responsible use of artificial intelligence

**Aipify is not built to replace people.**

**Aipify is built to empower people.**

---

## Vision

To become one of the world's leading providers of AI-powered business operating systems — helping organizations of all sizes work smarter, respond faster, and focus more on what truly matters.

Aipify should evolve into a **trusted companion** for businesses around the world.

---

## Company principles

- Build for trust before scale.
- Build for longevity before trends.
- Enterprise quality is the minimum standard.
- Human oversight remains essential.
- Technology should remove friction, not humanity.
- Every feature should solve a real problem.
- Global ambitions must be supported by local understanding.
- The best products are often built from frustrations experienced firsthand.

---

## Origin story

Aipify was born from a simple realization:

Modern businesses spend too much time navigating complexity, responding to repetitive questions, managing fragmented systems, and performing tasks that technology should already be assisting with.

The founders experienced these frustrations themselves. Instead of waiting for someone else to solve the problem, they decided to build the solution.

What started as an idea evolved into a vision: **an AI-powered Business Operating System** capable of supporting organizations in their daily operations.

## Corporate structure

**Parent company:** Aipify Group AS

Future subsidiaries, investments, or specialized entities may be established under Aipify Group AS as the company grows.

Aipify Group AS may invest in:

- Technology companies
- Digital products
- Artificial intelligence initiatives
- Strategic partnerships
- Real estate
- Other opportunities aligned with the company's long-term vision

**Platform Admin** (`/platform/*`) serves Aipify Group AS internal SaaS operations. **Customer App** serves paying customer tenants. Never conflate the two — see [multi-tenant separation](./.cursor/rules/multi-tenant-separation.mdc).
