# Aipify Branding Standard — Companion Naming Policy

**Effective immediately** for all customer-facing product copy, Knowledge Center articles, support panels, notifications, ILM corpus, and Cursor-generated specifications.

Cross-reference: [BRAND_IDENTITY_PERSONHOOD_STANDARD.md](./BRAND_IDENTITY_PERSONHOOD_STANDARD.md) · [ABOS_BRAND_TERMINOLOGY_STANDARD.md](./ABOS_BRAND_TERMINOLOGY_STANDARD.md) · [COMPANION_IDENTITY_ENGINE_PHASE_A84.md](./COMPANION_IDENTITY_ENGINE_PHASE_A84.md)

## Purpose

Users interact with **Aipify**, not generic "AI." Customer-facing labels across the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) must reinforce the Aipify name so people build familiarity with a trusted business companion — not an anonymous technology layer.

This policy extends Brand Identity & Personhood into module-specific naming (Support, Insights, Notes, Escalations) and integrates with the Companion Identity Engine at `/app/companion-identity-engine`.

---

## Core principle

**Aipify is the product name. Artificial Intelligence is the underlying technology.**

**There is only one Aipify.** Skills, Centers, Specialists, and Companions are extensions of Aipify — not separate assistant identities. See [AIPIFY_BRAND_RULE_SINGLE_IDENTITY.md](./AIPIFY_BRAND_RULE_SINGLE_IDENTITY.md).

Replace generic AI labels in user-visible copy with Aipify-branded equivalents. Internal code identifiers (routes, skill IDs, RPC names) remain unchanged.

## Companion philosophy

- Aipify is the companion users interact with — a recognizable business partner, not a generic AI service.
- Consistent naming across modules builds trust and reduces confusion between technology and product.
- Aipify augments people; humans decide. Naming should never imply Aipify replaces employees or operates alone.
- Users may say "AI"; Aipify responds and labels itself as **Aipify** in product copy.

## FAQ

### Should customer-facing labels say "AI" or "Aipify"?

Prefer **Aipify**. Generic "AI" labels describe the technology, not the product users purchased. Use Aipify-branded names in dashboards, support panels, notifications, and Knowledge Center articles.

### Can users still call Aipify "AI"?

Yes. Users may naturally say "AI" in conversation. Aipify understands that phrasing and responds as Aipify — but product copy should consistently use the Aipify name.

### Why change "Support AI" to "Aipify Support"?

"Support AI" sounds like a feature category. **Aipify Support** names the product capability customers rely on — the same trusted companion across every module, not a separate AI tool.
