# Aipify — Customer Feedback & Product Improvement Policy

## Purpose

Aipify continuously improves its services based on customer interactions, voluntary feedback, and aggregated usage insights.

---

## Principles

- We are transparent about how feedback is collected.
- We only collect information necessary to improve the platform.
- We do not sell customer feedback data.
- We do not use individual feedback for advertising purposes.
- Customers retain control over the information they choose to share.

---

## What we may collect

To improve the quality of Aipify, we may collect:

- Feature ratings (e.g. thumbs up / thumbs down)
- Voluntary feedback submissions
- Improvement suggestions
- Bug reports
- Feature requests
- Anonymous product usage statistics
- Interaction outcomes (e.g. whether a suggested action was helpful)
- Aggregated platform analytics

---

## Purpose of collection

This information may be used to:

- Improve existing features
- Identify usability issues
- Prioritize product development
- Detect recurring problems
- Enhance customer experiences
- Improve AI-assisted recommendations
- Understand how Aipify can better support organizations

---

## What we do not do

Aipify does **not**:

- Sell feedback data to third parties
- Use feedback for targeted advertising
- Share customer-specific feedback publicly without permission
- Monitor customer communications unrelated to Aipify services

---

## Voluntary participation

Providing written feedback is voluntary.

Customers may choose whether they wish to be contacted regarding their feedback.

---

## Transparency

Where feedback mechanisms exist within the platform, Aipify clearly indicates when information is collected to improve products and services.

Examples include:

- Feedback buttons
- Improvement forms
- Feature ratings
- Product surveys

---

## Trust principle

**Feedback is a gift.**

Aipify exists to serve its customers. Listening honestly and improving continuously are essential parts of that responsibility.
