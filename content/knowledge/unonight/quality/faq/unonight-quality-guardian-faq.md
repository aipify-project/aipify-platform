Aipify flags the upgrade flow when the membership upgrade link points to the wrong destination, Shopify checkout redirects fail, webhooks respond slowly, or return URLs do not match documented behaviour. This protects revenue and member activation after purchase.

Verification pages are core to trust and safety on Unonight. Aipify checks that instructions are visible, forms work on mobile, and admin verification queues remain accessible — because broken verification blocks new members and increases support load.
