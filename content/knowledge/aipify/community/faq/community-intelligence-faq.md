# Community Intelligence — FAQ

## What is Community & Collective Intelligence?

Collective Intelligence helps organizations learn from shared experiences, validated practices and community insights.

## Does Aipify share our confidential information?

No. Participation is voluntary and shared intelligence must be anonymized and governed.

## Can we disable Community participation?

Yes. Organizations retain full control over participation settings.

## How are contributions reviewed?

Contributions pass through moderation, governance and quality review processes before publication.

## Can organizations contribute their own best practices?

Yes. Organizations may choose to share approved experiences and lessons learned.

## What is Community Health Score?

Community Health Score measures the strength and quality of the Collective Intelligence ecosystem.

## What is Collective Contribution Score?

Contribution Score reflects the usefulness and quality of shared intelligence.

## Can community insights improve Knowledge Center content?

Yes. Validated learnings may strengthen guides, FAQs and best practices.

## Who owns the information we contribute?

Organizations retain ownership of their original information and determine what may be shared.

## Is this social media?

No. Community Intelligence focuses on professional learning, validated practices and collective improvement.
