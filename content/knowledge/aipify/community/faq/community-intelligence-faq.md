# Community Intelligence — FAQ

## What is Community & Collective Intelligence?

Community & Collective Intelligence helps organizations learn from anonymized experiences, validated best practices and shared insights.

## Is our information shared automatically?

No. Participation is voluntary and governed by approval processes.

## Can we disable Community participation?

Yes. Organizations retain complete control over participation settings.

## How are contributions reviewed?

Contributions undergo governance, moderation and anonymization checks before publication.

## Can our organization contribute best practices?

Yes. Organizations may choose to share approved experiences and recommendations.

## What is Community Health Score?

Community Health Score measures the overall strength and quality of the Collective Intelligence ecosystem.

## What is Collective Intelligence Score?

It reflects the usefulness, quality and validation level of community contributions.

## Can Community insights improve Aipify itself?

Yes. Validated learnings may improve Knowledge Center content, Blueprints, Business Packs and recommendations.

## Who owns contributed knowledge?

Organizations retain ownership of their original information and determine what may be shared.

## Is this a social media platform?

No. Community Intelligence focuses on professional learning, validated practices and collective improvement.
