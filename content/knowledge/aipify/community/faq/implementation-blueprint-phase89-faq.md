# Implementation Blueprint Phase 89 — Community & Collective Intelligence FAQ

## What is Blueprint Phase 89?

Blueprint Phase 89 extends Community & Collective Intelligence (repo Phase 89 + Blueprint Phase 24 + Phase 52) with collective experience insights, idea discovery, community recognition, and companion guidance that amplifies wisdom — at `/app/community`.

## How is this different from Gratitude & Recognition (A.89)?

**Gratitude & Recognition Engine Phase A.89** at `/app/gratitude-recognition-engine` handles recognition experiences. **Repo Community Phase 89** and **Blueprint Phase 89** at `/app/community` handle collective intelligence. Engine phase number collision only — cross-link for recognition, do not duplicate storage.

## How is this different from Blueprint Phase 24 and Phase 52?

**Blueprint Phase 24** and **Phase 52** fields are **preserved** on the dashboard. Blueprint Phase 89 appends `community_collective_intelligence_blueprint_phase89` with additional scaffolds — collective intelligence sources, idea discovery, community recognition, and shared-learning-not-surveillance privacy principles.

## What is the mission?

Strengthen learning, innovation, and adaptability through collective experience insights — from `_ccibp89_mission()`.

## What is the philosophy?

Wisdom rarely lives in one individual alone — collective intelligence strengthens people, it does not replace them.

## What does "shared learning, not surveillance" mean?

Aipify surfaces governed, anonymized collective patterns. No hidden profiling, no public exposure of private experiences without permission, no using contributions without approval, and no reducing individuals to data points.

## What collective intelligence sources are documented?

Customer feedback, Sales Expert experiences, support observations, internal communities, KC contributions, approved suggestions, and community discussions — from `_ccibp89_collective_intelligence_sources()`.

## What is idea discovery?

Feature requests, process improvements, customer needs, training opportunities, and product innovation themes — surfaced from governed contributions, never confidential records.

## How does companion guidance work?

Companion guidance amplifies wisdom — it does not dominate. 🦉🌹🔔 examples prepare optional insights; humans decide timing and adoption.

## How does community recognition connect to Gratitude A.89?

Recognition domains (contributions, learning, support, leadership) cross-link `/app/gratitude-recognition-engine` — recognition connection only.

## What learning organization surfaces connect?

Knowledge Center A.5, FAQ evolution, Learning & Training A.36, product evolution, and Employee Knowledge (EKE) — governed publication only.

## What are the Phase 89 success criteria?

Computed live by `_ccibp89_success_criteria(organization_id)`: collective intelligence sources, community observations, idea discovery, companion guidance, community recognition, learning organization connection, privacy principles, anonymization, Self Love connection, trust connection, integration links, and vision phrase.

## Where is the dashboard?

`/app/community` — RPCs `get_community_intelligence_dashboard()` and `get_community_intelligence_card()`. Admin at `/app/community/admin`.
