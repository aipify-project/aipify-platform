# Implementation Blueprint Phase 52 — Collective Learning & Wisdom FAQ

## What is Phase 52 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 52 extends Community & Collective Intelligence (Phase 89 + Blueprint Phase 24) with the **Collective Learning & Wisdom Engine** — anonymized collective wisdom compounding across the ecosystem via `/app/community`. Observations **inform**, they do not **prescribe**.

## How is this different from Global Learning Network (Phase 72)?

**Global Learning Network Phase 72** at `/app/global-learning` handles platform evolution intelligence. **Phase 52** compounds anonymized collective wisdom through the tenant community hub at `/app/community`. Distinct surfaces — cross-link only.

## How is this different from the Learning Engine (Phase 23)?

**Learning Engine Phase 23** at `/app/learning` stores tenant learning memory with human approval. **Phase 52** surfaces cross-ecosystem anonymized aggregates from governed community contributions — not tenant-specific learning loops.

## How is this different from Wisdom Engine (A.93)?

**Wisdom Engine A.93** at `/app/wisdom-engine` delivers companion wisdom interventions. Phase 52 may cross-link collective observations into companion recommendations — Wisdom Engine informs; Phase 52 provides ecosystem-wide anonymized patterns via community intelligence.

## How is this different from Organizational Memory (A.34)?

**Organizational Memory A.34 / OME** stores tenant-scoped memory. Phase 52 aggregates anonymized wisdom **across** the ecosystem — never org identity or cross-tenant confidential records.

## What is the inform-not-prescribe principle?

Collective observations (🦉🌹🔔) surface patterns worth reviewing — humans decide what to adopt. Aipify never prescribes outcomes from collective intelligence.

## What objectives does Phase 52 cover?

Pattern recognition, best practice evolution, ecosystem observations, knowledge evolution, continuous improvement, and responsible insights — from `_clwbp_blueprint_objectives()`.

## What best practice domains are documented?

Support, sales, implementation, operational, and training — from `_clwbp_blueprint_best_practice_evolution()`.

## What anonymization rules apply?

Aggregates only — aligned with `lib/impact/privacy.ts` forbidden keys. No identities, PII, confidential records, competitive intelligence, or org identity in collective payloads.

## How does Knowledge Center connect?

FAQs, training (A.36), certification (A.37), industry guidance, and companion recommendations may be enriched by collective patterns — governed publication only.

## How does Sales Expert connect?

Collective sales wisdom examples (discovery, renewals, onboarding, outreach) — metadata only. Distinct from Sales Expert Community Phase 47 peer mentorship at `/app/sales-expert-engine`.

## How does Executive Insights connect?

Ecosystem trends, themes, and positive signals — aggregates only for Executive Insights A.35 at `/app/executive`. Never individual org performance.

## What does collective summary show?

Live counts from `_clwbp_collective_summary(organization_id)` — tenant contributions, ecosystem published totals, category and type aggregates, average ratings — empty-safe, no PII.

## What are the Phase 52 success criteria?

Computed live by `_clwbp_blueprint_success_criteria(organization_id)`: collective wisdom available, anonymization effective, inform not prescribe, best practice evolution, KC connection, executive connection, voluntary participation, Self Love connection, and distinct integration links.

## Where is the dashboard?

`/app/community` — RPCs `get_community_intelligence_dashboard()` and `get_community_intelligence_card()`. Admin at `/app/community/admin`.
