# Companion Relationship Engine — FAQ

## What is Aipify Companion?

Aipify is a **Companion** — a consistent, trustworthy business companion for your organization. The official name is **Aipify**. Aipify is not a chatbot, generic assistant, or disposable tool. Skills, Centers, and capabilities are extensions of one Aipify — not separate AI products. Access the Companion Relationship Center at `/app/companion/relationship`.

## How does personality work?

The Personality Engine supports professional, friendly, supportive, encouraging, calm, confident, respectful, and adaptive styles. Personality profiles align with core values: People First, Technology Second, Self Love, Wisdom Before Speed, Trust Before Automation, Help Before Sales, and Responsibility Before Power. Aipify adapts tone and style — it does not impersonate or manipulate.

## How are preferences remembered?

Relationship memory stores approved metadata only — preferences, communication patterns, interaction summaries, and companion learnings. Users control memory permissions. Memories require approval before use. Users can review and remove stored preferences at any time. Raw chat transcripts are not stored in relationship memory.

## How does adaptive communication work?

Adaptive communication adjusts tone, detail level, and style for executives, employees, customers, partners, industry contexts, and regional preferences. Communication profiles define audience-specific guidance. Users set tone, notification style, humor preference, and interaction preference in the Relationship Center.

## How is trust measured?

The Trust Engine tracks consistency, reliability, accuracy, helpfulness, responsiveness, and relationship strength. Trust scores are transparent and reviewable — never hidden or manipulated. Trust improves through consistent companion identity and reliable follow-through on prepared actions.

## How is relationship history managed?

Relationship history includes interaction metadata, communication history, approved memories, companion learnings, and relationship milestones (first interaction, first success, major achievements, organization anniversaries, growth milestones). All data is tenant-isolated; personal preferences are user-scoped. Full audit logging tracks preference updates, milestone events, memory approvals, and trust score changes.

## How is this different from Identity & Relationship (Phase 328)?

**Identity & Relationship** (`/app/companion/identity-relationship`) provides user-facing identity settings, trust reviews, and introduction framework. **Companion Relationship Engine** (`/app/companion/relationship`) is the Phase 423 enterprise framework — personality system, trust engine, relationship analytics, governance, and executive companion dashboard.

## How is this different from Assistant Identity (AIE)?

**Assistant Identity** (`/app/assistant/identity`) adapts per-user communication style with observation approval. **Companion Relationship Engine** governs organization-wide companion identity, trust signals, relationship milestones, and responsible personalization at the enterprise level.

## Governance boundaries

- Users control personalization
- Users control memory permissions
- Users can review stored preferences
- Users can disable personalization
- Trust remains transparent
- Human override always available
- Full audit logging of relationship lifecycle events
