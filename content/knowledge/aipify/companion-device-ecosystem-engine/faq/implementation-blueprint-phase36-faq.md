# Implementation Blueprint Phase 36 — Companion Device Ecosystem FAQ

## What does Blueprint Phase 36 add?

Mission, philosophy, device priority roadmap (phases 1–4), companion continuity examples, voice/wearable scaffolds, Self Love and trust connections, dogfooding notes, success criteria, and ABOS principle — all via `_cdebp_*` SQL helpers.

## What is the phased device roadmap?

1. **Desktop** — Command Center + Desktop Companion (active)
2. **Mobile** — A.17 mobile-ready layer (native app future)
3. **Tablet** — executive dashboards, operations center, KC (scaffold)
4. **Emerging** — smartwatch, voice, automotive (future)

## What are companion continuity examples?

- 🌹 Continue earlier work across devices
- 🦉 Cross-device notes
- 🔔 Important follow-ups on the current device

## How does Self Love connect?

Gentle wellbeing nudges across devices — never intrusive. Cross-link to `/app/self-love-engine` (A.76).

## How does trust connect?

Transparent connected devices, permissions, sync scope, and revocation. Users always understand what is linked and can disconnect.
