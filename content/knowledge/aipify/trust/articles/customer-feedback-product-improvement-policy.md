# Customer Feedback & Product Improvement Policy

## Purpose

Aipify continuously improves its services based on customer interactions, voluntary feedback, and aggregated usage insights.

## Principles

- Transparent collection practices
- Only information necessary to improve the platform
- No sale of customer feedback data
- No use of individual feedback for advertising
- Customer control over shared information

## What we may collect

- Feature ratings (e.g. thumbs up / thumbs down)
- Voluntary feedback submissions, improvement suggestions, bug reports, and feature requests
- Anonymous product usage statistics and aggregated platform analytics
- Interaction outcomes (e.g. whether a suggested action was helpful)

## Purpose of collection

To improve features, identify usability issues, prioritize development, detect recurring problems, enhance experiences, improve AI-assisted recommendations, and understand how Aipify can better support organizations.

## What we do not do

Aipify does **not** sell feedback to third parties, use feedback for targeted advertising, share customer-specific feedback publicly without permission, or monitor communications unrelated to Aipify services.

## Voluntary participation

Written feedback is voluntary. You choose whether to be contacted regarding your submission.

## Transparency

Feedback buttons, improvement forms, feature ratings, and surveys clearly indicate when information is collected to improve products and services.

## Trust principle

> Feedback is a gift. Listening honestly and improving continuously are essential parts of serving our customers.
