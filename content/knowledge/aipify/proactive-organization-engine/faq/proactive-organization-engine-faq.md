# Proactive Organization & Anticipatory Support Engine — FAQ

## What is the Proactive Organization Center?

The Proactive Organization Center at `/app/proactive-organization-engine` is Aipify's organization-level early awareness and anticipatory support hub for the **Autonomous Organization Era (Phases 131–140)**. It surfaces emerging aggregate signals, support opportunities, organizational pulse metrics, and preventative recommendations — without replacing human judgment or monitoring individuals.

## How is this different from Proactive Companion (A.79)?

Proactive Companion at `/app/proactive-companion-engine` delivers **individual nudges and presence assistance** (Phases 25/56). Phase 135 operates at the **organization level** — early signals, org pulse, and preventative support scaffolds. Phase 135 cross-links A.79 for individual delivery but does **not duplicate** nudge delivery RPCs.

## Is this employee monitoring or surveillance?

**No.** Phase 135 follows a **care not surveillance** philosophy. All signals and pulse metrics are **organizational patterns and aggregate metadata only** — never individual employee monitoring, ranking, or PII surveillance.

## Can Aipify automatically intervene without approval?

**No.** Humans remain responsible. Any automated intervention requires **approved governance** via Human Oversight (A.40) and related approval workflows. Recommendations are pending until humans acknowledge or dismiss them — no silent auto-intervention.

## What early signal types are tracked?

Eight aggregate signal types: support volume trends, knowledge access patterns, workflow bottlenecks, transformation fatigue, companion adoption, learning participation, CSAT changes, and leadership overload awareness — all metadata, no individual surveillance.

## What preventative support does Phase 135 suggest?

Knowledge recommendations, training suggestions, workflow reviews, executive briefing scaffolds, communication guidance, governance reminders, and resource allocation awareness — all as **recommendations humans decide on**.

## Can I customize proactive organization settings?

Yes. Settings include proactive center enablement, anticipatory support, pulse monitoring, companion alerts, human governance requirements, and care-not-surveillance mode. Manage via `proactive_organization.manage` permission.

## How does Self Love connect?

Self Love (A.76) cross-links for empathy, patience, encouragement, balanced workload awareness, early supportive conversations, and compassionate leadership — especially for transformation fatigue and leadership overload signals.
