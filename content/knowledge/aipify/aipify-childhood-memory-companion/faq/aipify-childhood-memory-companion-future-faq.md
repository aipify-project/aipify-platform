# Aipify Childhood & Memory Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Childhood & Memory Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that helps families preserve childhood memories, celebrate milestones, and create lasting family archives across generations.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Family Companion, Legacy Companion, Memory, Presence, and Privacy foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Childhood happens once. The little moments often become the memories people cherish most — Aipify should help families preserve those moments intentionally.

## How is this different from Parenting Companion?

**Parenting Companion** supports caregiver routines, school coordination, and daily responsibilities. **Childhood & Memory Companion** focuses on **documenting, organizing, and celebrating** childhood milestones and family archives.

## Can Aipify share my child's memories publicly?

**No.** Aipify must never publicly share memories without consent. Families control what is documented, who has access, and sharing permissions.

## Can Aipify pressure me to document everything?

**No.** Aipify must never pressure families to document everything or create unhealthy comparison between families.

## What is the Family Archive Principle?

Create meaningful records — annual memory books, milestone timelines, family story collections — that families may revisit for decades.

## Can insights create guilt about not documenting enough?

No. Insights should encourage appreciation — never create guilt.

## What data does this module store?

When implemented, it stores **metadata, milestone labels, and archive organization summaries** — with exceptional care for children's information. Full media and private child content require governed parental consent.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include printing memory books, organizing photo collections, and generating annual reflection reports — all requiring **parental approval**.

## Who is Childhood & Memory Companion for?

Parents and families who want to intentionally preserve childhood moments and build generational archives without replacing parental involvement.
