# Enterprise Innovation, R&D & Future Technologies Engine — FAQ

## How does Innovation Center work?

Innovation Center at `/app/innovation` is the unified innovation operating system for your organization. It connects idea management, research programs, experiments, technology radar, opportunity portfolios, competitive research, analytics, and governance in one tenant-scoped workspace. Aipify helps make innovation systematic rather than accidental.

## How are ideas evaluated?

Ideas are captured from employees, customers, partners, executives, Companion signals, market opportunities, and innovation requests. Each idea progresses through a lifecycle — submitted, review, research, experiment, validation, approval, implementation, and measurement. Evaluation measures business value, strategic alignment, implementation complexity, customer impact, revenue potential, risk level, and an overall innovation score.

## How are experiments managed?

Experiments support product, workflow, feature, pricing, customer, and operational tests. Each experiment moves through statuses: planned, approved, running, paused, completed, validated, rejected, or archived. Experiments require approval before execution, and outcomes are logged in the innovation audit trail.

## What is Technology Radar?

Technology Radar tracks emerging technologies, AI developments, industry trends, infrastructure trends, security trends, and platform opportunities. Items are classified by maturity — adopt, trial, assess, or hold — so leadership can prioritize evaluation and investment decisions.

## How are opportunities prioritized?

The Opportunity Portfolio tracks new markets, Business Packs, Industry Packs, integrations, products, revenue streams, and strategic opportunities. Opportunities are prioritized by business impact, strategic alignment, and pipeline stage — discovery through scale. Companion innovation advisor signals highlight opportunities requiring executive attention.

## How is innovation measured?

Innovation Analytics tracks ideas submitted, ideas approved, active experiments, validation rate, innovation velocity, business impact, and revenue impact. Innovation Health Score and executive dashboard metrics provide leadership visibility into pipeline health and ROI.

## How is this different from Innovation Lab?

**Innovation Lab** (`/app/innovation-lab`) focuses on exploratory lab workflows and prototyping. **Innovation Center** (`/app/innovation`) is the enterprise innovation operating system — full idea-to-scale pipeline, R&D programs, technology radar, opportunity portfolios, and governance.
