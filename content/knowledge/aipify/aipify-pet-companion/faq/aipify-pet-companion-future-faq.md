# Aipify Pet Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Pet Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that helps pet owners coordinate routines, appointments, reminders, and practical pet care responsibilities.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Memory, Life Events, Presence, Family Companion, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Pets are family members. Aipify helps people care for them more consistently and with less stress.

## Can Aipify diagnose my pet or recommend treatments?

**No.** Aipify must never diagnose illnesses, recommend medical treatments, or replace veterinarians. Medication reminders are **scheduling only** — not medical advice.

## What pet types are supported?

When implemented, profiles may include dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, horses, fish, reptiles, and small animals.

## How does this relate to Family Companion?

**Family Companion** handles household coordination. **Pet Companion** focuses specifically on pet routines, milestones, and care — they cross-link for shared household pet responsibilities.

## Can insights create guilt?

No. Insights should encourage responsible care — never guilt or fear.

## What about pet emergencies?

Aipify supports **preparedness** (emergency vet contacts, documents, household instructions). **Emergency decisions remain human-led.**

## What data does Pet Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **pet metadata, routines, and reminder summaries only** — not clinical veterinary records. Owners control visibility and can delete data.
