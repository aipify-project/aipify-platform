# Aipify Home Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Home Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that helps individuals and families coordinate household routines, maintenance, service providers, and practical home responsibilities.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Family Companion, Memory, Presence, Action Framework, and Governance foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

A home should be a place of comfort. Aipify reduces the invisible workload required to keep everyday life running smoothly.

## How does this relate to Family Companion?

**Family Companion** focuses on family events and shared responsibilities. **Home Companion** focuses on the **physical household** — maintenance, operations, service providers, and seasonal preparation. They cross-link for shared household coordination.

## Can Aipify hire contractors or make payments?

**No** without approval. Service coordination is scheduling and preparation. Financial commitments require approval profiles and financial guardrails.

## Can Aipify replace professional judgment for repairs?

**No.** Aipify coordinates reminders and appointments — it does not replace electricians, plumbers, or other professional judgment.

## What about home emergencies?

Aipify supports **preparedness** (contacts, documents, checklists). **Emergency response remains human-led.**

## Can insights create anxiety?

No. Insights should encourage preparedness — never anxiety.

## What data does Home Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **household metadata, maintenance schedules, and reminder summaries only**. Users control access and can delete data.
