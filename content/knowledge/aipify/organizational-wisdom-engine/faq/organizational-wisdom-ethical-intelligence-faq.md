# Organizational Wisdom & Ethical Intelligence — FAQ

## What is the Organizational Wisdom Engine?

The **Wisdom Center** at `/app/organizational-wisdom-engine` helps organizations reflect wisely and act with integrity. Aipify supports ethical awareness, values alignment, and discernment — it does **not** determine right or wrong.

## How is this different from the Wisdom Engine (A.93)?

**Wisdom Engine A.93** (`/app/wisdom-engine`) synthesizes experience into guidance. **Phase 129** unifies organizational wisdom with ethical reflection workspaces, values alignment reviews, and culture theme insights. Cross-link only — do not duplicate Wisdom A.93 RPCs.

## How is this different from AI Ethics (A.46)?

**AI Ethics A.46** (`/app/ai-ethics-responsible-use-engine`) governs how Aipify is deployed ethically. Phase 129 focuses on organizational reflection and values alignment across operations. Cross-link only — do not duplicate AI Ethics RPCs.

## Does Aipify judge our ethics?

No. Aipify offers reflective questions, perspective expansion, and voluntary decision ethics prompts. Humans retain moral agency. No shaming. No moral superiority.

## What is culture insight?

The Culture Insight Engine surfaces **aggregate themes** — trust signals, learning participation, governance engagement — not individual employee evaluation or surveillance.

## What permissions are required?

- `organizational_wisdom.view` — view Wisdom Center dashboards and scaffolds
- `organizational_wisdom.manage` — update settings and reflection metadata

## Success metrics

Ethical awareness · values alignment · reflection participation · transparency comfort · culture health themes · governance engagement · trust strengthening · institutional wisdom
