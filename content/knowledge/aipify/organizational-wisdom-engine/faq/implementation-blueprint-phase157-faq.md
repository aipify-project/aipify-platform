# Organizational Wisdom Council & Ethical Foresight — Phase 157 FAQ

## What is Phase 157?

Legacy & Future Stewardship Phase 157 deepens the **Organizational Wisdom & Ethical Intelligence Engine** at `/app/organizational-wisdom-engine` with Wisdom Council reviews, ethical foresight workshops, stakeholder awareness snapshots, and wisdom memory entries.

## Does Phase 157 create a new route?

No. Phase 157 extends the existing Phase 129 route. All Phase 129 `_owebp129_*` dashboard fields are preserved.

## Is Phase 157 an ethical authority?

**No.** Phase 157 explicitly does **not** determine right/wrong, impose ideology, override executive authority, replace governance, or suppress alternative viewpoints. Wisdom Companion expands perspective — humans retain moral agency.

## How does Phase 157 relate to Phase 129?

Phase 129 established the Wisdom Center with ethical reflection workspaces, values alignment, and culture insight. Phase 157 is the Legacy & Future Stewardship layer — documented in `_owcebp157_distinction_note()`. Phase 157 adds council reviews, foresight workshops, and wisdom memory depth.

## How does Phase 157 relate to Collective Decision Council Phase 137?

Phase 137 at `/app/collective-decision-council-engine` focuses on collective decision council workflows. Phase 157 cross-links decision council context — it does not duplicate `_cdcc_*` RPCs or create a duplicate council center.

## How does Phase 157 relate to Wisdom Engine A.93 and AI Ethics A.46?

Wisdom Engine A.93 at `/app/wisdom-engine` remains authoritative for experience-to-guidance synthesis. AI Ethics A.46 at `/app/ai-ethics-responsible-use-engine` remains authoritative for deploying and governing Aipify ethically. Phase 157 cross-links only — never duplicates `_wie_*` or `_aere_*` RPCs.

## What does the Wisdom Companion do in Phase 157?

The Wisdom Companion supports perspective expansion, reflection prompts, knowledge discovery, stakeholder mapping, scenario exploration, and executive preparation. It does **not** define morality, override leadership, or replace governance.

## What data does Phase 157 store?

Metadata summaries and aggregate counts only. Council review scaffolds, foresight workshop themes, wisdom memory summaries (max 500 chars), and stakeholder awareness snapshots. No raw chat, email, PII, or individual surveillance in RPC payloads.

## What are the ethical foresight reflection questions?

- Who benefits?
- What unintended harm might emerge?
- What assumptions shape our thinking?
- What are the responsibilities of this opportunity?
- How might future generations view this?

## What are the stakeholder awareness groups?

Employees · customers · Growth Partners · communities · knowledge contributors · future leaders · society — awareness themes only, not surveillance.

## Which engines does Phase 157 cross-link?

Phases 151–156, Phase 129, Phase 137, Wisdom A.93, AI Ethics A.46, Board Governance Phase 123, Strategic Foresight Phase 122, Self Love A.76, and Inclusion A.83.

## What thin RPCs are available?

- `record_wisdom_council_review(...)` — record wisdom council review metadata
- `record_ethical_foresight_session(...)` — record ethical foresight workshop metadata
- `record_wisdom_memory_entry(...)` — record wisdom memory entry metadata
