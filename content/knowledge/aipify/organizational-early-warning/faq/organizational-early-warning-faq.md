# Organizational Early Warning & Predictive Signals — FAQ

## What are predictive signals?

Predictive signals are patterns Aipify detects from operational data — delays, blockers, approval backlogs, and execution trends — surfaced before they become major problems.

## Can Aipify predict the future?

**No.** Aipify estimates trends and health forecasts from available patterns. All outputs are guidance — not guarantees.

## How are warnings generated?

From Action Center execution data, Strategic Initiative Portfolio health, approval backlogs, blockers, and organizational indicators integrated across executive systems.

## How should leaders respond to warnings?

Review signal briefings, acknowledge or escalate, assign owners, and document decisions in the immutable audit trail. Humans interpret and act.

## How are confidence levels determined?

From signal severity, data availability, and historical pattern strength — always as **estimates**.

---

**Principle:** Aipify identifies patterns and signals. Humans remain responsible for interpretation and action.
