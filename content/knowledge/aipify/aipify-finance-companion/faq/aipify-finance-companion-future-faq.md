# Aipify Finance Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Finance Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports financial awareness, planning reminders, and coordination for individuals, families, and organizations.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Financial Guardrails, Memory, Presence, Governance, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Help people become more aware, organized, and intentional with their finances.

## Is this financial advice?

**No.** Aipify must never provide regulated financial advice, guarantee investment outcomes, or replace licensed financial advisors or accountants.

## How is this different from Financial Guardrails (Phase 296)?

**Financial Guardrails** enforces spending limits, approval thresholds, and budget boundaries at `/app/governance/financial-guardrails`. **Finance Companion** focuses on **awareness, planning, reminders, and organization** at `/app/companion/finance`. They cross-link but serve distinct purposes.

## Can Aipify connect to accounting systems?

When implemented, integrations (e.g. Fiken) provide **read-only visibility** — Aipify does not replace accounting functions.

## Can insights create fear about money?

No. Insights should increase awareness — never fear.

## What data does Finance Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **financial metadata, goals, and summary aggregates only** — not bank credentials or raw transaction PII without governed consent. Complete deletion is supported.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include scheduling reminders, printing summaries, and preparing review checklists — all requiring appropriate approvals via Trust & Action and financial guardrails where spend is involved.
