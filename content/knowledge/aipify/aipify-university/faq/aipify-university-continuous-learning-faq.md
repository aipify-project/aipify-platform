# Aipify University & Continuous Learning — FAQ

## What is Aipify University?

Aipify University is the centralized continuous learning environment at `/app/aipify-university` (Phase 115). It provides courses, micro-learning, role-specific pathways, Companion-assisted coaching, and wellbeing-aware education woven into everyday work.

## How is this different from the Learning Engine?

The Learning Engine at `/app/learning` (Phase 29/65) stores operational `customer_learning_memory` so Aipify learns WITH the customer. Aipify University educates **people** on how to work with Aipify — it does not store AI operational learning memory.

## How is this different from Learning & Training (A.36)?

Learning & Training Engine at `/app/learning-training-engine` provides formal talent development paths, assessments, and certification workflows (Blueprint 31/92). Aipify University is the **unified learning hub** with continuous micro-learning, executive programs, and cross-links — it does not duplicate A.36 RPCs.

## What learning experiences are available?

Courses, micro-learning sessions, interactive simulations, role-specific pathways, knowledge refreshers, executive programs, certification tracks, and Companion-assisted coaching.

## What are the nine learning pathways?

Executive Leadership, Support Excellence, Companion Adoption, Security Awareness, Governance Excellence, Commerce Excellence, Growth Partner, Department-Specific, and New Employee Onboarding.

## How does micro-learning work?

3-minute refreshers, weekly insights, scenario exercises, policy reminders, Companion guidance moments, and knowledge reinforcement — learning during work, not only outside it. Delivery is logged as metadata only.

## What does Companion coaching do?

Answer questions, offer encouragement, suggest resources, identify gaps gently, recommend next steps, and celebrate progress — support growth, never shame. Aipify Companion coaches, not generic AI tutors.

## How does onboarding acceleration help?

Welcome experiences, role introductions, Companion familiarization, Knowledge Center guidance, security training, departmental paths, milestone tracking, manager collaboration, and cultural orientation — at a healthy pace.

## What is the knowledge retention engine?

Knowledge capture, best practice libraries, lessons learned, expert contributions, searchable knowledge maps, context preservation, and Companion-assisted discovery — cross-links to Organizational Memory A.34 and Employee Knowledge Engine.

## What executive topics are covered?

Companion Strategy, Governance Principles, Change Leadership, Responsible Innovation, Executive Decision Support, Operational Intelligence, Organizational Design, and Human-Centered Transformation.

## What analytics are tracked?

Participation, completion, knowledge confidence, learning frequency, role preparedness, Companion utilization, knowledge retention, training effectiveness, and leadership engagement — **aggregates only**, no hidden employee scoring.

## What internal certifications are supported?

Aipify Foundations, Companion Practitioner, Support Excellence Specialist, Governance Steward, Commerce Excellence Specialist, Knowledge Leader, Executive Transformation Leader, and Growth Partner Professional — cross-link Certification & Achievement A.37.

## How does Self Love integrate?

Healthy pacing, reflection, progress recognition, curiosity, and confidence building. Mistakes are opportunities, not shame. Cross-link Self Love A.76 at `/app/self-love-engine`.

## What is wellbeing-aware learning?

Monitors overload signals (excessive mandatory training, repeated failures, burnout signs, low engagement). Companion may suggest reduced intensity, alternative formats, manager check-ins, encouragement, and support resources — **supportive, not punitive**.

## What Knowledge Center outputs are integrated?

FAQs, implementation guides, reference materials, scenario libraries, quick starts, best practices, and Companion learning cards — via Knowledge Center A.5.

## What security training programs exist?

Password hygiene, 2FA awareness, recovery code protection, phishing detection, data classification, responsible Companion usage, governance expectations, and incident reporting — cross-link 2FA at `/app/settings/two-factor`.

## Where does business logic live?

Supabase RPCs (`_auni_*` engine helpers, `_aubp115_*` blueprint helpers). API routes and dashboard panels are thin clients.

## What data is stored?

Pathway metadata, enrollment progress percentages, micro-learning delivery logs, certification progress metadata, aggregate analytics snapshots, and audit events — **no raw chat, assessment answers, or PII**.

## What are success metrics?

Faster onboarding, improved retention, greater Companion adoption, higher confidence, stronger leadership, reduced fatigue, improved security awareness, healthier cultures, and long-term success.
