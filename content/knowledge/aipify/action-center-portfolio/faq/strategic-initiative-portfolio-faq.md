# Strategic Initiative Portfolio — Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is a strategic initiative?

A strategic initiative is an approved action or program that Aipify tracks against business goals, ownership, execution progress, dependencies, and portfolio health.

## How does Aipify prioritize initiatives?

Aipify considers risk level, alignment score, strategic value, deadlines, and executive priority indicators. **Executives retain final authority** over what gets prioritized and executed.

## What is strategic alignment?

Strategic alignment shows how an initiative connects to business goals, departments, executive sponsors, expected strategic value, and confidence estimates.

## What does portfolio health mean?

Portfolio health summarizes whether initiatives are **On Track**, **At Risk**, **Blocked**, **Overdue**, or **Completed** — giving leadership a single view of execution against strategy.

## Who owns an initiative?

Each initiative has an assigned owner and may include an executive sponsor. Humans retain accountability; Aipify coordinates visibility and decision support.

## How does Aipify estimate initiative risk?

Risk analysis reflects high-risk concentration, unresolved blockers, missing owners, unclear outcomes, and low-confidence initiatives — always as **guidance**, not certainty.

## Can Aipify decide which initiatives to execute?

**No.** Aipify supports strategic decisions with visibility, alignment context, and recommendations. Humans retain responsibility and authority.
