# Audit Log & Accountability — FAQ

## What is an audit log?

An audit log is a historical record of important actions performed within Aipify.

## Why are audit logs important?

They provide transparency, accountability, and security across business operations.

## Can audit entries be modified?

No. Audit entries should remain immutable to preserve trust.

## Are AI actions audited?

Yes. Significant AI recommendations and executions are recorded.

## Who can access audit logs?

Access depends on organizational permissions and role assignments.

## Can organizations export audit data?

Yes. Authorized users can export logs for compliance and reporting purposes.

## Are login attempts tracked?

Yes. Authentication-related events should always be audited.

## Can audit logs help investigate issues?

Yes. Audit trails help organizations understand what happened, who initiated actions, and when events occurred.
