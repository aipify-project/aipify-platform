# Enterprise Value Realization, ROI & Business Impact Engine — FAQ

## How is ROI calculated?

ROI is calculated from subscription cost, implementation cost, operational savings, revenue impact, efficiency gains, and risk reduction. The ROI Formula Engine supports monthly, quarterly, annual, Business Pack, department, and organization-level calculations. Net ROI reflects total value generated minus total investment. All calculations are tenant-isolated and logged in the value audit trail.

## How is value measured?

Value is measured across financial, operational, workforce, customer, automation, and strategic dimensions. The Value Overview aggregates estimated value generated, hours saved, costs reduced, revenue influenced, workflows automated, customer impact, and a Value Health Score. If value cannot be measured, value cannot be proven — every metric connects to attributable Aipify activity.

## How are savings calculated?

Time Savings track hours saved, tasks automated, and manual work reduced across support, administrative, and meeting categories. Cost Savings track operational, support, workforce, vendor, technology, and process savings. Savings are recorded through governed actions and attributed to automations, workflows, Business Packs, and Digital Employee contributions.

## How is revenue impact measured?

Revenue Impact tracks revenue influenced, revenue generated, upsell impact, retention impact, lead conversion impact, and partner revenue impact. Metrics connect to customer lifecycle activity and automation outcomes without storing private customer business records — metadata and attributed outcomes only.

## How does Aipify track business impact?

The Value Attribution Engine connects actions, automations, workflows, Companion activity, Digital Employees, Business Packs, and Industry Packs to measurable outcomes. Workforce Impact covers productivity, capacity, efficiency, training, and knowledge growth. Strategic Impact covers risk reduction, decision quality, operational maturity, governance improvements, and strategic progress.

## How are executive reports generated?

Customer Success Reports include monthly value reports, quarterly value reports, executive ROI reports, Business Pack impact reports, automation impact reports, and strategic value reports. Reports are generated through governed actions with full audit logging. The Executive Value Dashboard summarizes ROI, business impact, revenue impact, cost savings, workforce impact, strategic impact, and long-term value.

## How is this different from other value routes?

**Value Realization Center** (`/app/value`) is the Phase 430 enterprise ROI and business impact engine. **Value Engine** (`/app/value-engine`) is the legacy value tracking module. **Value Realization Engine** (`/app/value-realization-engine`) focuses on outcome milestones and executive value reports (Phase A.48). Each module cross-links without duplicating tables.
