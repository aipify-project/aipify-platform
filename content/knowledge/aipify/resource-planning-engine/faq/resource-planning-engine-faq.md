# Resource Planning Engine — FAQ

## What is the Resource Planning Engine?

It helps organizations allocate and optimize resources required to achieve business objectives — personnel, time, budget, expertise, partners, and technology — with metadata-only tracking and human-approved plans.

## Can Aipify identify resource conflicts?

Yes. Aipify can highlight competing demands and recommend adjustments when allocations exceed planned capacity or when multiple priorities compete for the same resource types.

## Can planning scenarios be compared?

Yes. Organizations can evaluate alternative approaches before making decisions. Scenarios store allocation snapshots alongside the baseline plan for side-by-side review.

## Are resource planning activities audited?

Yes. Significant planning activities — plan creation, allocation changes, utilization overrides, approvals, and exports — are recorded for accountability.
