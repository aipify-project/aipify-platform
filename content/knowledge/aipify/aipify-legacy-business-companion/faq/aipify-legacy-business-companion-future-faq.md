# Aipify Legacy Business Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Legacy Business Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that helps entrepreneurs, founders, executives, and family businesses preserve institutional knowledge, document strategic wisdom, and prepare future generations of leaders.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Executive Companion, Legacy Companion, Governance, Memory, Presence, and Knowledge foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Businesses are built over decades. The experience, values, and lessons learned should not disappear when leaders retire, transition, or pass away.

## Can Aipify make succession decisions?

**No.** Aipify supports leadership transitions — people make leadership decisions. Aipify must never override succession decisions or influence outcomes.

## How is this different from Legacy Companion?

**Legacy Companion** (future) focuses on **personal legacy** and long-term purpose. **Legacy Business Companion** focuses on **organizational wisdom**, succession planning, and institutional knowledge for businesses and family enterprises.

## How is this different from Employee Knowledge Engine?

**Employee Knowledge Engine** manages approved operational knowledge for day-to-day work. **Legacy Business Companion** preserves **leadership wisdom, strategic context, and succession preparation** that spans generations. Cross-link only.

## Can Aipify speak on behalf of former leaders?

**No.** Aipify must never speak on behalf of former leaders without authorization or alter preserved information without transparency.

## What is the Knowledge Preservation Principle?

The goal is not to preserve everything — it is to preserve what future generations genuinely need.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include organizing leadership reflections, preparing transition documentation, and exporting approved knowledge archives — all requiring appropriate approvals.

## Who is Legacy Business Companion for?

Founders, executives, and family businesses preparing intentional leadership transitions and organizational continuity.
