# Human Flourishing Engine — FAQ (Phase 170)

## What is the Human Flourishing Center?

The Human Flourishing Center at `/app/human-flourishing-engine` is the **Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170) capstone**. It supports civilizational flourishing and human potential through development programs, belonging, lifelong learning, and strength-based growth — **not** human worth metrics, productivity-based worth scoring, or employee surveillance.

## How is Phase 170 different from Living Enterprise (Phase 160)?

Living Enterprise (`/app/living-enterprise-engine`) is the **Legacy & Future Stewardship Era (151–160) capstone** — organizational transcendence and institutional becoming via `_lete_*` RPCs. Phase 170 is a **different era capstone** focused on human flourishing and human potential — cross-link only, **do not duplicate** those helpers.

## How is Phase 170 different from Global Stewardship (Phase 150)?

Global Stewardship (`/app/global-stewardship-collective-future-engine`) closed the **Global Intelligence Era (141–150)** — a different era. Phase 170 closes the Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170).

## Does the Flourishing Companion define human worth?

**No.** The Flourishing Companion provides reflection prompts, learning recommendations, leadership briefings, growth opportunity insights, belonging summaries, and development resources. It **does not** determine human value, assign worth by productivity, replace human relationships, suppress individuality, or override leadership judgment.

## What may be stored?

Approved **metadata summaries** only: executive flourishing review summaries (max 500 characters), belonging reflection aggregates, strengths development notes, lifelong learning participation records — **not** human worth scores, productivity rankings, or surveillance data.

## What must never be stored?

Human worth metrics, productivity-based worth scoring, employee surveillance for flourishing metrics, individual ranking, or any data used to assign human value without human leadership.

**Yes.** Growth Partner — never Affiliate — throughout Phase 170 copy and ILM vocabulary.

## What is the human flourishing score?

An **organizational engagement indicator** based on readiness level, enabled development settings, executive reviews, belonging reflections, strengths notes, and learning participation — **not** a human worth metric or productivity ranking.
