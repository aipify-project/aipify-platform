# Civilizational Stewardship & Shared Prosperity Engine — Phase 167 FAQ

## What is the Shared Prosperity Center?

The Shared Prosperity Center is Aipify's framework for ecosystem stewardship and regenerative prosperity — helping organizations reflect on who benefits from growth, what opportunities they create, and how they strengthen healthy ecosystems across the Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170). It aggregates visibility without duplicating phase-specific RPCs. Stewardship through generosity — companions support awareness; leaders retain accountability for resource allocation.

## Does Aipify determine resource allocation?

No. The Stewardship Companion provides reflection prompts, knowledge recommendations, leadership briefings, and shared prosperity summaries. It does **not** determine resource allocation, override executive decisions, impose ideology, replace governance, or define organizational success. Humans decide allocation; Aipify prepares awareness.

## Is this obligation or guilt-based prosperity?

No. Shared Prosperity is **not** obligation, guilt, or imposed ideology. It supports reflective stewardship — gratitude, generosity, compassion, humility, and service — without pressuring organizations or employees.

## Is this employee surveillance?

No. Shared Prosperity stores metadata-only stewardship review summaries, opportunity initiative scaffolds, and prosperity memory entries. Employee growth themes are **aggregate only** — no employee surveillance for prosperity metrics.

## How does this relate to Phase 150 and Phase 149?

Phase 150 (Global Stewardship Engine) is the **Global Intelligence Era (141–150) capstone** at `/app/global-stewardship-collective-future-engine`. Phase 149 (Social Impact & Purpose) at `/app/social-impact-purpose-engine` covers social impact initiatives. Phase 167 is the **Post-Enterprise Era shared prosperity layer** at `/app/shared-prosperity-engine`. Different surfaces — cross-link only.

## Why Self Love in shared prosperity?

Self Love (A.76) connects gratitude, generosity, compassion, recognition, humility, and service to stewardship reflection. Responsible prosperity requires inner steadiness — not surveillance or judgment of people. Cross-link `/app/self-love-engine`; do not duplicate Self Love RPCs.
