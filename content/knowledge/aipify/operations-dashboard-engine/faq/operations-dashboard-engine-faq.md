# Operations Dashboard Engine — FAQ

## What is the Operations Dashboard?

A unified view of support, approvals, integrations, knowledge, notifications, and organization health.

## Who can see which widgets?

Widget visibility is role-based. Owners and administrators see all operational widgets; managers, support agents, and viewers see focused subsets.

## Can I customize my dashboard layout?

Yes. Users with `dashboard.configure` permission can enable widgets and set display order via preferences.

## How is organization health calculated?

Health score factors in failed integrations, pending approvals, critical alerts, and open support cases.

## What are operations alerts?

Alerts surface integration failures, approval backlogs, support escalations, and health degradation. Critical alerts require acknowledgment.

## Is dashboard data audited?

Yes. Preference changes, alert dismissals, and critical acknowledgments are recorded in audit logs.
