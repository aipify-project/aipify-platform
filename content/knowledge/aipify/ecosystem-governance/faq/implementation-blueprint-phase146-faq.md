# Global Ecosystem Certification & Professional Standards — FAQ (Phase 146)

## What is Phase 146?

Phase 146 extends the **Ecosystem Governance & Certification Engine (Phase 119)** at `/app/ecosystem-governance` with **Global Intelligence Era (141–150) professional standards depth** — certification pathways, professional directory, recertification reviews, and continuous learning cross-links.

## Does Phase 146 create a new certification center?

**No.** Phase 146 extends the existing Ecosystem Governance Center at `/app/ecosystem-governance`. It does **not** duplicate Partner Certification RPCs from Phase 91 or create a separate certification surface.

## How is Phase 146 different from Partner Certification (Phase 91)?

Partner Certification at `/app/partners` remains **authoritative** for Growth Partner tiers via `_pce_tier_label()`. Phase 146 cross-links and adds ecosystem-wide professional standards orchestration — it does **not** duplicate `_pce_*` RPCs.

## How is Phase 146 different from Certification & Achievement (A.37)?

Certification & Achievement at `/app/certification-achievement-engine` handles **internal tenant team certifications**. Phase 146 covers **ecosystem-wide professional standards** for Growth Partners, governance professionals, and training providers.

## Does the Certification Companion guarantee competence?

**No.** The Certification Companion provides learning recommendations, preparation support, and progress tracking. It does **not** guarantee competence, manipulate assessments, lower standards, or assign personal worth.

## Does the professional directory use star ratings?

**No.** The professional directory shows certification status, expertise areas, regional presence, and contributions — **professionalism not popularity**. Star ratings, popularity rankings, and social scores are explicitly prohibited.

## What tables does Phase 146 add?

| Table | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| `ecosystem_professional_directory_entries` | Verified professionals — metadata only |
| `ecosystem_certification_reviews` | Annual reviews, refreshers, recertification cycles |

Phase 119 tables (`ecosystem_governance_certification_programs`, etc.) remain authoritative and are **not** duplicated.

Professional excellence cultivated through **support not fear**. Standards build confidence — not bureaucracy or gatekeeping. **Growth Partner** terminology throughout — never Affiliate.
