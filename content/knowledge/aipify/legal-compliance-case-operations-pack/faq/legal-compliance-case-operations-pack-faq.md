# Legal & Compliance Operations Pack — FAQ

## How does Legal Pack work?

The Legal Pack is the Industry Pack home for law firms, legal departments, compliance teams, and advisory firms at `/app/legal`. It coordinates cases, clients, contracts, compliance, document governance, deadlines, and risk with strict confidentiality and audit logging on ABOS. **Aipify supports legal professionals; Aipify does not provide legal advice.** Install via Industry Packs at `/app/industry-packs`.

## How are cases managed?

Cases are tracked in `legal_cases` with case number, type, client, assigned team, priority, status (open, in review, awaiting action, pending response, completed, closed, archived), deadlines, and risk level. Manage cases from the Legal Center at `/app/legal/cases`.

## How are contracts governed?

Contracts in `legal_contracts` track parties, effective/renewal/expiration dates, risk status, and approval workflow status (draft, under review, approved, active, renewal due, expired, archived). Document governance includes version control scaffolds, access controls, and audit trails via `legal_audit_logs`.

## How are deadlines tracked?

Deadlines in `legal_deadlines` track filing, review, renewal, compliance, contract expiration, and case milestone dates with upcoming, due soon, overdue, and completed status.

## How are compliance reviews managed?

Compliance reviews in `legal_compliance_reviews` track regulatory, policy, audit, and program reviews with findings counts and completion status. Completion updates pack compliance status and is audited.

## How is risk measured?

Risk is tracked at case and contract level (low, moderate, high, critical) with mitigation planning metadata. Risk assessment updates are logged in `legal_audit_logs`. Companion legal advisor signals highlight exposure requiring review.
