# Growth Partner Portal Foundation — FAQ (Phase 331)

## What is a Growth Partner?

A Growth Partner is an independent business operator who helps organizations discover, implement and grow with Aipify.

## Is this an affiliate program?

No. Growth Partners build real businesses and customer relationships around the Aipify ecosystem.

## Do I need a registered business?

Yes. Growth Partners must operate through a verified business entity.

## Where is the portal?

The Growth Partner Portal is at `/partner` — separate from the Customer App (`/app/*`) and Platform Admin (`/platform/*`).

## What is required before activation?

Company registration verification, organization number validation, VAT verification, identity verification, and banking verification.
