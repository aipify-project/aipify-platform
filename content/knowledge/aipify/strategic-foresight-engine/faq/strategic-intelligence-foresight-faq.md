# Strategic Intelligence & Foresight — FAQ

## What is Phase 122?

The Strategic Intelligence & Foresight Engine at `/app/strategic-foresight-engine` helps organizations anticipate change, monitor emerging trends, explore scenario scaffolds, and assess future readiness — **foresight for preparedness, not prediction for certainty**.

## How is this different from Predictive Insights?

Predictive Insights (A.66) at `/app/predictive-insights-engine` focuses on **forecasts**. Phase 122 focuses on **foresight** — expanding perspective, rehearsing adaptability, and preparing without claiming to know the future.

## How is this different from Strategic Intelligence Foundation?

Strategic Intelligence Foundation (A.31) at `/app/strategic-intelligence-foundation-engine` provides strategic awareness. Phase 122 is a distinct **Strategic Intelligence Center** for foresight, trends, scenarios, and preparedness. Cross-link only — never duplicate `_sif_*` RPCs.

## Where do scenarios execute?

Phase 122 stores **scenario scaffolds** only. Full scenario execution lives at the Simulation & Decision Lab (`/app/simulations`). Cross-link there for interactive simulation runs.

## Does Aipify predict the future?

No. The Executive Foresight Companion expands perspective and supports reflection. It does **not** make certainty claims, guarantee results, or replace leadership accountability.

## What data is stored?

Metadata only — trend signals, scenario summaries, and readiness reflections. No customer email, chat, or PII.
