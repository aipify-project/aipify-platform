# Civic Collaboration Engine — FAQ (Phase 161)

## What is the Public Value Center?

The Public Value Center at `/app/civic-collaboration-engine` is the **Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170) opener**. It supports civic collaboration and community contribution through responsible participation — **not** political agendas, ideological promotion, or popularity scoring.

## How is Phase 161 different from Social Impact & Purpose (Phase 118/149)?

Social Impact & Purpose (`/app/social-impact-purpose-engine`) provides **community impact initiatives and social responsibility** via `_gisrb149_*` and `_sipbp118_*` RPCs. Phase 161 is a **dedicated civic collaboration center** with partnership programs, public value initiatives, and trust reflection scaffolds — cross-link only, **do not duplicate** those helpers.

## How is Phase 161 different from Community (Phase 89)?

Community (`/app/community`) provides **community engagement and professional communities** via `_ccsbp117_*`. Phase 161 focuses on **public value contribution, education partnerships, and civic stewardship** — cross-link only.

## Does the Civic Companion promote political agendas?

**No.** The Civic Companion provides community opportunity discovery, knowledge recommendations, partnership preparation, and reflection prompts. It **does not** promote political agendas, impose ideology, determine societal priorities, replace civic leadership, or suppress diverse perspectives.

## What may be stored?

Approved **metadata summaries** only: partnership program summaries (max 500 characters), public value initiative scaffolds, trust reflection records — **not** popularity scores, political profiling, or surveillance data.

## What must never be stored?

Political profiling, ideological scoring, popularity rankings, PII surveillance, or any data used to promote political agendas without human civic leadership.

**Yes.** Growth Partner — never Affiliate — throughout Phase 161 copy and ILM vocabulary.

## What is the public value score?

An **organizational engagement indicator** based on enabled settings, active partnerships, public value initiatives, and trust reflections — **not** a popularity score or political ranking.
