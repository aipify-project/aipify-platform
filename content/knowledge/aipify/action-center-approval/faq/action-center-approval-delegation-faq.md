# Action Center Approvals & Delegation — Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is delegation?

Delegation assigns review or execution responsibility to another person or role while preserving a complete accountability trail. Aipify records who delegated, to whom, and when — without replacing human judgment.

## What is an approval workflow?

An approval workflow defines how an action moves from recommendation to execution: no approval, single approver, multi-step sequence, parallel approvers, or executive-level sign-off. Rules may depend on category, risk, financial impact, and department ownership.

## Can Aipify approve actions automatically?

Not by default. Aipify prepares and explains actions. Humans approve, reject, delegate, or escalate. Automatic approval requires explicit future governance authorization — never silent execution.

## How are approvers selected?

Aipify applies organization policies (category, risk, impact, department) and may optionally recommend delegates based on role permissions, historical ownership, workload, and expertise. Recommendations are optional — your organization decides.

## What happens when approvals are delayed?

SLA monitoring tracks time awaiting approval, highlights bottlenecks, and supports escalation. Overdue items are surfaced for review. Final decisions remain with designated approvers and executives.

**Principle:** Aipify supports decision processes. Organizations retain authority.
