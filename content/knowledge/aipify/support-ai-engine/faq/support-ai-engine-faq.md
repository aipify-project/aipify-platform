# Support AI Engine — FAQ

## What is Support AI?

Support AI is an assistant that helps organizations provide faster and more accurate customer support.

## Does Support AI replace human agents?

No. Support AI assists teams while humans remain responsible for sensitive decisions.

## Can Support AI answer customers automatically?

Only approved low-risk responses may be automated.

## How does Support AI find answers?

Support AI searches approved Knowledge Center content before generating suggestions.

## What happens if Support AI cannot help?

The case is escalated to a human team member for review.

## Are Support AI activities audited?

Yes. Important support actions are recorded in audit logs.

## Can organizations choose how much autonomy Support AI has?

Yes. Organizations can configure support response modes and approval requirements.

## Can Support AI improve over time?

Yes. Feedback and knowledge improvements help increase effectiveness.
