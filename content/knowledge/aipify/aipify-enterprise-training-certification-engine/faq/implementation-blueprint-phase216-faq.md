# Aipify Enterprise Training & Certification Engine — FAQ (Phase 216)

## What is Enterprise Training & Certification Engine?

Enterprise Training & Certification helps organizations continuously develop employee capabilities through structured learning programs, internal certifications, and role-specific competency development — at \`/app/aipify-enterprise-training-certification-engine\`.

## Does the Learning Companion certify employees automatically?

**No.** The Learning Companion suggests learning paths and competency scaffolds — it does NOT auto-certify without approval or expose protected certification data.

## What does the Learning Center show?

Learning progress, certification center scaffolds, role-based training programs, competency tracking, leadership development hubs, and executive readiness dashboards — metadata only.

## How does this relate to Unified Workspace and Onboarding Center?

Cross-link only: Phase 202 unified workspace (\`/app/aipify-unified-workspace-engine\`), Phase 215 onboarding center (\`/app/aipify-onboarding-adoption-acceleration-engine\`). Never duplicate their RPCs.

## Why human approval for certification?

Humans retain certification authority. Aipify advises and prepares — it does not auto-certify or bypass approval for protected certification data.
