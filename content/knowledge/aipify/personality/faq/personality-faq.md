Yes. Organizations may choose different personality profiles at /app/personality — Professional, Warm Professional (recommended default), or Playful. Humor and emojis can also be toggled independently.

category: personality
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [personality, crisis, faq]

No. Humor is automatically reduced or disabled during high-risk scenarios including security incidents, Crisis/Incident Mode, compliance investigations, legal matters, serious HR situations, and emotional distress.
