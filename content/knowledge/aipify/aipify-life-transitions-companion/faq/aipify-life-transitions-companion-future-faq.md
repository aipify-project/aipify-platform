# Aipify Life Transitions Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Life Transitions Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports individuals and families during major life transitions — moving, career changes, relationship milestones, family expansion, retirement, and practical bereavement coordination.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Family Companion, Legacy Companion, Wellness Companion, Memory, Presence, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Life transitions are often exciting, overwhelming, and stressful at the same time. Aipify should help people navigate change with greater confidence, structure, and support.

## Can Aipify make major life decisions for me?

**No.** Aipify must never replace professional guidance, make major life decisions independently, override user autonomy, or create dependency.

## How is this different from Life Events (Phase 290)?

**Life Events (Phase 290)** provides proactive life event care within the companion ecosystem. **Life Transitions Companion** focuses on **coordinating practical preparation** across major change categories — checklists, deadlines, documentation, and support networks. Cross-link only; distinct scope.

## Does Aipify provide grief counseling?

**No.** For loss and bereavement, Aipify provides **practical support only** — administrative reminders, document organization, and family coordination. Grief support remains human-centered.

## What is the Support Network Engine?

A planned feature that helps users remember they do not need to navigate transitions alone — identifying trusted contacts and encouraging communication planning. Aipify supports relationships; people provide relationships.

## Can insights diminish the significance of my life changes?

No. Insights should encourage preparedness — never diminish the significance of life changes.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include generating preparation checklists, coordinating reminders, and organizing documentation — all requiring user approval.

## Who is Life Transitions Companion for?

Anyone facing a major life change who wants practical organization and support without losing autonomy or dignity.
