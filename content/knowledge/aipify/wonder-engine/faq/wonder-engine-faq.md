# Wonder Engine — FAQ

## What is the Wonder Engine?

The Wonder Engine (Phase A.88) preserves amazement and healthy possibility. Progress can be inspiring — not only practical. It offers authentic reflection prompts, wonder moments, and emotional appreciation of meaningful work.

## How is this different from Impact Engine (A.85)?

**Impact Engine A.85** measures what changed — outcome signals across five dimensions. **Wonder Engine A.88** reconnects you to why those changes matter — emotional appreciation, not measurement.

## How is this different from Legacy Engine (A.86)?

**Legacy Engine A.86** preserves stories and milestones for the long term. **Wonder Engine A.88** offers emotional reconnection in the present — authentic amazement, not archival narrative.

## How is this different from Curiosity & Discovery (A.87)?

**Curiosity & Discovery A.87** prompts exploration and learning forward. **Wonder Engine A.88** invites reflection backward and inward — appreciating how far you have come.

## How is this different from Humor and Playful bell moments?

**Humor/Playful bell** (`/app/personality`) provides light humor signature moments. **Wonder Engine** offers deeper, authentic amazement — never manufactured enthusiasm or empty motivation.

## How is this different from Growth & Evolution (A.81)?

**Growth & Evolution A.81** orchestrates growth recommendations and evolution paths. **Wonder Engine A.88** pauses to appreciate progress already made — wonder should inspire, not slow work.

## What are moments of wonder?

Five types: **Milestone**, **Challenge overcome**, **Customer impact**, **Team extraordinary**, and **Vision becoming reality**. Metadata-only records with title, summary, and significance note.

## What are reflection prompts?

Gentle prompts such as "Five years ago, would you have believed you would be here today?" and "Which small decisions made the biggest difference?" Users acknowledge or dismiss pending reflections.

## What is Self Love connection?

Self Love celebrates progress, recognizes effort, expresses gratitude, and protects the joy of building. Success should not become invisible through familiarity.

## What are authenticity guardrails?

Wonder must never feel artificial. Boundaries avoid excessive praise, empty motivation, manufactured emotion, constant celebration, and praise that obscures real challenges. Enable via `authenticity_guardrails` in settings.

## What is celebration cadence?

`low` or `normal` — controls how often wonder moments and reflection prompts surface. Low cadence respects focus; normal offers regular appreciation without intrusion.

## Who can acknowledge reflections?

`wonder_engine.reflections.acknowledge` is required. Owners, administrators, managers, and employees have this by default.

## What integrations does Wonder Engine use?

Impact Engine A.85, Legacy Engine A.86, Curiosity & Discovery A.87, Growth & Evolution A.81, Companion Identity, Personality, and Purpose & Values. See integration links on the dashboard.
