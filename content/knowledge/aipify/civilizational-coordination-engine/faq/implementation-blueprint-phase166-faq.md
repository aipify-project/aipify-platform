# Civilizational Coordination & Shared Action Engine — Phase 166 FAQ

## What is the Shared Action Center?

The Shared Action Center is Aipify's framework for voluntary cross-organization coordination — helping organizations align on shared action programs, opt-in partnerships, and milestone metadata across the Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170). It aggregates coordination visibility without duplicating phase-specific RPCs. Coordination strengthens autonomy — not centralized command.

## Does Aipify compel organizations to participate?

No. Participation is **transparent, optional, governed, and respectful**. Organizations opt in to partnership scaffolds. The Coordination Companion prepares briefings and alignment insights — it does **not** override autonomy, compel participation, suppress dissent, determine collective priorities, or replace human leadership.

## How does this differ from Joint Operations (Phase 143)?

Phase 143 at `/app/joint-operations-engine` provides **governed cross-org workspaces** via `_cojo_*` helpers. Phase 166 provides **voluntary shared action coordination scaffolds** via `_ccae_*` helpers. Cross-link only — never duplicate `_cojo_*` or `_cojobp143_*` RPCs.

## What metadata is stored?

Program summaries (max ~500 chars), opt-in partnership labels, milestone tracking scaffolds, and audit logs. No raw operational content, coercion tracking, or dissent suppression records.

## Why Self Love in coordination?

Self Love (A.76) connects empathy, patience, generosity, humility, recognition of effort, and compassion during complexity. Voluntary coordination across organizations requires inner steadiness — not pressure or judgment. Cross-link `/app/self-love-engine`; do not duplicate Self Love RPCs.

## What routes does Phase 166 cross-link?

**Era phases 161–165:** Civic Collaboration, Cross-Sector Intelligence, Civilizational Memory, Civilizational Learning, Civilizational Foresight.

**Extended cross-links:** Joint Operations 143, Global Governance Diplomacy 144, Ecosystem Orchestration 120, Self Love A.76.

## Is this centralized command?

No. Phase 166 explicitly preserves organizational autonomy. Shared action frameworks document roles, governance, and review cadences as **metadata scaffolds** — humans and leaders decide priorities and participation.
