# Aipify Travel Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Travel Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports personal and business travel — planning, preparation, itineraries, reminders, and logistics coordination.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Action Marketplace, Financial Guardrails, Memory, Presence, and Governance foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Travel should feel exciting and productive — not stressful and overwhelming.

## Can Aipify book travel for me?

Only with **explicit approval**. Aipify must never make unauthorized bookings. All reservations go through Trust & Action, approval profiles, and financial guardrails where applicable.

## How does this relate to Action Marketplace and Financial Guardrails?

**Action Marketplace (Phase 293)** provides approved booking actions. **Financial Guardrails (Phase 296)** enforces travel spend thresholds. Travel Companion coordinates planning and preparation — it does not bypass these controls.

## Can insights create travel anxiety?

No. Insights should encourage preparedness — never anxiety.

## What about travel safety?

Aipify supports **preparedness** (emergency contacts, document backups, family notifications). **Safety decisions remain human-led.**

## What data does Travel Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **travel metadata, itinerary summaries, and checklist state only** — not payment credentials or passport images without governed consent. Users and organizations control policies and deletion.
