# Aipify Constitution & Core Principles — FAQ

## What is the Aipify Constitution?

The Constitution is a collection of principles that guide how Aipify is developed, governed and improved over time.

## Why does Aipify need a Constitution?

Technology influences people and organizations. A Constitution provides a stable foundation for decision-making as the platform grows.

## Can the Constitution change?

Yes. However, changes should occur thoughtfully and preserve Aipify's fundamental values.

## Does the Constitution replace legal requirements?

No. The Constitution complements applicable laws, regulations and organizational responsibilities.

## Why is transparency emphasized?

Transparency strengthens trust and helps users understand how important recommendations are formed.

## Why does Aipify prioritize human oversight?

Organizations remain responsible for their decisions. Aipify is designed to augment human capabilities, not remove accountability.

## How do these principles affect customers?

The principles influence product design, governance practices and customer experiences.

## Do partners need to align with these principles?

Yes. Partners contributing to the ecosystem should demonstrate behaviors consistent with Aipify's values.

## Why is long-term thinking important?

Sustainable success depends on balancing innovation, trust and responsibility over time.

## Who benefits from the Constitution?

Customers, employees, partners and communities all benefit from clear principles and expectations.
