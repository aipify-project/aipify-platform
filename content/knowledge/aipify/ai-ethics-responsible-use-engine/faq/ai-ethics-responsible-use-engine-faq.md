# AI Ethics & Responsible Use Engine FAQ

## FAQ 1

**Question:** What is the AI Ethics & Responsible Use Engine?

**Answer:** It documents and governs how your organization uses AI — approved and restricted use cases, explainability requirements, prohibited examples, and scheduled ethics reviews. It extends Security & Trust (A.18), Compliance (A.29), Human Oversight (A.40), and Delegated Trust (A.41).

## FAQ 2

**Question:** How do I propose a new AI use case?

**Answer:** Users with `ethics.manage` can propose use cases with a name, category, and risk level. Proposed use cases require ethics review before approval. Critical risk use cases cannot be proposed for autonomous AI — they require explicit administrator involvement.

## FAQ 3

**Question:** What are prohibited use examples?

**Answer:** The ethics policy includes documented prohibited examples such as automated hiring without human review, covert employee sentiment profiling, automated legal/credit decisions without explainability, and impersonating humans in customer communications. These are visible in the dashboard for transparency.

## FAQ 4

**Question:** Can I override the ethics policy?

**Answer:** Yes, with `ethics.override` permission and a documented business justification. Overrides create time-limited policy exceptions that are fully audited. This integrates with Human Oversight Engine (A.40) for operational approval of high-risk actions.
