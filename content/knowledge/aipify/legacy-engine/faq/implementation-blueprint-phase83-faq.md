# Implementation Blueprint Phase 83 — Long-Term Stewardship Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 83 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 83 extends the Legacy Engine (Phase A.86) with long-term stewardship — responsible leadership, sustainable growth framing, legacy awareness, and companion guidance. It layers on top of A.86 (`_leg_*` helpers) at the same route `/app/legacy-engine`.

## How is this different from Legacy Engine A.86?

**A.86** (`_leg_*`) focuses on storytelling, milestone recognition, and wisdom preservation. **Phase 83** (`_ltbp_*`) adds stewardship mindset — five-year thinking, sustainable growth evaluation, values-driven framing, and intergenerational perspective. Both share the same route and legacy stories/milestones tables.

## How is this different from Inclusion & Humanity A.83?

**Inclusion & Humanity Engine (A.83)** at `/app/inclusion-humanity-engine` addresses humanity and inclusion in organizational culture. **ABOS Blueprint Phase 83** shares the phase number but maps to Long-Term Stewardship at `/app/legacy-engine` — phase number collision only.

## How is this different from /app/settings/personalization (Repo Phase 83)?

**Personalization & Workstyle (Repo Phase 83)** at `/app/settings/personalization` is personal workstyle preferences. **Long-Term Stewardship Blueprint Phase 83** is organizational leadership stewardship — distinct scope.

## How is this different from Purpose & Values (Blueprint Phase 64)?

**Purpose & Values (A.82 / Phase 64)** at `/app/purpose-values-engine` defines organizational values. **Long-Term Stewardship Phase 83** cross-links for values-driven decision-making — stewardship applies values to long-term choices.

## What are the stewardship questions with 🦉🌹❤️🔔?

🦉 Five-year decision influence. 🌹 Strengths to preserve as the organization grows. ❤️ Responsibilities that accompany opportunities. 🔔 Legacy built through current actions. All encourage reflection — responsibility not pressure.

## What is sustainable growth framing?

Healthy growth evaluation, team support during scaling, system resilience, and values visibility — growth should strengthen the organization, not weaken culture or care for people. Cross-link Growth & Evolution A.81.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self Love (A.76) supports sustainable expectations and perspective. *"Building something meaningful often requires patience."* Principle only — Long-Term Stewardship stores metadata, not wellbeing content.

## What are the Phase 83 success criteria?

Computed live on the dashboard via `_ltbp_success_criteria()` — long-term thinking, sustainable leadership, visible values, stewardship discussions, legacy awareness, leadership insights, trust connection, and cross-links.

## Where does dogfooding happen?

**Aipify Group** validates internally — ecosystem development, companion philosophy, leadership evolution, organizational sustainability. **Unonight** is the first external pilot.
