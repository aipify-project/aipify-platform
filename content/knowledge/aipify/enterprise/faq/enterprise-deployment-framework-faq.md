# Enterprise Deployment Framework — FAQ (Phase 92)

## What is the Enterprise Deployment Framework?

It is Aipify's structured approach to implementing the platform within larger organizations while addressing governance, security, and operational needs.

## Can Aipify be deployed on-premise?

Yes. Aipify is designed to support on-premise, private cloud, and hybrid deployment scenarios.

## Do enterprises need a pilot program?

A pilot deployment is strongly recommended. Pilots help validate assumptions, gather feedback, and reduce implementation risk.

## Can Aipify integrate with existing systems?

Yes. Aipify supports integration with a wide range of business applications and enterprise platforms.

## How does Aipify handle access management?

Aipify supports enterprise authentication standards such as SSO, SAML, and role-based permissions.

## Who owns the organization's data?

The customer retains ownership of their data. Aipify provides governance capabilities to support organizational requirements.

## Can enterprises define their own governance policies?

Yes. Organizations may establish approval workflows, retention standards, and operational policies.

## What support options are available?

Support offerings range from standard support to dedicated success and advisory services.

## How long does deployment take?

Implementation timelines vary depending on scope, integrations, and organizational complexity.

## Why is change management important?

Technology alone does not guarantee success. Adoption, communication, and training are critical for realizing business value.
