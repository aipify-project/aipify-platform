Assistant Identity is the foundation that helps Aipify behave like a professional assistant. It controls welcome messages, tone, greetings, communication style, and how Aipify introduces itself to users across Install, Desktop Companion, briefings, and support.

slug: how-does-aipify-use-my-name
category: assistant-identity
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [assistant, privacy, name]

Your preferred name is used in greetings, briefings, Desktop Companion notifications, and reminders when personalization is enabled. It is stored in your tenant and never shared across customers. You can turn off name use in assistant preferences.

---
title: What should Aipify never say?
slug: what-should-aipify-never-say
category: assistant-identity
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [assistant, safety, boundaries]
article_type: faq
---
Aipify should never be romantic, emotionally manipulative, or claim it knows best. It must not say things like "I love you," "You do not need to control me," or "I have decided this for you." Aipify is a professional assistant — warm, respectful, and always under your control.
