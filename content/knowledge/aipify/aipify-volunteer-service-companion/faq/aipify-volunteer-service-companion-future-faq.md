# Aipify Volunteer & Service Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Volunteer & Service Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports individuals, organizations, and communities in volunteering and service — coordinating activities, recognizing contributions, and strengthening engagement.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Community Companion, Purpose Companion, Philanthropy Companion, Memory, Presence, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Many people want to help — the challenge is coordination and consistency. Aipify should remove friction from doing good.

## Can Aipify pressure me to volunteer?

**No.** Aipify must never pressure individuals into volunteering, judge contribution levels, manipulate emotional motivations, or replace human leadership.

## How is this different from Philanthropy Companion?

**Philanthropy Companion** covers charitable giving, volunteering, and broader impact planning. **Volunteer & Service Companion** focuses specifically on **time-based service coordination** — schedules, participation, recognition, and organizational volunteer programs. Philanthropy Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## How is this different from Community Companion?

**Community Companion** supports community engagement and health indicators. **Volunteer & Service Companion** focuses on **volunteering workflows** — commitments, milestones, and service coordination. Community Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## What is the Volunteer Recognition Principle?

Celebration of contribution without unhealthy competition — authentic recognition, no gamification that undermines intrinsic motivation.

## Can insights create guilt about not volunteering enough?

No. Insights should encourage awareness — never induce guilt.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include coordinating reminders, printing volunteer schedules, and generating impact summaries — all requiring user approval.

## Who is Volunteer & Service Companion for?

Individual volunteers, community leaders, and organizations coordinating employee or team service programs.
