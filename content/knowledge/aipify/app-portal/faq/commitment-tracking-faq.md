# What is the Commitment Center?

The Commitment Center helps organizations document promises, monitor fulfillment and improve accountability across teams and stakeholders.

# Why should commitments be tracked?

Tracking commitments reduces the likelihood that important obligations are forgotten and strengthens trust within organizations and external relationships.

# Can Aipify fulfill commitments automatically?

No. Aipify supports visibility, follow-up and accountability, while people remain responsible for fulfilling commitments.
