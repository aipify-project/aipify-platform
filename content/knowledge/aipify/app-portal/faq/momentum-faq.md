# What is the Momentum Engine?

The Momentum Engine helps organizations understand whether execution is accelerating, stable or slowing down across important initiatives.

# How is momentum calculated?

Momentum combines progress signals from goals, follow-ups, decisions, commitments and other operational indicators within Aipify.

# Can Aipify increase organizational momentum automatically?

No. Aipify provides insights and recommendations, while leaders and teams remain responsible for execution.
