# What is the Business Pack Success Center?

The Business Pack Success Center helps organizations maximize the value of their installed Business Packs through onboarding guidance, adoption insights and best-practice recommendations.

# Can Aipify improve Business Pack adoption automatically?

No. Aipify provides recommendations and insights while organizations remain responsible for implementation.

# Why are adoption insights important?

Organizations often realize greater value when they actively engage with and optimize the capabilities they have already invested in.
