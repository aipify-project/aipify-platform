# Cross-Functional Intelligence Center FAQ

## What is Cross-Functional Intelligence?

Cross-Functional Intelligence helps organizations understand relationships, dependencies and collaboration patterns between teams and departments. Found under **Intelligence → Cross-Functional Intelligence**.

## Why is this important?

Many business outcomes depend on effective collaboration across departments rather than the performance of a single team.

## Who should use this module?

Owners, executives and authorized leaders responsible for organizational effectiveness and performance. Employees do not have access unless explicitly granted.
