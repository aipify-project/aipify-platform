# What is Aipify Academy?

Aipify Academy helps organizations educate employees, improve adoption and maximize the value of Aipify.

# Can managers assign learning?

Yes. Managers can assign learning paths, monitor completion and support employee development.

# Is Aipify Academy the same as the Partner Academy?

No. Aipify Academy is designed for customers using Aipify internally. Growth Partner training is provided through the Partner Portal.
