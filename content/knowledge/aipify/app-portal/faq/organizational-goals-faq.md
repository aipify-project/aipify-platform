# Goals & Objectives — APP Portal Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is the Goals & Objectives Center?

The Goals & Objectives Center helps organizations define priorities, track progress and align teams around meaningful outcomes.

## Can goals be updated over time?

Yes. Goals evolve as organizations learn, adapt and achieve milestones. Progress updates help maintain alignment and accountability.

## Does Aipify automatically complete goals?

No. Aipify supports organizations with visibility, recommendations and progress tracking. Human teams remain responsible for execution.
