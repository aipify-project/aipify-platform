# Organizational Follow-Up — APP Portal Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is Organizational Follow-Up?

The Follow-Up Center helps organizations remember commitments, track unfinished actions, and ensure important responsibilities receive appropriate attention.

## Does Aipify automatically complete follow-ups?

**No.** Aipify provides reminders and recommendations, but humans remain responsible for reviewing and completing organizational commitments.

## Can follow-ups be assigned to specific team members?

Yes. Follow-ups can be assigned to individuals to improve accountability and visibility across the organization.
