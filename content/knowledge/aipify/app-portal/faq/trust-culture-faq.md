# What is the Trust & Culture Center?

The Trust & Culture Center helps organizations understand cultural health through anonymous aggregate insights and continuous improvement opportunities.

# Are culture responses anonymous?

Yes. Individual responses are never displayed. Results are only shown in aggregate form when anonymity thresholds are met.

# Can Aipify determine whether an organization has a good culture?

No. Aipify provides indicators and recommendations to support reflection and improvement, while organizations define their own cultural aspirations.
