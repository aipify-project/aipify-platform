# Executive Insights — APP Portal Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is Executive Insights?

Executive Insights provides leaders with a concise overview of organizational health, priorities, risks, opportunities, and recommended next steps generated from activity across the Aipify ecosystem.

## Does Executive Insights make decisions automatically?

**No.** Executive Insights supports decision-making by presenting information and recommendations. Final decisions remain under human control.

## How often are Executive Insights updated?

Insights are refreshed automatically as new events occur throughout your organization.
