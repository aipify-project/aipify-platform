# APP Portal — Knowledge Center FAQ

Phase 260 structural foundation. Customer-facing APP workspace at `/app/*`.

## What is Aipify?

Aipify is the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) — an operations platform that helps your organization work with subscriptions, Business Packs, team collaboration, and operational insights. APP is your customer-facing workspace.

## How do I invite team members?

Open **Organization → Team Members** to view your team. Invitation workflows will expand in a future phase. Owners and admins can manage team access.

## How do I change subscriptions?

Open **Billing → Subscription** to review your current plan. Plan changes are managed through your subscription settings and may require owner or admin approval.

## How do I upgrade my plan?

Open **Billing → Upgrade Options** to compare plans. Upgrading unlocks additional modules and capabilities included in higher tiers.

## What are Business Packs?

Business Packs are licensed modular capabilities your organization can install on top of your subscription. View installed and available packs under **Business Packs** in the APP navigation.

## Where can I view invoices?

Open **Billing → Invoices** to review and download invoices for your organization subscription.

## How do I contact support?

Open **Support → Contact Support** to reach Aipify support. Include your organization name and a clear summary of your question.

## How does role management work?

Organization roles map to workspace permissions: owner, admin, manager, and member. **Roles & Permissions** under Organization shows your access framework.

## How does the Since Last Login feature work?

Since Last Login summarizes important updates, completed actions, new notifications, recommended next steps, and Business Pack highlights since your last visit. Open **Home → Since Last Login** for the full summary.

## Why can't I access certain features?

Features are gated by your active subscription and licensed modules. Unavailable areas show upgrade opportunities — direct URLs cannot bypass license enforcement.

## How do upgrades affect permissions?

Upgrading your plan may unlock additional modules and role capabilities. Existing team permissions remain until an admin updates roles.

## How do I update notification preferences?

Open **Account → Notification Preferences** to control alerts. Operational notifications respect your organization subscription and role.
