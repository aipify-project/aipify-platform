# Organizational Forecasting Center FAQ

## What is Organizational Forecasting?

Organizational Forecasting helps leadership understand potential future developments based on current trends and observations. Found under **Intelligence → Organizational Forecasting**.

## Are forecasts guaranteed?

No. Forecasts are projections designed to support planning and preparedness, not guarantees of future outcomes. Aipify always presents three models — conservative, expected and optimistic.

## Who should use this module?

Owners, executives and authorized leaders responsible for planning and organizational growth. Employees do not have access unless explicitly granted.
