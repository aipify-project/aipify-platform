# Success Center — APP Portal Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is the Success Center?

The Success Center helps organizations understand adoption, engagement and opportunities to get more value from Aipify.

## How is the Health Score calculated?

The Health Score is based on organizational activity, engagement, adoption patterns and operational indicators available within Aipify.

## Can Aipify predict organizational risk?

Aipify provides recommendations and risk indicators based on available information, but final decisions should always involve human judgment.
