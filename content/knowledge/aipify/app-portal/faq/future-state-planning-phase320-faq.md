# Future-State Planning — FAQ

## What is Future-State Planning?

Future-State Planning helps organizations define long-term goals and create structured plans for achieving them.

## Does Aipify create our strategy?

No. Leadership remains responsible for strategy. Aipify provides structure, visibility and planning support.

## Who should use this module?

Owners, executives and authorized leaders responsible for organizational direction and long-term planning.
