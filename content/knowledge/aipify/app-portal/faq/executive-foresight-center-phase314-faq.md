# Executive Foresight Center FAQ

## What is Executive Foresight?

Executive Foresight helps leaders think proactively about opportunities, risks and future organizational needs. It lives under **Intelligence → Executive Foresight** in the APP portal.

## Can Aipify predict the future?

No. Aipify provides structured insights and prompts to support strategic planning and preparedness — never certainty about future outcomes.

## Who should use Executive Foresight?

Executive Foresight is designed for owners, executives and authorized leaders responsible for long-term decision-making. Employees do not have access unless explicitly granted as managers or administrators.

## Is this the Assistant?

No. The Assistant supports daily execution. Executive Foresight is a strategic intelligence capability in the Intelligence section.
