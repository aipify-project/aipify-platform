# Strategic Opportunity Intelligence Center FAQ

## What are Strategic Opportunities?

Strategic Opportunities are areas where organizations may improve performance, create new value or strengthen their long-term position. Found under **Intelligence → Strategic Opportunities**.

## Does Aipify automatically implement opportunities?

No. Aipify provides recommendations and structure. Leadership teams remain responsible for all decisions and actions.

## Who should use this module?

Owners, executives and authorized leaders responsible for strategic planning and organizational development. Employees do not have access unless explicitly granted.
