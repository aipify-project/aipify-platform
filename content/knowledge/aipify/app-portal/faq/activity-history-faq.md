# Activity History — APP Portal Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is Activity History?

Activity History gives your organization a chronological overview of important actions, updates, decisions, approvals and system events across Aipify.

## Who can see Activity History?

Access depends on role permissions. Owners and administrators can view broader activity, while team members only see activity they are authorized to access.

## Can activity records be deleted?

Important audit and activity records should remain available for accountability, compliance and organizational memory.
