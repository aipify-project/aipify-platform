# What is the Business Pack Command Center?

The Business Pack Command Center provides a unified overview of Business Pack performance, adoption, value realization and optimization opportunities.

# Does the Command Center replace individual Business Pack pages?

No. The Command Center summarizes the ecosystem while allowing users to drill down into individual Business Packs when needed.

# Can Aipify manage Business Packs automatically?

No. Organizations remain responsible for all operational decisions while Aipify provides insights and recommendations.
