# Support Request Center — APP Portal Knowledge Center FAQ

## How do I contact Aipify Support?

You can create a support request from the Support Request Center. Aipify organizes your request, category, priority and status so your organization can follow progress clearly.

## Can I track support requests?

Yes. Each support request includes status, priority, category, timestamps and related activity so your organization can follow progress.

## Who can see support requests?

Support request access depends on organization permissions. Owners and administrators can view organization support requests, while team members only see requests they are authorized to access.

Route: `/app/support/requests` · API: `/api/aipify/support-requests`
