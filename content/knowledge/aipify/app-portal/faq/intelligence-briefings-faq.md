# What is the Intelligence Briefing Center?

The Intelligence Briefing Center provides concise executive summaries highlighting organizational priorities, risks, opportunities and recommended focus areas.

# How are executive briefings created?

Briefings combine information from approved organizational data sources within Aipify to provide leadership visibility and decision support.

# Can Aipify make executive decisions?

No. Aipify supports leaders through insights and recommendations, while people remain responsible for making decisions.
