# Decision Center — APP Portal Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is the Decision Center?

The Decision Center helps organizations document important decisions, preserve context, and evaluate outcomes over time.

## Why should decisions be documented?

Documenting decisions reduces knowledge loss, improves accountability, and helps future leaders understand the reasoning behind previous actions.

## Can Aipify make decisions automatically?

**No.** Aipify supports human decision-making through documentation, recommendations, and organizational memory. Final authority always remains with people.
