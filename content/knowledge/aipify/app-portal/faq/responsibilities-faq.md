# Responsibilities — APP Portal Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is the Responsibilities Center?

The Responsibilities Center helps organizations clarify ownership across goals, decisions, follow-ups, approvals, integrations and other operational areas.

## Why should responsibilities have backup owners?

Backup owners reduce operational risk by ensuring important responsibilities are covered when the primary owner is unavailable.

## Can Aipify assign responsibilities automatically?

No. Aipify may suggest ownership based on patterns and roles, but human administrators remain responsible for assigning ownership.
