# Enterprise Intelligence Command Center FAQ

## What is the Intelligence Command Center?

The Intelligence Command Center provides a consolidated executive overview of all intelligence insights across the organization. Found under **Intelligence → Intelligence Command Center**.

## Does it replace other intelligence modules?

No. It aggregates information from intelligence modules (311–318) while preserving each module's detailed capabilities.

## Who should use this module?

Owners, executives and authorized leaders responsible for organizational planning and decision-making. Employees do not have access unless explicitly granted.
