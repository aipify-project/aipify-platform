# Enterprise Readiness Center FAQ

## What is Enterprise Readiness?

Enterprise Readiness helps organizations understand how prepared they are for growth, complexity and long-term operational success. Found under **Intelligence → Enterprise Readiness**.

## Does Enterprise Readiness certify my company?

No. It provides structured assessments and recommendations but does not serve as a formal certification. Readiness scores are guidance tools.

## Who should use this module?

Owners, executives and authorized leaders responsible for organizational development and growth planning. Employees do not have access unless explicitly granted.
