# Capability Center — APP Portal Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is the Capability Center?

The Capability Center helps organizations understand their operational maturity and identify opportunities for continuous improvement.

## How are maturity levels determined?

Maturity levels combine operational signals, feature adoption and optional self-assessments to provide improvement guidance.

## Is this a comparison against other organizations?

No. The Capability Center focuses on your organization's own growth and development over time.
