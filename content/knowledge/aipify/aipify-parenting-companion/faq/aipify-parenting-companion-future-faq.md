# Aipify Parenting Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Parenting Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports parents and caregivers with routines, milestones, school activities, and family coordination.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, **Family Companion**, Life Events, Memory, Presence, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Reduce mental overload so parents can focus on being present — parenting is one of life's most important responsibilities.

## Can Aipify make parenting decisions?

**No.** Aipify must never replace parental judgment, override family values, or act as a substitute parent.

## How is this different from Family Companion?

**Family Companion** handles general household and family event coordination. **Parenting Companion** focuses specifically on **parent/caregiver support** — school routines, child activities, milestones, and education coordination. Family Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## How is this different from Education Companion?

**Education Companion** supports **learner goals and study**. **Parenting Companion** supports **caregivers** coordinating school events, routines, and family logistics around children.

## Can insights create parenting guilt?

No. Insights should support preparedness — never guilt. Self-love prompts for parents are supportive, never judgmental.

## What data does Parenting Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **coordination metadata and schedule summaries only** — not children's private communications, grades, or health records without explicit parental consent.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include coordinating reminders, printing activity schedules, and generating event checklists — all requiring appropriate approvals.
