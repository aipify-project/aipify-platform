# Construction & Field Operations Pack — FAQ

## How does Construction Pack work?

The Construction Pack is the Industry Pack home for contractors, builders, and project organizations at `/app/construction`. It supports projects, sites, workforce, equipment, materials, safety, and field operations on the shared ABOS foundation. Install via Industry Packs at `/app/industry-packs`.

## How are projects managed?

Projects are tracked in `construction_projects` with types, budgets, timelines, status, and completion percentage. Manage projects from the Construction Center at `/app/construction/projects`.

## How are site operations tracked?

Sites are tracked in `construction_sites` with site managers, safety status, compliance, and daily activity summaries. Open Sites from `/app/construction/sites`.

## How is safety compliance managed?

Safety incidents and inspections are tracked in `construction_safety_incidents` with severity, investigation status, and corrective actions. Open Safety from `/app/construction/safety`.

## How are materials tracked?

Material records in `construction_material_records` track deliveries, consumption, costs, supplier status, and risk levels. Open Materials from `/app/construction/materials`.

## How is project profitability measured?

Project financials combine budget amounts, material costs, equipment utilization, and profitability labels in the overview and executive dashboard at `/app/construction/intelligence`.
