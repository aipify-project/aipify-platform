title: Does Aipify make strategic decisions?
slug: does-aipify-make-strategic-decisions
category: strategy
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [strategy, governance, faq]
article_type: faq

No. Aipify recommends; leaders decide. Strategic Intelligence may analyze, forecast, recommend, prioritize, and explain — but must never execute strategic decisions autonomously, override Governance, or initiate organizational change independently.

title: What is Opportunity Confidence?
slug: what-is-opportunity-confidence
category: strategy
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [strategy, confidence, faq]
article_type: faq
---
Opportunity Confidence reflects how strongly available evidence supports a recommendation. Levels: HIGH (strong evidence), MEDIUM (moderate evidence), LOW (exploratory recommendation). Confidence explains data quality, pattern strength, and supporting evidence.
