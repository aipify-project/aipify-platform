# Implementation Blueprint Phase 88 — Ecosystem Intelligence & External Relationship FAQ

## What is Phase 88 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 88 extends the Ecosystem Intelligence & External Relationship Engine (Repo Phase 88) with relationship stewardship scaffolding — awareness, partnership health, community connection, and limitation principles. It layers on top of `_eco_*` helpers at the same route `/app/ecosystem`.

## How is this different from Repo Phase 88?

**Repo Phase 88** (`_eco_*`) provides relationship maps, dependency analysis, Ecosystem Health Score, and executive briefings. **Blueprint Phase 88** (`_eierbp88_*`) adds stewardship framing — relationship insights, partnership health dimensions, community connection, companion guidance, and live success criteria. Both share the same route and ecosystem tables.

## How is this different from Wonder Engine (A.88)?

**Wonder Engine Phase A.88** at `/app/wonder-engine` supports personal wonder and curiosity. **ABOS Blueprint Phase 88** shares the phase number but maps to external ecosystem relationships at `/app/ecosystem` — engine phase number collision only.

## How is this different from Blueprint Phase 84 (/app/simulations)?

**Blueprint Phase 84 — Ecosystem Scenario Planning** at `/app/simulations` explores ecosystem preparedness through scenario modeling. **Blueprint Phase 88** maps and stewards declared relationships at `/app/ecosystem` — cross-link for preparedness context, not duplicate.

## How is this different from RSI (/app/assistant/relationships)?

**Relationship & Social Intelligence (RSI)** supports personal relationships — partners, family, colleagues. **Ecosystem Intelligence Phase 88** maps external organizational relationships — customers, suppliers, strategic partners. Cross-link only.

## What are the limitation principles?

- **No manipulative relationship scoring**
- **No hidden profiling** of partners or customers
- **No excessive automation** of human connection
- **Stewardship not surveillance** — humans govern relationships

## What are relationship insights with 🦉🌹🔔?

🦉 Wisdom phrases invite stewardship review without surveillance. 🌹 Gratitude phrases celebrate mutual value and authentic connection. 🔔 Bell moments surface dependency awareness for calm leadership attention. All encourage reflection — not manipulative scoring.

## What is partnership health?

Five dimensions scaffold leadership review: communication, mutual value, dependency balance, strategic alignment, and collaboration opportunities. Aipify does not score partners manipulatively.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self Love (A.76) supports patience, gratitude, and authentic connection. *"Strong ecosystems grow through genuine care, not transactional scoring."* Principle only — Ecosystem Intelligence stores metadata, not personal wellbeing content.

## What are the Phase 88 success criteria?

Computed live on the dashboard via `_eierbp88_success_criteria()` — relationship categories, insights, partnership health, community connection, companion guidance, limitations, cross-links, and ecosystem baseline.

## Where does dogfooding happen?

**Aipify Group** validates internally — Sales Expert community, customer success patterns, technology partnerships, strategic alliances, and user communities. **Unonight** is the first external pilot.
