# Ecosystem Intelligence — FAQ

## What is Ecosystem Intelligence?

Ecosystem Intelligence helps organizations understand the external relationships and dependencies that influence success.

## How does Aipify map relationships?

Relationship Maps identify categories, ownership structures, dependencies and strategic importance.

## How are external risks identified?

Risks are evaluated through dependency analysis, relationship concentration assessments and continuity reviews.

## Can organizations define their own relationship categories?

Yes. Ecosystem models may be customized to reflect organizational realities.

## What is Ecosystem Health Score?

Ecosystem Health Score represents the resilience and effectiveness of the organization's external ecosystem.

## Can Aipify monitor external organizations without consent?

No. Transparency, authorization and privacy principles always apply.

## How does Ecosystem Intelligence improve continuity?

By identifying critical dependencies and supporting diversification and backup planning.

## Can ecosystem insights influence strategy?

Yes. Strategic Intelligence incorporates ecosystem findings into long-term recommendations.

## What happens when a critical dependency is identified?

Aipify generates recommendations for mitigation, diversification or continuity planning.

## Does Ecosystem Intelligence replace relationship management?

No. It strengthens human decision-making through greater visibility and structured analysis.
