# Aipify Core Platform — FAQ

## What is Aipify Core?

Aipify Core is the central platform that powers all Aipify services and modules.

## Why is Aipify built separately from Unonight?

Unonight is a pilot customer. Building Aipify independently ensures scalability for future customers.

## What is a tenant?

A tenant represents a customer organization operating within Aipify.

## Why are audit logs important?

They provide accountability, transparency, and security for all actions.

## Can AI perform actions automatically?

Only low-risk actions may be automated. Medium and high-risk actions require human approval.

## What are modules?

Modules are specialized capabilities that can be enabled or disabled per customer.

## Why use a Knowledge Center?

It allows Aipify to provide accurate, context-aware assistance based on approved information.

## Will Aipify support external integrations?

Yes. Integrations are a core part of the platform strategy.

## Is Aipify intended only for large companies?

No. Aipify is designed to scale from small businesses to enterprise environments.

## What is the purpose of the Unonight pilot?

To validate Aipify in real-world conditions before broader market expansion.
