# Implementation Blueprint Phase 155 — Organizational Renewal & Reinvention FAQ

## What is Phase 155?

Phase 155 extends the Change Management Engine (Phase A.47 + Blueprint Phases 62 & 127) with **Legacy & Future Stewardship organizational renewal** — renewal planning, identity preservation, reinvention intelligence, executive renewal reviews, and renewal companion support at `/app/change-management-engine`.

## How is Phase 155 different from Phase 127?

**Phase 127** orchestrates transformation execution with adoption intelligence and change companion support. **Phase 155** deepens **intentional reinvention rooted in wisdom** — identity preservation, executive renewal reviews, and renewal memory. All Phase 127 fields are preserved.

## Does Phase 155 create a new route?

No. Phase 155 extends the existing `/app/change-management-engine` dashboard and card RPCs. **Do NOT create a duplicate Renewal Center.**

## What helper prefixes are used?

Engine: `_cme_*` (A.47). Blueprint Phase 62: `_cmbp_*`. Blueprint Phase 127: `_tcobp127_*`. Blueprint Phase 155: `_orrebp155_*` — must not collide.

## How does identity preservation work?

Five dimensions: values definition, strengths that endure, meaningful traditions, what must evolve, responsibilities of growth. Cross-links Purpose & Values Phase 138 — does not duplicate PVE RPCs. **Renewal strengthens identity — does not erase it.**

## What are Renewal Companion limitations?

Never determine strategy · Never force transformation · Never replace executive leadership · Never suppress alternative viewpoints · Never override governance.

## What cross-links are mandatory?

Future Leaders 151 · Org Legacy 152 · Decision Heritage 153 · Resilience 154 · Transformation 127 · Purpose 138 · Growth A.81 · Continuous Improvement 134 · Strategic Foresight 122 · Innovation Lab · Innovation Impact · Knowledge Exchange 141 · Self Love A.76.

## What thin RPCs are available?

- `record_executive_renewal_review(...)` — executive renewal review metadata scaffold
- `register_renewal_memory_entry(...)` — renewal memory metadata scaffold
- `create_renewal_scenario(...)` — future scenario exploration scaffold

## Where is the dashboard?

`/app/change-management-engine` — `get_change_management_engine_dashboard()` returns all Phase A.47, Phase 62, Phase 127, and Phase 155 fields.

## How does Self Love connect?

Curiosity, courage, patience, self-awareness, recognition of progress, and compassion during uncertainty — principle cross-link to `/app/self-love-engine`, not wellbeing content storage.
