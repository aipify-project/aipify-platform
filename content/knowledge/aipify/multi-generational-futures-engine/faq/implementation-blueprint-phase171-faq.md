# Multi-Generational Futures Engine — FAQ (Phase 171)

## What is the Multi-Generational Futures Center?

The Multi-Generational Futures Center at `/app/multi-generational-futures-engine` is the **Cosmic Stewardship & Multi-Generational Futures Era (171–180) opener**. It supports long-horizon stewardship, intergenerational responsibility, and legacy continuity through reflective futures practice — **not** future prediction, fear-based messaging, or destiny claims.

## How is Phase 171 different from Civic Collaboration (Phase 161)?

Civic Collaboration (`/app/civic-collaboration-engine`) **opened** the Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170). Phase 171 **opens a new era** (171–180) focused on cosmic stewardship and multi-generational futures — cross-link only, **do not duplicate** `_ccve_*` RPCs.

## How is Phase 171 different from Human Flourishing (Phase 170)?

Human Flourishing (`/app/human-flourishing-engine`) **closed** the Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170). Phase 171 begins the next era with long-horizon and intergenerational stewardship themes — cross-link only.

## Does the Futures Companion predict destiny?

**No.** The Futures Companion provides horizon reflection prompts, stewardship briefings, legacy continuity summaries, intergenerational perspective themes, hope and patience guidance, and responsibility resources. It **does not** predict destiny, claim future certainty, use fear-based messaging, or override leadership judgment.

## What may be stored?

Approved **metadata summaries** only: executive futures review summaries (max 500 characters), long-horizon reflection aggregates, legacy continuity metadata — **not** prediction scores, destiny rankings, or surveillance data.

## What must never be stored?

Future prediction claims, destiny scoring, fear-based futures surveillance, prophetic authority assertions, or any data used to claim certainty about organizational or civilizational futures without human leadership.
