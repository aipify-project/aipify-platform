# Platform Excellence Engine — FAQ

## How is platform quality measured?

Platform quality is measured through dimensional scores — design, UX, performance, accessibility, consistency, Companion, and governance — aggregated into a Platform Quality Score and Excellence Health Score. Open the Platform Excellence Center at `/app/platform/excellence` to view live scores, review history, and trend signals for your organization.

## How are standards enforced?

The Platform Standards Library defines UI, UX, copy, Companion, governance, development, and quality standards. Consistency checks validate design patterns, terminology, navigation, loading states, and Companion voice against these standards. Issues are logged with severity and tracked until resolved. For operational quality monitoring, see Quality Guardian at `/app/quality-guardian-engine`.

## How is accessibility validated?

Accessibility reviews validate keyboard navigation, screen reader support, color contrast, focus states, and ARIA compliance. Reviews can be scheduled or generated on demand from the Excellence Center. Accessibility scores contribute to the overall Excellence Health Score.

## How are quality reviews performed?

Quality reviews cover quality, consistency, UX, performance, and accessibility across pages, workflows, modules, Business Packs, Industry Packs, Knowledge Center content, Companion experiences, and admin surfaces. Reviews follow scheduled, release, regression, and pack-specific cadences. Completed reviews are audited and reflected in quality scores.

## How is consistency maintained?

The Consistency Engine validates design patterns, terminology, navigation, colors, typography, icons, loading states, and Companion presence. Copy consistency checks tone, translations, success messages, and error messages against ABOS terminology and enterprise communication standards. Issues trigger advisor and intelligence signals with recommended remediation.

## How are improvements prioritized?

Excellence Intelligence and the Companion Excellence Advisor surface gaps by impact and confidence. Performance declines, accessibility gaps, workflow complexity, and design standard violations include effort estimates. Executive dashboard summarizes platform quality, health, consistency, performance, accessibility, and continuous improvement status.

## How is this different from Quality Guardian?

**Quality Guardian** (`/app/quality-guardian-engine`) monitors operational quality signals and governance integration. **Platform Excellence Center** (`/app/platform/excellence`) is the unified excellence layer — standards library, multi-dimensional scoring, scheduled reviews, consistency validation, and executive excellence analytics.
