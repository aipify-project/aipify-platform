# Personal Productivity Engine — FAQ

## What is the Personal Productivity Engine?

It helps individual users manage daily priorities, briefings, reminders, and focus recommendations — with metadata-only tracking and full user control. It is not employee surveillance.

## How is this different from organizational tasks or assistant memory?

Organizational tasks (A.62) are team-wide commitments assigned through the Unified Task engine. PAME assistant memory stores personal life context at `/app/assistant/memory`. Personal Productivity is per-user operational support — preferences, briefings, and gentle reminders about work commitments.

## Can Aipify automatically change my priorities?

No. Aipify prepares briefings and focus recommendations. You control preferences, quiet hours, and whether reminders are created or dismissed.

## Is my productivity data shared with managers?

Personal productivity profiles and reminders are scoped to you within your organization. Managers see organizational task assignments through separate engines — not private productivity preference content.
