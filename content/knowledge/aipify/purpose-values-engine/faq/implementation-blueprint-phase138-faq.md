# Implementation Blueprint Phase 138 — Organizational Purpose Alignment & Values Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 138 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 138 extends the Purpose & Values Engine (Phase A.82 + Blueprint Phases 64 & 95) with **Autonomous Organization Era purpose alignment depth** — deepening how organizations align leadership, companions, and daily practice with stated purpose and values. All A.82, Phase 64, and Phase 95 dashboard fields are preserved.

## How is Phase 138 different from Blueprint Phases 64 and 95?

**Blueprint Phase 64** provides purpose discovery, values in action, and organizational storytelling. **Phase 95** adds cultural alignment framing and belonging. **Phase 138** adds purpose alignment center capabilities, values framework engine, alignment review engine, culture health aggregates, executive purpose reviews, and values memory — reflection not enforcement.

## How is this different from Social Impact & Purpose Phase 118?

**Social Impact & Purpose Engine (Phase 118)** at `/app/social-impact-purpose-engine` holds social impact initiatives, community programs, and strategic commitments. **Organizational Purpose Alignment (Phase 138)** holds tenant purpose/values alignment, culture health aggregates, and leadership reflection scaffolds — cross-link only, do not duplicate.

## How is this different from Collective Decision Council Phase 137?

**Collective Decision & Human-Companion Council (Phase 137)** at `/app/collective-decision-council-engine` coordinates collective deliberation with human-companion perspectives. **Phase 138** provides purpose and values alignment reflection frameworks — decision reflection cross-link only.

## What are the companion limitations?

Companions **never** impose beliefs, claim objective truth about purpose, replace leadership definition, suppress diversity, or override governance. Companions facilitate reflection — humans define purpose.

## What is culture health?

Culture health uses **organizational aggregates only** — knowledge sharing patterns, recognition, psychological safety signals, learning participation, community engagement. It is **NOT** employee surveillance or hidden individual scoring.

## What data does the engine store?

Metadata only — `purpose_alignment_reviews`, `purpose_values_memory` summaries, `culture_health_snapshots`, stated values, alignment signals, and reflection prompts. No raw customer content, chat, PII, or individual behavior tracking.

## What are the Phase 138 success criteria?

Live criteria include purpose alignment center, values framework, alignment review engine, purpose companion, culture health engine, purpose integration, values memory, executive purpose reviews, companion limitations, Self Love connection, security requirements, Phase 64/95 preservation, integration links, and dogfooding.

## What is the helper prefix?

Engine helpers use `_pve_*`. Blueprint Phase 64 uses `_pvbp_*`. Phase 95 uses `_pvcaebp95_*`. Phase 138 uses `_opabp138_*` — they must not collide.

## What cross-links does Phase 138 include?

Phases 131–137 (Autonomous Organization Era), Social Impact 118, Organizational Wisdom 129, Strategic Alignment A.55, Inclusion A.83, Self Love A.76, Companion Workforce 132, Growth Partner Operations 114, and Collective Decision Council 137.
