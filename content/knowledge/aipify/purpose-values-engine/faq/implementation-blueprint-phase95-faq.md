# Implementation Blueprint Phase 95 — Purpose, Values & Cultural Alignment Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 95 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 95 extends the Purpose & Values Engine (Phase A.82 + Blueprint Phase 64) with **cultural alignment framing** — aligning everyday actions with values and purpose while strengthening belonging. All A.82 and Phase 64 dashboard fields are preserved.

## How is Phase 95 different from Blueprint Phase 64?

**Blueprint Phase 64** provides purpose discovery, values in action, decision alignment, organizational storytelling, and leadership insights. **Phase 95** adds cultural alignment — purpose/values reflection questions (🦉🌹❤️🔔), cultural observations (awareness not judgment), onboarding connection, companion guidance, recognition connection, and alignment-not-control privacy principles.

## How is this different from Sales Expert A.95?

**Sales Expert Operating System (Phase A.95)** at `/app/sales-expert-engine` is a distinct engine for sales culture and field operations. **Blueprint Phase 95** shares the number 95 but maps to Purpose & Values at `/app/purpose-values-engine` — phase number collision only.

## How is this different from Inclusion & Humanity A.83?

**Inclusion & Humanity** provides the Human Values Charter and communication conduct patterns. **Purpose & Values Cultural Alignment** holds tenant-specific purpose, values reflection, and cultural awareness scaffolds — charter vs organizational articulation. Cross-link only.

## How is this different from Business DNA?

**Business DNA** at `/app/settings/business-dna` stores tenant products, tone profiles, and support knowledge. **Purpose & Values** stores organizational purpose, stated values, and cultural alignment metadata — operational tone vs identity/culture.

## What are the privacy principles?

**Alignment not control:** no hidden cultural scoring, no public ranking, no values-as-compliance framing, no surveillance-based cultural evaluation. Metadata summaries only — humans decide.

## What are the Phase 95 success criteria?

Live criteria include daily values alignment, belonging strengthening, purpose direction, cultural awareness, onboarding integration, recognition reinforcement, companion guidance, leadership connection, privacy principles, trust connection, Self Love connection, Phase 64 preservation, integration links, and dogfooding.

## What is the vision phrase?

*"We know who we are, why we exist and how we aspire to treat one another."*

## What data does the engine store?

Metadata only — stated values, alignment signal summaries, reflection prompts, and cultural observation scaffolds. No hidden cultural scores, individual behavior metrics, raw customer content, chat, or PII.

## What is the helper prefix?

Engine helpers use `_pve_*`. Blueprint Phase 64 helpers use `_pvbp_*`. Blueprint Phase 95 helpers use `_pvcaebp95_*` — they must not collide.

## What cross-links does Phase 95 include?

Strategic Alignment A.55 / Blueprint Phase 68, Inclusion & Humanity A.83, Companion Identity A.84, Business DNA A.46, Install Onboarding A.22, Employee Knowledge EKE, Organizational Memory A.34 / Blueprint Phase 94, AI Ethics A.46, Gratitude & Recognition A.89, Self Love A.76, and Sales Expert A.95 (collision note only).
