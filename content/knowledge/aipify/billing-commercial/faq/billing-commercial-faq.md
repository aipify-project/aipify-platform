# Billing & Commercial Information — FAQ

## Why does Aipify use subscription models?

Subscriptions provide predictable access to ongoing innovation, support, and platform improvements.

## Can we start small and expand later?

Yes. Aipify is designed to grow alongside your organization. Customers may begin with essential capabilities and expand as requirements evolve.

## Are all features included in every package?

No. Aipify uses a modular approach so organizations only pay for the capabilities they require.

## Can we purchase additional functionality later?

Yes. Business Packs and add-on modules can be added as organizational needs change.

## Do enterprise customers receive different options?

Yes. Enterprise customers may access additional services, deployment models, and commercial arrangements.

## Can billing be managed internally by our finance team?

Yes. Authorized users may access invoices, payment methods, and subscription information through the billing portal.

## Can partners sell Aipify solutions?

Yes. Approved partners may participate through reseller, referral, or strategic partnership programs.

## What happens if we downgrade our subscription?

Aipify provides clear guidance regarding the impact on features, users, and retained data, including grace periods.

## Does Aipify support international customers?

Yes. The commercial framework is designed to support multiple currencies and regional considerations.

## Why are Business Packs important?

Business Packs allow organizations to tailor Aipify to their specific needs without unnecessary complexity.
