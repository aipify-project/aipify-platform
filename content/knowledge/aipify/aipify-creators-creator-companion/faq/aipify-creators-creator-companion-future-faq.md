# What is Aipify Creators?

Aipify Creators is a future Business Pack seed for creators, influencers, and content producers. The Creator Companion helps you get more value from tools you already use — without replacing your creativity.

# Is this available today?

No. This Business Pack is intentionally postponed until the Companion platform and Desktop Companion foundation are mature.

# Will Aipify post on my behalf?

No. Aipify prepares drafts and suggestions. You review and approve before anything is published or sent externally.
