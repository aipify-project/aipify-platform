# Continuous Improvement Engine — Blueprint Phase 134 FAQ

## FAQ 1

**Question:** What is Phase 134 — Adaptive Organization & Continuous Optimization Engine?

**Answer:** Phase 134 extends the Continuous Improvement Engine (A.33 + A.49 + Blueprint Phase 90) at `/app/continuous-improvement-engine` with Autonomous Organization Era adaptive optimization depth — experimentation framework, adaptation insights, change fatigue protection, and improvement portfolio. Same route, no duplicate improvement center. Helpers use `_aoabp134_*`.

## FAQ 2

**Question:** How is Phase 134 different from Blueprint Phase 90?

**Answer:** Phase 90 covers organizational evolution habits — learning cycles, improvement questions, and maintain · improve · transform modes. Phase 134 adds adaptive optimization depth for the Autonomous Organization Era (131–140): bounded experiments, adaptation insight engine, change fatigue protection, and improvement portfolio tracking. Both live on the same route and preserve all prior fields.

## FAQ 3

**Question:** Does Phase 134 auto-optimize the organization?

**Answer:** No. Aipify identifies opportunities and prepares experiment scaffolds — organizations decide change. The Optimization Companion highlights opportunities, connects knowledge, suggests experiments, tracks outcomes, and encourages reflection — it never dictates change or issues mandates.

## FAQ 4

**Question:** What is change fatigue protection?

**Answer:** Organizational capacity signals — excessive initiatives, learning overload, companion overexpansion, communication fatigue, transformation saturation, and organizational exhaustion. These are aggregate indicators, NOT individual employee surveillance or punitive tracking.

## FAQ 5

**Question:** What is the adaptive learning loop?

**Answer:** Observe → Reflect → Experiment → Evaluate → Learn → Adapt. Phase 134 extends Phase 90's cycle with an explicit evaluation step. Humans approve all experiments and initiatives — no silent auto-implementation.

## FAQ 6

**Question:** Which engines does Phase 134 cross-link?

**Answer:** Phase 90 (same route), Learning Engine `/app/learning`, Innovation Lab `/app/innovation-lab`, Growth & Evolution A.81, Autonomous Organization Era 131–133 (Human Oversight A.40, Companion Workforce 132, Organizational Autonomy 133), Executive Operations 130, Organizational Memory A.34, Transformation 127, Aipify University 115, Growth Partner Operations 114, Community 117, and Self Love A.76.

## FAQ 7

**Question:** What new tables does Phase 134 add?

**Answer:** Three optional scaffold tables (metadata only): `adaptive_organization_experiments`, `adaptive_organization_improvement_portfolio`, and `adaptive_organization_fatigue_signals`. These complement existing `improvement_initiatives` from A.49.

## FAQ 8

**Question:** What is the Phase 134 vision?

**Answer:** "Organizations improve at a healthy pace — curious, reflective, and sustainable — because adaptation serves people and outcomes, not change for its own sake." Wisdom before speed. People First.
