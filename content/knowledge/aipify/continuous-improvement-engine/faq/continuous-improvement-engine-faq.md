# Continuous Improvement Engine FAQ

## FAQ 1

**Question:** What is the Continuous Improvement Engine?

**Answer:** It helps Aipify evolve by identifying opportunities to improve operational outcomes.

## FAQ 2

**Question:** Does Aipify automatically change itself?

**Answer:** No. Improvements should remain subject to human review and governance policies.

## FAQ 3

**Question:** How are improvement opportunities identified?

**Answer:** Through feedback, analytics, quality monitoring, and operational outcomes.

## FAQ 4

**Question:** Can organizations reject recommendations?

**Answer:** Yes. Organizations retain full control over improvement decisions.

## FAQ 5

**Question:** Are improvement activities audited?

**Answer:** Yes. Significant improvement events are recorded for accountability.
