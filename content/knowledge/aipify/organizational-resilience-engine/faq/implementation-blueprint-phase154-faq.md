# Implementation Blueprint Phase 154 — Organizational Resilience & Adaptive Continuity Engine FAQ

## What is Legacy & Future Stewardship Phase 154?

Phase 154 extends the Organizational Resilience Engine (Phase A.50 / ABOS Resilience Engine) with Blueprint Phase 81, Phase 91, Phase 128, and Phase 136 with an **Organizational Resilience & Adaptive Continuity** layer — adaptive continuity planning, scenario preparedness, leadership readiness reviews, organizational flexibility, and continuity memory with **preparedness and flexibility, not fear or rigid control**. It layers on the same route `/app/organizational-resilience-engine` — no duplicate Resilience Center.

## How is Phase 154 different from Phase 128 and Phase 136?

**Phase 128** (`_rccbp128_*`) = continuity companion — prepare/adapt/recover with readiness not command. **Phase 136** (`_shobp136_*`) = self-healing operations — detect degradation and coordinate transparent recovery. **Phase 154** (`_oracbp154_*`) = Legacy era adaptive continuity depth — scenario preparedness, leadership resilience reviews, organizational flexibility, and continuity memory for long-horizon stewardship. All phases share A.50 resilience metadata.

## How is Phase 154 different from Continuity Phase 80?

**Continuity Phase 80** at `/app/continuity` handles crisis continuity — backup ownership, incident mode, readiness score, and crisis briefings. **Organizational Continuity Blueprint 73** cross-links only. **Phase 154** focuses on **adaptive continuity planning and leadership readiness** — preparedness exercises, scenario scaffolds, and continuity memory — not crisis command.

## What is the Adaptive Resilience Center?

Ten capabilities extending Phase 128 Resilience Center: continuity planning · scenario preparedness · operational recovery frameworks · leadership readiness reviews · companion resilience support · adaptive learning programs · resilience dashboards · knowledge preservation · employee support visibility · organizational flexibility.

## What scenarios does the scenario preparedness engine cover?

Seven scenario types: leadership changes · economic downturns · rapid growth · technology disruptions · operational incidents · knowledge loss · global market changes — scaffolds only, no panic framing.

## What are Resilience Companion limitations in Phase 154?

No guaranteed outcomes · no replacing executive leadership · no overriding emergency procedures · no suppressing uncertainty · no acting beyond governance. The Companion supports preparedness — **does NOT eliminate uncertainty**.

## Does Phase 154 monitor employees?

No. The employee support framework provides organizational support — communication clarity, psychological safety, recognition, and learning opportunities — **NOT surveillance**. Aggregate metadata only; no individual scoring.

## What are the Phase 154 success criteria?

Computed live via `_oracbp154_success_criteria()` — objectives, Adaptive Resilience Center, adaptive continuity engine, scenario preparedness, leadership reviews, Resilience Companion, organizational flexibility, employee support, continuity memory, limitations, security requirements, cross-links, success metrics, Phase 136/128 field preservation, and continuity workflow validation.
