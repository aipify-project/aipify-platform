# Implementation Blueprint Phase 91 — Organizational Resilience & Recovery Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 91 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 91 extends the Organizational Resilience Engine (Phase A.50 / ABOS Resilience Engine) and Blueprint Phase 81 Risk Navigation with recovery focus, adversity learning, and hope-strengthening companion guidance. It layers on the same route `/app/organizational-resilience-engine` — no new route or tables.

## How is Phase 91 different from Phase 81 Risk Navigation?

**Phase 81** (`_rnbp_*`) focuses on **navigation and preparedness** — risk awareness, preparedness planning, and balanced decision-making. **Phase 91** (`_orrbp91_*`) focuses on **recovery and adversity learning** — structured recovery, hope-strengthening reflection, and learning through difficulty. Both share the same resilience plans, reviews, vulnerabilities, and simulations metadata.

## How is this different from Partner Certification (Repo Phase 91)?

**Partner & Certification Ecosystem (Repo Phase 91)** at `/app/partners` is the partner portal and certification program. **ABOS Blueprint Phase 91 Organizational Resilience & Recovery** maps to Organizational Resilience at `/app/organizational-resilience-engine` — phase number collision only. Cross-link, do not duplicate.

## How is this different from Dedication Engine (A.91)?

**Dedication Engine A.91** at `/app/dedication-engine` supports dedication and commitment practices. **Blueprint Phase 91** is organizational recovery and adversity learning on the resilience engine — repo engine phase number collision only.

## What are the resilience domains?

- **Operational** — process continuity, service recovery, workflow adaptation
- **People** — wellbeing, leadership support, sustainable workloads
- **Strategic** — priority decisions, adaptation, capability rebuilding
- **Cultural** — shared purpose, trust, connection, and hope through difficulty

## What are resilience questions with 🦉🌹❤️🔔?

🦉 Wisdom — what can we learn? 🌹 Reflection — what helped? ❤️ Hope — how strengthen connection during recovery? 🔔 Preparedness — what would support us if similar conditions arise? All encourage honest reflection — not pressure to move on before ready.

## What are the limitation principles?

- **No toxic positivity** or unrealistic optimism
- **No minimizing** legitimate concerns
- **No predictable recovery assumptions** — every recovery is unique
- **Humans decide pace** — Aipify informs and supports

## What is recovery reflection?

Structured scaffold for what happened, what helped, what hindered, and lessons — metadata only via resilience reviews. Humans decide what to capture; Aipify never stores raw incident content or PII.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self Love (A.76) supports compassionate recovery periods and sustainable pace. *"Moving forward despite difficulty — learning through adversity with compassion."* Principle only — Recovery Blueprint stores metadata, not wellbeing content.

## What are the Phase 91 success criteria?

Computed live on the dashboard via `_orrbp91_success_criteria()` — recovery preparedness, structured recovery, adversity learning, people resilience, cultural strengthening, limitation principles, and cross-links.

## Where does dogfooding happen?

**Aipify Group** validates internally — operational disruptions, product incidents, ecosystem changes, leadership transitions, support escalations. **Unonight** is the first external pilot.
