# Implementation Blueprint Phase 81 — Risk Navigation Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 81 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 81 extends the Organizational Resilience Engine (Phase A.50 / ABOS Resilience Engine) with balanced risk awareness and preparedness planning. It layers on the same route `/app/organizational-resilience-engine` — no new route or tables.

## How is this different from Organizational Resilience A.50?

**A.50** (`_ore_*`) focuses on scenario-based continuity plans, simulations, vulnerability tracking, and structured reviews. **Phase 81** (`_rnbp_*`) adds risk navigation framing — risk categories, preparedness questions, companion guidance, risk/opportunity balance, and limitation principles. Both share resilience plans, vulnerabilities, and simulations metadata.

## How is this different from /app/strategy (Repo Phase 81)?

**Strategic Intelligence & Opportunity (Repo Phase 81)** at `/app/strategy` is the legacy strategic scorecard. **ABOS Blueprint Phase 81 Risk Navigation** maps to Organizational Resilience at `/app/organizational-resilience-engine` — phase number collision only. Cross-link, do not duplicate.

## How is this different from Strategic Intelligence (Blueprint Phase 79)?

**Strategic Intelligence Phase 79** (`/app/strategic-intelligence-foundation-engine`) cultivates strategic awareness and pattern recognition. **Risk Navigation Phase 81** focuses on preparedness and balanced risk attention — cross-link for strategic context.

## How is this different from Continuity (Phase 80)?

**Continuity Phase 80** (`/app/continuity`) handles backup ownership, incident mode, and readiness score. **Risk Navigation Phase 81** complements with risk awareness and preparedness planning on the resilience engine — cross-link only.

## What are the limitation principles?

- **No fear-based communication** — preparedness not alarm
- **No catastrophic interpretations** — uncertainty is not inevitability of failure
- **No uncertainty as inevitability** — navigable with wisdom and preparation
- **Humans decide** — Aipify informs and prepares

## What are risk questions with 🦉🌹🔔?

🦉 Wisdom questions surface critical assumptions for review. 🌹 Self Love questions identify early warning signals with calm framing. 🔔 Bell moments invite preparedness if conditions change. All encourage planning — not anxiety.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self Love (A.76) supports perspective, courage, reflection, and confidence in preparation. *"Preparedness often reduces fear more effectively than avoidance."* Principle only — Risk Navigation stores metadata, not wellbeing content.

## What are the Phase 81 success criteria?

Computed live on the dashboard via `_rnbp_success_criteria()` — risk awareness, stronger preparedness, leadership confidence, organizational resilience, balanced opportunity pursuit, limitation principles, and cross-links.

## Where does dogfooding happen?

**Aipify Group** validates internally — ecosystem growth, operational scaling, strategic investments, leadership resilience. **Unonight** is the first external pilot.
