# Board & Investor Intelligence — Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is Board Intelligence?

Board Intelligence transforms operational and strategic data into governance-ready insights for leadership teams, boards of directors, and future investors.

## How does Aipify support executive governance?

Through board dashboards, decision registers, governance health assessments, meeting summaries, and immutable audit trails — integrated with Decision Cockpit, Portfolio, and Early Warning systems.

## Can Aipify replace board decision-making?

**No.** Aipify prepares intelligence and documentation. Human leadership and boards retain full decision authority.

## How are investor insights generated?

From execution outcomes, portfolio health, and governance patterns — always as **illustrative indicators**, not audited financial reporting.

## How should leaders interpret confidence scores?

As estimates of data quality and pattern strength — never as guarantees of financial or strategic outcomes.

---

**Principle:** Aipify strengthens governance and strategic awareness. Human leadership remains responsible for decisions.
