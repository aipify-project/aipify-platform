# Aipify Spiritual & Reflection Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Spiritual & Reflection Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports reflection, mindfulness, spiritual practices, gratitude, and personal meaning-making while respecting diverse beliefs and worldviews.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Purpose Companion, Legacy Companion, Wellness Companion, Memory, and Presence foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Many people seek moments of stillness and connection beyond productivity. Aipify should support these moments without defining what they should look like.

## Does Aipify promote a specific religion?

**No.** Aipify must never promote specific religions, challenge personal beliefs, judge belief systems, or provide spiritual guidance beyond user-defined boundaries. The module is **belief-neutral** and supports secular and spiritual users equally.

## How is this different from Purpose Companion?

**Purpose Companion** focuses on values, goals, and life alignment. **Spiritual & Reflection Companion** focuses on **mindfulness, gratitude, rituals, and contemplative practices** — with strict belief neutrality. Purpose Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## How is this different from Wellness Companion?

**Wellness Companion** supports general personal wellbeing and sustainable habits. **Spiritual & Reflection Companion** goes deeper into **reflection, gratitude, and user-defined spiritual practices**. Wellness Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## Can reflection prompts or insights create guilt?

No. Reflection prompts invite reflection — never prescribe answers. Insights encourage awareness — never induce guilt.

## What data does this module store?

When implemented, it stores **metadata, ritual schedules, and gratitude summaries only** — not full journal content or sensitive spiritual narratives without governed consent.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include scheduling reflection sessions, printing gratitude journals, and coordinating quiet time reminders — all requiring user approval.

## Who is Spiritual & Reflection Companion for?

Anyone who wants intentional space for pause, gratitude, and reconnection — on their own terms and within their own beliefs.
