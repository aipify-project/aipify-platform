# Implementation Blueprint Phase 104 — Commerce Performance & Profit Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 104 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 104 extends the Commerce Performance & Profit Engine (repo Phase 104) at `/app/commerce-performance` with **ABOS blueprint scaffolding** — meaningful profitability insights, cost visibility, growth quality analysis, and leadership framing. All baseline `_cpp_*` dashboard fields are preserved.

## How is Blueprint Phase 104 different from repo Phase 104 baseline?

**Repo Phase 104** ships performance scores, profit intelligence, product profitability, customer value signals, revenue trends, opportunity center, loss prevention, strategic recommendations, and executive reporting. **Blueprint Phase 104** adds mission/philosophy metadata, six objectives, Commerce Companion guidance, pricing insights, growth quality analysis, limitation principles, Self Love connection, trust connection, live success criteria, and integration cross-links — layered on top without replacing baseline RPC behavior.

## How is this different from Commerce Intelligence Blueprint Phase 101?

**Commerce Intelligence Phase 101** at `/app/commerce-intelligence` focuses on product discovery, trend awareness, margin before commitment, and supplier evaluation. **Commerce Performance Phase 104** focuses on profit operations, product profitability, revenue trends with margin context, and executive reporting — cross-link only, do not duplicate.

## How is this different from Revenue Intelligence Blueprint Phase 39?

**Revenue Intelligence Blueprint Phase 39** at `/app/commercial` covers subscription MRR/ARR and billing commercial intelligence. **Commerce Performance Phase 104** covers product and commerce profitability — distinct surfaces documented in `_cppbp104_distinction_note()`.

## What is the Commerce Companion?

The **Commerce Companion** is Aipify's sustainability-guided profitability framing — not a generic AI finance bot. It scaffolds margin review with optional prompts (🦉🌹🔔). Humans retain financial oversight; `auto_actions_disabled` remains default true.

## What are the limitation principles?

**Sustainability not extraction:** no growth-at-any-cost framing, no short-term-only optimization ignoring margin, no ignoring customer experience for volume metrics, no treating metrics as the sole definition of success.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Sustainable pacing in commercial ambition — *"Profitability clarity before scale protects wellbeing — rushed growth creates avoidable operational stress."* Cross-link `/app/self-love-engine` — principle only.

## What is the Fiken distinction?

**Fiken** remains the accounting source of truth. **Aipify** provides the operational intelligence layer — profit metadata and trend summaries, not replacement accounting records.

## What are the Phase 104 success criteria?

Live criteria include profit visibility, product performance, revenue intelligence, cost awareness, strategic prioritization, sustainable growth, profit intelligence dimensions, product performance insights, pricing insights, Commerce Companion guidance, leadership insights, limitation principles, trust connection, Self Love connection, commerce strategy connection, baseline preservation, integration links, dogfooding (Sportsklær.no, Shopify, Fiken, Stripe), and ABOS principle.

## What is the vision phrase?

*"We finally understand what truly drives profitability in our business."*

## What data does the engine store?

Profit intelligence metadata, product profitability profiles, revenue trend summaries, opportunity and risk indicators, and audit events — no raw orders, payment records, or PII. Does not replace professional financial expertise.

## What is the helper prefix?

Engine helpers use `_cpp_*`. Blueprint Phase 104 helpers use `_cppbp104_*` — they must not collide.
