# Commerce Performance & Profit — FAQ

## What is the Commerce Performance & Profit Engine?

The Commerce Performance & Profit Engine helps organizations understand how their business is performing and where opportunities for sustainable improvement may exist.

## Does Aipify replace accountants or financial advisors?

No. Aipify provides decision-support insights but does not replace professional financial expertise.

## Why focus on profitability instead of revenue alone?

Revenue growth without profitability may create long-term challenges. Healthy businesses require both growth and sustainability.

## Can Aipify identify underperforming products?

Yes. The engine may highlight products requiring review based on profitability and performance observations.

## Can Aipify help identify growth opportunities?

Yes. Opportunity insights may reveal areas where businesses can strengthen their performance.

## What is a Commerce Performance Score?

It is an aggregated view of commercial health, reflecting multiple dimensions of business performance.

## Can Aipify warn about emerging risks?

Yes. The engine may identify signals suggesting performance deterioration or operational concerns.

## Why are customer value insights important?

Long-term customer relationships often contribute significantly to sustainable profitability.

## Will Aipify make decisions automatically?

No. Organizations remain responsible for commercial actions. Aipify provides guidance and recommendations.

## Who benefits from this functionality?

Store owners, commerce teams, executives and decision makers seeking improved visibility into business performance.
