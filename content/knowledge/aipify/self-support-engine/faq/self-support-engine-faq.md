# Self-Support Engine — FAQ

## What is the Aipify Self-Support Engine?

Self-Support helps customers resolve questions about Aipify quickly using approved Knowledge Center content, with human escalation when needed.

## Does Self-Support replace human support?

No. Self-Support handles routine questions automatically. Sensitive or low-confidence topics escalate to your support team.

## How does Self-Support find answers?

Self-Support searches the Knowledge Center before generating a response. Only published, tenant-approved content is used.

## What are confidence levels?

High confidence allows automatic responses. Medium confidence shows a draft recommendation. Low confidence escalates to support.

## What happens if Self-Support cannot help?

The conversation is escalated to a human team member and a knowledge gap may be recorded for improvement.

## Is feedback collected?

Yes. Customers can mark responses as helpful or unhelpful and suggest improvements.

## Are Self-Support activities audited?

Yes. Automated responses, escalations, closures, feedback, and Knowledge Center recommendations are logged.

## What channels are supported?

Initially: embedded support widget, dashboard, and Knowledge Center search. Email, messaging, and voice are planned for future phases.
