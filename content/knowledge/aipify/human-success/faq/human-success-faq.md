title: What is Adoption Score?
slug: what-is-adoption-score
category: human-success
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [human-success, adoption, faq]
article_type: faq
---
Adoption Score (0–100) measures how effectively Aipify capabilities are being used. Components include usage consistency, feature discovery, knowledge engagement, learning completion, value recognition, and workflow success. Bands: Exceptional (90+), Strong (75–89), Growth Opportunity (60–74), Adoption Challenges (40–59), Critical Risk (below 40).
