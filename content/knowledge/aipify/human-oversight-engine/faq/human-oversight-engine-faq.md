# Human Oversight Engine FAQ

## FAQ 1

**Question:** What is the Human Oversight Engine?

**Answer:** It provides organizational accountability for AI recommendations and operational actions — humans decide, Aipify explains and prepares.

## FAQ 2

**Question:** Can AI execute critical actions automatically?

**Answer:** No. Critical actions are prohibited for AI per Trust & Action Engine rules. Human confirmation is always required.

## FAQ 3

**Question:** How does this relate to Secure AI Actions?

**Answer:** Human Oversight extends Secure AI Actions (A.3) with organization-wide oversight levels, override tracking, and accountability metrics — it does not duplicate the action execution queue.

## FAQ 4

**Question:** What happens when someone applies an override?

**Answer:** Overrides require a business justification, are fully audited, and may trigger a follow-up review depending on organization settings.

## FAQ 5

**Question:** Are oversight decisions recorded in the audit log?

**Answer:** Yes. Approvals granted, rejections, overrides, critical confirmations, and rationale updates are all recorded for accountability.
