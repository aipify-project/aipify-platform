# Aipify Academy & Learning Ecosystem — FAQ

## What is Aipify Academy?

Aipify Academy is the learning ecosystem designed to help individuals and organizations develop the knowledge and skills required to use Aipify effectively.

## Do I need technical expertise to participate?

No. Learning experiences are designed for a wide range of users, from beginners to advanced specialists.

## Are certifications available?

Yes. Certain learning paths support professional and partner certification opportunities.

## Can organizations track employee progress?

Yes. Administrators may monitor participation, completion rates and readiness indicators.

## Will learning recommendations be personalized?

Yes. Recommendations may be tailored based on role, usage patterns and organizational objectives.

## Can executives benefit from the Academy?

Yes. Executive learning focuses on governance, strategy, risk management and business transformation.

## Does the Academy replace the Knowledge Center?

No. The Knowledge Center provides operational guidance, while the Academy focuses on structured education and capability development.

## Can partners access specialized training?

Yes. Partner-specific learning experiences support implementation excellence and ecosystem growth.

## Why is education important in AI adoption?

Technology alone does not guarantee success. People require confidence, understanding and practical skills to achieve meaningful outcomes.

## Will the Academy evolve over time?

Yes. The learning ecosystem is designed to grow alongside Aipify and adapt to emerging customer needs.
