# Aipify Elder Care Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Elder Care Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports elderly individuals and their families with reminders, appointments, routines, and practical coordination — while preserving dignity and independence.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Life Events, Memory, Presence, **Family Companion**, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Help people remain independent for as long as possible — strengthen support systems around them. Aipify does not replace family members or caregivers.

## Is this a medical or healthcare service?

**No.** Aipify must never diagnose health conditions, make medical decisions, or replace healthcare professionals. Medication reminders are **scheduling only** — not medical advice.

## What about emergencies?

Aipify coordinates **preparedness** (contact lists, document organization, notification preferences). **Emergency response remains human-led.**

## How does this relate to Family Companion?

**Family Companion** is a **prerequisite** — general family coordination. **Elder Care Companion** extends this with dignity-first support specifically for aging individuals and caregiver coordination.

## Can family members control what is shared?

Yes. Families define who may assist, which reminders are shared, notification preferences, emergency contact visibility, and permission levels.

## Can insights create anxiety?

No. Insights should promote confidence and support — never fear or anxiety.

## What data does Elder Care Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **coordination metadata and reminder summaries only** — not clinical records. Consent-based sharing with complete data control.
