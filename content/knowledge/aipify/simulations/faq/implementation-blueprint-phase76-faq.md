# Implementation Blueprint Phase 76 — Scenario Simulation Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 76 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 76 aligns the Simulation & Decision Lab (Phase 78) with ABOS Scenario Simulation requirements — strategic scenario types, simulation questions, multiple futures, companion guidance, collaborative simulation, limitation principles, and live success criteria scaffolds.

## How is this different from Trust Engine repo Phase 76?

**Trust Engine (Repo Phase 76)** at `/app/trust` provides decision explanations, Trust Score, and explainability. **ABOS Blueprint Phase 76** at `/app/simulations` is **structured scenario exploration** — quantitative simulation with production isolation. Same phase number, different product surface.

## How is this different from Blueprint Phase 22?

**Blueprint Phase 22** (`_sdlbp_*`) provides decision comparison framework, simulation objectives, and engagement summary for the Decision Lab. **Blueprint Phase 76** (`_ssbp_*`) adds strategic scenario exploration, multiple futures, and limitation principles — layered on the same `/app/simulations` route.

## How is this different from Decision Support Engine Phase 60?

**Decision Support Engine** at `/app/assistant/decisions` provides reflection scaffolding for personal decisions. Phase 76 provides **quantitative scenario simulation** with assumptions, confidence, and production isolation — distinct surface.

## How is this different from Future Readiness Blueprint Phase 63?

**Future Readiness Phase 63** at `/app/future-tech` focuses on **reflection and scenario preparedness** — not operational prediction. Phase 76 explores strategic options with visible assumptions in the Simulation Lab — perspective not prediction.

## How is this different from Predictive Operations Blueprint Phase 74?

**Predictive Operations Phase 74** at `/app/predictive-insights-engine` provides forward-looking operational trend and bottleneck signals. Phase 76 provides **structured what-if scenario exploration** before significant decisions — cross-link for preparedness context.

## How is this different from Innovation Lab Phase 96?

**Innovation Lab Phase 96** at `/app/innovation-lab` runs controlled experiments with governance approval. Phase 76 **simulates and never acts** — production isolated; no experiment execution.

## How is this different from Organizational Resilience A.50?

**Organizational Resilience A.50** at `/app/organizational-resilience-engine` handles crisis scenario planning and tabletop exercises. Phase 76 provides operational and strategic simulation forecasting — cross-link for resilience context.

## What are the six scenario simulation objectives?

- **Strategic simulations** — growth, restructuring, investments
- **Scenario exploration** — structured what-if with assumptions
- **Consequence awareness** — unintended consequences and cross-department influence
- **Risk preparation** — challenging futures without fear narratives
- **Opportunity evaluation** — optimistic and expected futures
- **Leadership reflection** — workshops and cross-functional reviews

## What are limitation principles?

`_ssbp_limitation_principles()` forbids simulations as guarantees, overstating certainty, fear-driven narratives, and auto-execution. Required: explicit assumptions, multiple futures framing, human decision authority, production isolation.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Curiosity, perspective, confidence, and acceptance that no organization controls every variable — *"Preparedness often begins by asking better questions."* Cross-link `/app/self-love-engine` (principle only).

## What are the Phase 76 success criteria?

Greater preparedness, strategic conversations, risk awareness, opportunity exploration, decision confidence, production isolation, limitation principles, companion guidance, collaborative simulation, trust connection, integration links, and ABOS principle — computed live via `_ssbp_success_criteria()`.

## Where does Unonight fit?

Unonight is the first external pilot for commerce growth and operational scenario exploration. Aipify Group validates ecosystem growth, product expansion, and Sales Expert scaling internally first.

## Does Phase 76 add new database tables?

No. Phase 76 extends `get_simulation_lab_dashboard()` and `get_simulation_lab_card()` with `_ssbp_*` blueprint metadata and `_ssbp_engagement_summary()` only — all Phase 78 and Phase 22 fields preserved.
