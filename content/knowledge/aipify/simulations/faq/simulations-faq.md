---
title: Do simulations affect production systems?
slug: do-simulations-affect-production
category: simulations
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [simulations, safety, faq]
article_type: faq
priority: 9
---
No. Simulations never execute actions in production environments. They do not modify data, trigger automations, send notifications, publish content, approve requests, or bypass Governance or Security controls.

---
title: How accurate are simulations?
slug: how-accurate-are-simulations
category: simulations
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [simulations, confidence, faq]
article_type: faq
---
Accuracy depends on available data, Digital Twin maturity, and historical evidence. Simulation Confidence indicates reliability: High (strong evidence), Medium (partial evidence), Low (significant assumptions — human review recommended).

---
title: Can Decision Lab make decisions automatically?
slug: can-decision-lab-decide-automatically
category: simulations
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [simulations, governance, faq]
article_type: faq
---
No. Human approval remains mandatory. Decision Lab answers "What could happen if we make this change?" — not "Should we automatically implement this?"

---
title: What is Simulation Confidence?
slug: what-is-simulation-confidence
category: simulations
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [simulations, confidence, faq]
article_type: faq
---
Simulation Confidence indicates how reliable Aipify believes its predictions are, based on Twin data quality, historical patterns, and assumption strength. Low-confidence results should be reviewed before acting.
