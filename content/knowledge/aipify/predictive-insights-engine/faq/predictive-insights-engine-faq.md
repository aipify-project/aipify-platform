# Predictive Insights Engine — FAQ

## What is the Predictive Insights Engine?

It provides forward-looking operational predictions with confidence and risk scoring — metadata only. Predictions inform decisions; humans always decide what action to take.

## How are predictions generated?

Aipify analyzes aggregate operational signals from integrated engines (analytics, capacity, OKRs, health, and training) to surface likely outcomes. No raw customer records or PII are used.

## Can Aipify act on predictions automatically?

No. Predictions are advisory only. Sensitive actions still require human approval through the Trust & Action Engine.

## What prediction types are supported?

Support backlog, workload overload, missed objectives, declining adoption, capacity shortage, customer satisfaction trends, and training completion — each with confidence and risk levels.

## Are prediction reviews audited?

Yes. Generation, dismissal, and export events are recorded for accountability.
