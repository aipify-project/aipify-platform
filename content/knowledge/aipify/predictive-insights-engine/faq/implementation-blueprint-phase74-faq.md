# Implementation Blueprint Phase 74 — Predictive Operations Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 74 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 74 aligns the Predictive Insights Engine (Phase A.66) with ABOS Predictive Operations requirements — operational trend awareness, bottleneck forecasting, resource awareness, scenario observations, companion guidance, limitation principles, and live success criteria scaffolds.

## How is this different from Multi-Agent Collaboration repo Phase 74?

**Multi-Agent Collaboration (Repo Phase 74)** at `/app/agents` is specialist agent registry and orchestrated collaboration. **ABOS Blueprint Phase 74** at `/app/predictive-insights-engine` is **predictive operations preparedness** — operational trends, bottlenecks, and responsible forecasts. Same phase number, different product surface.

## How is this different from Predictive Intelligence Engine PIE Phase 52?

**PIE Phase 52** at `/app/predictions` provides legacy trend rules. **Predictive Insights A.66** (extended by Phase 74) uses confidence scoring, risk levels, and integration with Strategic Intelligence, Executive Insights, Analytics, Health, and OKR engines. Cross-link PIE for historical context; do not duplicate storage.

## How is this different from Future Readiness Blueprint Phase 63?

**Future Readiness Phase 63** at `/app/future-tech` focuses on **reflection and scenario preparedness** — not operational prediction. Phase 74 provides forward-looking operational trend and bottleneck signals with explicit limitation principles (no guarantees, no fear-based copy).

## How is this different from Autonomous Operations Center Phase 79?

**Autonomous Operations Center Phase 79** at `/app/operations` handles autonomous operational execution. Phase 74 **informs and prepares** — predictions never auto-execute; humans dismiss, act, or ignore.

## How is this different from Capacity & Workload and Resource Planning?

**Capacity & Workload A.64** and **Resource Planning A.63** provide capacity profiles and allocation planning. Phase 74 `_popbp_resource_awareness()` cross-links utilization trends and planning opportunities — predictive context only, not duplicate capacity tables.

## How is this different from Cross-Functional Intelligence Phase 70?

**Cross-Functional Intelligence Blueprint Phase 70** on OCF A.32 at `/app/operations-center-foundation-engine` surfaces cross-functional observations and information flow. Phase 74 `_popbp_bottleneck_forecasting()` cross-links dependency context for bottleneck awareness — distinct storage, cross-link only.

## What are the six predictive operations objectives?

- **Operational trend awareness** — pattern recognition from metadata
- **Bottleneck forecasting** — workflow pressure if trends continue
- **Resource planning support** — workload and capacity trends
- **Risk anticipation** — emerging constraints with confidence
- **Capacity observations** — utilization and planning opportunities
- **Preparedness enhancement** — earlier planning and scenarios

## What is operational pattern recognition?

`_popbp_operational_pattern_recognition()` provides companion signals (🦉🌹🔔) for support activity trends, concurrent milestones, and recurring operational strain — encourage preparation, not alarm.

## What are limitation principles?

`_popbp_limitation_principles()` forbids forecasts as guarantees, fear-based communication, deterministic conclusions, and auto-execution. Required: confidence scoring, uncertainty transparency, human review, preparedness framing.

## What is companion guidance?

`_popbp_companion_guidance()` provides examples (🦉🌹🔔) for earlier planning, teams well positioned, and additional preparation — reduce anxiety, not increase it.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Preparation, perspective, confidence, sustainable responses — *"Awareness provides opportunities to prepare thoughtfully."* Cross-link `/app/self-love-engine` (principle only).

## What are the Phase 74 success criteria?

Earlier preparation, operational trend awareness, bottleneck forecasting, resource planning, fewer surprises, strengthened resilience, preparedness confidence, limitation principles, trust connection, integration links, dogfooding, and ABOS principle — computed live via `_popbp_success_criteria()`.

## Where does Unonight fit?

Unonight is the first external pilot for commerce operational preparedness — support backlog trends, adoption forecasts, and human-reviewed dismissal. Aipify Group validates product roadmap, Sales Expert growth, and operational scaling internally first.

## Does Phase 74 add new database tables?

No. Phase 74 extends `get_predictive_insights_engine_dashboard()` and `get_predictive_insights_engine_card()` with `_popbp_*` blueprint metadata and `_popbp_engagement_summary()` only.
