# Organizational Legacy & Succession Intelligence — Phase 152 FAQ

## What is Phase 152?

Legacy & Future Stewardship Phase 152 deepens the **Organizational Memory Engine** at `/app/organizational-memory-engine` with succession intelligence, critical knowledge registry, executive legacy reviews, continuity readiness, and institutional memory library extension.

## Does Phase 152 create a new route?

No. Phase 152 extends the existing A.34 route. All Phase 55 (`_mcebp_*`), Phase 94 (`_omlebp94_*`), and Phase 126 (`_omlebp126_*`) dashboard fields are preserved.

## Does Phase 152 choose successors?

**No.** Phase 152 explicitly does **not** choose successors, rank individuals, or reveal confidential succession information in default UI. Legacy Companion supports preparedness — humans retain executive judgment and governance authority.

## How does Phase 152 relate to Phase 126?

Phase 126 established Enterprise Intelligence memory archives and succession scaffolding. Phase 152 is the Legacy & Future Stewardship layer — documented in `_olsibp152_distinction_note()`. Phase 152 extends Phase 126 heritage library and succession depth.

## How does Phase 152 relate to Future Leaders Phase 151?

Phase 151 at `/app/future-leaders-engine` focuses on leadership development. Phase 152 cross-links development pathways — it does not duplicate Phase 151 RPCs or create ranking systems.

## Does Phase 152 duplicate the Legacy Engine?

No. Legacy stories and milestones live in **Legacy Engine A.86** at `/app/legacy-engine`. Phase 152 stores metadata scaffolds and cross-link counts only — never duplicates `_leg_*` tables or RPCs.

## What does the Legacy Companion do in Phase 152?

The Legacy Companion supports knowledge preservation guidance, succession preparation support, leadership reflection prompts, institutional memory retrieval, continuity planning assistance, and legacy review summaries. It does **not** determine succession outcomes, replace executive judgment, suppress alternative pathways, reveal confidential succession info, or override governance.

## What data does Phase 152 store?

Metadata summaries and aggregate counts only. Succession plan scaffolds without confidential successor names in default dashboards. No raw chat, email, PII, or individual ranking in RPC payloads.

## What are the continuity readiness dimensions?

- Leadership continuity
- Knowledge continuity
- Operational continuity
- Governance continuity
- Growth Partner continuity
- Customer relationship continuity

## Which engines does Phase 152 cross-link?

Future Leaders Phase 151, Organizational Memory Phase 126, Legacy A.86, Global Stewardship Phase 150, Continuity Phase 73, Decision Intelligence Phase 125, Records Retention A.60, and Self Love A.76.

## What thin RPCs are available?

- `record_executive_legacy_review(...)` — schedule executive legacy review metadata
- `register_critical_knowledge_entry(...)` — register critical knowledge registry entry
- `record_continuity_readiness_review(...)` — record continuity readiness dimension review
