# Goals & OKR Engine — FAQ

## What is the Goals & OKR Engine?

It helps organizations define, track, and review Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) with hierarchy alignment, progress tracking, and executive visibility — metadata only, distinct from personal Goals & Dreams.

## How is this different from personal goals?

Personal goals live in the Assistant Goals & Dreams Engine (`/app/assistant/goals`). The Goals & OKR Engine is organizational — company, department, and team objectives with measurable key results.

## Can Aipify activate objectives automatically?

No. Objectives start as drafts and require human approval to activate. Completion also requires explicit approval before outcomes are captured in organizational memory.

## Does OKR progress integrate with tasks?

Yes. When enabled, activating objectives or detecting at-risk key results may generate follow-up tasks via the Unified Task & Follow-Up Engine — always with human oversight.

## Are OKR changes audited?

Yes. Objective creation, activation, progress updates, completion approval, and exports are recorded for accountability.
