# What is ABOS?

**ABOS** stands for **Aipify Business Operating System**.

ABOS is the core philosophy and architecture behind Aipify. It is the operational layer that sits alongside existing business systems and helps organizations work smarter.

Aipify does not replace every system. Aipify connects, understands, and assists across them.

## Six pillars

1. **Knowledge** — documentation, FAQs, organizational memory, learning
2. **Assistance** — admin, support, executive, and workspace assistants
3. **Automation** — workflows, approvals, notifications, routine execution
4. **Intelligence** — insights, trends, recommendations, decision support
5. **Governance** — permissions, audit, compliance, human oversight
6. **Operations** — tasks, collaboration, support queues, workspace management
