# Governance and Approvals

ABOS embeds **human oversight** at every sensitive boundary through the **Action & Approval Engine**.

## Five action tiers

| Tier | Scope | Approval |
|------|-------|----------|
| **1 — Informational** | Q&A, reports, summaries | None |
| **2 — Assisted** | Drafts and suggestions | Review recommended |
| **3 — Approval-based** | Publish, respond, automate | Explicit approval |
| **4 — High-risk** | Financial, access, org-wide changes | Multi-approver |
| **5 — Restricted** | Deletion, legal, regulatory | **AI prohibited** |

## Aipify responsibilities

Every proposed action includes: what, why, expected outcomes, risks, and a clear approval request. All executions are audit-logged.

## Workflows

Organizations configure single approvers, multi-step chains, department and executive approvals, and emergency escalation. Emergency stop pauses pending AI actions instantly.
