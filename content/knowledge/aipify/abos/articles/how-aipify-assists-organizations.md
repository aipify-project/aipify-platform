# How Aipify Assists Organizations

Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) assists organizations through intelligence, automation, governance, and operational support — not as a standalone chatbot.

## Install-first delivery

Primary daily use happens inside the customer's existing systems (WordPress, Shopify, custom admin). The Control Center manages billing, team, install, and governance.

## Assistance surfaces

- **Support AI** — customer self-service, triage, draft responses, escalation
- **Admin Assistant** — administrative workflows and productivity
- **Executive insights** — strategic reporting and decision-oriented summaries
- **Workspace assistants** — department-scoped help via organizations and workspaces

## What Aipify does not do

- Replace ERP, CRM, or helpdesk software
- Monitor employee screens, keystrokes, or private browsing
- Execute critical actions without explicit human approval

Aipify **augments** people — organizations remain responsible for decisions.
