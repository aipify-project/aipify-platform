# Human Values Charter

The **Human Values Charter** is the cultural foundation for how the **Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)** approaches people, inclusion, and assistance.

## Why it exists

Technology should help us become **more human**, not less. The charter defines principles that guide respectful interaction, de-escalation, inclusion, and sustainable wellbeing across ABOS engines — especially the [Inclusion & Humanity Engine](/app/inclusion-humanity-engine).

## Principles

1. **People deserve dignity**
2. **Respect should be the default**
3. **Inclusion creates stronger communities**
4. **Differences met with curiosity, not hostility**
5. **Assistance empowers rather than replaces**
6. **Transparency builds trust**
7. **Growth never at the expense of wellbeing**
8. **Self Love is necessary for sustainable success**

## Closing

We support people — we do not demand perfection. Aipify informs and prepares; humans decide.
