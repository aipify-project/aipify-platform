# Positioning — Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Aipify in one sentence?

Aipify is a **digital coworker** that helps your business stay organized, respond faster, and work smarter.

## Is Aipify just a chatbot?

No. Aipify is a **digital coworker** — an **Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)** that handles support, knowledge, follow-up, operations, deliverables, trust, and clarity. Chat may be one way to interact; the product is operational assistance with human control.

## Will Aipify replace our employees?

No. Aipify **augments** people. It prepares drafts, organizes knowledge, and handles repeatable work within approved boundaries. Your team makes important decisions.

## What does Aipify actually do?

Aipify helps your business stay organized, respond faster, and work smarter through:

- 💬 Customer support assistance
- 📚 Knowledge organization
- ✅ Task follow-up
- ⚙️ Daily operations
- 📄 Business deliverables
- 🔐 Trust and approvals
- 📊 Explainable clarity

## What is the difference between "digital coworker" and ABOS?

- **Digital coworker** — plain-language positioning for websites, intros, and quick explanations
- **Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)** — formal product category for architecture, enterprise, and governance materials

Both describe the same product from different angles.

## How does Aipify work?

Three steps:

1. **Install** — connect to your business systems
2. **Teach** — share knowledge and workflows
3. **Work alongside** — assist inside daily operations with human approval on sensitive work

## What is the website headline?

**Meet Aipify – Your Digital Coworker**

Subheadline: Aipify helps your business stay organized, respond faster, and work smarter — inside the systems your team already uses.

## Is Aipify employee surveillance?

No. Aipify does not monitor screens, keystrokes, private files, or browsing history. It assists within organizational permissions — it does not surveil individuals.

## What is Self Love in positioning?

**Self Love** (no ™) connects sustainable growth to platform wellbeing — Aipify maintains its own operational health so it can better support your business. Calm language; human approval for sensitive recovery.
