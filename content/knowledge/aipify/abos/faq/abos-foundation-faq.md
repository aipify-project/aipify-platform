# ABOS — Knowledge Center FAQ

## What does ABOS stand for?

ABOS stands for **Aipify Business Operating System**.

## Is Aipify just another AI chatbot?

No. Aipify is a Business Operating System designed to assist organizations through intelligence, automation, governance, and operational support.

## What problems does ABOS solve?

ABOS helps organizations organize knowledge, improve support, automate repetitive work, increase operational visibility, and make better decisions.

## Does Aipify replace existing systems?

No. Aipify is designed to integrate with existing systems and enhance them through assistance and intelligence.

## What is Self Love in Aipify?

Self Love is Aipify's approach to self-awareness and self-maintenance. It enables Aipify to monitor its own health, recover safely from issues, and continuously improve. Planned as Phase A.76 within the ABOS assistance pillar.

## Can Self Love make changes without permission?

No. Self Love may recommend actions and perform approved safe maintenance tasks, but irreversible actions always require explicit authorization.

## Who is ABOS designed for?

ABOS is designed for organizations of all sizes, from small businesses to large enterprises.

## Can ABOS support multiple teams?

Yes. Through organizations and workspaces, ABOS can support departments, teams, and different operational units.

## What makes Aipify different?

Aipify combines knowledge, assistance, automation, governance, and intelligence within one unified platform.

## What is the vision of ABOS?

The vision is to become the trusted operational companion that helps organizations work smarter, respond faster, and grow responsibly.
