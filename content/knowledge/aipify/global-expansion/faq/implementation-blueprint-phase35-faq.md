# Localization & Global Expansion Engine (ABOS Phase 35) — FAQ

## What is Blueprint Phase 35?

Blueprint Phase 35 is the **Localization & Global Expansion Engine** — ABOS alignment metadata extending the Global Expansion & Localization Framework (Phase 95) at `/app/global-expansion`.

## Is Blueprint Phase 35 the same as Context Engine Phase 35?

No. **Context Engine Phase 35** covers personal calendar orchestration at `/app/assistant/context`. **Blueprint Phase 35** covers localization and global expansion. They share a phase number in different numbering systems — see `localization_distinction_note` in the dashboard RPC.

## What languages does Aipify prioritize?

English, Norwegian, Swedish, and Danish ship first. German, French, Spanish, Dutch, and Portuguese follow validated market demand.

## Is localization the same as translation?

No. Localization includes language, regional payment preferences, country playbooks, cultural adaptation, companion personality, and operational guidance — not word-for-word conversion alone.

## How does companion localization work?

Aipify preserves companion personality (🌹 warm, 🦉 wise, 🔔 encouraging, ❤️ compassionate) across locales. Tone and phrasing adapt per language — personality is not merely translated.

## Does Aipify support Nordic payment methods?

The platform scaffolds metadata for Norway (Fiken, Vipps), Sweden (Swish), and Denmark (MobilePay), plus international Stripe expansion. Customers configure actual integrations in Billing & Commercial — Aipify does not provide legal or tax advice.

## How does Sales Expert localization relate?

Sales Expert OS (A.95) cross-links localized portal surfaces, currencies, and market-sensitive guidance. Blueprint Phase 35 documents the alignment — it does not duplicate the Sales Expert engine.

## How do training and certification localize?

Learning & Training (A.36) and Certification (A.37) cross-link localized examples and region-specific scenario scaffolds. Academy (Phase 94) localization projects are tracked in Phase 95 translation projects.

## What is dogfooding for localization?

Aipify Group validates Nordic language quality internally. Unonight is the first external pilot for multilingual customer operations.
