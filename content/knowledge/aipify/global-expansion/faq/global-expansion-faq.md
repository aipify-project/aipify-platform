# Global Expansion & Localization Framework — FAQ

## What is localization?

Localization is the process of adapting experiences, communications and content to align with regional expectations and cultural contexts.

## Is localization the same as translation?

No. Translation focuses on language conversion, while localization addresses broader cultural, operational and regional considerations.

## Can Aipify support multiple languages?

Yes. The platform is designed to support multilingual experiences across user interfaces, communications and knowledge resources.

## Can organizations choose their preferred language?

Yes. Users and organizations may configure language preferences appropriate for their environments.

## Why is localization important?

People engage more effectively with experiences that reflect their language, expectations and context. Localization improves trust and adoption.

## Can Aipify support international growth?

Yes. The framework is specifically designed to help organizations expand into new regions successfully.

## Does Aipify provide legal compliance advice?

No. Aipify supports compliance readiness but does not replace professional legal counsel.

## Can notifications and emails be localized?

Yes. Organizations may deliver communications in appropriate languages and regional formats.

## How does Aipify handle time zones?

The platform is designed to recognize user locations and present information in relevant time contexts.

## Will localization evolve over time?

Yes. Aipify shall continuously expand its localization capabilities as new markets are supported.
