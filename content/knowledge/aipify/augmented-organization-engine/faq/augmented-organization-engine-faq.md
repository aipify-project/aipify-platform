# Augmented Organization Engine — FAQ

## What is the Augmented Organization Center?

The Augmented Organization Center at `/app/augmented-organization-engine` is the **Autonomous Organization Era (131–140) capstone**. It unifies human-companion symbiosis visibility — partnership, not replacement — across all era phases without duplicating their RPCs.

## How is Phase 140 different from Phase 120 Ecosystem Orchestration?

Phase 120 (`/app/ecosystem-orchestration`) is the **Ecosystem & Marketplace Era (111–120) capstone** — collective ecosystem evolution. Phase 140 is the **Autonomous Organization Era capstone** — human-companion symbiosis inside the organization. Different eras, different centers; cross-link only.

## What does symbiosis mean in Aipify?

Symbiosis means **partnership, not replacement**. Humans contribute judgment, empathy, creativity, purpose, leadership, ethics, and relationships. Companions contribute knowledge, patterns, operational support, coordination, preparation, administrative relief, and reflection. Humans lead; Companions support.

## What is the maturity model?

Five levels — **maturity not speed**:

1. **Awareness** — understanding partnership potential  
2. **Adoption** — companions integrated with human guidance  
3. **Coordination** — coordinated companion workforce with oversight  
4. **Governance** — mature governance and trust frameworks  
5. **Symbiosis** — full partnership with strengthened human agency  

New tenants start at Level 1 by default.

## Does Phase 140 monitor employees?

**No.** Phase 140 stores **aggregate metadata only** — symbiosis assessments, trust signals, and agency checkpoints. No employee surveillance, no ranking people by symbiosis score.

## What is confidence not dependence?

Companions should strengthen human capability and confidence — never create unhealthy reliance. The augmented experience engine and agency protection framework enforce freedom of choice, appeal processes, and dependence prevention checkpoints.

## How does Phase 140 relate to Relationship Intelligence (RSI A.78)?

Phase 140 **cross-links** RSI at `/app/relationship-intelligence-engine` for relationship domains (employees, executives, Growth Partners, customers, communities, companions). It does **not** duplicate RSI RPCs.

## What permissions are required?

- `augmented_organization.view` — view the Augmented Organization Center  
- `augmented_organization.manage` — update symbiosis settings and governance metadata  

Owners and administrators receive both by default.

## What is Growth Partner terminology?

Aipify uses **Growth Partner** — never "Affiliate" — in all symbiosis and era documentation.
