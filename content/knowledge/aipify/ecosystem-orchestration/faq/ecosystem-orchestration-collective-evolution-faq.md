# Ecosystem Orchestration & Collective Evolution — FAQ

## What is Phase 120?

The **Ecosystem Orchestration & Collective Evolution Engine** is the capstone of the Ecosystem & Marketplace Era (Phases 111–120). It provides an Ecosystem Orchestration Center with aggregate visibility across trust, knowledge flow, resilience, opportunities, and ecosystem memory — without acting as a central authority.

## How is this different from `/app/orchestration`?

**Workflow Orchestration (Phase 68)** coordinates cross-module events and flows inside your tenant. **Ecosystem Orchestration (Phase 120)** focuses on collective ecosystem evolution across the era 111–120 surfaces — health, knowledge circulation, GP trends, community participation, and stewardship scaffolding.

## How is this different from `/app/ecosystem`?

**Ecosystem Intelligence (Phase 88)** maps external relationships and partnership health. Phase 120 aggregates era-wide evolution signals and cross-links era phases — it does not duplicate Phase 88 RPCs.

## Does Aipify rank people or teams?

No. Phase 120 stores **metadata and anonymized aggregates only**. There is no surveillance framing and no ranking of individuals. Humans guide priorities; Companions may summarize patterns and suggest resources.

## What is the Ecosystem Stewardship Council?

An **optional governance scaffold** documenting six participant types (customers, GPs, executives, knowledge leaders, community reps, governance advisors) and five responsibilities (feedback, risk identification, theme review, inclusivity, values protection). Implementation is human-led.

## Where does ecosystem memory live?

Phase 120 captures milestone and lesson **metadata** in `ecosystem_orchestration_memory_entries`. **Organizational Memory (A.34)** at `/app/organizational-memory-engine` remains authoritative — Phase 120 cross-links only.

## What permissions are required?

- `ecosystem_orchestration.view` — view the orchestration center
- `ecosystem_orchestration.manage` — update settings and stewardship metadata (owners, administrators, managers)
