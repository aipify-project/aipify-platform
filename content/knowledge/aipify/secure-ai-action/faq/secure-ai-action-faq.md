# Secure AI Action Engine — FAQ

## What is an AI action?

An AI action is an operational task Aipify recommends or performs within approved security boundaries.

## Can Aipify take actions automatically?

Only approved low-risk actions may execute automatically.

## Why are approvals necessary?

Approvals ensure humans remain in control of sensitive business operations.

## Are AI actions logged?

Yes. All important AI actions are recorded in audit logs.

## Can AI change permissions on its own?

No. Permission changes are considered high-risk actions requiring approval.

## What happens if an AI action fails?

Failures are logged and surfaced for review by administrators.
