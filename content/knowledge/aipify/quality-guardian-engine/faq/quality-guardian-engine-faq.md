title: How are quality alerts classified?
slug: quality-alert-severity
category: quality-guardian-engine
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [quality, severity, alerts]
keywords: [severity, low, medium, high, critical]
article_type: faq

---
Alerts are classified as low, medium, high, or critical based on business impact and signal count. Critical and high alerts should be reviewed first. Severity guides prioritization — it does not bypass human approval for operational changes.
