# Cross-Sector Intelligence Engine — Phase 162 FAQ

## What is the Societal Resilience Center?

The Societal Resilience Center at `/app/cross-sector-intelligence-engine` is the **Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170)** cross-sector preparedness and collective learning surface. It scaffolds shared readiness across private organizations, Growth Partners, educational institutions, professional associations, community organizations, knowledge networks, and public institutions.

## How is Phase 162 different from Civic Collaboration (Phase 161)?

Civic Collaboration (`/app/civic-collaboration-engine`) focuses on **civic engagement and public value contribution** via `_ccvebp161_*` RPCs. Phase 162 is **cross-sector societal resilience** — preparedness reflection, learning programs, and mutual support networks. Cross-link only — **do not duplicate** `_ccvebp161_*`.

## How is Phase 162 different from Organizational Resilience (Phase 154)?

Organizational Resilience (`/app/organizational-resilience-engine`) provides **org-level adaptive continuity** via `_oracbp154_*`. Phase 162 operates at **cross-sector societal preparedness** — collective learning across sectors. Cross-link only.

## Does the Resilience Companion predict crises?

**No.** The Resilience Companion supports readiness reflection, knowledge discovery, and leadership briefings. It does **not** predict certainty, replace emergency planning, override leadership, suppress uncertainty, or determine societal priorities.

## Is participation mandatory?

**No.** New organizations default to **opt-out** (`enabled = false`). Organizations must explicitly enable participation before contributing to networks or recording preparedness reflections.

## What are the permissions?

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `cross_sector_intelligence.view` | View societal resilience center and preparedness metadata |
| `cross_sector_intelligence.manage` | Update participation settings and review programs |
| `cross_sector_intelligence.contribute` | Register network participation and record reflections |

## Does Phase 162 create centralized societal control?

**No.** Phase 162 scaffolds **voluntary, collaborative resilience** — shared preparedness and collective learning. It does not create dependency, centralized command, or crisis prediction authority.
