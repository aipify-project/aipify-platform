# Evolution Governance & Change Management — FAQ

## How does Aipify improve itself?

Aipify continuously identifies opportunities for improvement through learning, feedback, and operational insights. The Evolution Governance Engine structures these into proposals for human review.

## Can Aipify change systems automatically?

Only within explicitly approved boundaries. Most changes require human review. Aipify never modifies systems autonomously or bypasses Governance.

## What is Evolution Governance?

Evolution Governance ensures improvements occur through controlled, transparent processes with risk classification and an approval matrix.

## What is Evolution Board?

Evolution Board is the central interface at `/app/evolution` for reviewing, approving, and tracking proposed organizational improvements.

## How are risks evaluated?

Changes are classified as low, medium, high, or critical risk. Each level maps to reviewer, governance, or executive approval requirements.

## Can we disable self-improvement suggestions?

Yes. Organizations may configure evolution settings to disable suggestions or control low-risk auto-publication.

## Can proposals be rejected?

Yes. Human decision-makers retain final authority over all proposals.

## Are rejected proposals deleted?

No. Rejected proposals remain available for audit and learning purposes.

## What happens if an implemented change causes problems?

Rollback guidance and change history support safe recovery procedures. Use the Rollback action on the Evolution Board.

## Does Evolution Governance replace human leadership?

No. Humans remain responsible for all significant organizational decisions. Aipify proposes evolution; humans approve evolution.
