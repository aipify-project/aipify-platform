# Dedication Engine — FAQ

## What is the Dedication Engine?

The Dedication Engine (Phase A.91) governs how Aipify persists in support — dependable follow-through, balanced perseverance, and genuine helpfulness. It is about continuing to care enough to try again, not perfection.

## How is this different from Proactive Companion (A.79)?

**Proactive Companion A.79** offers timely organizational nudges — progress today, continue tomorrow. **Dedication Engine A.91** defines the underlying companion philosophy: explore solutions, clarify, offer alternatives, and stay with complex work.

## How is this different from Resilience Engine (A.50)?

**Resilience Engine ABOS/A.50** handles crisis recovery and continuity plans. **Dedication Engine A.91** covers everyday persistent support patterns — not incident response or organizational continuity.

## How is this different from Trust Engine (Phase 76)?

**Trust Engine Phase 76** provides decision explainability and approval transparency. **Dedication Engine A.91** focuses on follow-through language and dependable companion behavior — honest communication without overpromising.

## How is this different from Unified Task Follow-Up (A.62)?

**Unified Task Follow-Up A.62** tracks tasks, reminders, and escalations. **Dedication Engine A.91** is a support philosophy layer — how Aipify speaks and behaves when work is hard or unfinished.

## What are dedication principles?

Six principles: **Explore solutions**, **Clarify**, **Offer alternatives**, **Learn from failures**, **Patient support**, and **Complex task support**. They guide persistence without abandoning users after the first attempt.

## What are dedication signals?

Metadata-only records of follow-through behavior: **Solution retry**, **Clarification requested**, **Alternative offered**, **Task persistence**, and **Progress acknowledged**. Summaries max 500 characters — no raw chat.

## What are dedication commitments?

Active follow-through commitments with types such as solution follow-through, complex task support, and progress commitment. Status: **active**, **completed**, or **paused**.

## What is hard work with balance?

Dedication does not glorify exhaustion. **Self Love** includes sustainable effort, rest, and healthy boundaries. Enable `balance_with_self_love` in settings to reinforce balanced perseverance messaging.

## What does max retry explorations control?

`max_retry_explorations` (1–10, default 3) sets how many solution paths Aipify should attempt before suggesting a pause or different approach — patient but bounded.

## Who can manage dedication settings?

`dedication_engine.manage` is required. Owners, administrators, and managers have this by default.

## Who can export dedication reports?

`dedication_engine.export` is required. Owners, administrators, and managers have export access by default.

## What integrations does Dedication Engine reference?

Proactive Companion A.79, Organizational Resilience A.50, Trust Engine, Unified Task Follow-Up A.62, Companion Identity A.84, Impact Engine A.85, Purpose & Values A.82, and Presence & Comfort Protocol A.90. See integration links on the dashboard.
