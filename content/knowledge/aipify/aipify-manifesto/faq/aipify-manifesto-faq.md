# Aipify Manifesto & Founding Vision — FAQ

## What is the AIPIFY Manifesto?

The Manifesto describes the purpose, aspirations and long-term vision that guide AIPIFY's evolution.

## How is the Manifesto different from the Constitution?

The Constitution defines principles and expectations. The Manifesto explains why AIPIFY exists and what future it seeks to help create.

## Why does AIPIFY have a Manifesto?

A clear vision helps organizations remain focused during periods of growth, change and uncertainty.

## Does the Manifesto influence product decisions?

Yes. The Manifesto provides strategic direction and helps ensure that development remains aligned with AIPIFY's purpose.

## Does AIPIFY seek to replace people?

No. AIPIFY exists to support people and strengthen their ability to perform meaningful work.

## Why does AIPIFY emphasize trust?

Trust forms the foundation of sustainable relationships between technology and people.

## Can the Manifesto evolve?

Yes. The expression of the vision may mature over time, while preserving the spirit of AIPIFY's founding beliefs.

## Who should read the Manifesto?

Customers, employees, partners and leaders may benefit from understanding the values and aspirations that shape AIPIFY.

## Why is human potential emphasized?

Technology should create opportunities for people to focus on creativity, relationships and strategic work.

## What is AIPIFY ultimately trying to achieve?

To build trusted AI systems that simplify complexity, empower people and contribute positively to the future of work.
