# Global Knowledge Exchange Engine — FAQ

## What is the Global Knowledge Exchange?

The Global Knowledge Exchange at `/app/global-knowledge-exchange-engine` is the **Global Intelligence & Interorganizational Era (141–150) opener**. It enables voluntary, consensual, governed knowledge exchange across participating organizations — wisdom before speed, anonymization mandatory.

## How is Phase 141 different from Cross-Tenant Intelligence (A.71)?

Cross-Tenant Intelligence (`/app/cross-tenant-intelligence-engine`) provides **anonymized industry and operational trends** via `_ctie_*` RPCs. Phase 141 is a **governed knowledge exchange** for approved shareable artifacts, exchange programs, and interorganizational learning — cross-link only, **do not duplicate** `_ctie_*`.

## Is participation mandatory?

**No.** New tenants default to **opt-out** (`enabled = false`). Organizations must explicitly enable participation and configure sharing boundaries before contributing or viewing shared benchmarks.

## What may be shared?

Approved **metadata summaries** only: lessons learned, best practices, transformation experiences, support excellence patterns, companion adoption insights, governance frameworks — max 500 characters per summary, anonymized by default.

## What must never be shared?

PII, raw emails, chats, orders, financial records, proprietary secrets, or any identifiable cross-tenant operational data.

## What are the permissions?

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `global_knowledge_exchange.view` | View exchange center and anonymized benchmarks (when opted in) |
| `global_knowledge_exchange.manage` | Update settings and review contributions |
| `global_knowledge_exchange.contribute` | Submit knowledge artifacts for governance review |

## Does the Companion reveal confidential information from other tenants?

**No.** The Collective Wisdom Companion guides discovery and benchmark interpretation — it never reveals confidential information or identities of other organizations.
