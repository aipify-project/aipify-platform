**No.** Phase 159 extends the existing Digital Twin at `/app/digital-twin`. Repo Phase 77 = engine baseline; Blueprint Phase 77 = ABOS scaffolding; Phase 124 = Enterprise Intelligence layer; Phase 159 = Legacy & Future Stewardship systemic awareness layer.
