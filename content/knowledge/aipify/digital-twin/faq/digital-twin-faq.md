A model representing how responsibilities and processes operate within your organization. The Twin answers who owns a process, who approves an action, who should be notified, and who has relevant expertise.

---
title: Does Aipify monitor employees?
slug: does-aipify-monitor-employees
category: digital-twin
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [digital-twin, privacy, faq]
article_type: faq
priority: 9
---
No. The Twin models responsibilities and workflows — not individual performance. It must never be used for employee surveillance, micromanagement, performance scoring, hidden monitoring, behavioral ranking, or secret profiling.

---
title: What is Twin Confidence?
slug: what-is-twin-confidence
category: digital-twin
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [digital-twin, confidence, faq]
article_type: faq
---
A measure of how certain Aipify is about a specific organizational relationship. High (90–100%), Medium (70–89%), Low (below 70% — requires human validation). Example: Support Lead at 96%, Quality Owner at 42% with review recommended.

---

category: digital-twin
language: en
visibility: authenticated

title: What is Twin Health Score?
slug: what-is-twin-health-score
category: digital-twin
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [digital-twin, analytics, faq]
article_type: faq
---
Twin Health Score (0–100) measures process coverage, ownership completeness, escalation clarity, confidence levels, and knowledge ownership health. Low-confidence owners and bottlenecks reduce the score until reviewed.
