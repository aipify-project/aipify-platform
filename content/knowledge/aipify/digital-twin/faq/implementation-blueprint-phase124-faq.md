# Implementation Blueprint Phase 124 — Organizational Digital Twin Engine FAQ

## What is Enterprise Intelligence Phase 124?

Phase 124 deepens the Organizational Digital Twin at `/app/digital-twin` with Enterprise Intelligence era scaffolding — system relationships, dependency intelligence, simulation workspaces, transformation impact, knowledge networks, resilience visualization, and Executive Digital Twin Companion.

## Does Phase 124 create a new route?

**No.** Phase 124 extends the existing Digital Twin at `/app/digital-twin`. Repo Phase 77 = engine baseline; Blueprint Phase 77 = ABOS scaffolding; Phase 124 = Enterprise Intelligence layer.

## How is Phase 124 different from Blueprint Phase 77?

**Phase 77** established responsibility-centric organizational model, companion observations, simulation connection, and privacy principles via `_odtbp_*` helpers. **Phase 124** adds Enterprise Intelligence depth via `_odtbp124_*` — Digital Twin Center, dependency intelligence, transformation impact, Executive Digital Twin Companion, and twin memory engine.

## What helper prefixes are used?

Engine: `_dtw_*`. Blueprint Phase 77: `_odtbp_*`. Enterprise Phase 124: `_odtbp124_*` only — never mix prefixes.

## What are the eight Phase 124 objectives?

- System relationships
- Visualize dynamics
- Explore consequences (simulation — not certainty)
- Decision quality
- Resilience planning
- Reduce unintended outcomes
- Organizational learning
- Institutional understanding

## What does the Twin reflect?

People (responsibilities), departments, processes, companions, knowledge, governance, customers, Growth Partners, communities, and technology — metadata only, evolving continuously.

## What is dependency intelligence?

Single points of failure, knowledge bottlenecks, leadership concentration, companion reliance, governance dependencies, and operational constraints — **system signals only**, never individual scoring.

## How does simulation work?

Seven scenario types (growth, restructuring, knowledge transfer, companion expansion, governance changes, customer growth, workforce transition) cross-link `/app/simulations`. Simulations explore possibilities — **never modify production data or claim certainty**.

## What are companion limitations?

No predictive certainty, no context-free recommendations, no replacing judgment, no ignoring human considerations, no concealed uncertainty.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Human capacity, psychological safety, change fatigue, learning requirements, support structures, sustainable pacing — cross-link `/app/self-love-engine` (principle only).

## What is the Twin memory engine?

Captures transformation milestones, structural changes, lessons learned, governance adaptations, knowledge growth, and companion expansion histories — cross-link Organizational Memory A.34 at `/app/organizational-memory-engine`.

## What are mandatory cross-links?

Executive Intelligence Phase 121, Strategic Foresight Phase 122, Board Governance Phase 123, Simulation Lab Phase 78, Org Memory A.34, Ecosystem 88/120, Self Love A.76.

## Does Phase 124 add new database tables?

No. Phase 124 extends `get_digital_twin_dashboard()` and `get_digital_twin_card()` with `_odtbp124_*` blueprint metadata — **all Phase 77 fields preserved**.

## What are privacy principles?

**NO** employee surveillance, individual scoring, predictive certainty presented as fact, or punitive dependency interpretations. Twin models **responsibilities NOT people**.
