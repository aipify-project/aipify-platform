# Enterprise Pricing Philosophy — FAQ

## Why does Aipify avoid token-based pricing?

Aipify is a **Business Operating System (ABOS)** — not a consumer chatbot. Token or per-message pricing treats intelligence as a commodity meter. Aipify pricing reflects **operational scope**: installs, licensed modules, governance depth, and business outcomes such as support resolution and knowledge retention. Recommended plan ranges in Settings → Billing are **sales guidance** — your live subscription follows your contracted plan, not a hidden token counter.

## How is Enterprise priced?

**Enterprise subscription** is custom — typically from **$1,500+/month** in USD guidance, scoped to suites, users, installs, and governance needs. **Enterprise implementation** is separate: discovery, integration, governance design, rollout, and training commonly range **NOK 100,000–500,000+**, depending on complexity. Implementation is often delivered by a **Sales Expert** or Aipify success team alongside the subscription.

## What do Sales Experts charge?

Sales Experts are **independent businesses**. They bill customers directly for consulting and implementation — not through the Aipify subscription. Typical NOK guidance: discovery **NOK 2,500–5,000**, SMB implementation **NOK 50,000–150,000**, enterprise rollout **NOK 150,000–500,000+**, training workshops **NOK 15,000–30,000**, and advisory retainers **NOK 5,000–15,000/month**. The Aipify subscription remains **Customer ↔ Aipify**; services remain **Customer ↔ Sales Expert**.

## Is Aipify a chatbot?

**No.** Aipify is the **Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS)** — an install-first operations platform with companions such as **Aipify Support**, **Meeting Companion**, and **Onboarding Companion**. It works inside your existing systems, respects human approval for sensitive actions, and prices on operational value — not chat volume. Comparing Aipify to a generic chatbot subscription misses the product: governance, audit, modular suites, and long-term operational partnership.
