# Decision Intelligence & Executive Advisory — FAQ

## What is the Decision Intelligence Center?

The Decision Intelligence Center at `/app/decision-intelligence-engine` helps leaders think clearly about important decisions. It provides structure, perspective, and wisdom — **not** decisions on your behalf.

## How is this different from personal Decision Support (DSE)?

Personal DSE at `/app/assistant/decisions` (Phase 38/60) supports individual decision guidance. Phase 125 is an **executive advisory** surface for leadership decision workspaces, journals, assumption reviews, and outcome learning. Cross-link only — do not duplicate DSE RPCs.

## How is this different from Organizational Decision Support (ODSE)?

ODSE A.54 at `/app/organizational-decision-support-engine` manages the organizational decision register and approvals. Phase 125 focuses on **advisory scaffolds** for executive thinking. Cross-link only — do not duplicate ODSE RPCs.

## Does Aipify decide for leaders?

No. The Executive Advisory Companion expands thinking with thoughtful questions, blind spots, trade-offs, and historical context. **Humans decide.** Companion limitations prohibit executive decisions, guaranteed outcomes, suppressing disagreement, certainty framing, and overriding judgment.

## What data is stored in decision journals?

Metadata summaries only — dates, role labels for participants, assumptions, alternatives, expected outcomes, rationales, and follow-up actions. **No raw meeting transcripts, email content, chat, or PII.**

## What are decision workspaces?

Ten structured fields: decision statement, objectives, constraints, alternatives, dependencies, stakeholders, benefits, risks, unknowns, and supporting knowledge. Workspaces organize thinking — they do not produce binding recommendations.

## How does outcome learning work?

Five reflection questions: what worked, surprises, incorrect assumptions, what to repeat, and what to change. Learning without blame — cross-link Organizational Memory A.34 for institutional wisdom.
