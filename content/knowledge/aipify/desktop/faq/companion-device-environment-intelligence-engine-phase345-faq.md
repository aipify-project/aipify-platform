# Device & Environment Intelligence — FAQ

## What is Device & Environment Intelligence?

Device & Environment Intelligence helps Aipify understand local workspace conditions such as storage, project location, network status and development readiness.

## Does Aipify change my computer automatically?

No. This phase only observes, explains and recommends. Any system-changing action requires explicit approval.

## Why does project location matter?

Development projects can become slower or unstable when stored inside cloud-synced folders such as iCloud Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive.
