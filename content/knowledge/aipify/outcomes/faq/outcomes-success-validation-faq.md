# Outcomes, ROI & Success Validation — FAQ

## How does Aipify measure value?

Aipify compares expected outcomes against actual results using predefined validation metrics from the Value Engine, evolution initiatives, and strategic recommendations.

## What is ROI Validation?

ROI Validation evaluates whether initiatives generated the expected return on investment by comparing estimated ROI against actual ROI and documenting variance transparently.

## What happens if an initiative fails?

Aipify captures what happened, why it happened, what was learned, and what should change. Failed initiatives are never hidden.

## Can organizations define their own KPIs?

Yes. Organizations may configure custom KPIs that reflect their goals and priorities through the KPI framework.

## What is Success Validation?

Success Validation determines whether initiatives achieved their intended outcomes using hypothesis templates, measurement windows, and human review.

## Are failed initiatives hidden?

No. Transparency is essential for organizational learning. Failed initiatives appear on the Outcomes dashboard.

## How often are outcomes reviewed?

Reviews may occur at 7-day (immediate), 30-day (short-term), 90-day (medium-term), and 365-day (long-term) intervals.

## Can validated outcomes influence future recommendations?

Yes. Strategic Intelligence and the Learning Engine use validated outcomes and lessons learned to improve future recommendations.

## How is ROI calculated?

ROI calculations include time savings from value events, cost reductions, and productivity improvements — always with documented evidence and limitations.

## Does Aipify guarantee success?

No. Aipify provides evidence-based validation to support better decision-making. Humans interpret what value means.
