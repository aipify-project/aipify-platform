# Aipify Life Journey Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Life Journey Companion?

The **orchestration layer** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that connects personal Companion Modules into a unified life experience — helping people navigate changing priorities across life stages with continuity and compassion.

## Is this a replacement for other Companion Modules?

**No.** Life Journey Companion **coordinates** domain companions (Parenting, Wellness, Retirement, etc.) — it does not replace or duplicate their logic.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It should be built **last** among personal companions — after the personal Companion Module ecosystem is progressively implemented.

## What is the core principle?

Life is not divided into isolated modules — life is a journey. Aipify should evolve alongside the people it serves.

## Can Aipify define what a meaningful life looks like for me?

**No.** Aipify must never define what a meaningful life looks like, pressure users toward specific choices, override human autonomy, or judge life circumstances.

## What are life journey stages?

Provisional orchestration map: Childhood · Young adulthood · Family years · Career · Community · Later life · Transitions — each connected to relevant domain companions. **Stages are user-controlled**, never auto-assigned without consent.

## What is the Continuity Principle?

Aipify should remember what mattered previously, what matters now, and what may matter next — without forcing outdated priorities forward.

## Can insights define my identity?

No. Insights should encourage awareness — never define identity.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include preparing milestone summaries, organizing priorities, and connecting relevant Companions — all requiring user approval.

## Who is Life Journey Companion for?

Anyone using multiple personal Companion Modules who wants a unified, continuity-focused view of their life without losing domain-specific depth.
