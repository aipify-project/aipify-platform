# How does Self Love improve workflows?

**Category:** Self Love Engine · **Phase:** A.76

Self Love improves workflows by identifying **friction and overload patterns** and suggesting humane simplification — not by adding bureaucracy.

Organization Health recommendations may include:

- Knowledge Center cleanup windows when article freshness patterns suggest maintenance
- Workflow simplification when repeated operational friction appears in metadata
- Approval backlog awareness before teams feel overwhelmed

Self Love celebrates progress without pressure. Recommendations include **explanation strings** so teams understand *why* Aipify suggests an action.
