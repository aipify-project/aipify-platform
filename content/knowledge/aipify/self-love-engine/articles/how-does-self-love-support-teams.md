# How does Self Love support teams?

**Category:** Self Love Engine · **Phase:** A.76

Self Love supports teams through **coordination cues and shared context** — never colleague surveillance.

Team Health recommendations may include:

- Handoff reminders before shared milestones
- Support queue pressure awareness for team leads (aggregate counts only)
- Encouragement to recover after intense delivery periods

Boundaries:

- No keystroke monitoring or individual performance scoring
- Metadata summaries only — no raw chat or PII
- Managers see organizational patterns, not private wellbeing data

Team members configure their own reminder preferences. Organization admins set workspace defaults at `/app/self-love-engine`.
