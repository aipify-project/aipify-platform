# Enterprise Ecosystem, Partner Network & Global Business Platform Engine — FAQ

## What is the Aipify Ecosystem?

The Aipify Ecosystem at `/app/ecosystem` is the global business platform where organizations, partners, Growth Partners, developers, consultants, service providers, and industry specialists collaborate. A platform creates value; an ecosystem multiplies value through partner networks, marketplace participation, and governed certifications.

## How do Partner Programs work?

Partner Programs support technology, integration, consulting, implementation, strategic, regional, and industry partners. Each partner profile tracks company, services, regions, industries, certifications, ratings, partnership status, and performance. Partners require verification before active participation.

## How do Growth Partners participate?

Growth Partners integrate with the Growth Partner Portal at `/app/growth-partner/resource-center`. They access certifications, training, commission tracking, payouts, sales activity, and partner success metrics. Certification levels range from Certified through Master Partner.

## How are certifications earned?

The Partner Certification Framework supports Certified, Advanced, Professional, Expert, Enterprise, Industry Specialist, and Master Partner tiers. Certifications require governance review and are logged in the ecosystem audit trail. Administrators with manage permissions can grant or revoke certifications.

## How does the Marketplace work?

The Ecosystem Marketplace provides Business Packs, Industry Packs, integrations, Skills, templates, workflows, and partner services. Listings require marketplace governance approval. Developer ecosystem assets include extensions, Skills, apps, API integrations, and custom modules.

## How are partner payouts managed?

Commission and payout events are governed and audited. Growth Partner commission rates are tracked per partner. Payout governance ensures transparent revenue sharing — all commission payments are logged with actor, context, and timestamp in the ecosystem audit trail.

## How is this different from Ecosystem Intelligence?

**Ecosystem Intelligence** (`/app/ecosystem-intelligence`) focuses on external relationship intelligence and partnership health signals (Phase 88). **Ecosystem Center** (`/app/ecosystem`) is the enterprise partner network operating system — partner programs, Growth Partners, marketplace, certifications, and global expansion.
