# Customer Experience, Adoption & Delight Engine — FAQ

## How do I get started?

Open the Customer Experience Center at `/app/platform/customer-experience`. Aipify tracks setup progress, first impressions, and recommended quick wins so your team succeeds without learning software mechanics first. For guided installation, visit `/app/install`. For onboarding workflows, see `/app/customer-onboarding-engine`.

## How does onboarding work?

Onboarding replaces generic checklists with guided setup, Companion guidance, progress tracking, recommended actions, and milestone confirmation. Steps are tenant-scoped — your organization sees only its own progress. Owners and admins with manage permissions can advance onboarding steps from the Experience Center.

## How does Companion help me?

Companion presence is designed to feel helpful, not intrusive. Micro-moments include welcome-back guidance, improvement identification, task completion recognition, and attention signals when something needs review. Companion does not replace human decisions — it prepares context and recommends next steps.

## How do milestones work?

Success moments celebrate first login, first integration, first workflow, first customer, revenue events, automations, and organizational milestones. Milestones can be recorded manually by administrators or surfaced automatically as adoption signals improve. Celebrations respect enterprise tone — recognition without noise.

## How is progress tracked?

Getting Started progress covers setup, organization, integration, Companion, knowledge, and Business Pack adoption with an overall success score. Experience analytics track onboarding completion, feature adoption, Companion engagement, retention, and customer health — all metadata, never raw operational content.

## How can I improve adoption?

Review adoption metrics and Experience Intelligence signals in the Customer Experience Center. Undiscovered features, underutilized workflows, and knowledge gaps are surfaced with recommendations. The Companion Experience Advisor suggests onboarding completion, feature exploration, and celebration moments. For deeper success operations, open `/app/customer-success-engine`.

## How is this different from Customer Onboarding Engine?

**Customer Onboarding Engine** (`/app/customer-onboarding-engine`) focuses on structured onboarding workflows. **Customer Experience Center** (`/app/platform/customer-experience`) is the unified experience layer — first impressions, delight, retention, Companion presence, and executive experience analytics.
