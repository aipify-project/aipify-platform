# Aipify Match Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## Is Aipify Match a dating app?

No. Aipify Match Companion is a **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS). It helps people navigate dating more intentionally — it is not a standalone dating platform.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Aipify Verify, and the Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Better matches, not more matches.

## How does Aipify Verify relate?

Verify is a **prerequisite**: identity verification, adult verification, anti-catfish protection, and trust indicators must be operational before Match ships.

## What data does Match store?

When implemented, Match will store **compatibility metadata and dimension summaries only** — not raw dating messages or sensitive intimate content. Users control what is shared.

## How is this different from Relationship Intelligence (RSI)?

RSI strengthens **existing** relationships with thoughtful suggestions. Match supports **intentional new connections** through compatibility insights — both preserve human agency and never impersonate users.

## Can Aipify decide who I should date?

No. Aipify surfaces compatibility indicators and encourages understanding. People build relationships; Aipify supports — never decides, manipulates, or pressures.
