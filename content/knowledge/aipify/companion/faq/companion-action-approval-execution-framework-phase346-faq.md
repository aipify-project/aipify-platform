# Companion Action Approval & Execution — FAQ

## What is the Companion Action Approval Framework?

The Companion Action Approval Framework lets Aipify propose actions, request approvals, follow policies, and execute safely with full audit trails — before advanced automation is introduced.

## Does Aipify change systems automatically?

No. This phase establishes trust, approvals, and governance. System-changing actions require explicit approval. Execution is logged and traceable.

## How does this relate to the Trust & Action Engine?

Companion actions use companion-scoped policies and workflows at `/app/companion/actions`. Organization-wide sensitive actions continue through `/app/approvals` and the Trust & Action Engine.
