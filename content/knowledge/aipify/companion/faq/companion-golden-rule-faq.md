# Companion Golden Rule — FAQ

**Applies to:** All Companion modules, Business Packs, and customer-facing insights

## What is the Companion Golden Rule?

The Companion Golden Rule is Aipify's global design principle: **Aipify must never stop at information.** Every insight, alert, reminder, nudge, and recommendation should help users understand why it matters, what it affects, what to do next, how difficult the action is, and what value acting may create.

## Why does Aipify need this rule?

Traditional software creates awareness through counts and status labels. Aipify is designed as a **Companion** — a trusted advisor that creates understanding and action, not noise.

> Information alone creates awareness. Context creates understanding. Recommendations create action. Companionship creates value.

## What should a good Companion message include?

Whenever possible:

1. **Observation** — what happened
2. **Explanation** — why it happened or why you are seeing it
3. **Impact** — what it affects
4. **Recommendation** — what should happen next
5. **Effort** — how difficult the action is
6. **Value** — what benefit acting may create

## What is a bad example?

- `Task overdue.`
- `You have 14 notifications.`
- `Customer has not been contacted.`
- `Support requests increased.`

These create awareness but little value.

## What is a good example?

> Support requests increased by 18%. Most requests relate to onboarding. Recommended action: Review onboarding documentation. Potential impact: Reduced support workload and improved customer experience.

## Does this apply to executives?

Yes. The **Executive Standard** requires clarity, prioritization, context, and actionability — high signal, low noise. Executives do not need more raw information.

## Which modules does this affect?

Companion Context Engine, Memory Engine, Recommendation Engine, Proactive Insights, Personalization, Daily Briefing, Work Prioritization, Follow-Up, Relationship Intelligence, Executive Companion, Companion Briefing banners, and future Business Packs.

## Do humans still decide?

Yes. Aipify informs, explains, and recommends. Humans approve and act. The Golden Rule strengthens companionship — it does not remove human control.
