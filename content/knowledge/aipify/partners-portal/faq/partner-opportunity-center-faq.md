# What is the Opportunity Center?

The Opportunity Center helps Growth Partners manage prospects, opportunities and sales pipelines.

# Why track opportunities?

Consistent opportunity management improves close rates and partner performance.

# Can Aipify recommend next steps?

Yes. Aipify can provide recommendations and follow-up suggestions based on opportunity activity.

# What pipeline stages are supported?

Lead, Contacted, Discovery, Qualified, Demo Scheduled, Proposal Sent, Negotiation, Closed Won, and Closed Lost.

# Who can create opportunities?

Partner Owners and Managers have full access. Sales representatives manage assigned opportunities. Viewers have read-only access.
