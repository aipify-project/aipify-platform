# Why is compliance required?

Compliance protects both Growth Partners and Aipify by ensuring legal and accurate settlement processing.

# Can I receive payments without verification?

No. Verification is required before settlement generation and payment processing.

# Why must I accept a Self-Billing Agreement?

The agreement authorizes Aipify Group AS to generate invoices on behalf of your business during settlement processing.

# What is the Compliance Center?

The Compliance Center manages business verification, tax profiles, banking details, self-billing agreements, and settlement eligibility for Growth Partners.

# Who can access the Compliance Center?

Partner Owners have full access. Partner Managers have read-only access. Sales representatives and viewers do not have compliance access.
