# What is the Sales Materials Center?

The Sales Materials Center provides approved Aipify sales, marketing and onboarding materials for Growth Partners.

# Can I customize materials?

Certain materials may support customization while maintaining Aipify branding standards.

# How often are materials updated?

Materials are updated continuously as Aipify evolves.

# What formats are supported?

PDF, PPTX, DOCX, XLSX, video, image assets, Canva templates, and Adobe templates.

# Who can access materials?

Partner Owners and Managers have full access. Sales representatives can access assigned materials. Viewers have read-only access.
