# Are Growth Partners employees of Aipify?

No. Growth Partners are independent businesses and suppliers — not employees. No payroll, holiday pay, or employer contributions apply.

# Are commissions recurring?

No. Commissions apply only to first-time purchases. No residual or lifetime commissions.

# What is self-billing?

After you approve a settlement, Aipify Group AS generates a legally compliant invoice on behalf of your business, then processes payment as a supplier expense.
