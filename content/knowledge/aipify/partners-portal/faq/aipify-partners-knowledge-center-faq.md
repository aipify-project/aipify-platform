# Aipify Partners — Knowledge Center FAQ

Official partner program workspace at `/partners/*`.

## What is the Aipify Partners program?

Aipify Partners is the dedicated workspace for individuals and businesses that partner with Aipify to promote and sell Aipify products. Partners operate independently while collaborating through approved partner programs — they are not Aipify employees.

## How do I become an Aipify Partner?

Apply through the Partner Program onboarding flow. Complete Partner Academy requirements and receive approval before accessing the full Partners workspace at `/partners`.

## How do partner commissions work?

Commissions are tracked through referral attribution and revenue attribution modules. Payout schedules and rates depend on your partner tier and program terms. View details under **Performance → Revenue Attribution** and **Referrals → Commissions & Rewards**.

## How are referrals tracked?

Referral links and lead assignments connect prospects to your partner account. **Referrals → Referral Performance** shows active, converted, and invited referrals.

## Where can I access partner marketing materials?

Open **Marketing** in the Partners portal: Marketing Assets, Campaign Templates, Email Templates, and Product Catalog.

## How do I complete partner certifications?

Complete training programs under **Academy**. Certification progress appears on your Partners Dashboard and under **Academy → Certifications**.

## How do I contact Aipify regarding partnership questions?

Use support channels listed in your partner onboarding materials or contact your assigned Partner Manager through approved partner communication paths.

## How can I monitor my partner performance?

Your **Partners Dashboard**, **Performance Summary**, **Rankings**, and **Performance Metrics** modules show leads, conversions, referrals, and certification progress.

## How do I view customers associated with my partner account?

Open **Customers → Leads**, **Pipeline**, and **Prospects** to view customers and prospects linked to your partner account.

## What happens if a customer upgrades their subscription?

When a referred customer upgrades, attribution and commission rules follow your partner program terms. **Performance → Revenue Attribution** reflects updated subscription tiers.
