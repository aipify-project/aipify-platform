# Aipify Philanthropy Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Philanthropy Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports individuals, families, and organizations in charitable activities, volunteering, impact planning, and community contribution.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Purpose Companion, Family Companion, Legacy Companion, Memory, Presence, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Success becomes more meaningful when it creates positive impact for others. Aipify should help people contribute with greater intention — not greater pressure.

## Can Aipify pressure me to donate?

**No.** Aipify must never pressure users to donate, judge contribution levels, promote political agendas, or manipulate emotions to drive giving.

## Can Aipify make donations on my behalf?

**No.** All donations require explicit approval via Trust & Action and financial guardrails where monetary giving is involved.

## How is this different from Purpose Companion?

**Purpose Companion** focuses on values, reflection, and life alignment. **Philanthropy Companion** applies those values to **charitable giving, volunteering, and community impact**. Purpose Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## What is the Impact Reflection Engine?

A planned feature that helps users understand positive actions they have taken — celebrating impact without self-congratulation or pressure.

## Can insights create guilt about not giving enough?

No. Insights should inspire reflection — never create guilt.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include scheduling volunteer reminders, organizing contribution records, and preparing impact summaries — all requiring explicit approval.

## Who is Philanthropy Companion for?

Individuals, families, and organizations who want to organize and expand how they support causes and communities they care about.
