# Platform Integrity — FAQ

## How does Aipify ensure its own quality?

Aipify continuously reviews the effectiveness, relevance, and consistency of its own capabilities through Integrity Reviews across ten quality domains.

## What is Integrity Score?

Integrity Score (0–100) reflects Aipify's overall platform health across trust quality, knowledge freshness, support effectiveness, recommendation accuracy, customer value, experience consistency, marketplace effectiveness, and governance alignment.

## How are outdated resources identified?

Integrity Reviews analyze freshness, usage patterns, feedback signals, outcome data, and knowledge gap analysis. Assets are flagged for review — never removed automatically.

## Can Integrity Reviews be disabled?

Yes. Organizations may configure Integrity settings according to their Governance requirements.

## What happens when Integrity issues are found?

Findings generate recommended actions and may initiate Governance-controlled review processes. Critical items require executive or governance oversight.

## Does Aipify automatically fix Integrity problems?

No. Significant improvements remain subject to human oversight and Governance controls. Aipify identifies concerns; humans determine implementation.

## Can Integrity findings influence Strategic Intelligence?

Yes. Integrity findings help prioritize future investments and long-term quality initiatives.

## Are failed recommendations hidden?

No. Transparency is essential for continuous learning and trust. Failed outcome validations appear as integrity findings.

## What are Deprecated Assets?

Deprecated Assets are resources that may no longer deliver sufficient value — such as outdated FAQs or repeatedly dismissed recommendations — flagged for human review.

## Does Integrity Engine replace Governance?

No. Integrity Engine supports Governance by identifying quality opportunities and concerns.
