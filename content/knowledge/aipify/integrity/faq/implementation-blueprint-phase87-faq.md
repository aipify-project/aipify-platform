# Implementation Blueprint Phase 87 — Self-Awareness & Platform Integrity Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 87 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 87 extends the Self-Awareness & Platform Integrity engine (repo Phase 87) with ABOS blueprint scaffolding — continuous self-monitoring, honest capability boundaries, integrity safeguards, and responsible self-improvement. It layers on top of repo Phase 87 (`_int_*` helpers) at the same route `/app/integrity`.

## How is this different from repo Phase 87?

**Repo Phase 87** (`_int_*`) implements integrity reviews, findings, scores, deprecated assets, and governance-gated actions. **Blueprint Phase 87** (`_sapibp87_*`) adds self-awareness scaffolding — platform health monitoring dimensions, self-observation examples, capability boundaries, companion guidance, and live success criteria. Both share the same route and integrity tables.

## How is this different from Curiosity & Discovery A.87?

**Curiosity & Discovery Engine (Phase A.87)** at `/app/curiosity-discovery-engine` focuses on exploration and opportunity discovery. **ABOS Blueprint Phase 87** shares the phase number but maps to Self-Awareness & Platform Integrity at `/app/integrity` — **engine phase number collision only**.

## How is this different from Blueprint Phase 80 Opportunity Exploration?

**Blueprint Phase 80** extends Curiosity & Discovery A.87 at `/app/curiosity-discovery-engine` with opportunity exploration scaffolding. It is **unrelated** to Self-Awareness & Platform Integrity Blueprint Phase 87.

## What are the self-observation examples with 🦉🌹🔔?

🦉 Pattern worth review — similar knowledge gaps may benefit from focused review. 🌹 Strengths to preserve — integration health and delivery remain strong. 🔔 Attention deserved — workflow completion may benefit from calm review. All encourage reflection — humility strengthens intelligence.

## What are capability boundaries?

Uncertainty, missing information, integration constraints, and confidence levels — Aipify states honest limits rather than implying certainty when evidence is incomplete. Cross-link Trust Engine `/app/trust` for explainability.

## What are integrity safeguards?

Audit logging, approval checkpoints, governance reviews, escalation for critical findings, and Companion Evolution Council oversight (Phase 65 at `/app/ai-ethics-responsible-use-engine`). No inflated scores, no concealed concerns, no autonomous major corrections.

## What are the privacy principles?

Integrity requires restraint — no hidden diagnostics, unauthorized access, unnecessary collection, or expanding visibility without approval. Metadata only; cross-link Security & Trust A.18.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self-awareness with compassion, not self-criticism. *"Aipify is continuously learning how to serve us better without losing sight of the principles that matter most."* Principle only — Platform Integrity stores metadata, not wellbeing content.

## What are the Phase 87 success criteria?

Computed live on the dashboard via `_sapibp87_success_criteria()` — monitoring dimensions, self-observations, capability boundaries, improvement opportunities, safeguards, companion guidance, privacy, trust, objectives, integration links, dogfooding, and vision.

## Where does dogfooding happen?

**Aipify Group** validates internally — Meeting Companion, Sales Expert operations, support workflows, Executive Companion. **Unonight** is the first external pilot for commerce operational integrity.
