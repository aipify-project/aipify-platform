# Human Potential & Augmented Work Engine — FAQ

## What is the Human Potential Center?

The Human Potential Center at `/app/human-potential-augmented-work-engine` is Aipify's hub for **human potential, augmented work, and growth companion support** in the **Autonomous Organization Era (Phases 131–140)**. It offers learning recommendations, reflection tools, recognition scaffolds, and career development support — without ranking human value or surveilling employees.

## How is this different from Human Success (Phase 82)?

Human Success at `/app/human-success` focuses on **platform adoption and value realization** for customers using Aipify. Phase 139 focuses on **augmenting human work** — strengths reflection, meaningful work, growth companion coaching, and user-owned personal reflections. Phase 139 cross-links Human Success but does **not duplicate** adoption scoring or surveillance patterns.

## Does Aipify rank employees or score human worth?

**No.** Phase 139 follows a strict **no ranking** philosophy. The augmentation engagement score measures participation in growth scaffolds (reflections, learning recommendations) — **NOT human value or performance ranking**. There are no leaderboards, comparative scoring, or hidden performance algorithms.

## Who can see my reflection entries?

**You.** Personal reflection entries and growth profile content are **user-owned**. Administrators see aggregate counts only — never personal reflection text. This follows PAME-style privacy principles.

## What is augmented work?

Augmented work means technology that **amplifies strengths** and **reduces administrative burdens** — decision preparation, summarization, knowledge discovery, communication preparation, and workflow coordination — so people have more time for meaningful contribution.

## What does the Growth Companion do?

The Growth Companion offers learning recommendations, reflection prompts, encouragement, knowledge suggestions, capability guidance, and recognition reminders. It **supports growth — it never evaluates human worth** and does not replace managers or mentors.

## How does Self Love connect?

Self Love (A.76) cross-links for self-awareness, compassion, rest, celebrating progress, healthy boundaries, and continuous learning without pressure — fundamental to sustainable growth.
