# Global Impact & Social Responsibility — Phase 149 FAQ

> **Important:** Phase 149 encourages authentic social responsibility awareness. It does **not** assign social scores, rank organizations, or judge organizational worth.

## FAQ 1

**Question:** What is Phase 149 Global Impact & Social Responsibility?

**Answer:** Phase 149 extends the Social Impact & Purpose Engine (Phase 118) at `/app/social-impact-purpose-engine` with Global Intelligence Era depth — community initiative tracking, employee wellbeing program scaffolds, impact reporting, executive reflection records, and Impact Companion guidance. It does not create a duplicate impact center.

## FAQ 2

**Question:** Does Aipify score or rank organizations on social responsibility?

**Answer:** No. Phase 149 explicitly prohibits social scoring, organizational worth rankings, and performative ESG gamification. The Impact Companion encourages awareness and reflection — it does not assign morality ratings or judge organizations.

## FAQ 3

**Question:** How is Phase 149 different from Impact Engine A.85?

**Answer:** Impact Engine A.85 at `/app/impact-engine` remains authoritative for outcome measurement across dimensions. Phase 149 focuses on social responsibility stewardship — community initiatives, wellbeing programs, contribution reporting, and leadership reflection. They cross-link but serve different purposes.

## FAQ 4

**Question:** How is Phase 149 different from Platform Anonymised Impact?

**Answer:** Platform Anonymised Impact at `/platform/impact` is marketing proof with anonymised aggregates across tenants. Phase 149 is tenant-scoped social responsibility metadata for the customer's own Purpose Center — not platform marketing metrics or social scoring.

## FAQ 5

**Question:** What are community initiatives?

**Answer:** Community initiatives track volunteer activities, partnerships, educational outreach, scholarships, local economic contributions, and professional development programs. Only aggregate participation counts are stored — no individual PII.

## FAQ 6

**Question:** Are wellbeing programs employee surveillance?

**Answer:** No. Wellbeing programs are organizational initiative scaffolds — adoption counts and program metadata only. Phase 149 does not monitor individual employees. Cross-link Employee Experience Phase 96 at `/app/organizational-health-engine` for aggregate wellbeing awareness.

## FAQ 7

**Question:** What can the Impact Companion do?

**Answer:** The Impact Companion may provide reflection prompts, identify impact opportunities, recommend knowledge resources, guide reporting, support community initiatives, and summarize leadership reflections. It must not assign social scores, judge worth, impose ideology, publish sensitive information, or replace executive accountability.

## FAQ 8

**Question:** What are executive impact reviews?

**Answer:** Executive impact reviews are leadership reflection records across seven dimensions: employee experiences, community relationships, technology impacts, organizational culture, purpose alignment, Growth Partner contributions, and stewardship outcomes. Humans retain accountability.

## FAQ 9

**Question:** What Growth Partner impact support is included?

**Answer:** Growth Partner impact program covers mentorship, educational activities, knowledge sharing, community leadership, professional development, and responsible business practices — cross-linking `/app/growth-partner-operations`. Growth Partner terminology — never Affiliate.

## FAQ 10

**Question:** Is impact data private to my organization?

**Answer:** Yes. All Phase 149 data is tenant-scoped with RBAC via `social_impact_purpose.view` and `social_impact_purpose.manage` permissions. Metadata only — no PII, no individual surveillance. Impact events are auditable via `social_impact_purpose_audit_logs`.
