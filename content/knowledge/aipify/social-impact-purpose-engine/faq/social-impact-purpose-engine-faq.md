# Social Impact & Purpose Engine — FAQ

## What is the Social Impact & Purpose Engine?

The Social Impact & Purpose Engine (Repo Phase 118) provides a Purpose Center and social impact initiative stewardship — aligning organizational activities with stated purpose, tracking commitments, and measuring social contributions with metadata only. Purpose is action, not marketing.

## How is this different from Purpose & Values (A.82)?

**Purpose & Values A.82** (`/app/purpose-values-engine`) handles organizational purpose statements, stated values, and values reflection prompts. **Social Impact & Purpose Phase 118** focuses on Purpose Center visibility, social impact initiatives, wellbeing support, and impact tracking — cross-link only, do not duplicate Purpose & Values RPCs.

## How is this different from Impact Engine (A.85)?

**Impact Engine A.85** (`/app/impact-engine`) orchestrates outcome measurement across operational, customer, human, knowledge, and strategic dimensions. **Social Impact & Purpose Phase 118** tracks purpose initiative progress and social contribution metadata — cross-link Impact Engine for authoritative outcome measurement.

## How is this different from Platform Anonymised Impact?

**Platform Anonymised Impact** (`/platform/impact`) provides anonymised marketing proof aggregates across tenants. **Social Impact & Purpose Phase 118** is tenant-scoped purpose and social impact stewardship — never platform marketing proof.

## How is this different from Community (Phase 117/89)?

**Community** (`/app/community`) provides community spaces, mentorship, events, and collective success programs. **Social Impact & Purpose Phase 118** tracks community impact program participation and cross-links Community — do not duplicate community RPCs.

## How is this different from Self Love (A.76)?

**Self Love A.76** (`/app/self-love-engine`) supports individual wellbeing principles. **Social Impact & Purpose Phase 118** applies Self Love principles at the organizational level — respect, compassion, celebration of progress — cross-link only.

## How is this different from Gratitude & Recognition (A.89)?

**Gratitude & Recognition A.89** (`/app/gratitude-recognition-engine`) enables peer appreciation and digital rose gestures. **Social Impact & Purpose Phase 118** tracks recognition activity as an impact indicator — cross-link only.

## What are the ten social impact initiative types?

Volunteer programs · Employee support · Knowledge sharing projects · Community partnerships · Education sponsorships · Mentorship initiatives · Accessibility improvements · Wellbeing campaigns · Local impact projects · Global contribution programs.

## What are the eight Purpose Center components?

Purpose statements · Strategic commitments · Community programs · Employee initiatives · Environmental objectives · Governance goals · Social contribution programs · Impact dashboards — visible not hidden in reports.

## What are Companion responsibilities?

Companions **may** organize initiatives, track commitments, highlight opportunities, suggest resources, encourage reflection, and summarize progress. Companions **must avoid** moral judgment, shaming, performative scoring, and manipulative recommendations. Humans define purpose; Companions support action.

## What is the Growth Partner connection?

Growth Partners may facilitate purpose workshops, leadership facilitation, wellbeing programs, governance coaching, community partnerships, knowledge initiatives, and transformation support — cross-link Growth Partner Operations Phase 114 at `/app/growth-partner-operations`. Strengthen relationships, not sales tactics.

## What permissions are required?

`social_impact_purpose.view` to view the dashboard. `social_impact_purpose.manage` to update settings, initiatives, and commitments. Owners, administrators, and managers have manage by default.

## What data is stored?

Metadata only — initiative summaries, commitment progress, alignment reflection snapshots, and aggregate impact indicators. No PII, no moral judgment automation, no performative ESG scoring.

## What are the limitation principles?

No performative scoring · No moral judgment automation · No PII in social impact tables · Do not duplicate Purpose & Values or Impact Engine RPCs · No manipulative participation pressure · Companions encourage support, never replace human care.

## What integrations does Social Impact & Purpose use?

Purpose & Values A.82, Impact Engine A.85, Inclusion & Humanity A.83, Self Love A.76, Gratitude & Recognition A.89, Community Phase 117/89, Growth Partner Ops Phase 114, AI Ethics Blueprint 98. See integration links on the dashboard.
