# Implementation Blueprint Phase 96 — Employee Experience & Wellbeing Companion Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 96 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 96 extends the Organizational Health Engine (Phase A.56 + Blueprint Phase 61) with **employee experience and Wellbeing Companion metadata** — engagement awareness, sustainable performance, recognition practices, optional companion check-ins, and employee journey cross-links.

## How is Phase 96 different from Phase 61?

**Blueprint Phase 61** adds wellbeing-aware organizational health domains, workload sustainability, and leadership insights. **Phase 96** adds employee experience questions (🦉🌹❤️🔔), wellbeing observations, Wellbeing Companion check-ins, recognition practices, employee journey connections, and live wellbeing success criteria. All Phase 61 and A.56 dashboard fields are preserved.

## Does Phase 96 collide with Innovation Lab Phase 96?

Yes — **Innovation Lab & Experimentation repo Phase 96** lives at `/app/innovation-lab`. **ABOS Blueprint Phase 96** (this spec) extends `/app/organizational-health-engine`. Same phase number, different engines — documented in `_eewcbp96_distinction_note()`.

## Does Phase 96 collide with Companion Device Phase A.96?

Yes — **Companion Device Ecosystem Phase A.96** is at `/app/companion-device-ecosystem-engine`. Blueprint Phase 96 is employee experience on organizational health — device orchestration is a separate surface.

## What is the Wellbeing Companion?

Aipify **Wellbeing Companion** offers optional reflection check-ins — not an AI wellness bot. Support not control; no diagnosis, no surveillance scoring, no mandatory tracking.

## What Self Love quote is included?

*"You do not need to earn rest through exhaustion."* — from `_eewcbp96_self_love_connection()`. Cross-link Self Love A.76 at `/app/self-love-engine` — principle only.

## How does Gratitude A.89 relate?

Recognition practices cross-link **Gratitude & Recognition (A.89)** at `/app/gratitude-recognition-engine`. Organizational health stores aggregate metadata, not recognition content.

## How does Human Success repo Phase 82 relate?

**Human Success** at `/app/human-success` covers adoption journeys and friction detection. Phase 96 cross-links employee transitions — does not duplicate Human Success logic.

## How does Attention Guardian relate?

**Attention Guardian** at `/app/assistant/attention` protects focus and protected time — personal attention cross-link, distinct from aggregate org health signals.

## How does EKE employee onboarding relate?

**Employee Knowledge Engine (EKE)** at `/app/settings/employee-knowledge` owns approved onboarding paths. Phase 96 cross-links onboarding as an employee journey stage — metadata only on org health dashboard.

## What privacy principles apply?

No hidden monitoring, mental health diagnosis, surveillance scoring, or intrusive mandatory wellness tracking — from `_eewcbp96_privacy_principles()`.

## What are the Phase 96 success criteria?

Computed live by `_eewcbp96_success_criteria(org_id)`: employee experience questions, wellbeing observations, recognition practices, companion check-ins, Self Love connection, leadership connection, employee journey, trust, privacy, integration links, Phase 61 preserved, dogfooding, and org health engagement.

## Where does dogfooding happen?

**Aipify Group** — Sales Expert sustainability, leadership pacing, product development wellbeing, community support, companion philosophy. **Unonight** — first external pilot for commerce team engagement and peak-period sustainability.
