# Multi-Store Management — FAQ

## What is the Multi-Store Orchestration Engine?

The Multi-Store Orchestration Engine helps organizations manage and understand multiple commerce environments through a centralized intelligence framework.

## Can Aipify support more than one store?

Yes. The framework is specifically designed to support organizations operating multiple stores.

## Can stores remain independent?

Yes. The engine supports centralized visibility while preserving local decision-making authority.

## Will Aipify automatically synchronize products?

No. Aipify may recommend synchronization opportunities, but organizations remain responsible for execution.

## Can Aipify compare performance across stores?

Yes. Cross-store insights may help organizations identify patterns, strengths and opportunities.

## Why are governance insights important?

Consistent governance practices strengthen trust, reduce risk and improve operational quality.

## Can Aipify help with international expansion?

Yes. The framework may provide guidance related to regional considerations and expansion readiness.

## Who benefits from multi-store functionality?

Brand portfolios, enterprise organizations, franchise operations and businesses operating multiple commerce environments.

## Will Aipify make portfolio decisions automatically?

No. Organizations retain responsibility for strategic decisions.

## What is a Portfolio Health Score?

It is a high-level indicator reflecting the overall health and performance of the connected store portfolio.
