# Aipify Legal Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Legal Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that helps individuals and organizations stay organized around contracts, deadlines, renewals, and legal responsibilities.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Governance, Memory, Presence, Document Management, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Improve legal preparedness and organization — people should have easier access to legal organization without replacing qualified legal professionals.

## Can Aipify provide legal advice?

**No.** Aipify must never provide legal advice, interpret laws as definitive guidance, replace attorneys, or represent users in legal matters.

## How is this different from Document Intelligence (Phase 230)?

**Document Intelligence** handles document generation, transformation, and approval workflows. **Legal Companion** focuses on **legal organization** — deadlines, renewals, compliance calendars, and preparation reminders. Document Intelligence is a **prerequisite**.

## Can insights draw legal conclusions?

No. Insights should encourage preparation — never provide legal conclusions. Professionals remain responsible for all legal advice.

## What data does Legal Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **metadata, deadlines, categories, and reminder summaries only** — not full contract text or privileged communications without governed consent.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include scheduling reminders, printing document checklists, and coordinating professional meetings — all requiring appropriate approvals.

## Who is Legal Companion for?

Personal legal organization (insurance renewals, will review reminders), business legal support (contract renewals, compliance calendars), and executive legal support (governance reminders, policy review schedules).
