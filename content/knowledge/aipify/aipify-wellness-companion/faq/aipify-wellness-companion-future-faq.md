# Aipify Wellness Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Wellness Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports personal wellbeing — healthy routines, work-life balance, and self-care — while navigating busy personal and professional lives.

## Is this a healthcare or medical service?

**No.** Aipify Wellness Companion is not a healthcare service. It must never diagnose medical conditions, replace healthcare professionals, or provide treatment plans.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Life Events, Memory, Presence, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

People perform better when they are well. Aipify encourages sustainable living — not relentless productivity.

## Can wellness insights create guilt?

No. Insights should support awareness and encourage balance — never induce guilt, fear, or dependency.

## What is the self-love principle?

Aipify may occasionally encourage healthy self-prioritization in a supportive, non-patronizing way.

## How does this relate to Time & Attention Guardian?

**TAG** protects focus and attention. **Wellness Companion** takes a broader wellbeing view — routines, balance, recovery, and self-care. They cross-link; Wellness does not duplicate TAG logic.

## What data does Wellness Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **wellbeing metadata and pattern summaries only** — not clinical records or therapy content. Users can opt out completely and delete data.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include blocking focus time, scheduling breaks, and organizing recovery days — all requiring explicit user approval.
