# Implementation Blueprint Phase 80 — Opportunity Exploration Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 80 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 80 extends the Curiosity & Discovery Engine (Phase A.87) with disciplined opportunity exploration — identification, evaluation, innovation connection, and limitation principles. It maps to the same route `/app/curiosity-discovery-engine` and uses `_oebp_*` helpers layered on `_cde_*`.

## How is this different from Curiosity & Discovery A.87?

**A.87** (`_cde_*`) provides exploration prompts, discovery categories, and question-led culture. **Phase 80** (`_oebp_*`) adds opportunity-specific framing — sources, evaluation criteria, innovation connection, leadership insights, and explicit limitation principles (no FOMO).

## How is this different from /app/continuity (Repo Phase 80)?

**Continuity, Resilience & Crisis (Repo Phase 80)** at `/app/continuity` covers organizational continuity (Blueprint Phase 73). **ABOS Blueprint Phase 80** shares the phase number but maps to Opportunity Exploration at `/app/curiosity-discovery-engine` — phase number collision only.

## How is this different from /app/strategy (Phase 81)?

**Legacy Strategic Intelligence & Opportunity (Phase 81)** at `/app/strategy` is the legacy strategic scorecard. **Opportunity Exploration Phase 80** cultivates exploration prompts and evaluation — cross-link only.

## How is this different from Innovation Lab (Phase 96)?

**Innovation Lab Phase 96** (`/app/innovation-lab`) governs controlled experiments with approval workflows. **Opportunity Exploration Phase 80** suggests possibilities for human evaluation — cross-link for innovation connection when experiments are warranted.

## How is this different from Strategic Intelligence (Blueprint Phase 79)?

**Strategic Intelligence A.31 / Blueprint Phase 79** cultivates strategic awareness and pattern recognition. **Opportunity Exploration Phase 80** focuses on opportunities worthy of exploration with evaluation criteria — complementary, not duplicate.

## What are the limitation principles?

- **No FOMO** — no missed-opportunity pressure
- **No excessive chasing** — recognize right opportunities, not every signal
- **No guarantees** — possibilities are not promised outcomes
- **Exploration not urgency** — thoughtful consideration before pursuit

## What are opportunity questions with 🦉🌹🔔?

🦉 Wisdom phrases invite reflection on insufficiently attended opportunities. 🌹 Self Love phrases connect strengths to growth paths. 🔔 Bell moments surface unmet customer need patterns for thoughtful review — curiosity creates possibility, not urgency.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self Love (A.76) supports patience, perspective, and sustainable ambition. *"Not every opportunity must be pursued immediately."* Principle only — Opportunity Exploration stores metadata, not wellbeing content.

## What are the Phase 80 success criteria?

Computed live on the dashboard via `_oebp_success_criteria()` — meaningful attention, strategic curiosity, disciplined innovation, intentional pursuit, long-term adaptability, limitation principles, and cross-links.

## Where does dogfooding happen?

**Aipify Group** validates internally — ecosystem expansion, product innovation, Sales Expert evolution, organizational development. **Unonight** is the first external pilot.
