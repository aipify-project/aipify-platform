# Capacity & Workload Management Engine — FAQ

## What is the Capacity & Workload Management Engine?

It helps organizations understand workload pressure and distribute work more effectively across teams and individuals — with metadata-only tracking and human-approved reassignment.

## Can Aipify detect overloaded employees?

Yes. Aipify can identify when assigned work exceeds configured capacity thresholds and generate warnings for managers.

## Can Aipify reassign work automatically?

Only if the organization has explicitly approved that workflow via capacity settings. Otherwise, Aipify recommends reassignment for human approval.

## Can workload be viewed by teams?

Yes. Managers can review workload by user, team, source, priority, and deadline through the capacity dashboard.

## Are workload changes audited?

Yes. Significant workload and capacity changes — profile updates, reassignments, overrides, and exports — are recorded for accountability.
