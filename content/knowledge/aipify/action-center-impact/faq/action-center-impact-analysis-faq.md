# Impact Analysis — Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is Impact Analysis?

Impact Analysis is the Action Center capability that explains expected outcomes, risks, effort, confidence, and business influence **before** you approve an Aipify action. It extends the Autonomous Execution Framework with decision support — not autonomous certainty.

## How does Aipify estimate outcomes?

Aipify estimates outcomes using:

- Available organizational and operational signals
- Similar previous actions in your workspace
- Knowledge Center context
- Approval and validation history

Estimates are labeled clearly as estimates. They improve when you submit post-execution feedback through the learning loop.

## Can Aipify predict the future?

No. Aipify does not predict the future. It provides structured decision support so leaders and operators can understand trade-offs before acting.

## How are confidence scores calculated?

Confidence scores reflect:

- Data availability in your workspace
- Historical success rates for similar actions
- Knowledge Center alignment
- Human validation patterns

Scores map to levels from **Very Low** to **Very High** and should be read alongside risk and effort analysis.

## Who remains responsible for decisions?

**Your organization and designated approvers.** Aipify prepares recommendations, explains impact, and logs audit events. Humans approve, reject, execute, and remain accountable for outcomes.

---

**Principle:** Aipify provides decision support. Humans retain accountability.
