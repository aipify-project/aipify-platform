# Global Talent & Expert Network Engine — FAQ

## What is the Global Expert Network Center?

The Global Expert Network Center at `/app/global-talent-expert-network-engine` is **Phase 147** of the Global Intelligence & Interorganizational Era (141–150). It enables professional expert discovery and Growth Partner matching — built on trust and verification, **not** a gig economy or anonymous freelancer marketplace.

## Is this a freelancer marketplace?

**No.** Phase 147 is professional discovery and matching metadata — quality **not** popularity. There are no star ratings, popularity rankings, or anonymous gig listings.

## How is Phase 147 different from Ecosystem Governance Phase 146?

Ecosystem Governance at `/app/ecosystem-governance` provides the **professional directory and certification standards**. Phase 147 focuses on **expert discovery, GP matching, and engagement charter scaffolds** — cross-link only, do not duplicate Phase 146 RPCs.

## What does the Talent Companion do?

The Talent Companion supports expert recommendations, GP discovery, advisory matching, capability gap identification, knowledge navigation, and relationship preparation. It **does not** guarantee outcomes, misrepresent expertise, manipulate visibility, or replace procurement or hiring decisions.

## What are the permissions?

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `global_talent_expert_network.view` | View expert profiles, discovery metadata, and engagement scaffolds |
| `global_talent_expert_network.manage` | Register profiles, create engagement charters, update settings |

## Is participation mandatory?

**No.** New organizations default to **opt-out** (`enabled = false`). Enable the center explicitly before activating discovery workflows.

## What data is stored?

**Metadata only** — expertise areas, industry focus, languages, certification keys, GP status flags, regional presence summaries, engagement charter scaffolds, and contribution counts. No PII surveillance.

## Does Aipify replace procurement or hiring?

**No.** Discovery scaffolds support human judgment. Organizations remain accountable for selection, contracting, and engagement decisions.
