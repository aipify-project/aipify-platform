# Implementation Blueprint Phase 99 — Human-Aipify Partnership & Companion Evolution FAQ

## What is Phase 99 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 99 aligns the **Companion Identity Engine (Phase A.84)** with ABOS human-Aipify partnership and intentional companion evolution framing — healthy empowering relationships with Aipify Companions.

## Does Phase 99 create a new engine route?

No. Phase 99 **extends** the existing engine at `/app/companion-identity-engine`. Do not duplicate a separate Human-AI Partnership engine route.

## How does Phase 99 relate to Phase A.84 and Blueprint Phase 6?

**Phase A.84** provides unified companion identity orchestration. **Blueprint Phase 6** adds companion identity foundation fields. **Phase 99** (`_haipcebp99_*`) adds partnership and evolution framing — **all prior dashboard and card fields are preserved**, including Learning Journey, Companion Naming (`_cnp_*`), and Aipify-First Language Policy (`_aflp_*`).

## What are the relationship evolution stages?

Assistant → Coach → Trusted Companion → Strategic Partner. Progression is **intentional** — never automatic escalation without user context and consent.

## What healthy dependency principles apply?

Encourage independence, decision-making, skills, and confidence. Avoid emotional dependency, reduced agency, and excessive reliance.

## What privacy principles apply?

Partnership not manipulation — avoid manipulative personalization, emotional exploitation, dependency encouragement, and influence without consent.

## What RPCs were updated?

`get_companion_identity_engine_dashboard()` and `get_companion_identity_engine_card()` — all A.84, Phase 6, Learning Journey, naming, and `_aflp_*` fields preserved; Phase 99 `human_ai_partnership_companion_evolution_blueprint` block appended.

## Where does Unonight fit?

Unonight is the first external pilot for healthy partnership patterns in daily operations. Aipify Group validates Sales Expert, Executive Companion, Meeting Companion, Self Love, and KC personalization internally first.
