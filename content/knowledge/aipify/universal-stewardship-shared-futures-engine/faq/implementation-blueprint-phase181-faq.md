# Universal Stewardship & Shared Futures Engine — FAQ (Phase 181)

## What is the Universal Stewardship Center?

The Universal Stewardship Center at `/app/universal-stewardship-shared-futures-engine` is part of the **Universal Stewardship & Shared Futures Era (181–190)**. Stewardship Companion supports reflection and metadata scaffolds — **not** replacement of human judgment, responsibility, or dignity.

## Does Stewardship Companion decide for humans?

**No.** Stewardship Companion provides informational support only. Humans retain final accountability.

## What are the permissions?

| Permission | Purpose |

| `universal_stewardship_futures.view` | View center and scaffolds |
| `universal_stewardship_futures.manage` | Update settings |
| `universal_stewardship_futures.steward` | Record executive reviews and metadata |
