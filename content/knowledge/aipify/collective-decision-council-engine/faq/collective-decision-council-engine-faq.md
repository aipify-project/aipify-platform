# Collective Decision & Human-Companion Council Engine — FAQ

## What is the Collective Decision Council?

The **Collective Decision & Human-Companion Council Engine (Phase 137)** at `/app/collective-decision-council-engine` is Aipify's collective council center for major organizational decisions. It brings human and companion perspectives together with stakeholder mapping, disagreement frameworks, transparency records, and council memory — while humans remain the decision-makers.

## How is this different from Decision Intelligence (Phase 125)?

Phase 125 at `/app/decision-intelligence-engine` is the **executive advisory workspace** for individual leader thinking — journals, assumption reviews, and trade-off analysis. Phase 137 is the **collective council** with human-companion perspectives and disagreement frameworks. Cross-link only — do not duplicate `_dein_*` or `_deibp125_*` RPCs.

## How is this different from Organizational Decision Support (A.54)?

ODSE at `/app/organizational-decision-support-engine` is the **organizational decision register** and approval workflow. Phase 137 focuses on **collective deliberation scaffolds** with companion advisory context. Cross-link only — do not duplicate `_odse_*` RPCs.

## How is this different from the AI Ethics council (A.46)?

AI Ethics Responsible Use at `/app/ai-ethics-responsible-use-engine` governs **responsible AI use policies**. Phase 137 is a **collective decision council** for major business decisions — distinct purpose, cross-link only.

## Do companions vote in the council?

**No.** Companions expand awareness through advisory perspectives — knowledge summaries, trend context, alternative viewpoints, and question generation. They **never vote** and never override human judgment or governance.

## What is the disagreement framework?

Six principles: respectful challenge, constructive dialogue, psychological safety, evidence-based discussion, curiosity, and humility. Collective wisdom — **not consensus at all costs**. Disagreement strengthens wisdom.

## Who participates in the council?

Seven role types: executives, department leaders, knowledge stewards, Growth Partners, governance representatives, subject matter experts, and relevant companions. **Humans are responsible**; companions advise.

## What stakeholder groups are mapped?

Eight groups: employees, customers, Growth Partners, communities, leadership, boards, knowledge contributors, and future stakeholders. Role labels only — no individual surveillance.

## What permissions are required?

- `collective_decision.view` — view council dashboard and workspaces
- `collective_decision.manage` — configure settings and workspace metadata (owner/administrator/manager)

## Is Growth Partner the same as Affiliate?

No. Aipify uses **Growth Partner** terminology everywhere — never Affiliate.

## Where does Self Love connect?

Phase 137 cross-links **Self Love A.76** at `/app/self-love-engine` for reflection, patience, humility, empathy, courage, and respect in collective deliberation.

## Where does Organizational Memory connect?

Council memory cross-links **Organizational Memory A.34** at `/app/organizational-memory-engine` for lessons learned and decision histories — complement, do not duplicate A.34 RPCs.

## What data is stored?

Metadata only — workspace summaries, perspective metadata, stakeholder impact summaries, transparency records, and council memory. No raw meeting transcripts, email content, chat, or PII.
