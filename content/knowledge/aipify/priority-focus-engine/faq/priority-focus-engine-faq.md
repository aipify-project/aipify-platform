# Priority & Focus Engine — FAQ

## What is the Priority & Focus Engine?

The Priority & Focus Engine (Phase A.80) is an organizational ABOS **Operations/Assistance** capability that transforms overwhelming workloads into clear priorities. It uses five dimensions and a P1–P4 framework with gentle focus recommendations — never pressure or guilt.

## How is this different from TAG (Time & Attention Guardian)?

**TAG (Phase 37)** protects **personal** time and focus at `/app/assistant/attention` — individual attention states, focus sessions, and protected time blocks. **Priority & Focus (A.80)** is the **organizational** engine for team-wide priority dimensions and P1–P4 levels. The dashboard links to TAG for personal focus context.

## How is this different from Goals & OKR (A.65)?

**Goals & OKR (A.65)** manages objectives and key results with hierarchy and progress tracking. **Priority & Focus (A.80)** provides a cross-cutting priority framework across five dimensions — operational through relationship. Integration is via dashboard links only; OKRs are not replaced.

## What are the five priority dimensions?

**Operational**, **Strategic**, **Human**, **Knowledge**, and **Relationship**. Each dimension captures a distinct lens for organizational priorities — from day-to-day workflows to people wellbeing and relationship coordination.

## What do P1–P4 priority levels mean?

- **P1 Critical** — organizational impact if delayed; review with calm focus.
- **P2 Important** — significant value; schedule deliberately without urgency pressure.
- **P3 Planned** — steady progress work on the schedule.
- **P4 Optional** — safe to defer when capacity is limited.

## Can I resolve or dismiss focus recommendations?

Yes. Every recommendation supports **resolve** or **dismiss** via `priority_focus.recommendations.resolve`. Aipify suggests focus gently; humans always decide.

## Does Priority & Focus use guilt or urgency language?

No. The engine is designed to **never** increase pressure or guilt. Copy follows ILM corpus principles — gentle, professional, and supportive. Self Love (A.76 planned) may soften recommendations when fatigue signals appear.

## What integrations does Priority & Focus use?

Dashboard links connect to TAG personal focus (A.37), Goals & OKR (A.65), Unified Tasks (A.62), Proactive Companion (A.79), Executive Insights (A.35), and Capacity & Workload (A.64) — metadata summaries only.

## Who can manage priority items?

`priority_focus.manage` is required to create and update organizational priority items. Owners, administrators, and managers have this by default. Viewers can see the dashboard and active items.

## What data does Priority & Focus store?

Metadata only — titles, summaries (max 500 characters), dimensions, priority levels, due hints, and recommendation types. No raw emails, chats, keystrokes, or colleague surveillance data.
