# Partner Success Engine — FAQ

## What is the Partner Success Engine?

It helps implementation partners and customer success teams improve adoption and long-term outcomes through portfolio dashboards, onboarding tracking, and success recommendations — metadata only.

## Can Aipify support onboarding?

Yes. Aipify assists with onboarding status tracking, adoption monitoring, and training or intervention recommendations. Humans approve significant actions.

## Can partner activities be monitored?

Yes. Dashboards provide visibility into customer health, adoption scores, open risks, renewal readiness, and success opportunities across your partner portfolio.

## Are partner activities audited?

Yes. Partner relationship changes, success reviews, onboarding overrides, and exports are recorded for accountability.
