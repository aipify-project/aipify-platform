# Marketplace Governance — FAQ

## Why do we need Marketplace Governance?

As businesses scale, quality risks increase. Marketplace Governance helps identify problems before they damage customer trust or profitability.

## What is Marketplace Governance?

Marketplace Governance is the process of monitoring quality, trust, compliance, and operational health within commerce environments.

## Can Aipify prevent every problem?

No. Aipify functions as an early warning system designed to identify emerging risks before they escalate.

## Will Aipify automatically remove products?

Only if administrators have enabled automated actions. Otherwise, recommendations and alerts are provided.

## Can we create our own quality standards?

Yes. Organizations may define custom policies, thresholds, and governance rules.

## How does Aipify identify quality concerns?

Through analysis of customer reviews, refund patterns, support tickets, supplier performance, product content, and marketplace behavior.

## Can Aipify monitor dropshipping suppliers?

Yes. Aipify can identify supplier risks, track trends, and recommend alternative suppliers.

## Who should use this functionality?

Shopify businesses, WooCommerce stores, enterprise commerce teams, marketplace operators, digital product businesses, and dropshipping businesses.

## Does this replace legal compliance advice?

No. Aipify assists with internal governance and operational standards but does not replace legal counsel.
