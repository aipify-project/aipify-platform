# Identity & Permissions — FAQ

## Why does Aipify use roles?

Roles simplify access management and ensure users only receive permissions relevant to their responsibilities.

## Can permissions differ between organizations?

Yes. Permissions are tenant-aware and can be customized per organization.

## Why are approvals required?

Approvals reduce risk and ensure sensitive actions receive human oversight.

## Can AI perform actions automatically?

Only low-risk actions may execute automatically. Medium and high-risk actions require approval.

## What happens if a user's access changes?

Role and permission updates take effect immediately and are recorded in audit logs.

## Why are failed logins monitored?

Monitoring failed logins helps identify suspicious activity and improves security.

## Can users belong to multiple organizations?

Yes. Access is evaluated independently within each organization.

## Why are audit logs important?

Audit logs provide transparency and accountability for identity-related activities.
