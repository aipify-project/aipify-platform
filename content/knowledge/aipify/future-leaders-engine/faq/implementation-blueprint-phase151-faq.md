# Future Leaders Engine — FAQ (Phase 151)

## What is the Future Leaders Center?

The Future Leaders Center at `/app/future-leaders-engine` is the **Legacy & Future Stewardship Era (151–160) opener**. It supports intergenerational leadership development through mentorship, reflection, and intentional growth — **not** leadership rankings or employee surveillance.

## How is Phase 151 different from Organizational Memory Legacy (Phase 126)?

Organizational Memory Legacy (`/app/organizational-memory-engine`) provides **succession intelligence and heritage** via `_omlebp126_*` RPCs. Phase 151 is a **dedicated leadership development center** with pathways, mentorship programs, and reflection frameworks — cross-link only, **do not duplicate** `_omlebp126_*`.

## How is Phase 151 different from Learning & Training (A.36 + Phase 92)?

Learning & Training (`/app/learning-training-engine`) provides **talent development paths and training completion** via `_hptdbp92_*`. Phase 151 focuses on **intergenerational leadership, mentorship metadata, and leadership memory** — cross-link only.

## Does the Leadership Companion rank employees?

**No.** The Leadership Companion provides reflection prompts, learning recommendations, and development resources. It **does not** determine leadership worth, create rankings, or replace human mentorship.

## What may be stored?

Approved **metadata summaries** only: mentorship goals and agreements, leadership memory entries (max 500 characters), succession readiness review scaffolds — **not** performance scores or surveillance data.

## What must never be stored?

Individual leadership rankings, performance surveillance, employee scoring, or any data used to determine leadership worth without human judgment.

## What are the permissions?

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `future_leaders.view` | View Future Leaders Center and development scaffolds |
| `future_leaders.manage` | Update settings and mentorship programs |
| `future_leaders.contribute` | Record leadership memory and mentorship participation metadata |

## Is Growth Partner terminology used?

**Yes.** Growth Partner — never Affiliate — throughout Phase 151 copy and ILM vocabulary.

## What is the development score?

An **organizational engagement indicator** based on enabled settings, active pathways, mentorship programs, and leadership memory entries — **not** an individual leadership ranking.
