# Execution Coordination — Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is execution coordination?

Execution coordination tracks approved Aipify actions from assignment through completion — with clear ownership, dependencies, blockers, timelines, and accountability trails.

## How does Aipify track progress?

Aipify uses lifecycle stages, collaboration logs, milestones, SLA indicators, and immutable audit records. Progress is visible in the Execution Coordination Center alongside Impact Analysis and Approvals.

## Can Aipify execute actions automatically?

Only within approved Autonomous Execution Framework limits and governance settings. The Coordination Center tracks progress and surfaces blockers — humans retain execution accountability.

## What happens when actions become blocked?

Teams register blockers with category, severity, owner, resolution plan, and target date. Relevant stakeholders are notified. Leadership dashboards reflect blocked and overdue initiatives.

## How are execution confidence scores calculated?

Scores reflect resource availability, historical completion performance, dependency stability, complexity, and team workload. They are **estimates** — Aipify never implies certainty.

---

**Principle:** Aipify coordinates execution. Humans remain responsible for execution decisions.
