# Innovation Lab & Experimentation — FAQ

## What is the Innovation Lab?

The Innovation Lab is Aipify's framework for testing, validating and refining new ideas before broader implementation.

## Why is experimentation important?

Experimentation allows organizations to learn quickly, reduce uncertainty and identify the initiatives most likely to create value.

## Can customers participate in pilots?

Yes. Selected customers may participate in structured pilot programs and beta initiatives.

## How does Aipify manage innovation risks?

Through governance controls, sandbox environments, approval processes and continuous monitoring.

## What happens if an experiment fails?

Unsuccessful outcomes are treated as learning opportunities and contribute to future improvements.

## Will experiments impact all users?

No. Experiments are designed to occur within controlled environments involving selected participants.

## Can organizations define their own experimentation policies?

Yes. Businesses may establish guidelines, approval requirements and acceptable risk thresholds.

## Why are feature flags important?

Feature flags enable controlled releases and reduce the risks associated with large-scale changes.

## Can executives monitor innovation activities?

Yes. Executive dashboards provide visibility into innovation pipelines, outcomes and strategic impact.

## Does innovation require abandoning governance?

No. Responsible innovation balances creativity with accountability and operational discipline.
