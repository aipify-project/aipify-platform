# Social Cohesion Engine — Phase 169 FAQ

## What is the Social Cohesion Center?

The **Social Cohesion Center** at `/app/social-cohesion-engine` supports intentional trust development across your organization and ecosystem. It provides reflection programs, relationship health dashboards, leadership credibility reviews, and trust repair scaffolds — metadata only.

## Is this a trust score or reputation system?

**No.** Phase 169 explicitly prohibits trust scores, star ratings, and social rankings. It is distinct from **Trust Network Phase 142** at `/app/trust-reputation-engine`, which handles voluntary verification. Cross-link only — do not duplicate `_tnvebp142_*` RPCs.

## Does the Trust Companion manipulate emotions or assign trust?

**No.** The Trust Companion supports awareness through reflection prompts, knowledge recommendations, and stewardship summaries. It does **not** manipulate emotions, determine who deserves trust, override leadership, suppress criticism, or replace human relationships.

## What data does Phase 169 store?

Metadata only:

- Executive trust review summaries (max ~500 chars)
- Relationship health reflection themes (aggregates, not surveillance)
- Trust repair scaffolds
- Trust memory entries

No PII surveillance, trust scores, or popularity metrics.

## How does Phase 169 relate to other civilizational era phases?

Phase 169 cross-links Phases 161–168, Trust Network 142, Global Governance Diplomacy 144, Relationship Intelligence A.78, and Self Love A.76. Each remains authoritative for its domain — Social Cohesion aggregates visibility without duplicating RPCs.

## What permissions are required?

- `social_cohesion.view` — view the center and scaffolds
- `social_cohesion.manage` — update settings and programs
- `social_cohesion.steward` — record executive trust reviews and repair actions

## What is Growth Partner terminology?

Aipify uses **Growth Partner** — never Affiliate — throughout civilizational era engines including Social Cohesion.
