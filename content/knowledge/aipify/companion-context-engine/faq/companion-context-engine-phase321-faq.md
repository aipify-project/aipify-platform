# Companion Context Engine — FAQ

## What is the Companion Context Engine?

The Companion Context Engine helps Aipify understand what is happening across your work environment so assistance becomes more relevant and useful.

## Does Aipify access everything automatically?

No. Aipify only uses information from systems and sources that have been explicitly connected and authorized.

## Why is context important?

Context allows Aipify to provide assistance that reflects current priorities, tasks and organizational activity rather than generic responses.
