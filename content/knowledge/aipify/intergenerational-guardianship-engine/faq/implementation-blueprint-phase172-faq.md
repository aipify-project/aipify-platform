# Intergenerational Guardianship Engine — Phase 172 FAQ

## What is the Guardianship Center?

The **Guardianship Center** at `/app/intergenerational-guardianship-engine` supports intergenerational stewardship and human continuity reflection. It provides executive guardianship reviews, values continuity reflection scaffolds, and legacy resilience metadata — not ownership control or organizational value definition.

## Does the Guardianship Companion define our values?

**No.** Phase 172 explicitly states that the Guardianship Companion does **not** define organizational values or determine future priorities. Humans and leadership define values — the Companion supports reflection only. Cross-link **Purpose & Values A.82** at `/app/purpose-values-engine` for values definition.

## Is this about controlling the future?

**No.** Guardianship is stewardship through responsibility — not ownership control. Phase 172 cross-links **Multi-Generational Futures Phase 171** at `/app/multi-generational-futures-engine` for long-horizon perspective support.

## What data does Phase 172 store?

Metadata only:

- Executive guardianship review summaries (max ~500 chars)
- Values continuity reflection themes (aggregates, not surveillance)
- Legacy resilience entry scaffolds (8 seeded types)

No PII surveillance, guardianship scores, or value definition by Companion.

## How does Phase 172 relate to other phases?

Phase 172 cross-links Phase 171, Phases 161–170, Future Leaders 151, Legacy A.86, Organizational Memory, Civilizational Memory 163, Hope Engine, Self Love A.76, and Purpose Values A.82. Each remains authoritative for its domain.

## What permissions are required?

- `intergenerational_guardianship.view` — view the center and scaffolds
- `intergenerational_guardianship.manage` — update settings and programs
- `intergenerational_guardianship.steward` — record executive reviews and continuity reflections

## What is Growth Partner terminology?

Aipify uses **Growth Partner** — never Affiliate — throughout Cosmic Stewardship era engines including Intergenerational Guardianship.
