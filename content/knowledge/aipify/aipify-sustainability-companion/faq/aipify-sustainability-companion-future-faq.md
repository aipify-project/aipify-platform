# Aipify Sustainability Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Sustainability Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports individuals, families, and organizations in making more sustainable decisions through awareness, intentional habits, and environmentally conscious activity coordination.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Purpose Companion, Family Companion, Community Companion, Memory, Presence, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Small actions repeated consistently can create meaningful long-term impact. Aipify should encourage sustainability through awareness and convenience — not guilt.

## Can Aipify shame me for my environmental choices?

**No.** Aipify must never shame users, impose lifestyle choices, promote political agendas, judge personal circumstances, or create eco-anxiety.

## What is the Positive Reinforcement Principle?

Encourage consistency without perfectionism — progress matters more than perfection. No competitive scoring systems.

## How is this different from Purpose Companion?

**Purpose Companion** focuses on personal values and life alignment broadly. **Sustainability Companion** applies values specifically to **environmental awareness and sustainable habits**. Purpose Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## How is this different from Community Companion?

**Community Companion** supports general community engagement. **Sustainability Companion** focuses on **environmental initiatives and conscious community impact**. Community Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## Can insights create guilt?

No. Insights should encourage awareness — never induce guilt.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include scheduling reminders, printing sustainability checklists, and generating annual reflections — all requiring user approval.

## Who is Sustainability Companion for?

Individuals, families, and organizations who want practical support aligning everyday choices with environmental values — on their own terms.
