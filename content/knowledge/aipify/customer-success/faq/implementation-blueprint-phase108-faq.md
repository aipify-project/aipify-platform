# Implementation Blueprint Phase 108 — Customer Journey Intelligence Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 108 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 108 extends **Customer Lifecycle & Success Orchestration (repo Phase 86)** at `/app/customer-lifecycle` with **ABOS blueprint scaffolding** — customer journey mapping, experience optimization, onboarding and adoption intelligence, advocacy identification, and Customer Success Companion guidance. All baseline `_cso_*` dashboard fields are preserved.

## How is Blueprint Phase 108 different from repo Phase 86 baseline?

**Repo Phase 86** ships lifecycle stages, Customer Success Score, quick wins, playbooks, recommendations, and briefings. **Blueprint Phase 108** adds mission/philosophy metadata, six objectives, eight customer journey stages (awareness → advocacy), journey insights, experience dashboard dimensions, onboarding/adoption intelligence, privacy principles, Self Love connection, live success criteria, and integration cross-links — layered on top without replacing baseline RPC behavior.

## How is this different from Blueprint Phase 86 Autonomous Operations?

**Blueprint Phase 86 Autonomous Operations Orchestration** is at `/app/workflow-orchestration-engine` — org workflow orchestration, not customer journey. **Blueprint Phase 108 Customer Journey Intelligence** extends repo Phase 86 at `/app/customer-lifecycle`. Same phase number, different surfaces — documented in `_cjibp108_distinction_note()`.

## What is the Customer Success Companion?

The **Customer Success Companion** is Aipify's stewardship-guided customer success framing — not a generic AI sales bot. It scaffolds journey understanding with optional prompts (🦉🌹🔔). Humans maintain customer relationships; recommendations are dismissible with `no_pressure` default.

## What are the privacy principles?

**Customer success not extraction:** no manipulative journey design, no hidden profiling, no sales-only optimization ignoring wellbeing, no pressure expansion. Transparent Customer Success Score components remain visible on the dashboard.

## What are the Phase 108 success criteria?

Live criteria include journey mapping, journey insights, customer experience dashboard, onboarding intelligence, adoption intelligence, customer success opportunities, advocacy identification, Customer Success Companion guidance, meeting companion connection, Growth Partner connection, privacy principles, Self Love connection, trust connection, baseline preservation, integration links, dogfooding, and ABOS principle.

## What is the vision phrase?

*"We understand our customers more deeply than ever before."*

## What data does the engine store?

Customer success metadata — success scores, milestone summaries, recommendation categories, playbook content, briefing summaries, and audit events. No raw customer communications, orders, or PII. No manipulative journey design in stored summaries.

## What is the helper prefix?

Engine helpers use `_cso_*`. Blueprint Phase 108 helpers use `_cjibp108_*` — they must not collide.

## What cross-links does Phase 108 include?

Customer Success Engine A.26, Customer Onboarding A.10 + Blueprint Phase 28, Growth Partner Blueprint Phase 107, Meeting Companion Blueprint Phase 72 / A.61, Value Realization A.48, Blueprint Phase 44 Renewal & Expansion, Partner Success A.73, Outcomes Phase 85, Human Success, and Self Love A.76.
