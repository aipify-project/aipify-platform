# Customer Lifecycle & Success — FAQ

## What is Customer Success?

Customer Success focuses on helping organizations achieve meaningful outcomes through effective use of Aipify throughout their entire journey.

## What happens after installation?

Customers enter a guided onboarding process designed to help them achieve early wins and long-term value.

## What are Quick Wins?

Quick Wins are early successes — like publishing a first FAQ or generating a first briefing — that demonstrate value and build confidence.

## What is Customer Success Score?

Customer Success Score (0–100) measures the overall health and likelihood of long-term success based on onboarding, adoption, value, learning, expansion readiness, and retention.

## How does Aipify identify at-risk customers?

By analyzing adoption trends, onboarding progress, value realization, and engagement signals — never through hidden or manipulative scoring.

## Can Aipify recommend additional capabilities?

Yes. Recommendations are based on customer needs and expected value. Expansion follows success, not pressure.

## Does Customer Success focus only on sales?

No. Customer Success prioritizes outcomes and value realization before expansion opportunities.

## Can organizations customize success journeys?

Yes. Playbooks and settings may be tailored to organizational goals and audiences.

## How are milestones tracked?

Milestones reflect meaningful progress throughout the customer lifecycle — from first FAQ to advocacy readiness.

## Can Customer Success features be disabled?

Yes. Organizations maintain control through Customer Success settings.
