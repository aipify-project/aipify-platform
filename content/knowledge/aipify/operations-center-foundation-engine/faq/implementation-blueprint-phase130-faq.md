# Implementation Blueprint Phase 130 — Executive Operations Center & Enterprise Command Engine FAQ

## What is the Executive Operations Center (EOC)?

Phase 130 is the **Enterprise Intelligence Era (121–130) capstone** on Operations Center Foundation Engine A.32 at `/app/operations-center-foundation-engine`. It extends Phase 75 Executive Operations Center with enterprise command dashboard, initiative orchestration, executive alignment, decision execution, companion network, health monitoring, review cycles, and era cross-links. Helpers use `_eoccep130_*` — never collide with `_eocbp_*` (Phase 75), `_ocf_*` (Phase 18), or `_cfibp_*` (Phase 70).

## Does Phase 130 centralize control?

**No.** Enterprise command is **stewardship, not command-and-control**. Clarity enables leadership; overwhelm disables it. Aipify informs and prepares; humans decide. Metadata only — no employee surveillance or punitive individual scoring.

## Where does it live?

`/app/operations-center-foundation-engine` — no new route. All Phase 18, 70, and 75 dashboard fields are preserved.

## How is this different from Command Center Phase 26?

Command Center at `/app/command-center` handles presence and notifications. Phase 130 is executive situational awareness and enterprise stewardship — cross-link only, do not duplicate notification delivery.

## How is this different from Executive Intelligence Phase 121?

Executive Intelligence at `/app/executive-intelligence` is the Leadership Companion hub opening the era. Phase 130 **unifies** executive operations across era companions on A.32 — cross-link only, do not duplicate Phase 121 RPCs.

## Can companions decide for leaders?

**No.** Companions prepare context, surface patterns, and suggest scaffolds. They never override authority, issue directives, replace accountability, conceal uncertainty, or suppress dissent. Humans retain decision authority.

## Why connect decisions to execution?

So leadership sees whether intentions became outcomes. Decision execution links decision visibility, action tracking, outcome learning, and organizational memory — cross-link Decision Intelligence Phase 125 and Org Memory Phase 126. Aipify tracks metadata; humans decide and reflect.

## Why connect Self Love?

Enterprise leadership is isolating. Self Love supports reflection, recovery, perspective, boundaries, and recognition — sustainable stewardship, not burnout. Cross-linked to `/app/self-love-engine` as a principle only; Operations Center stores metadata, not wellbeing content.

## What is the enterprise command dashboard?

Unified org view dimensions: strategic objectives, operational health, priorities, transformation, risk, companion activity, knowledge signals, Growth Partner updates, community trends, and purpose metrics — metadata summaries only.

## What is the executive companion network?

Cross-links to era companions: Leadership (121), Foresight (122), Governance (123), Decision (125), Transformation (127), Resilience (128), and Wisdom (129 planned — interim Wisdom Engine A.93 at `/app/wisdom-engine`).

## What about Phase 129?

Organizational Wisdom & Ethical Intelligence (Phase 129) is **not yet implemented** as a dedicated surface. Phase 130 cross-links Wisdom Engine A.93 at `/app/wisdom-engine` with a distinction note until Phase 129 ships.

## What is the Enterprise Intelligence Era capstone?

Phases 121–130: Executive Intelligence · Strategic Foresight · Board Governance · Digital Twin · Decision Intelligence · Org Memory Legacy · Transformation · Resilience Companion · Organizational Wisdom (planned) · **Executive Operations & Enterprise Command (130)**.

## Does Phase 130 add new database tables?

No. Phase 130 extends dashboard and card RPCs with `_eoccep130_*` blueprint helpers. All prior phase fields are preserved.

## What are the Phase 130 success criteria?

Enterprise command dashboard, initiative orchestration, executive alignment, decision execution, companion network, health monitoring, review cycles, enterprise memory integration, companion limitations, Self Love connection, and era capstone cross-links — computed live via `_eoccep130_success_criteria()`.
