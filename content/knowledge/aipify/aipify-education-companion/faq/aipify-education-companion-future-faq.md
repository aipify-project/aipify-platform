# Aipify Education Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Education Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports lifelong learning — study planning, motivation, concept reinforcement, and skill development for individuals and organizations.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Memory, Presence, Knowledge, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Learning should adapt to people — not rigid systems.

## Can Aipify complete my homework or exams?

**No.** Aipify must never complete assessments dishonestly, encourage academic misconduct, or replace educators. It supports learning through planning, summaries, practice, and review — not cheating.

## How is this different from the Learning Engine?

The **Learning Engine** governs how Aipify learns *with* your organization to improve the product. **Education Companion** supports *your* personal and professional learning goals — distinct purposes.

## How does this relate to Employee Knowledge Engine?

**EKE** handles organizational knowledge and employee onboarding. **Education Companion** supports broader learning goals including personal and academic development — they may cross-link for organizational training.

## What data does Education Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **learning metadata, goals, and progress summaries only** — not raw assessment submissions or plagiarized content. Users can delete learning history.

## Can insights create pressure or guilt?

No. Insights should encourage growth and celebrate progress — never guilt or unhealthy pressure.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include scheduling study sessions, printing study plans, and creating revision reminders — all requiring user approval where appropriate.
