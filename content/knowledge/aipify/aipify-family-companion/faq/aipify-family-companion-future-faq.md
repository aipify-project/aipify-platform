# Aipify Family Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Family Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that helps families coordinate events, responsibilities, reminders, and practical life tasks — reducing mental load while strengthening human connection.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Life Events, Action Framework, and Memory foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Families should spend less time remembering everything and more time enjoying life together. Aipify reduces stress — it does not replace relationships.

## Can Aipify make parenting decisions?

No. Aipify must never replace parenting decisions, override parental authority, or make important family choices independently.

## How does this relate to LifeOS and PAME?

**LifeOS** supports individual life priorities. **PAME** stores personal memory metadata. **Family Companion** coordinates **shared family logistics** — it cross-links these systems without duplicating memory storage.

## What about calendars?

Family Companion cross-links the **Context Engine** for calendar awareness. Aipify orchestrates above calendars — it never replaces a calendar app.

## What data does Family Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **coordination metadata and reminder summaries only** — not raw family conversations or children's private content. Families control who sees what.

## Can insights create guilt?

No. Companion insights should reduce stress and celebrate coordination — never guilt or pressure.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include shopping lists, print schedules, reminders, approved gifts, and vacation checklists — all gated by appropriate permissions, financial guardrails, and Trust & Action approvals.
