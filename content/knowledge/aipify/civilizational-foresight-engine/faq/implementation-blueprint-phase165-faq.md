The Long-Horizon Center is Aipify's framework for civilizational-scale foresight — helping organizations explore plausible futures, conduct executive foresight reviews, and build preparedness visibility across the Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170). It aggregates visibility without duplicating phase-specific RPCs. **Preparation — not prophecy.**

## Does Aipify predict the future?

No. The Foresight Companion provides reflection prompts, scenario summaries, and preparedness recommendations. It does **not** claim predictive certainty, replace executive judgment, determine priorities, suppress alternative futures, or override governance. Humans decide; Aipify prepares perspective.
