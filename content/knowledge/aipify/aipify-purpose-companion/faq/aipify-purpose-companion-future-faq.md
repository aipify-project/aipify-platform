# Aipify Purpose Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Purpose Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that helps individuals identify, pursue, and maintain alignment with personal values, meaningful goals, and long-term aspirations.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Wellness Companion, Legacy Companion, Family Companion, Memory, Presence, and Reflection foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Productivity without purpose often leads to emptiness. Aipify should help people remember why they are doing what they are doing.

## Can Aipify define my purpose?

**No.** Aipify must never define purpose for users, impose beliefs or values, judge life choices, or replace professional counseling.

## How is this different from Goals & Dreams Engine?

**Goals & Dreams Engine** supports aspirations with milestones and accountability. **Purpose Companion** focuses on **values, reflection, and life alignment** — why goals matter and whether daily actions align with stated intentions. Cross-link only.

## What is the Life Alignment Engine?

A planned feature within Purpose Companion that helps users notice alignment or disconnect between intentions and actions — informative, never judgmental.

## Can reflection prompts or insights create guilt?

No. Reflection prompts encourage self-discovery — never prescribe answers. Insights support awareness — never induce guilt.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include scheduling reflection sessions, printing goal summaries, and generating annual reflection reports — all requiring user approval.

## Who is Purpose Companion for?

Individuals at any life stage who want intentional living, meaningful goal setting, and greater alignment with what matters most.
