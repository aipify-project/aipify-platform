# Product Automation — FAQ

## What is the Product Automation Engine?

The Product Automation Engine helps businesses prepare products for publication by automating translation, rewriting, SEO improvements and category recommendations.

## Will Aipify automatically publish products?

No. Products require human approval before publication.

## Can Aipify translate products into different languages?

Yes. The engine can generate translations adapted to the store's preferred language settings.

## Why rewrite product descriptions?

Rewriting improves readability, reduces duplicate content and helps align products with the brand.

## Can Aipify optimize products for search engines?

Yes. Aipify provides SEO recommendations designed to improve visibility while preserving customer value.

## Can Aipify recommend categories?

Yes. Category recommendations help businesses organize their catalog more effectively.

## Can I review changes before products go live?

Yes. Human review remains a mandatory step before publication.

## Can I automate multiple products at once?

Yes. Bulk actions support large product catalogs and high-volume operations.

## Will Aipify preserve our brand voice?

Yes. Organizations may define brand guidelines that shape rewriting recommendations.

## Does automation replace human expertise?

No. Aipify accelerates workflows while leaving final decisions in human hands.
