# Customer Onboarding Engine — FAQ

## What is Customer Onboarding?

A guided 10-step setup flow that helps new organizations activate Aipify modules confidently.

## Who can manage onboarding?

Owners and administrators can advance steps, complete checklist items, and finish onboarding.

## What is the checklist for?

The checklist tracks concrete setup tasks such as inviting team members, publishing knowledge, and connecting integrations.

## How are recommendations generated?

Onboarding recommendations combine step-specific hints with Knowledge Center articles retrieved via `retrieve_knowledge_for_ai`.

## When can onboarding be completed?

Onboarding can be completed once at least half of the checklist items are done.

## Is onboarding progress audited?

Yes. Starting, step advances, checklist completions, and final completion are recorded in audit logs.
