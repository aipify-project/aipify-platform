slug: what-is-impact-score
category: value-engine
language: en
visibility: authenticated

tags: [value-engine, impact]

---
Impact Score is a 0–100 summary of value Aipify has created for your organization, based on traceable value events in categories like support, knowledge, quality, automation, governance, and productivity. It is conservative and explainable — not a marketing number.

---
title: How does Aipify estimate value?
slug: how-aipify-estimates-value
category: value-engine
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [value-engine]
article_type: faq
---
Aipify records value events from module activity (support drafts, FAQ resolution, briefings, actions, quality findings, governance blocks). Each event uses conservative time-saved rules with documented evidence. Monetary ROI is calculated only when you enable ROI settings.

---
title: How are time savings calculated?
slug: how-time-savings-calculated
category: value-engine
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [value-engine]
article_type: faq
---
Time savings use fixed conservative rules — for example, 5 minutes for an accepted support draft, 3 minutes for FAQ resolution, 10 minutes when a briefing replaces manual review. Quality detections without clear time savings record risk reduction with zero minutes until configured.
