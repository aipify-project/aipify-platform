# Organizational Recognition & Appreciation (ABOS Phase 97) — FAQ

## What is Blueprint Phase 97?

**Organizational Recognition & Appreciation Engine** extends Gratitude & Recognition Engine A.89 at `/app/gratitude-recognition-engine` with organizational celebration scaffolding — peer, leadership, customer, and Sales Expert appreciation without competition.

## How is this different from Phase 9 Recognition Foundation?

**Phase 9** documents bell moments, recognition categories, and digital roses. **Phase 97** layers organizational recognition moments, leadership insights, customer appreciation, and Sales Expert milestone cross-links — all Phase 9 fields are preserved.

## How is this different from Phase 53 Human Moments?

**Phase 53** covers consent-based life events (birthdays, anniversaries, certifications). **Phase 97** covers everyday organizational appreciation — launches, customer praise, mentorship, kindness — with aggregate metadata only. Phase 53 fields remain on the dashboard.

## How is this different from Presence & Comfort A.90?

**Presence & Comfort A.90** (`/app/presence-comfort-protocol`) sends comfort roses during difficulty. **Gratitude A.89 + Phase 97** celebrates contribution and appreciation — recognition roses, not comfort roses.

## How is this different from Future Technologies repo Phase 97?

**Future Technologies & Emerging Interfaces** lives at `/app/future-tech` (Blueprint Phase 63 Future Readiness extends that surface). **Blueprint Phase 97** on A.89 is organizational recognition only — a phase number collision documented in `_oraebp97_distinction_note()`.

## What privacy principles apply?

Aipify must avoid forced public recognition, popularity contests, output-only metrics, and ignoring quiet contributors. Recognition is voluntary; summaries are metadata-only with no PII.
