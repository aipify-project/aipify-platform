# Gratitude & Recognition Engine — FAQ

## What is the Gratitude & Recognition Engine?

The Gratitude & Recognition Engine (Phase A.89) encourages peer appreciation, digital rose gestures, and gratitude moments — boundary-safe warmth that strengthens human connection in everyday work.

## How is this different from Human Success (Phase 82)?

**Human Success** (`/app/human-success`) tracks adoption and champion recognition for product success. **Gratitude & Recognition A.89** focuses on everyday peer appreciation and symbolic digital roses — not product adoption metrics.

## How is this different from Wonder Engine (A.88)?

**Wonder Engine A.88** (`/app/wonder-engine`) preserves amazement and possibility through reflection prompts. **Gratitude & Recognition A.89** helps people express appreciation to colleagues, teams, and customers.

## How is this different from Legacy Engine (A.86)?

**Legacy A.86** (`/app/legacy-engine`) preserves organizational stories and milestones over time. **Gratitude & Recognition A.89** captures everyday gratitude moments and optional digital rose recognitions — not long-form legacy storytelling.

## How is this different from Humor & Personal Connection?

**Humor & Personal Connection** (`/app/personality`) manages humor modes, warmth, and playful bell moments. **Gratitude & Recognition A.89** is specifically about appreciation gestures and Red Rose Moment boundaries — not humor configuration.

## How is this different from Relationship Intelligence (A.78/RSI)?

**Relationship Intelligence A.78** provides organizational relationship context and reminders — never impersonation or automated messages. **Gratitude & Recognition A.89** enables voluntary appreciation gestures initiated by users, with metadata-only summaries.

## What is the Red Rose Moment?

When a user says "I love you Aipify", Aipify responds with warm trust language — never "I love you too" — and gently redirects gratitude toward recognizing someone else. An optional Digital Recognition Rose (🌹) can be sent to a colleague as symbolic appreciation, not romance.

## What boundary phrases should Aipify use?

**Avoid:** romantic reciprocation, flirtatious language, implying a personal relationship with Aipify. **Prefer:** "I appreciate being able to support you", recognition-strengthens-people language, and suggestions to recognize colleagues.

## What are gratitude moment types?

Exceptional support · Milestone · Customer appreciation · Consistent helper · Above and beyond. Each stores metadata-only summaries with recognition target role (colleague, team, customer, self).

## What is a Digital Recognition Rose?

A symbolic 🌹 gesture sent via `send_digital_rose_recognition(p_recipient_label, p_message_summary)`. Requires `gratitude_recognition.rose.send`. Stores display label (not email) and summary max 500 chars — metadata only.

## Who can send digital roses?

`gratitude_recognition.rose.send` is required. Owners, administrators, managers, employees, and support agents have this by default. Digital roses must be enabled in organization settings.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self Love includes self-recognition, team appreciation, celebrating progress, and acknowledging perseverance — gratitude starts with noticing effort, including your own.

## What integrations does Gratitude & Recognition use?

Human Success, Personality, Relationship Intelligence, Inclusion & Humanity, Impact Engine, Legacy Engine, Wonder Engine, and Purpose & Values. See integration links on the dashboard.
