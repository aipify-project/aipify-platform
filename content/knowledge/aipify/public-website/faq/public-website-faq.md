The Aipify public website at aipify.ai explains the product story for prospective customers. It positions Aipify as an **AI Business Operating System** — install-first operational intelligence — not a generic chatbot.

---
title: Does Aipify monitor employees?
slug: does-aipify-monitor-employees
category: public-website
audience: public
---

**No.** Aipify does not monitor employees, keystrokes, screens, or workplace activity. The companion presence orb reflects **system operational state** (online, working, attention needed) — not individual surveillance. This is stated prominently on the security page and in the site footer.

---
title: What is the companion orb on the marketing site?
slug: what-is-the-companion-orb-marketing
category: public-website
audience: public
---

The marketing site includes an interactive **Companion Orb Demo** showing presence states: online, working, attention, critical, disconnected, and quiet hours. It reuses presence animation concepts from the product — click the orb or use state buttons to explore. It is a demonstration only; it does not connect to a live installation.

---
title: How does early access work?
slug: how-does-early-access-work
category: public-website
audience: public
---

Submit the form at `/early-access` with your name, company, work email, company size, industry, and primary interest. Requests are stored as metadata-only leads (`marketing_early_access_leads`) or logged server-side when the database is unavailable. Aipify does not sell lead data.

---
title: What modules does Aipify showcase?
slug: what-modules-does-aipify-showcase
category: public-website
audience: public
---

The module showcase highlights ten operational capabilities: Support Operations, Install Engine, Executive Briefing, Action Center, Knowledge Center, Quality Guardian, Governance & Trust, Analytics & Insights, Workflow Orchestration, and Desktop Companion. Customers enable modules per their licensed package.

---
title: What is the Unonight pilot story?
slug: what-is-the-unonight-pilot-story
category: public-website
audience: public
---

Unonight is Aipify's first pilot customer for **operational validation** — multi-module install, support triage with human review, action policies, executive briefings, and metadata-only platform metrics. The pilot story focuses on how Aipify runs in production, not on any specific industry vertical.
