# Human Identity & Meaning Engine — FAQ (Phase 173)

## What is the Meaning & Identity Center?

The Meaning & Identity Center at `/app/human-identity-meaning-engine` is part of the **Cosmic Stewardship & Multi-Generational Futures Era (171–180)**. It supports meaning discovery, identity humility, and agency preservation — **not** identity definition, purpose imposition, productivity-based worth scoring, or employee surveillance.

## How is Phase 173 different from Identity Engine AIE (Phase 34)?

Identity Engine AIE (`/app/assistant/identity`) adapts **communication style** from approved observations — `_aie_*` RPCs remain authoritative there. Phase 173 is a **different center** focused on organizational meaning discovery and humanity reviews — cross-link only, **do not duplicate** AIE helpers.

## How is Phase 173 different from Human Flourishing (Phase 170)?

Human Flourishing (`/app/human-flourishing-engine`) closed the **Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170)** with development and flourishing scaffolds via `_cfhp_*`. Phase 173 is in the **Cosmic Stewardship era** and focuses on meaning, identity humility, and agency — cross-link only.

## Does the Meaning Companion define identity?

**No.** The Meaning Companion provides discovery prompts, meaning reflection scaffolds, belonging summaries, agency awareness insights, purpose exploration links, and discovery resources. It **does not** define personal identity, impose purpose, assign worth by productivity, replace human relationships, suppress cultural identity, or override leadership judgment.

## What may be stored?

Approved **metadata summaries** only: executive humanity review summaries (max 500 characters), meaning reflection aggregates, agency preservation notes — **not** identity profiles, worth scores, or surveillance data.

## What must never be stored?

Identity definitions imposed by Companion, productivity-based worth scoring, purpose assignments without human leadership, individual ranking, or surveillance used to score human value.
