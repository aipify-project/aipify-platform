---
title: Does Aipify learn from our data?
slug: does-aipify-learn-from-our-data
category: global-learning
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [global-learning, privacy]
article_type: faq
---
Aipify has three intelligence levels. Local and organizational intelligence stay within your tenant. Global Learning uses only anonymized, structured patterns — never raw customer content, names, emails, or secrets.

---
title: What information contributes globally?
slug: what-contributes-to-global-learning
category: global-learning
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [global-learning]
article_type: faq
---
Only structured outcome signals may contribute: category, problem type, resolution type, feedback polarity, confidence scores, adoption rates, and similar aggregates. Full conversations, documents, and personal identifiers never contribute.

---
title: How is information anonymized?
slug: how-global-learning-anonymizes
category: global-learning
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [global-learning, privacy]
article_type: faq
---
Before any signal reaches Global Learning, Aipify removes identifiers, strips blocked fields (email, phone, names, secrets, raw content), converts the signal into a structured pattern, runs Policy Engine checks, and respects your participation settings.

---
title: Can we opt out of Global Learning?
slug: can-we-opt-out-global-learning
category: global-learning
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [global-learning, settings]
article_type: faq

Yes. Open Global Learning settings and choose "No Global Learning." Your organization will contribute nothing to the Global Learning Network.

---
title: What is the Global Learning Network?
slug: what-is-global-learning-network
category: global-learning
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [global-learning]
article_type: faq
---
The Global Learning Network is Aipify's opt-in system for improving Aipify Core from anonymous patterns observed across deployments — such as which FAQ topics reduce support load or which Blueprint recommendations are commonly accepted.

---
title: What is Core Evolution?
slug: what-is-core-evolution
category: global-learning
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [global-learning, evolution]
article_type: faq
---
Core Evolution transforms anonymous global patterns into governed proposals to improve Blueprints, Marketplace Packs, Knowledge templates, Briefing defaults, Desktop behavior, and Quality checks. Proposals require review — nothing auto-modifies your environment.

language: en

Yes. The Contributions dashboard shows category counts and signal types your organization has contributed. It never displays raw customer content.

---
title: What happens if we reject participation?
slug: what-happens-if-we-reject-participation
category: global-learning
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [global-learning]
article_type: faq
---
If you opt out or reject extended participation, no new anonymized signals are submitted from your tenant. Local and organizational learning within your tenant continue to work normally.

---
title: Can Global Learning improve Industry Blueprints?
slug: can-global-learning-improve-blueprints
category: global-learning
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [global-learning, industry-blueprints]
article_type: faq
---
Yes. Anonymous Blueprint performance patterns (such as frequently modified FAQ sections) can generate Core Evolution proposals to update official Blueprint manifests. Changes apply only after platform review and your normal governance controls.
