# Education & Learning Operations Pack — FAQ

## How does Education Pack work?

The Education Pack is the Industry Pack home for schools, universities, training providers, academies, certification organizations, and corporate learning departments at `/app/education`. It extends the Academy framework into a complete educational operations platform on the shared ABOS foundation. Install via Industry Packs at `/app/industry-packs`.

## How are students managed?

Students are tracked in `education_students` with profile metadata, enrollment status (prospective, enrolled, active, on leave, graduated, completed, archived), progress, engagement, certifications, and learning history. Manage students from the Education Center at `/app/education/students`.

## How are certifications tracked?

Certifications in `education_certifications` track certification programs, requirements, validity, renewals, achievement records, and status (in progress, awarded, renewal due, expired, revoked). Open Certifications from `/app/education/certifications`.

## How are assessments managed?

Assessments in `education_assessments` support quizzes, assignments, projects, practical exercises, exams, certification tests, and peer reviews with scores and completion status. Open Assessments from `/app/education/assessments`.

## How is learning progress measured?

Learning progress combines student progress percent, engagement scores, course completion rates, assessment results, and learning outcomes in the Education overview and Student Success signals.

## How are programs structured?

Programs in `education_programs` track program name, type (academic, professional training, corporate learning, certification, industry, executive, custom), duration, requirements, courses, certifications, completion criteria, and status.

## Student Management

Track student profiles, enrollment, courses, programs, progress, certifications, achievements, and learning history with full audit logging for enrollment events.

## Course Management

Manage course content metadata, lessons, assignments, assessments, resources, learning paths, and course analytics in `education_courses`.

## Programs

Structure academic, professional, corporate, certification, industry, executive, and custom programs with duration, requirements, and completion criteria.

## Assessments

Support quizzes, assignments, projects, practical exercises, exams, certification tests, and peer reviews with audit logging when completed.

## Certifications

Track certification programs, validity, renewals, and achievement records with renewal alerts in the Companion education advisor.

## Learning Analytics

Measure course completion, program completion, assessment results, engagement, student satisfaction, instructor performance, and certification success in Education Intelligence.
