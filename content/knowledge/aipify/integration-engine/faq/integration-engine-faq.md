# Integration Engine — FAQ

## What is an integration?

An integration connects Aipify with external systems and services.

## Can each organization choose its own integrations?

Yes. Integrations are configured independently for each tenant.

## How are credentials protected?

Sensitive credentials should be encrypted and never exposed to frontend systems.

## What happens if an integration fails?

Failures are monitored, logged, and surfaced to administrators for action.

## Are integrations audited?

Yes. Significant integration events are recorded in audit logs.

## Can integrations be disabled?

Yes. Organizations can disable integrations when appropriate.

## Why is Unonight the first integration?

Unonight serves as the pilot environment used to validate Aipify functionality.
