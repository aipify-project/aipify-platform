# Innovation & Impact Engine FAQ

## FAQ 1

**Question:** What is the Innovation & Impact Engine?

**Answer:** It measures how Aipify improves operational performance and customer outcomes.

## FAQ 2

**Question:** Why are baseline measurements important?

**Answer:** Baselines allow organizations to demonstrate measurable improvements over time.

## FAQ 3

**Question:** Can impact reports be exported?

**Answer:** Yes. Authorized users can generate reports for internal and external purposes.

## FAQ 4

**Question:** Why track pilot outcomes?

**Answer:** Pilot outcomes help validate the effectiveness of Aipify before broader adoption.

## FAQ 5

**Question:** Are impact activities audited?

**Answer:** Yes. Significant impact-related activities are recorded for transparency.
