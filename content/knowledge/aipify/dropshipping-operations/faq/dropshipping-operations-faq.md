# Dropshipping Operations — FAQ

## What is the Dropshipping Operations Center?

The Dropshipping Operations Center helps businesses monitor supplier performance, identify operational risks and discover improvement opportunities.

## Will Aipify manage my business automatically?

No. Aipify provides recommendations and insights, while organizations remain responsible for decisions.

## Can Aipify monitor supplier health?

Yes. Supplier monitoring helps businesses identify potential concerns before they become major issues.

## What is a Product Watchlist?

A Product Watchlist allows businesses to track products they are evaluating or actively monitoring.

## Can Aipify warn me about delivery risks?

Yes. The Operations Center may identify indicators suggesting potential delivery disruptions.

## Can Aipify suggest alternative suppliers?

Yes. Where appropriate, Aipify may recommend reviewing alternative supplier options.

## Does Aipify automatically remove products?

No. Human approval remains essential for operational changes.

## Why are operational insights important?

Operational problems often emerge gradually. Early visibility supports better decision making.

## Can Aipify identify growth opportunities?

Yes. Opportunity alerts help businesses recognize positive trends and potential areas for expansion.

## Who benefits from this functionality?

Dropshipping businesses, commerce operators, store managers and growing e-commerce teams can all benefit from operational visibility.
