# Human Wonder & Exploration Engine — FAQ (Phase 179)

## What is the Wonder & Exploration Center?

The Wonder & Exploration Center at `/app/human-wonder-exploration-engine` is part of the **Cosmic Stewardship & Multi-Generational Futures Era (171–180)**. Wonder Companion supports reflection and metadata scaffolds — **not** replacement of human judgment, responsibility, or dignity.

## Does Wonder Companion decide for humans?

**No.** Wonder Companion provides informational support only. Humans retain final accountability.

## What are the permissions?

| Permission | Purpose |

| `human_wonder_exploration.manage` | Update settings |
| `human_wonder_exploration.steward` | Record executive reviews and metadata |

## Wonder Companion supports curiosity ?

Wonder Companion supports curiosity — does NOT replace exploration or determine what deserves wonder.
