# Implementation Blueprint Phase 123 — Board & Governance Companion FAQ

## What is Phase 123?

Enterprise Intelligence **Phase 123 — Board & Governance Companion Engine** deepens the Governance & Policy Engine (Phase A.14) at `/app/governance-policy-engine` with board intelligence, briefing, governance memory, committee support, and effectiveness insights — metadata only, directors accountable.

## How is Phase 123 different from Blueprint Phase 67?

**Blueprint Phase 67** (`_bgcbp_*`) established board preparation, meeting support, strategic oversight, and risk awareness scaffolds. **Phase 123** (`_bgbp123_*`) layers the Enterprise Intelligence companion — Board Intelligence Center, briefing engine, governance memory, committee support, and effectiveness insights. All Phase 67 and A.14 dashboard fields are preserved.

## Does Phase 123 create a new route?

No. Phase 123 extends `/app/governance-policy-engine` — the same route as A.14 and Blueprint Phase 67.

## How does Executive Intelligence Phase 121 relate?

Executive Intelligence at `/app/executive-intelligence` provides leadership context and executive briefings. Phase 123 cross-links for board leadership priorities — does not duplicate RPCs.

## How does Strategic Foresight Phase 122 relate?

Strategic Foresight at `/app/strategic-foresight-engine` provides scenario and foresight planning. Phase 123 cross-links for strategic visibility — governance config remains at A.14.

## What are the companion limitations?

From `_bgbp123_companion_limitations()` — never vote, override directors, suppress information, influence independence, create conflicts of interest, or replace judgment. Effectiveness insights are process improvement — not director evaluation.

## Are financial summaries stored?

No raw financial records. Financial summaries are metadata/scaffold framing only — documented in `_bgbp123_board_dashboard_displays()`.

## How does Self Love connect?

Self Love in governance supports reflection, preparedness, respect, healthy challenge, thoughtful pacing, and long-term stewardship — principle cross-link to `/app/self-love-engine`, not personal wellbeing storage in governance tables.
