Industry Blueprints package vertical operating models — recommended skills, marketplace packs, workflows, and knowledge categories for industries like hospitality, healthcare, agency, and SaaS.

**Developer role:**
- Extend blueprints with category: industry_blueprint
- Declare required_modules and dependencies in manifest
- Coordinate with Marketplace packs and Skill Store installs
- Blueprint Agent recommends and detects mismatches — extensions should align with blueprint metadata

**Apply flow:** Blueprints apply through governed Marketplace/Skill Store paths — not direct Core modification.

See /app/industry-blueprints for tenant blueprint profiles.
