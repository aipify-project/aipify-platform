Developers publish approved apps to the Marketplace ecosystem after review.

**Partner tiers:**
- internal — Aipify-built extensions
- verified_developer — Approved external developer
- agency_partner — Implementation partner
- enterprise_partner — Large org internal apps

**Relationship to Marketplace (Phase 69):**
- marketplace_items catalog business packs, skills, and playbooks
- ecosystem_apps registry holds developer platform apps with manifests
- Approved ecosystem apps can surface in Marketplace after publish workflow

**Commercial models:** Future Marketplace pricing (free, one_time, monthly, enterprise) may apply. V1 focuses on governed distribution — not revenue share billing.

Install flow uses precheck_ecosystem_app_install with Policy Engine validation before tenant installation.
