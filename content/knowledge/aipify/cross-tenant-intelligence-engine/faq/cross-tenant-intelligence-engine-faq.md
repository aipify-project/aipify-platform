# Cross-Tenant Intelligence Engine — FAQ

## What is the Cross-Tenant Intelligence Engine?

It surfaces anonymized industry and operational trends aggregated across participating organizations — metadata only, with strict tenant isolation. Trends inform decisions; humans approve recommendations.

## How is tenant data protected?

Global insights never store organization identifiers. Participation is opt-in (`disabled`, `internal_only`, or `anonymized_contributor`), with configurable anonymization levels and category filters.

## Can my organization contribute anonymized signals?

Yes, when participation is set to `anonymized_contributor`. Only aggregate metadata counts are contributed — never raw records, PII, or identifiable business data.

## How do recommendations become organizational memory?

Approved recommendations create org-scoped outcomes that may link to Organizational Memory (A.34) via metadata-only capture hooks — always with human approval first.
