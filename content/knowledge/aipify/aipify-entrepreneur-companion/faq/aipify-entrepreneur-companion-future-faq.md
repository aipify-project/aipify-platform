# Aipify Entrepreneur Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Entrepreneur Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports entrepreneurs, founders, and small business owners with priorities, operations, growth, and sustainable business building.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Executive Companion, Finance Companion, Wellness Companion, Memory, Presence, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Entrepreneurs often carry the responsibilities of an entire organization. Aipify should help them think more clearly, stay organized, and focus on what truly moves the business forward.

## Can Aipify make strategic decisions for my business?

**No.** Aipify must never replace leadership judgment, make strategic decisions independently, override business owners, or create dependency.

## How is this different from Finance Companion?

**Finance Companion** focuses on financial awareness and planning. **Entrepreneur Companion** coordinates the **full founder workload** — operations, growth, customer relationships, financial organization, and personal balance. Finance Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## How is this different from Wellness Companion?

**Wellness Companion** supports personal wellbeing and sustainable habits. **Entrepreneur Companion** integrates balance prompts within a **business-owner context**. Wellness Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## Can insights create guilt or pressure?

No. Insights should promote awareness — never induce guilt. Self-love prompts are supportive, never patronizing.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include preparing executive summaries, organizing meetings, and generating action checklists — all requiring appropriate approvals.

## Who is Entrepreneur Companion for?

Founders and small business owners who need help coordinating competing priorities without sacrificing themselves in the process.
