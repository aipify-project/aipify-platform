# Partner & Certification Program — FAQ

## Why does Aipify have a partner program?

Partners help organizations implement, optimize, and expand their use of Aipify successfully.

## What is an Aipify Certified Partner?

A Certified Partner has demonstrated knowledge, completed training requirements, and met Aipify's quality standards.

## Do I need a partner to use Aipify?

No. Many organizations successfully deploy Aipify independently. Partners are available for organizations seeking additional expertise.

## Can consultants become certified?

Yes. Independent consultants may complete certification programs and participate in the partner ecosystem.

## Will certifications expire?

Yes. Renewal ensures certified professionals remain current with platform developments and best practices.

## How do I verify a certification?

Aipify provides digital credentials and public verification mechanisms in the Partner Portal.

## Can development agencies become partners?

Yes. Agencies specializing in integrations, custom development, and implementation services are welcome.

## How are partners evaluated?

Aipify considers customer feedback, implementation success, certification status, and ongoing performance.

## Can customers search for certified experts?

Yes. The Partner Directory allows customers to locate qualified professionals based on their needs.

## Why are certifications important?

Certifications increase confidence by ensuring that professionals possess validated expertise.
