# Sales Expert Operating System — FAQ

## What is the Sales Expert Portal?

The **Sales Expert Operating System** at `/app/sales-expert-engine` is the professional partner workspace for Aipify Sales Representatives and Sales Experts. It covers Customers, Opportunities, Pipeline, Commission Overview, training cross-links, Partner Resources, and one-to-one email — not affiliate hustle language.

## How is this different from Partner Success (A.73)?

**Partner Success A.73** tracks portfolio health for organizations managing partner relationships. **Sales Expert OS** is the partner-facing portal for experts managing their own pipeline, commissions, and customer follow-ups.

## How is this different from `/app/partners`?

**Partner Certification Phase 91** at `/app/partners` is the certification ecosystem and program directory. **Sales Expert OS** is the daily operating workspace for sales activity.

## Does Aipify support mass email campaigns?

**No.** Mass email is explicitly not supported. The portal allows **one-to-one** email with template metadata only — no raw email bodies stored in logs.

## What are the official partner tier names?

- Aipify Sales Representative
- Aipify Sales Expert
- Aipify Certified Partner
- Aipify Expert Partner

Never use **Affiliate** in customer-facing copy. See [PARTNER_TERMINOLOGY_UPDATE.md](../../../../PARTNER_TERMINOLOGY_UPDATE.md).

## How do subscriptions vs consulting work?

**Aipify subscription:** Customer ↔ Aipify (software license). **Consulting and implementation:** Customer ↔ Sales Expert (paid directly to the expert).

## Where do commissions come from?

Sales Expert commissions integrate with `organization_sales_expert_commissions` and optionally `commercial_partner_commissions` from Billing Commercial Phase 93 — metadata only.
