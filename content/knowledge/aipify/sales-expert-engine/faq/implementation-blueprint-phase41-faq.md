# Sales Performance & Recognition — FAQ (Phase 41)

## What is the Performance & Recognition tab?

**Blueprint Phase 41** adds a **Performance & Recognition** tab to the Sales Expert Operating System at `/app/sales-expert-engine`. It gives Sales Experts visibility into commissions, milestones, bell moments, and encouragement-oriented leaderboards — metadata only, tenant-scoped.

## How is this different from Gratitude & Recognition (A.89)?

**Gratitude & Recognition A.89** (Blueprint Phase 9) is organization-wide peer recognition at `/app/gratitude-recognition-engine`. **Phase 41** is Sales Expert-specific performance visibility and milestone celebration within the partner portal. Recognition Roses from customers use A.89 infrastructure — Phase 41 cross-links, it does not duplicate the engine.

## What are Bell Moments?

Bell Moments (🔔) are gentle celebrations when a Sales Expert reaches meaningful milestones — first customer, certification completion, or sustained customer outcomes. They celebrate progress, not comparison.

## What are Recognition Roses?

Recognition Roses (🌹) are **voluntary** appreciation from customers to Sales Experts. They are never required. Examples: *"Thank you for helping our organization succeed."* Distinct from Presence & Comfort A.90 comfort roses.

## Are leaderboards competitive or punitive?

**No.** Leaderboards emphasize encouragement — Most Improved, Customer Satisfaction, Knowledge Champion, Onboarding Excellence, Community Contributor. Aipify avoids aggressive competition, public shaming, and vanity metrics without customer outcomes.

## How does Self Love connect?

Sales can be emotionally demanding. Phase 41 cross-links **Self Love A.76** at `/app/self-love-engine` for sustainable pacing and celebrating progress without burnout. Performance stores milestone metadata only — Self Love influences tone.

## How are metrics measured?

Milestone progress derives from tenant-scoped customer and commission metadata — active customers, subscriptions, renewals, enterprise plan keys, and program tenure. No raw email content or PII. See the Trust connection section in the Performance tab.
