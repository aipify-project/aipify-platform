# Sales Legacy & Success — FAQ (Phase 50)

## What is the Legacy / Journey tab?

**Blueprint Phase 50** adds a **Legacy / Journey** tab (`legacy`) to the Sales Expert Operating System at `/app/sales-expert-engine`. It helps partners reflect on their Aipify journey — tenure, organizations supported, authentic milestones, impact counts, mentorship legacy, and optional recognition experiences. Metadata only.

## How is this different from OME Phase 50 at `/app/memory`?

**OME Phase 50** is **Organizational Memory** — institutional memory, decision records, and lessons learned for a **tenant organization**.

**Blueprint Phase 50** is **Sales Expert personal/business legacy** inside the **partner portal** only. Documented in `_slsbp_distinction_note()`.

## How is this different from Legacy Engine A.86?

**Legacy Engine A.86** at `/app/legacy-engine` preserves **tenant organizational wisdom** — storytelling and milestones for the customer's company.

**Phase 50** celebrates the **independent Sales Expert's** contribution arc — not org-wide legacy storage.

## What data appears on the legacy dashboard?

Aggregate metadata from Sales Expert tables:

- Tenure years
- Organizations supported and customers retained
- Demonstrations and training sessions
- Community contributions (Phase 47)
- Mentorship relationships (Phase 47)
- Milestones achieved (Phase 41)

**No customer PII**, email content, or cross-tenant benchmarks.

## What is the success timeline?

Five authentic **firsts** with companion emojis (🌹🔔🦉❤️🌍):

- First customer
- First demonstration
- First renewal conversation
- First mentee
- First international customer

Dates derive from earliest tenant records — empty until real activity exists.

## What are impact insights?

Illustrative aggregate counts such as *"47 organizations supported"* or *"123 training sessions"* — **metadata only**. Distinct from Impact Engine A.85 organizational proof; Phase 50 is partner-portal scoped.

## Are recognition experiences mandatory?

**No.** Community Builder, Trusted Advisor, Five-Year Milestone, and Legacy Contributor are **optional** scaffolds with an **authentic, not boastful** tone. Cross-link Gratitude & Recognition A.89 for voluntary appreciation.

## How does Phase 50 connect to other Sales Expert phases?

- **Phase 41** — performance milestones and encouragement leaderboards
- **Phase 47** — community stories and voluntary mentorship
- **Phase 48** — operations and service tracking metadata
- **Self Love A.76** — reflection prompts and sustainable pacing
- **Gratitude A.89** — recognition experiences (cross-link only)
- **Impact A.85** — organizational impact proof (distinct scope)

Integrate — never duplicate full engines.

## What trust rules apply?

- Milestones must be **authentic** — derived from metadata, never inflated
- Recognition is **optional**
- Legacy celebrates **character and contribution**, not revenue alone
- Experts should understand distinction from OME, Legacy A.86, and Resilience A.50
