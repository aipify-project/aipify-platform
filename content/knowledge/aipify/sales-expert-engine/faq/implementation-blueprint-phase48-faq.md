# Sales Operations & Business Management — FAQ (Phase 48)

## What is the Operations / Business tab?

**Blueprint Phase 48** adds an **Operations / Business** tab to the Sales Expert Operating System at `/app/sales-expert-engine`. It gives independent Sales Experts a business dashboard, goal scaffolds, capacity awareness, service tracking, and forecasting support — metadata only.

## How is this different from Value Realization Engine A.48?

**Value Realization Engine Phase A.48** at `/app/value-realization-engine` measures tenant outcomes — baselines, milestones, and executive value reports for customer organizations. **Blueprint Phase 48 Sales Operations** is for **independent Sales Expert businesses** — revenue visibility, goals, capacity, and service obligations in the partner portal. Documented in `_sobmbp_distinction_note()`.

## How is this different from the Dashboard and Commission Overview tabs?

The **Dashboard** tab shows engagement summary metrics. **Commission Overview** lists commission records. **Operations / Business** synthesizes implementation revenue, commission estimates, support obligations, goals, capacity guidance, and forecasting tone — a holistic independent-business view cross-linking existing SEOS data.

## Are forecasts guarantees or targets?

**No.** Forecasted commissions and trend notes are **informative metadata** — they help you plan. Phase 48 uses an **inform-not-pressure** tone. Adjust plans without guilt if reality differs.

## What are business goals?

Scaffold records in `sales_expert_business_goals` — monthly customers, revenue aspiration, certification progress, learning sessions. **You set and adjust your own targets.** Aipify provides realistic defaults, not dictates.

## How does capacity awareness work?

Companion examples 🌹❤️🦉 highlight overload, preparation, and capacity checks — sustainable pacing for independent experts. Cross-links Resource Planning A.63 and Personal Productivity A.70 without duplicating those engines.

## What about outstanding invoices?

Outstanding invoice tracking is a **metadata scaffold** — you invoice customers independently for consulting and implementation. Aipify does not replace your accounting.

## How does Self Love connect?

Building an independent Aipify business takes time. Phase 48 cross-links **Self Love A.76** for humane growth pacing — aligned with the portal **independent business notice**.

## Where is Revenue Intelligence?

**Blueprint Phase 39 Revenue Intelligence** on `/app/commercial` complements partner commission visibility — cross-linked from forecasting support, not duplicated here.
