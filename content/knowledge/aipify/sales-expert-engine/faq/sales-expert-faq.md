title: How do Sales Expert commissions work?
slug: how-do-sales-expert-commissions-work
category: sales-expert-engine
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [sales-expert, commissions, faq]
keywords: [recurring commission, partner program, stripe connect]
article_type: faq
priority: 98
---
Sales Experts receive recurring commissions when organizations subscribe to Aipify through their Sales Expert links. Commissions continue while the customer maintains an active subscription and the Sales Expert remains in good standing. Commissions are distributed through approved payout systems such as Stripe Connect. Payment schedules are determined by the Partner Program and may vary by country and compliance requirements.
