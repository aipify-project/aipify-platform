# Sales Community & Mentorship — FAQ (Phase 47)

## What is the Community tab?

**Blueprint Phase 47** adds a **Community** tab to the Sales Expert Operating System at `/app/sales-expert-engine`. It provides peer learning, voluntary mentorship scaffolds, a success story library, community recognition badges, and regional group metadata — tenant-scoped, metadata only.

## How is this different from `/app/community` (Phase 89)?

**Phase 89** at `/app/community` is **tenant org-wide** knowledge sharing and collective intelligence for the customer's organization.

**Phase 47** is the **Sales Expert peer community** — partners helping partners within the Sales Expert portal. Documented in `_scmbp_distinction_note()`.

## Is mentorship mandatory?

**No.** Mentorship is **voluntary**. Experienced Sales Experts may offer guidance; newer partners may accept or decline. Either party may pause without penalty.

## What can success stories contain?

Story **summaries only** — max **500 characters**, author display name metadata, category tags. **No customer PII**, email content, or identifiable business records.

## What are the recognition badges?

- **Mentor** 🌹 — voluntarily guides newer experts
- **Community Contributor** 🔔 — shares helpful discussions
- **Knowledge Champion** 🦉 — curates best practices

Recognition is **encouraging, not shaming** — cross-links Phase 41 leaderboards that avoid aggressive competition.

## How does this connect to the Coach tab?

**Phase 45/46 Coach** may recommend relevant community discussions, mentors, and resources. Community complements daily coaching — it does not replace it.

## Are regional groups live?

**Scaffold only.** Nordic (no/sv/da/en), Europe, and Global groups are metadata scaffolds. UI supports i18n in en/no/sv/da.

## What trust rules apply?

Respect, professionalism, constructive dialogue. **No harassment, shaming, or misleading advice** about Aipify capabilities.

## Does Aipify Group participate?

**Yes — dogfooding.** Aipify Group Sales Experts validate community tone, mentorship workflow, and recognition badges before broader partner rollout.
