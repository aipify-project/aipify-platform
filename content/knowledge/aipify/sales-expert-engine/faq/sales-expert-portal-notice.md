# Important Information for Aipify Sales Experts

Welcome to the Aipify Sales Expert Program. Before getting started, understand how the program operates.

## Independent business principle

Aipify Sales Experts operate as **independent businesses** — not as Aipify employees. By participating, you acknowledge acting as a self-employed professional or through your own registered company.

## Starting your journey

Use an existing company if you already operate a business. If not, establish an appropriate legal entity (sole proprietorship, limited company, or other approved structure in your country) before actively selling Aipify services. Consult local regulations and advisors.

## The Aipify relationship

**Aipify manages:** subscriptions, subscription commissions, Sales Expert certifications, partner resources, and product support.

**Sales Experts manage:** their own businesses, consulting services, pricing, tax obligations, and customer implementation agreements.

## Expectations

Operate professionally, represent Aipify honestly, prioritize customer success, continue learning, and respect applicable laws.
