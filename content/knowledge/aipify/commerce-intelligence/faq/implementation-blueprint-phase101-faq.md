# Implementation Blueprint Phase 101 — Commerce Intelligence Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 101 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 101 extends the Commerce Intelligence Engine (repo Phase 101) at `/app/commerce-intelligence` with **ABOS blueprint scaffolding** — wisdom-guided product discovery, trend awareness, margin analysis, and supplier insights. All baseline `_cie_*` dashboard fields are preserved.

## How is Blueprint Phase 101 different from repo Phase 101 baseline?

**Repo Phase 101** ships trend products, product discovery, margin analysis, supplier insights, opportunity scores, risk detection, store fit, and the recommendation dashboard. **Blueprint Phase 101** adds mission/philosophy metadata, six objectives, Commerce Companion guidance, limitation principles, Self Love connection, trust connection, live success criteria, and integration cross-links — layered on top without replacing baseline RPC behavior.

## How is this different from Commerce Performance Phase 104?

**Commerce Performance & Profit (Phase 104)** at `/app/commerce-performance` focuses on profit intelligence, product profitability, revenue trends, and executive reporting. **Commerce Intelligence Phase 101** focuses on discovery, trends, margins before commitment, and supplier evaluation — cross-link only, do not duplicate.

## How is this different from Revenue Intelligence Blueprint Phase 39?

**Revenue Intelligence Blueprint Phase 39** at `/app/commercial` covers subscription MRR/ARR and billing commercial intelligence. **Commerce Intelligence Phase 101** covers product and catalog opportunity intelligence — distinct surfaces.

## What is the Commerce Companion?

The **Commerce Companion** is Aipify's wisdom-guided commerce framing — not a generic AI commerce bot. It scaffolds research and evaluation with optional prompts (🦉🌹🔔). Humans approve every product import; `auto_import_disabled` remains default true.

## What are the limitation principles?

**Wisdom not speculation:** no guaranteed success promises, no impulsive trend-chasing, no popularity-only recommendations that ignore store fit or values, no ignoring margin thresholds or supplier risk.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Patience and disciplined decisions in commercial growth — *"Patience and disciplined decisions beat impulsive product launches — sustainable commerce grows one thoughtful choice at a time."* Cross-link `/app/self-love-engine` — principle only.

## What are the Phase 101 success criteria?

Live criteria include trend intelligence, product opportunity discovery, margin intelligence, supplier insights, store fit analysis, commerce strategy connection, commerce insight sources, Commerce Companion guidance, limitation principles, trust connection, Self Love connection, baseline preservation, integration links, dogfooding (Sportsklær.no), and ABOS principle.

## What is the vision phrase?

*"We understand our commercial opportunities more clearly than ever before."*

## What data does the engine store?

Product opportunity metadata, trend signal summaries, margin analyses, supplier insight scores, store fit reports, and audit events — no raw customer orders, chat, or PII. No guaranteed success language in stored summaries.

## What is the helper prefix?

Engine helpers use `_cie_*`. Blueprint Phase 101 helpers use `_cibp101_*` — they must not collide.
