# Commerce Intelligence — FAQ

## What is the Commerce Intelligence Engine?

The Commerce Intelligence Engine helps businesses discover product opportunities, analyze trends, estimate margins and evaluate suppliers.

## Can Aipify find trending products?

Yes. Aipify can identify products and categories showing signs of increased interest, but it also checks whether the opportunity makes business sense.

## Will Aipify automatically import products?

No. In this phase, Aipify provides intelligence and recommendations. Automatic product import belongs to the Product Automation Engine.

## What is Product Discovery?

Product Discovery is the process of finding products that may fit the store, customer audience, market demand and profit goals.

## How does Aipify calculate margin potential?

Aipify considers supplier cost, shipping, platform fees, payment fees, estimated ad cost, expected refunds and recommended selling price.

## Can Aipify warn me about bad products?

Yes. Aipify can detect risk signals such as poor supplier reliability, low margin, long delivery time, weak reviews or high refund risk.

## What is Supplier Insight Score?

Supplier Insight Score is Aipify's evaluation of supplier reliability, based on quality, delivery, stock stability, price stability and history.

## Does Aipify know what fits my store?

Aipify evaluates store fit based on your catalog, brand, audience, price level, market and previous performance.

## Can Aipify help dropshipping stores?

Yes. The Commerce Intelligence Engine is especially useful for dropshipping, because it helps identify trends, margins and supplier risks before launch.

## Should I sell every trending product?

No. A trending product is not always a good product. Margin, supplier quality, store fit and customer trust must also be considered.
