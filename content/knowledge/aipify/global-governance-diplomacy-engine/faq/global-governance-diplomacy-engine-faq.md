# Global Governance & Digital Diplomacy — FAQ

## What is the Global Governance Center?

The Global Governance Center at `/app/global-governance-diplomacy-engine` provides interorganizational governance frameworks, digital diplomacy scaffolds, partnership charter metadata, and policy library template references for cross-border collaboration.

## How is this different from Governance & Policy A.14?

**Governance & Policy A.14** at `/app/governance-policy-engine` manages **tenant-internal** policies, approvals, and compliance workflows.

**Global Governance & Digital Diplomacy (Phase 144)** focuses on **interorganizational** partnership governance, diplomacy preparation, and executive alignment — cross-link only, do not duplicate `_gpe_*` RPCs.

## How is this different from Ecosystem Governance Phase 119?

**Ecosystem Governance** at `/app/ecosystem-governance` spans GP/companion/certification ecosystem oversight.

**Phase 144** addresses partnership charters, digital diplomacy, and cross-cultural collaboration for interorganizational leadership — cross-link only, do not duplicate `_egce_*` / `_egcbp119_*`.

## How is this different from Joint Operations Phase 143?

**Joint Operations** at `/app/joint-operations-engine` provides governed shared workspaces and partnership records for cross-org collaboration.

**Phase 144** provides governance principles, charter scaffolds, and diplomacy preparation — charters may link to joint operations partnerships when both are active.

## Does Aipify replace legal counsel?

**No.** Phase 144 complements legal processes — it never replaces legal or regulatory advice. Policy library entries are template keys and metadata summaries referencing the Knowledge Center, not raw legal documents. Consult qualified counsel for binding agreements.

## Does governance impose uniformity?

No. Principled collaboration — not centralized control. Organizations retain autonomy. Companions support preparation and navigation; executives decide outcomes.

## What can companions do?

Governance companions may help with policy navigation, partnership preparation, governance recommendations, knowledge retrieval, reflection, and alignment summaries. They do **not** override executive authority, dictate outcomes, enforce ideology, or suppress dissent.

## What security requirements apply?

Governance audit logs, executive approval histories, partnership access controls, RBAC, and 2FA enforcement (`/app/settings/two-factor`).

## What terminology should I use?

Always use **Growth Partner** — never Affiliate. Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) — not generic AI chatbot category.
