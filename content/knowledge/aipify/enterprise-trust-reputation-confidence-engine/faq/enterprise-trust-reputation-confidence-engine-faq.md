# Enterprise Trust, Reputation & Customer Confidence Engine — FAQ

## How is trust measured?

Trust is measured through a composite Trust Score combining reliability, transparency, service quality, customer confidence, and trust health metrics. Open the Trust Center at `/app/trust-center` to view live scores, trust signals, and trend intelligence for your organization.

## How is reliability measured?

The Reliability Engine tracks availability, incident history, service stability, recovery performance, and operational reliability. Metrics contribute to the Reliability Score and Platform Availability percentage displayed in the Trust Overview.

## How are incidents handled?

Incidents are recorded with severity, root cause, resolution status, recovery actions, and lessons learned. The Trust Recovery Framework supports incident communication, status updates, customer notifications, and post-incident reviews. All incident lifecycle events are audited.

## How is transparency maintained?

The Transparency Engine provides visibility into platform status, maintenance, service changes, feature rollouts, and operational history. Administrators with manage permissions can publish transparency updates that increase the Transparency Score.

## How is customer confidence measured?

Customer Confidence combines satisfaction signals, retention trends, Companion trust, platform trust, support experience, and operational confidence. Confidence milestones such as 30 days without incident or 99.9% availability are tracked and celebrated.

## How does Aipify build trust?

Aipify builds trust through thousands of small interactions — consistent reliability, honest transparency, enterprise security, quality service, and Companion presence that prepares rather than pressures. Trust metadata is tenant-scoped; your organization sees its own confidence signals without cross-tenant exposure.

## How is this different from Trust & Reputation Engine?

**Trust & Reputation Engine** (`/app/trust-reputation-engine`) focuses on verified organization ecosystems and reputation networks. **Trust Center** (`/app/trust-center`) is the unified enterprise trust layer — reliability, transparency, incident management, reputation, and customer confidence analytics.
