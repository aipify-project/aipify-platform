# Strategic Intelligence Foundation Engine FAQ

## FAQ 1

**Question:** What is Strategic Intelligence?

**Answer:** Strategic Intelligence helps organizations identify opportunities, risks, and priorities using operational insights.

## FAQ 2

**Question:** Does Aipify make decisions automatically?

**Answer:** No. Strategic Intelligence supports human decision-making with recommendations and analysis.

## FAQ 3

**Question:** Can organizations ignore recommendations?

**Answer:** Yes. Organizations retain full control over strategic decisions.

## FAQ 4

**Question:** What information is used to generate insights?

**Answer:** Insights are based on approved organizational data and operational patterns.

## FAQ 5

**Question:** Are strategic activities audited?

**Answer:** Yes. Significant strategic actions and decisions are recorded.
