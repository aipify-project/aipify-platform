# Implementation Blueprint Phase 79 — Strategic Intelligence Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 79 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 79 extends the Strategic Intelligence Foundation Engine (Phase A.31) with deeper strategic awareness — pattern recognition, opportunity identification, leadership briefings, and companion guidance. It layers on top of Blueprint Phase 17 (`_sif_*` helpers) at the same route `/app/strategic-intelligence-foundation-engine`.

## How is this different from Phase 17?

**Phase 17** (`_sif_*`) focuses on operational signal detection — insight categories, scan capability, and explainable metadata from support, quality, and customer success. **Phase 79** (`_sibp79_*`) adds strategic awareness — connecting dots across organizational knowledge, meeting insights, cross-functional intelligence, and external market context. Both share the same route and `strategic_insights` table.

## How is this different from /app/operations (Repo Phase 79)?

**Autonomous Operations Center (Repo Phase 79)** at `/app/operations` is a distinct product surface for autonomous operations. **ABOS Blueprint Phase 79** shares the phase number but maps to Strategic Intelligence at `/app/strategic-intelligence-foundation-engine` — phase number collision only.

## How is this different from /app/strategy (Phase 81)?

**Strategic Intelligence & Opportunity (Phase 81)** at `/app/strategy` is the legacy strategic scorecard. **Strategic Intelligence Foundation Engine A.31 + Blueprint Phase 79** is the canonical ABOS Strategic Intelligence surface — cross-link only.

## How is this different from Predictive Insights (Blueprint Phase 74)?

**Predictive Insights A.66 / Blueprint Phase 74** (`/app/predictive-insights-engine`) offers forecasts and preparedness. **Strategic Intelligence Phase 79** cultivates understanding and strategic awareness — not prediction. Cross-link for forward-looking context.

## What are the limitation principles?

- **No intelligence as certainty** — insights are possibilities, not conclusions
- **No fear-driven interpretations** — calm, reflective strategic copy
- **No oversimplification** — complex contexts acknowledged
- **Understanding not prediction** — humans decide strategy

## What are strategic observations with 🦉🌹🔔?

🦉 Wisdom phrases invite reflection on emerging themes. 🌹 Self Love phrases celebrate collaborative execution strength. 🔔 Bell moments surface leadership attention on evolving trends. All encourage reflection — not conclusions.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self Love (A.76) supports perspective, confidence, humility, and continuous learning. *"Wisdom often emerges through sustained reflection rather than immediate certainty."* Principle only — Strategic Intelligence stores metadata, not wellbeing content.

## What are the Phase 79 success criteria?

Computed live on the dashboard via `_sibp79_success_criteria()` — strategic awareness, leadership conversations, opportunity attention, organizational learning, long-term thinking, limitation principles, and cross-links.

## What intelligence sources are planned?

Organizational knowledge, strategic initiatives, Meeting Companion A.61, operational observations, Cross-Functional Intelligence Phase 70, customer feedback themes, and external market developments — all metadata only, future approved sources.

## Where does dogfooding happen?

**Aipify Group** validates internally — ecosystem evolution, product strategy, Sales Expert growth, organizational development. **Unonight** is the first external pilot.
