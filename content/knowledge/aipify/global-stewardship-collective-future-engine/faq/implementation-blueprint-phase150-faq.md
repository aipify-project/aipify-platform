# Global Stewardship & Collective Future Engine — Phase 150 FAQ

## What is Global Stewardship?

Global Stewardship is Aipify's framework for long-term responsible leadership — helping organizations reflect on collective future, executive preparedness, and stewardship of influence. It aggregates visibility across the Global Intelligence Era (141–150) without duplicating phase-specific RPCs. Stewards not rulers — companions support perspective; executives retain accountability.

## Does Aipify define organizational priorities?

No. The Stewardship Companion provides reflection prompts, scenario exploration scaffolding, and knowledge recommendations. It does **not** determine priorities, impose ideology, assign moral superiority, or override executive leadership. Humans define priorities; Aipify prepares perspective.

## How does this relate to Phase 140?

Phase 140 (Augmented Organization Engine) is the **Autonomous Organization Era (131–140) capstone** at `/app/augmented-organization-engine` — focused on human-companion symbiosis. Phase 150 is the **Global Intelligence Era (141–150) capstone** at `/app/global-stewardship-collective-future-engine` — focused on long-term stewardship and collective future reflection. Different eras, different capstones — cross-link only.

## Why Self Love in stewardship?

Self Love (A.76) connects compassion, empathy, humility, curiosity, mentorship, recognition, lifelong learning, and collective care to stewardship reflection. Responsible leadership requires inner steadiness — not surveillance or judgment of people. Cross-link `/app/self-love-engine` for wellbeing principles; do not duplicate Self Love RPCs.
