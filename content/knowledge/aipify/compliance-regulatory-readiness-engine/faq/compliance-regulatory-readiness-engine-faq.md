# Compliance & Regulatory Readiness Engine FAQ

## FAQ 1

**Question:** What is the Compliance Engine?

**Answer:** The Compliance Engine helps organizations maintain visibility over important governance and regulatory activities.

## FAQ 2

**Question:** Does Aipify provide legal advice?

**Answer:** No. Aipify supports operational readiness and documentation but does not replace professional legal guidance.

## FAQ 3

**Question:** Can organizations define their own review schedules?

**Answer:** Yes. Review frequencies can be configured according to organizational requirements.

## FAQ 4

**Question:** Why are retention policies important?

**Answer:** Retention policies help organizations manage information responsibly and consistently.

## FAQ 5

**Question:** Are compliance activities audited?

**Answer:** Yes. Significant compliance-related activities are recorded for transparency.
