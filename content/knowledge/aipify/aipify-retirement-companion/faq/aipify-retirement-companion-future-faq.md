# Aipify Retirement Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Retirement Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports individuals transitioning into retirement with planning, routines, social engagement, wellbeing, and meaningful life goals.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Wellness Companion, Finance Companion, Legacy Companion, Memory, Presence, and Action Framework foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Retirement should not feel like the end of purpose — it should create space for new experiences, relationships, and opportunities.

## Can Aipify provide financial or medical advice?

**No.** Aipify must never replace financial advisors or healthcare professionals, or make major life decisions independently.

## How is this different from Elder Care Companion?

**Elder Care Companion** supports aging and caregiver coordination with dignity-first design. **Retirement Companion** focuses on the **retirement transition** — lifestyle planning, purpose, social engagement, and fulfillment. Distinct scopes.

## How is this different from Wellness Companion?

**Wellness Companion** supports general personal wellbeing. **Retirement Companion** applies wellbeing, routines, and balance within a **retirement life-stage context**. Wellness Companion is a **prerequisite**.

## Can insights create pressure?

No. Insights should encourage fulfillment — never create pressure. Life transition support is supportive, never prescriptive.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include coordinating reminders, printing travel plans, and generating retirement planning checklists — all requiring appropriate approvals.

## Who is Retirement Companion for?

Individuals navigating retirement who want structure, social connection, and purpose without sacrificing autonomy or dignity.
