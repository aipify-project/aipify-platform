# Implementation Blueprint Phase 73 — Organizational Continuity Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 73 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 73 extends Continuity, Resilience & Crisis Management (Phase 80) with Organizational Continuity framing — knowledge continuity, role continuity, succession support, and transition preparation alongside existing crisis/incident capabilities.

## Where does it live?

`/app/continuity` — no new route. Blueprint helpers use `_ocontbp_*`; engine helpers remain `_cnt_*`.

## How is this different from Value Engine repo Phase 73?

Value Engine at `/app/value` provides impact analytics and ROI tracking. ABOS Blueprint Phase 73 shares the number 73 but is organizational continuity at `/app/continuity` — a repo phase number collision, not the same feature.

## How is this different from Organizational Memory Phase 55?

Organizational Memory Engine at `/app/organizational-memory-engine` (Blueprint Phase 55) focuses on companion/memory continuity across operational, relationship, learning, and companion categories. Phase 73 focuses on people/knowledge/succession/transitions for critical processes — cross-link only, do not duplicate.

## How is this different from Organizational Resilience A.50?

Organizational Resilience at `/app/organizational-resilience-engine` provides scenario simulations and structured reviews. Phase 73 complements with backup ownership, knowledge transfer, and succession awareness — cross-link only.

## Is Phase 80 incident mode preserved?

Yes. Crisis/incident mode, recovery actions, readiness scoring, and all Phase 80 dashboard fields are preserved. Phase 73 adds a people/knowledge/succession framing layer.

## What is knowledge continuity?

- Institutional knowledge preservation (KC, OME cross-links)
- Documentation support for critical processes
- Knowledge transfer recommendations
- Cross-training via backup ownership chains

Metadata only — no individual performance evaluation.

## What is role continuity?

- 🦉 Responsibilities concentrated in a single role — cross-training opportunity
- 🌹 Cross-training strengthens sustainable coverage
- 🔔 Transition planning deserves early preparation

Preparedness not blame.

## What is succession support?

Leadership transition preparation, responsibility mapping from backup assignments, knowledge transfer guidance, and continuity planning — advisory only; leaders decide timing.

## What are the privacy principles?

- NO individual judgment
- NO punitive interpretations
- NO hidden evaluations
- Metadata only — strengthen systems, not evaluate personal worth

## What is the Self Love connection?

Shared responsibility, reduced dependency, sustainable workloads, and collective appreciation. *"No individual should feel the entire organization depends exclusively upon them."* Cross-linked to `/app/self-love-engine` as a principle only.

## What are the Phase 73 success criteria?

Operational resilience, knowledge continuity, improved transitions, reduced dependency risks, onboarding effectiveness, companion guidance, leadership insights, privacy/trust principles, and integration distinctions — computed live via `_ocontbp_success_criteria()`.

## What is dogfooding?

Aipify Group validates leadership transitions, product knowledge, Sales Expert continuity, and operational resilience internally. Unonight pilots commerce operational continuity.
