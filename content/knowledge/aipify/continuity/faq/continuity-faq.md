title: How is Continuity Readiness measured?
slug: how-is-continuity-readiness-measured
category: continuity
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [continuity, readiness, faq]
article_type: faq
---
Continuity Readiness Score (0–100) measures backup coverage, recovery preparedness, escalation readiness, process redundancy, incident documentation, and crisis communication preparedness. Bands: Highly Prepared (90+), Prepared (75–89), Improvement Recommended (60–74), Resilience Concerns (40–59), Critical Gap (below 40).

title: What are incident severity levels?
slug: incident-severity-levels
category: continuity
language: en
visibility: authenticated
status: published
tags: [continuity, incidents, faq]
article_type: faq
---
Level 1: Localized (minor disruption). Level 2: Departmental (support degradation). Level 3: Organizational (critical outage). Level 4: Critical Crisis (major cybersecurity event or extended shutdown).
