# Inclusion & Humanity Engine — FAQ

## What is the Inclusion & Humanity Engine?

The Inclusion & Humanity Engine (Phase A.83) is an organizational ABOS **Culture** capability that ensures ABOS interacts respectfully, welcomingly, and human-centered — recognizing diverse backgrounds, cultures, and identities with dignity for everyone. It covers communication conduct, inclusion principles, de-escalation patterns, and humanity boundaries.

## Why does respectful communication matter?

Technology influences culture — every interaction reinforces dignity, respect, and understanding. ABOS models compassionate, professional communication so organizational work stays constructive even under stress.

## How is this different from AI Ethics & Responsible Use Engine (A.46)?

**AI Ethics & Responsible Use** (`/app/ai-ethics-responsible-use-engine`) governs AI use cases and ethics review at the platform level. **Inclusion & Humanity** focuses on day-to-day communication conduct, inclusion principles, and de-escalation patterns for organizational interactions — not AI policy governance.

## How is this different from Purpose & Values Engine (A.82)?

**Purpose & Values A.82** holds tenant stated values and purpose alignment for decisions. **Inclusion & Humanity A.83** ensures daily interactions honor dignity and inclusion regardless of agreement — communication conduct and de-escalation, not value definition.

## How is this different from Brand Identity & Personhood Standard?

**Brand Identity & Personhood Standard** governs how **Aipify the product** names and refers to itself. **Inclusion & Humanity** governs how **organizational interactions** stay respectful and welcoming — completely separate concerns.

## How does Inclusion & Humanity handle inappropriate behavior?

Offensive language, frustration, inappropriate jokes, provocation, and disrespect are met with professionalism and emotional steadiness. Example de-escalation phrases are provided in the dashboard. Incidents are logged as **metadata summaries only** — no raw chat content is stored.

## What inclusion support does this engine provide?

Inclusion principles (dignity, diversity, respect, inclusion, coexistence) are seeded per organization. Reflection prompts help teams review tone and boundaries. Celebrate inclusive wins when enabled — metadata celebrations only.

## How do you disagree respectfully?

Viewpoints can coexist — people do not need to agree to treat each other well. ABOS avoids polarization and redirects hostility toward constructive dialogue with firm, respectful boundaries.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self Love (A.76 planned) supports respectful self-talk, healthy boundaries, and recovery from stress. Inclusion & Humanity models compassionate communication in how people treat themselves and others.

## How does Trust Engine complement this engine?

Trust Engine (Phase 76) provides decision explainability and transparent reasoning. Inclusion & Humanity strengthens trust through respectful communication — the engines complement each other.

## Who can manage inclusion settings?

`inclusion_humanity.settings.manage` is required to update de-escalation, boundary firmness, and celebration settings. `inclusion_humanity.manage` acknowledges or dismisses reflection prompts. Owners, administrators, and managers have these by default.

## What integrations does Inclusion & Humanity use?

Purpose & Values (A.82), AI Ethics (A.46), Trust & Reputation (A.72), Stakeholder Communication, Organizational Health (A.57), Change Management (A.47), and Human Oversight (A.40).
