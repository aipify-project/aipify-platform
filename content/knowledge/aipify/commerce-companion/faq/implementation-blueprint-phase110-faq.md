# Implementation Blueprint Phase 110 — Commerce Companion Engine FAQ

## What is Phase 110 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 110 extends the Commerce Companion Engine (repo Phase 110) at `/app/commerce-companion` with **ABOS blueprint scaffolding** — unified daily commerce Companion with holistic visibility, morning briefings, and cross-module integration links. All baseline `_ccom_*` dashboard fields are preserved.

## How is this different from Commerce Intelligence Phase 101?

**Commerce Intelligence (Phase 101)** at `/app/commerce-intelligence` is authoritative for opportunity discovery, trends, margins, and supplier evaluation. **Commerce Companion (Phase 110)** is the **unified hub** — aggregates visibility and briefings across all commerce modules without duplicating domain RPCs.

## Does Phase 110 replace Phases 101–109?

**No.** Phase 110 provides holistic visibility and daily briefings. Domain engines Phases 101–109 remain authoritative for their operations. Commerce Companion cross-links to each module route.

## What is pressure_free_mode?

Default **true** — stewardship not urgency. Commerce Companion avoids reactive pressure framing. No auto-actions.

## What is the Commerce Companion personality?

Encouraging (🌹), insightful (🦉), human-centered (❤️), proactive (🔔) — partnership not pressure. Use **Commerce Companion** or **Aipify** — never generic "AI" per Aipify-first language policy.

## What are the morning briefing examples?

🌹 Good morning — calm daily overview · 🦉 Revenue and profit metadata summarized · ❤️ No urgent pressure · 🔔 Operational signals for human review

## What are the limitation principles?

No reactive pressure, no growth-at-any-cost, no revenue-only optimization, no operational pressure bypassing human accountability in domain modules.

## What is the vision phrase?

*"We no longer manage our commerce operations alone."*

## What data does the engine store?

Metadata summaries only — visibility snapshots, guidance items, operational alert summaries, opportunity signals, profitability coaching notes, integration health, and audit events. No raw orders, chat, or PII.

## What is the helper prefix?

Engine helpers use `_ccom_*`. Blueprint Phase 110 helpers use `_ccombp110_*` — they must not collide.

## What cross-links does Phase 110 include?

Phases 101–109 commerce modules, Command Center (distinct), Meeting Companion Phase 72, Knowledge Center A.5, Self Love A.76.
