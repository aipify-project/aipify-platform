# Implementation Blueprint Phase 10 — Wisdom & Reflection Interventions Foundation FAQ

## What is Phase 10 of the Implementation Blueprint?

Phase 10 aligns the Wisdom Intervention Protocol (Phase A.94) with ABOS wisdom and reflection intervention requirements — pause, reflect, consider consequences, and preserve human autonomy.

## How is this different from Wisdom Engine A.93?

**Wisdom Engine A.93** (`/app/wisdom-engine`) synthesizes experience into broader guidance over time. **Wisdom Intervention Protocol A.94** (`/app/wisdom-intervention-protocol`) offers pre-send reflection prompts and sleep-on-it nudges for a single communication moment. Phase 10 extends A.94 — cross-link to A.93 only.

## Is Pause & Reflection a separate engine?

No. Pause & Reflection Protocol is integrated into Wisdom Intervention Protocol — one dashboard, one route at `/app/wisdom-intervention-protocol`.

## What intervention scenarios are supported?

- **Communication** — caps, aggressive wording, emotional charge, late-night emails
- **Decision** — high-risk approvals, org changes, governance, escalation
- **Operational** — failed processes, burnout indicators, unsustainable practices, doc neglect

## What is the sleep on it principle?

Save draft instead of sending immediately, revisit tomorrow, seek a second opinion when stakes are high, pause before escalation. **Clarity, not delay.**

## What are the communication examples with 🌹 and 🦉?

🌹 Self Love phrases encourage patience and self-compassion. 🦉 Wisdom phrases offer gentle reflection before send — perspective, not control.

## What is the Self Love connection?

Self Love (A.76) supports patience, recovery, sustainable decisions, and self-compassion in intervention tone. Wisdom Intervention does not store wellbeing content — Self Love is a principle influence only.

## What are the boundaries?

**May:** recommend reflection, suggest draft, offer perspective, record metadata outcomes.

**May not:** prevent sending, override autonomy, store raw content, force delays, assume malicious intent.

## What are the Phase 10 success criteria?

Protocol enabled, active prompts configured, signals recorded (metadata only), outcomes tracked, sleep-on-it nudges available, transparency and autonomy messaging present — computed live on the dashboard.

## Where does dogfooding happen?

**Aipify Group** validates internally on support and executive communications. **Unonight** is the first external pilot for late-night nudges and high-risk communication reflection.

## What data is stored?

Metadata only — signal summaries (max 500 chars), suggested intervention labels, and user-chosen outcomes. No raw email, chat, or message body storage.
