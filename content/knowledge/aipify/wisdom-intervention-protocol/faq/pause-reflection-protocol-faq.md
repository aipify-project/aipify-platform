# Pause & Reflection Protocol — FAQ

**Implemented via Wisdom Intervention Protocol (Phase A.94)** at `/app/wisdom-intervention-protocol`.

## What is the Pause & Reflection Protocol?

A companion guidance layer within Wisdom Intervention that offers a brief pause before sending emotionally charged communications. It creates space between emotion and action — without removing your autonomy.

## Is this a separate product module?

No. Pause & Reflection is integrated into Wisdom Intervention Protocol (A.94). One dashboard, one route — no duplicate engine.

## When does Aipify suggest a pause?

- Aggressive or confrontational wording
- Excessive caps or shouting tone
- Emotionally charged drafts
- Important messages composed late at night
- High-risk communications under stress
- Repeated rapid send attempts

## Can Aipify block me from sending?

Never permanently. Aipify may suggest reflection, saving a draft, or revisiting tomorrow — you always decide whether to send, revise, postpone, or dismiss.

## What data does Aipify store?

Metadata only: signal type, approved summary (max 500 characters), suggested intervention label, and your chosen outcome. No raw email, chat, or message body content.

## What are Self Love rose phrases?

Gentle 🌹 reminders that strong feelings are valid and that pausing can be kindness to your future self — for example, *Tomorrow often brings clarity — sleeping on it is wisdom, not weakness.*

## How is this different from Wisdom Engine (A.93)?

Wisdom Engine synthesizes experience into broader guidance over time. Wisdom Intervention (including Pause & Reflection) focuses on a single pre-send moment — pause between impulse and action.

## How is this different from Inclusion & Humanity (A.83)?

Inclusion & Humanity supports organizational de-escalation and inclusion. Pause & Reflection supports personal pause before a message you might regret.
