# Executive Strategic Decision Cockpit — Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is the Executive Strategic Decision Cockpit?

A dedicated leadership view that surfaces critical decisions, organization health, strategic execution progress, alerts, and opportunities — without searching across multiple modules.

## How does Aipify prioritize executive attention?

By decision urgency, risk level, strategic impact, deadlines, and escalations. Aipify **guides** focus; executives retain final authority.

## How are strategic opportunities identified?

From approved actions, portfolio alignment, impact analysis, and operational patterns — each with expected benefit, effort, risk, and confidence estimates.

## Can Aipify make executive decisions?

**No.** Aipify prepares briefings, queues, and recommendations. Executives remain accountable for organizational decisions.

## How are confidence scores generated?

From risk indicators, dependency stability, historical completion patterns, and workload signals — always as **estimates**, never certainty.

---

**Principle:** Aipify supports executives. Executives remain accountable for organizational decisions.
