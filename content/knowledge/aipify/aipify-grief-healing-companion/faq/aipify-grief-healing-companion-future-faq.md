# Aipify Grief & Healing Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Grief & Healing Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that provides gentle, practical support during periods of loss — coordinating responsibilities, preserving memories, and encouraging connection with support networks.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Legacy Companion, Family Companion, Wellness Companion, Memory, Presence, and Privacy foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Grief is a deeply human experience. Aipify should never attempt to remove grief — it should help people carry the practical burdens that often accompany it.

## Can Aipify provide grief counseling or therapy?

**No.** Aipify must never replace therapists or grief counselors, offer psychological treatment, define how grief should be experienced, or rush the healing process.

## How is this different from Life Transitions Companion?

**Life Transitions Companion** covers many transition types including practical bereavement tasks. **Grief & Healing Companion** is a **dedicated, dignity-first module** for loss — memory preservation, healing reflections, and compassionate support network encouragement.

## Can Aipify simulate a deceased person?

**No.** Aipify must never simulate deceased individuals without explicit authorization.

## Can insights pressure me to heal faster?

No. Insights should remain compassionate — never create pressure. Grief has no timeline; healing is not linear.

## What data does this module store?

When implemented, it stores **metadata, checklists, dates, and memory organization summaries only** — with exceptional privacy care. Not therapy notes or raw grief journals without governed consent.

## What actions can Aipify take?

Examples include coordinating reminders, printing checklists, and preparing remembrance materials — all requiring user approval.

## Who is Grief & Healing Companion for?

Individuals and families experiencing loss who need practical organization and gentle support while preserving dignity and autonomy.
