# Aipify Community Companion — FAQ (Future Module)

## What is Aipify Community Companion?

A **Companion Module** within the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS) that supports communities, clubs, and membership organizations with engagement, safety, onboarding, and moderator assistance.

## When will it be available?

The module is **strategically approved and planted** for future development. It will not ship until Companion Core, Aipify Verify, Community Safety, Memory, Presence, and Governance foundations are established.

## What is the core principle?

Communities thrive when people feel welcomed, valued, and connected.

## Can Aipify replace human moderators?

**No.** Aipify supports moderators by surfacing violations, prioritizing review queues, and preparing summaries — **human moderation remains responsible for final decisions.**

## Can Aipify manipulate member engagement?

**No.** Recommendations must prioritize member value — not platform addiction. Aipify must never manipulate member behavior or encourage unhealthy engagement patterns.

## How does Verify integrate?

**Aipify Verify** is a prerequisite — member verification indicators, trust signals, community safety enhancements, and anti-fraud support.

## How is this different from Community & Collective Intelligence (Phase 89)?

**Phase 89** focuses on organizational collective learning and wisdom. **Community Companion** focuses on **member journey, engagement, and community health** for clubs, platforms, and membership organizations.

## What are community health levels?

When implemented: **Vulnerable · Stable · Healthy · Thriving** — based on participation, retention, moderation workload, and safety indicators (aggregates only).

## What data does Community Companion store?

When implemented, it stores **community aggregates and engagement metadata only** — not raw member conversations or DMs. Community leaders control governance and privacy settings.
