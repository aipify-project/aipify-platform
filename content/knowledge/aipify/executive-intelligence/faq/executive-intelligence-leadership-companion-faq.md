# Executive Intelligence & Leadership Companion — FAQ

## What is the Executive Intelligence Center?

The Executive Intelligence Center at `/app/executive-intelligence` is Aipify's unified Leadership Companion hub for the Enterprise Intelligence Era (Phases 121–130). It brings together strategic visibility, executive briefings, decision support links, priority alignment, risk and opportunity signals, organizational health aggregates, executive memory, and Executive Companion guidance — without replacing human judgment.

## How is this different from Executive Insights (A.35)?

Executive Insights at `/app/executive-insights-engine` focuses on executive reporting, organization health summaries, and Since Last Time metadata. Phase 121 is the **unified Executive Intelligence Center** — a Leadership Companion hub that cross-links Executive Insights and related surfaces but does **not duplicate** Executive Insights RPCs.

## Does Aipify make decisions for leaders?

No. The Executive Companion **informs, prepares, and challenges respectfully**. Humans remain accountable. Companions never override authority, issue binding directives, hide uncertainty, suppress dissent, or replace human judgment.

## What data does executive memory store?

Executive memory stores **metadata summaries only** — past decisions, commitments, priorities, lessons learned, meeting outcomes, key discussions, follow-up obligations, and milestones. No raw meeting transcripts, chat content, email, or PII.

## Is this employee surveillance?

No. Organizational health signals are **aggregate trends only** — early awareness, not surveillance. Individual employee monitoring is explicitly out of scope.

## What briefings are supported?

Five briefing types: daily executive brief, weekly leadership review, monthly strategic review, quarterly business summary, and annual reflection report. Briefings emphasize relevance, context, actionability, and transparency.
