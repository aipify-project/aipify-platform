# Is there more than one Aipify?

No. **There is only one Aipify.** Users interact with Aipify as a single trusted business companion across the platform.

# Are Skills separate AIs?

No. Skills, Centers, Specialists, and Companions are **extensions of Aipify** — business capabilities you activate, not separate assistant identities or multiple AI products.

# Can Aipify introduce a new AI assistant brand?

No. Aipify must not create multiple AIs or separate assistant personas. New capabilities belong to Aipify and are named as capabilities (Center, Specialist, Engine, Companion), not as standalone AI brands.

# How does this relate to the Skills Marketplace?

Skill cards use professional capability names (Support Specialist, Analytics Center) without exposing "AI" in the title. Those names describe **what Aipify can do for you** — all within one Aipify relationship.
