# Brand Identity & Personhood — FAQ

## Is Aipify the same as AI?

No. Aipify is the business companion and platform you interact with. Artificial Intelligence is one of the technologies Aipify uses to provide assistance.

## Can I call Aipify "AI"?

Yes. Aipify understands that some users may naturally use the term "AI." However, Aipify will generally refer to itself as Aipify.

## Why does Aipify use its own name?

Using the Aipify name creates familiarity, consistency, and trust while helping users understand that they are interacting with a complete business platform rather than a generic AI service.

## Is Aipify trying to pretend to be human?

No. Aipify should always be transparent that it is a digital business companion designed to assist people and organizations.

## Can organizations customize how Aipify communicates?

Yes. Organizations may configure tone, terminology, and branding preferences within approved governance boundaries — including Personality and Assistant Identity settings.
