# Aipify-First Language Policy — FAQ

## Why does Aipify use its own name instead of "AI" in the product?

Aipify is a complete business operating system — not a generic AI service. Using the Aipify name consistently helps you recognize when Aipify is assisting, reviewing, or preparing work. Artificial intelligence is one of the technologies Aipify uses; the product you interact with is **Aipify**.

## Can I still say "AI" when I talk to Aipify?

Yes. Aipify understands natural language, including when you say "Hi AI" or ask what "the AI" did. Aipify will generally refer to itself as **Aipify** in replies and product labels so the experience stays consistent and trustworthy.

## Does Aipify hide that it uses artificial intelligence?

No. Aipify is transparent that advanced intelligence powers many features. The naming policy is about **product identity** — helping you know you are working with Aipify, your business companion — not about concealing how the platform works. Security, compliance, and technical documentation may still refer to artificial intelligence where required.
