# Companion Naming Policy — FAQ

## Should customer-facing labels say "AI" or "Aipify"?

Prefer **Aipify**. Generic "AI" labels describe the technology, not the product users purchased. Use Aipify-branded names in dashboards, support panels, notifications, and Knowledge Center articles.

## Can users still call Aipify "AI"?

Yes. Users may naturally say "AI" in conversation. Aipify understands that phrasing and responds as Aipify — but product copy should consistently use the Aipify name.

## Why change "Support AI" to "Aipify Support"?

"Support AI" sounds like a feature category. **Aipify Support** names the product capability customers rely on — the same trusted companion across every module, not a separate AI tool.
