# Aipify Group AS — Brand Foundation

**Official company name:** Aipify Group AS  
**Headquarters:** Bergen, Norway

## Official brand statement

**Bergen.**  
**Norway. For the world.**

## Brand meaning

**Bergen** — Great ideas do not only come from Silicon Valley. Aipify was founded in Bergen, shaped by Norwegian values of trust, quality, and long-term thinking.

**Norway** — Technology should be built responsibly, transparently, and with people at its center.

**For the world** — Business challenges are universal. Aipify supports organizations everywhere, regardless of geography, language, or industry.

## Brand principles

- Humble enough to listen.
- Ambitious enough to think globally.
- Disciplined enough to build for the long term.
- Responsible enough to earn trust.
- Curious enough to keep improving.

## Founding principle

> Our ambition is global. Our values remain Norwegian.

**Aipify Group AS** — Bergen. Norway. For the world.
