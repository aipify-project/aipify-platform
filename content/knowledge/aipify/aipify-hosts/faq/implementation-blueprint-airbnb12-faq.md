# Aipify Hosts — Reporting & Executive Insights (Phase Airbnb 12)

## What is the Hosts Reports Center?

Executive visibility for hospitality operators — occupancy, revenue, property performance, guest experience, and operational KPIs in one dashboard.

## What executive metrics are shown?

Occupancy rate, revenue this month, revenue YTD, average length of stay, guest satisfaction score, active incidents, open maintenance tasks, and team completion rate.

## Can I compare properties?

Yes. Property comparison includes revenue, occupancy, incidents, guest satisfaction, and maintenance burden side by side.

## What report categories are available?

Occupancy, revenue, property performance, guest experience, team performance, cleaning, maintenance, incidents, and licensing reports.

## Can I export reports?

Yes. PDF, Excel, and CSV exports are supported. Every export is audit logged.

## Can I schedule recurring reports?

Yes. Daily, weekly, or monthly delivery by email or dashboard. Schedule changes are audit logged.

## Route

`/app/aipify-hosts/reports`
