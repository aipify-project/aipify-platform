# Aipify Hosts — Property Acquisition & Expansion Intelligence (Phase Airbnb 06)

## Does Aipify make investment decisions for hosts?

No. Aipify Hosts provides expansion intelligence, opportunity scores, and playbooks as recommendations only. Final investment decisions remain with the host.

## What is the Expansion Readiness Score?

An organizational preparedness indicator based on operational maturity, team capacity, financial stability, and governance readiness — not a guarantee of acquisition success.
