# Aipify Hosts — Tasks Center (Phase Airbnb 18)

## What is the Hosts Tasks Center?

A workspace to standardize hospitality operations — active, scheduled, and completed tasks plus repeatable playbooks and templates.

## What task categories are supported?

Cleaning, Maintenance, Inspection, Guest Preparation, Compliance, and Team Administration.

## What playbooks are included?

Arrival, Departure, Cleaning, Maintenance, Inspection, and Incident — each with structured steps.

## Does it support recurring tasks?

Yes. Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual recurrence via templates and scheduled tasks.

## Are task and playbook changes audited?

Yes. Task creation, status changes, assignments, and playbook initiation are audit logged.
