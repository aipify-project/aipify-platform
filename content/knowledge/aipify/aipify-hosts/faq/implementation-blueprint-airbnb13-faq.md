# Aipify Hosts — Operations Center (Phase Airbnb 13)

## What is the Hosts Operations Center?

A single operational workspace for daily hospitality — arrivals, departures, cleaning, maintenance, guest requests, incidents, and approvals in one place.

## What sections are available?

Today, Arrivals, Departures, Cleaning, Maintenance, Guest Requests, Incidents, and Approvals.

## What does the Today dashboard show?

Arrivals today, departures today, open guest requests, pending approvals, cleaning status, maintenance status, and active incidents.

## Are operational actions audited?

Yes. Status changes, assignments, and approvals are audit logged with role-based permissions enforced.

## What notifications are supported?

Critical incidents, late cleaning completion, unassigned maintenance tasks, and outstanding approvals.

## Route

`/app/aipify-hosts/operations`
