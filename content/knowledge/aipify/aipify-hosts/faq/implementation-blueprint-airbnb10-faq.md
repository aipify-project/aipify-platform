# Aipify Hosts — Referral & Growth Engine (Phase Airbnb 10)

## What is the Referral & Growth Engine?

A transparent referral system for Aipify Hosts. Hosts, service providers, and Growth Partners can generate referral links, track conversions, monitor rewards, and download referral assets.

## Can I refer myself?

No. Self-referrals are blocked by governance rules. Duplicate referrals are also prevented and all events are audited.

## What referral statuses are tracked?

Invited → Registered → Trial Started → Converted → Active → Rewarded.

## What rewards are supported?

Fixed rewards, percentage rewards, account credits, and free property licenses. Example: refer 1 host for 1 month free additional property; refer 5 hosts for a Hosts Solo upgrade credit.

## What can Growth Partners do?

Multi-account oversight, referral reporting, commission summaries, and conversion tracking — when Growth Partner mode is enabled for the tenant.

`/app/aipify-hosts/referrals`
