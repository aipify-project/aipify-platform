# Aipify Hosts — Knowledge Center FAQ

## What is Aipify Hosts?

Aipify Hosts is an **operations platform** for hospitality businesses — guest experiences, daily operations, approvals, maintenance, and performance from one workspace. It is **not** a booking platform.
