# Aipify Hosts — Guest Intelligence & Loyalty (Phase Airbnb 04)

## What is Guest Intelligence & Loyalty?

A relationship layer that helps hosts recognize returning guests, monitor satisfaction, and act on loyalty opportunities — without treating every booking as a one-time transaction.

## Is guest data stored as chat transcripts?

No. Aipify Hosts stores operational metadata only — stay patterns, satisfaction trends, and host-approved notes. Raw guest conversations are not stored.
