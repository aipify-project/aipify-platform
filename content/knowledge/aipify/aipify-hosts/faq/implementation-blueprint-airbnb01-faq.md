# Aipify Hosts — Phase Airbnb 01

## What is Aipify Hosts?

Aipify Hosts is the **Business Operating System for modern hospitality businesses** — the operational layer around Airbnb, Booking.com, Vrbo, Expedia, and direct bookings. It does not replace booking platforms.

## Who is it for?

Short-term rental operators, Airbnb hosts, property managers, and hospitality businesses from 1 property to enterprise portfolios.

## What modules are included?

Ten operations modules: Guest Operations, Property Operations, Cleaner Operations, Guest Experience Companion, Revenue Intelligence, Property Health Score, Incident & Claims, Team Operations, Hospitality Knowledge Center, and Executive Operations Dashboard.

## What packages are available?

- **Hosts Starter** — 1–2 properties  
- **Hosts Professional** — 3–25 properties  
- **Hosts Enterprise** — 25+ properties with advanced governance

## Does Aipify compete with Airbnb?

No. Aipify Hosts empowers hosts to operate more professionally. Booking platforms remain the channel; Aipify Hosts is the operations layer.
