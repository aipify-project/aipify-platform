# Aipify Hosts — Finance Center (Phase Airbnb 19)

## What is the Hosts Finance Center?

Financial visibility for hospitality operations — overview metrics, revenue, payouts, expenses, forecasts, and exportable reports.

## What does the overview show?

Revenue this month, revenue YTD, upcoming payouts, outstanding expenses, and net performance.

## What reports can hosts export?

Revenue, expense, net performance, and property financial reports — in PDF, Excel, or CSV.

## Are financial changes audited?

Yes. Expense recording and report exports are audit logged with role-based permissions.

## Route

`/app/aipify-hosts/finance`
