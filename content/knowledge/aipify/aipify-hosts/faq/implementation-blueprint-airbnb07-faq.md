# Aipify Hosts — Hospitality Companion (Phase Airbnb 07)

## Does the Companion make decisions for hosts?

No. Aipify Companion prepares briefings, surfaces priorities, and recommends actions. Hosts approve every sensitive decision.

## What is the Daily Briefing Engine?

A proactive morning and evening summary covering arrivals, departures, maintenance, occupancy forecast, and recommended next steps.
