# Aipify Hosts — Hospitality Automation (Phase Airbnb 03)

## What is Hospitality Automation?

Aipify Hosts Hospitality Automation coordinates real-world hospitality activities through approved workflows — arrival preparation, playbooks, vendor coordination, and supply management.

## Does Aipify execute actions automatically?

No. Humans remain in control. Level 3 actions require host approval. Level 4 actions require enterprise authorization. All actions are audit logged.

## What is the Hospitality Assistant Companion?

Your daily operational briefing surface — arrivals, departures, pending tasks, and Aipify Recommendations. It is not an AI assistant and does not replace your team.
