# Aipify Hosts — Trust & Compliance (Phase Airbnb 05)

## Does Aipify provide legal advice?

No. Aipify Hosts provides regulatory reminders and compliance checklists as recommendations only. Legal responsibility remains with the host.

## What is the Trust Score Dashboard?

An operational responsibility indicator based on safety completion, incident response, community concerns, and compliance readiness — not a public guest rating.
