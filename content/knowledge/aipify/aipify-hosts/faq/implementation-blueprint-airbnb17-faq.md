# Aipify Hosts — Team Center (Phase Airbnb 17)

## What is the Hosts Team Center?

A workspace to manage hospitality teams — members, roles, permissions, invitations, and activity history.

## What roles are supported?

Owner, Property Manager, Cleaner, Maintenance, and Support — each with least-privilege permissions.

## Can I assign properties to team members?

Yes. Single or multi-property assignments control what each member can access.

## How do invitations work?

Send email invitations with role and property assignment. Statuses: Pending, Accepted, Expired, Revoked.

## Are role and permission changes audited?

Yes. Invitations, role changes, property assignments, and removals are audit logged.
