# Aipify Hosts — Knowledge Center & Self-Service (Phase Airbnb 11)

## What is the Hosts Knowledge Center?

Self-service operational guidance for Aipify Hosts — browse by topic, search globally, and read curated articles without contacting support.

## What sections are available?

Getting Started, Guest Management, Property Management, Cleaning Operations, Maintenance, Team Management, Trust & Compliance, Revenue Optimization, Incident Handling, and Billing & Licensing.

## Can I search for guidance?

Yes. Global search covers all published Hosts articles. Suggested, recently viewed, and popular articles appear on the dashboard.

## Are article updates audited?

Yes. Article versions are recorded and operational events are audit logged.
