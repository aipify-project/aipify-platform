# Aipify Hosts — Guest Center (Phase Airbnb 15)

## What is the Hosts Guest Center?

A single workspace for every guest and guest interaction — active stays, upcoming arrivals, history, requests, operational notes, and timeline events.

## What sections are available?

Active Guests, Upcoming Guests, Guest History, Guest Requests, Guest Notes, and Guest Timeline.

## What guest stay statuses are supported?

Upcoming, Checked In, Checked Out, and Cancelled.

## What request types can hosts track?

Early check-in, late check-out, maintenance, and general questions — with statuses from New through Closed.

## How are returning guests identified?

First Stay, Returning Guest, and Frequent Guest indicators help teams prepare appropriately.

## Are guest notes and request changes audited?

Yes. Note creation, profile changes, and request status updates are audit logged with role-based permissions enforced.

## What notifications are supported?

New guest requests, late check-out requests, and guests requiring attention.

## Route

`/app/aipify-hosts/guests`
