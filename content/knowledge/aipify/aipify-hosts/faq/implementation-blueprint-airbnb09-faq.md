# Aipify Hosts — Marketplace & Service Network (Phase Airbnb 09)

## Does the Marketplace process payments?

No. Phase Airbnb 09 is foundation only — hosts coordinate trusted providers through Aipify Hosts. Payment and commission models are prepared for a future phase.

## How are providers verified?

Providers must pass verification and approval workflows before marketplace publication. Only verified, published providers appear in host search results.
