# Civilizational Peacebuilding & Constructive Dialogue Engine — Phase 168 FAQ

## What is the Constructive Dialogue Center?

The Constructive Dialogue Center is Aipify's framework for healthy disagreement and civilizational peacebuilding across the Post-Enterprise & Civilizational Era (161–170). It helps organizations navigate conflict with curiosity and respect — **not** forced agreement or suppression of dissent.

## Does Aipify force consensus or suppress dissent?

No. Phase 168 explicitly supports healthy disagreement. The Dialogue Companion prepares understanding — it does **not** force consensus, suppress perspectives, or determine moral superiority. Leaders retain accountability; companions support reflection.

## How is this different from Collective Decision Council Phase 137?

Phase 137 at `/app/collective-decision-council-engine` provides the **disagreement framework** for collective decision councils — companions advise, humans decide. Phase 168 focuses on **peacebuilding and constructive dialogue** across the organization. Cross-link only — never duplicate `_cdcc_*` RPCs.

## Is this employee surveillance or dialogue scoring?

No. Constructive dialogue stores metadata-only program summaries, aggregate psychological safety themes, and executive review scaffolds. **No employee surveillance for dialogue scoring.** No ranking individuals on dialogue behavior.

## What does the Dialogue Companion do?

Reflection prompts, communication resources, perspective summaries, leadership briefings, relationship health insights (aggregate), and learning recommendations. It does **not** determine who is right, override leadership, or replace human relationships.

## Why Self Love in constructive dialogue?

Self Love (A.76) connects empathy, patience, humility, listening, compassion, and respect to difficult conversations. Cross-link `/app/self-love-engine`; do not duplicate Self Love RPCs.
