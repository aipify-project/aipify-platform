**Feature owner:** Customer App

This document defines **Phase 90 — Continuous Improvement & Organizational Evolution Engine** of the Aipify Business Operating System (ABOS). It extends the existing Continuous Improvement Engine with organizational evolution scaffolding — learning cycles, experimentation principles, companion curiosity, and cross-links to related engines.

> **Mapping:** ABOS Implementation Blueprint Phase 90 maps to **Continuous Improvement Engine Phase A.33 + A.49** at `/app/continuous-improvement-engine`. Do not duplicate Growth & Evolution A.81, Innovation Lab Phase 96, or Evolution Governance Phase 84 — extend CIE RPCs, dashboard, and ILM vocabulary only.

## Mission

Sustainable habits of learning, experimentation, and evolution through reflection and adaptation.

## Core philosophy

**Progress not perfection** — curious, humble, intentional evolution.

## ABOS principle

**Meaningful evolution through learning together** — improvement serves people and outcomes, not change for its own sake.

## Companion guidance

Curiosity not criticism — 🦉 observe patterns, 🌹 suggest gentle experiments, 🔔 celebrate learning milestones.

## Experimentation principles

Small experiments, pilots, learning reviews, safe innovation — visible stages with human approval; distinct from Innovation Lab Phase 96 validation pipeline.

## Organizational evolution

Three modes: **maintain** (preserve what works), **improve** (incremental refinement), **transform** (intentional larger change with governance).

## Learning cycle

Observe → Reflect → Experiment → Learn → Adapt → Repeat.

## Limitation principles

Avoid endless change, improvement for its own sake, and excessive optimization pressure. Meaningful evolution, not constant disruption.

## Vision

"We are becoming better because we are willing to learn together."
